Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ lavō (“wash”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.lu.oː/, [ˈeːɫ̪uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.lu.o/, [ˈɛːluo]

Verb[edit]

ēluō (present infinitive ēluere, perfect active ēluī, supine ēlūtum); third conjugation

  1. to wash out or away; to rinse
  2. to elute
