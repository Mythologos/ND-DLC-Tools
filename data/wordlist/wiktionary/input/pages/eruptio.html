Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English eruption), ultimately from Latin ēruptiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈeruptio/, [ˈe̞rupˌt̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): e‧rup‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

eruptio

  1. eruption

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From erumpō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈrup.ti.oː/, [eːˈrʊpt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈrup.t͡si.o/, [eˈrupt̪͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

ēruptiō f (genitive ēruptiōnis); third declension

  1. an eruption
  2. outburst
  3. (military) onrush, onslaught

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
