Estonian[edit]

Noun[edit]

edus

  1. inessive singular of edu

Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

etu +‎ -us

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈedus/, [ˈe̞dus̠]
  • Rhymes: -edus
  • Syllabification(key): e‧dus

Noun[edit]

edus

  1. front (of a house etc.)
  2. (archaic) representation
    Synonym: edustus

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Alternative form of haedus, showing 'rustic' monophthongization of /ae̯/ to /eː/.

Noun[edit]

ēdus m (genitive ēdī); second declension

  1. Alternative form of haedus: young goat, kid
