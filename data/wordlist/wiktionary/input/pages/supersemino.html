Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ semino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈseː.mi.noː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈs̠eːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈse.mi.no/, [superˈsɛːmino]

Verb[edit]

supersēminō (present infinitive supersēmināre, perfect active supersēmināvī, supine supersēminātum); first conjugation

  1. to plant or sow upon or over
