Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • judiciarius

Etymology[edit]

iūdicium (“judgement”) +‎ -ārius (“-ary”, “pertaining to”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i̯uː.di.kiˈaː.ri.us/, [i̯uːd̪ɪkiˈäːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ju.di.t͡ʃiˈa.ri.us/, [jud̪it͡ʃiˈäːrius]

Adjective[edit]

iūdiciārius (feminine iūdiciāria, neuter iūdiciārium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. judicial, judiciary

Declension[edit]

First/second-declension adjective.
