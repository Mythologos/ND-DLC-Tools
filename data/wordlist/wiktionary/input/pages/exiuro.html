Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ēiūrō

Etymology[edit]

From ex +‎ iūrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eksˈi̯uː.roː/, [ɛks̠ˈi̯uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eksˈju.ro/, [eksˈjuːro]

Verb[edit]

exiūrō (present infinitive exiūrāre, perfect active exiūrāvī, supine exiūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to abjure
  2. to forswear, disown
  3. (of a judge, on oath) to reject
  4. to resign
