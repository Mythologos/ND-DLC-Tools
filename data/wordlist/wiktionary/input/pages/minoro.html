Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From minor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /miˈnoː.roː/, [mɪˈnoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /miˈno.ro/, [miˈnɔːro]

Verb[edit]

minōrō (present infinitive minōrāre, perfect active minōrāvī, supine minōrātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to reduce or diminish (make smaller)
