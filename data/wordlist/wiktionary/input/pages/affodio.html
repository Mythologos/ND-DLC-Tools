Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adfodiō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards, at”) +‎ fodiō (“dig”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /afˈfo.di.oː/, [äfˈfɔd̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /afˈfo.di.o/, [äfˈfɔːd̪io]

Verb[edit]

affodiō (present infinitive affodere, perfect active affōdī, supine affossum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to dig in addition to, add by digging
