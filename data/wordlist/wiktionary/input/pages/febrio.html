Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From febris +‎ -iō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfe.bri.oː/, [ˈfɛbrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfe.bri.o/, [ˈfɛːbrio]

Verb[edit]

febriō (present infinitive febrīre, perfect active febrīvī, supine febrītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to have fever, to be feverish
  2. to be insane
