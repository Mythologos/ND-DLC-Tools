Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /uːˈber.toː/, [uːˈbɛrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /uˈber.to/, [uˈbɛrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

ūbertō (present infinitive ūbertāre, perfect active ūbertāvī, supine ūbertātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of ubero
