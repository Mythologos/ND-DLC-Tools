Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

inversus, perfect passive participle of invertō (“to invert”) +‎ -tiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈu̯er.si.oː/, [ɪnˈu̯ɛrs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈver.si.o/, [iɱˈvɛrsio]

Noun[edit]

inversiō f (genitive inversiōnis); third declension

  1. inversion
    inversiō matricis ― retroverted uterus
  2. (rhetoric) ironical inversion of meaning; allegory; transposition
