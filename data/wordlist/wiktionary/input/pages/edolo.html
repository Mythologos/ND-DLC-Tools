Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ dolō (“to hew”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.do.loː/, [ˈeːd̪ɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.do.lo/, [ˈɛːd̪olo]

Verb[edit]

ēdolō (present infinitive ēdolāre, perfect active ēdolāvī, supine ēdolātum); first conjugation

  1. to hew out, cut out
  2. to prepare, finish
