Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

lalli +‎ -us

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑlːus/, [ˈlɑ̝lːus̠]
  • Rhymes: -ɑlːus
  • Syllabification(key): lal‧lus

Noun[edit]

lallus

  1. wimp (someone who lacks confidence, is irresolute and wishy-washy)
