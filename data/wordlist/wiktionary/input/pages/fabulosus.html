Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From fābula +‎ -ōsus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /faː.buˈloː.sus/, [fäːbʊˈɫ̪oːs̠ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /fa.buˈlo.sus/, [fäbuˈlɔːs̬us]

Adjective[edit]

fābulōsus (feminine fābulōsa, neuter fābulōsum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. fabled, fabulous (celebrated in fable), legendary
