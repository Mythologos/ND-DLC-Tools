Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From de- +‎ servio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈser.u̯i.oː/, [d̪eːˈs̠ɛru̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈser.vi.o/, [d̪eˈs̬ɛrvio]

Verb[edit]

dēserviō (present infinitive dēservīre, perfect active dēservīvī, supine dēservītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to serve zealously
  2. to devote (oneself) to
  3. to be subject to

Conjugation[edit]

  • Perfect and supine forms are post-Classical.
