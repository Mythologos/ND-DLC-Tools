Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtrāctō
  • adtrectō
  • attrectō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /atˈtrak.toː/, [ät̪ˈt̪räkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /atˈtrak.to/, [ät̪ˈt̪räkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

attractō (present infinitive attractāre, perfect active attractāvī, supine attractātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attrectō
  2. (Medieval Latin) to attract, persuade
  3. (Medieval Latin) to collect
