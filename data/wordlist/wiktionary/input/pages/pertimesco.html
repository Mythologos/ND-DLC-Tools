Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.tiˈmeːs.koː/, [pɛrt̪ɪˈmeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.tiˈmes.ko/, [pert̪iˈmɛsko]

Verb[edit]

pertimēscō (present infinitive pertimēscere, perfect active pertimuī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. to become very frightened
  2. (transitive) to fear greatly, to dread
