Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnal.toː/, [ɪˈnäɫ̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈnal.to/, [iˈnäl̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

inaltō (present infinitive inaltāre, perfect active inaltāvī, supine inaltātum); first conjugation

  1. to raise on high; to exalt
