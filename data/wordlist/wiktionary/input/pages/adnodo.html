Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • annōdō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ nōdō (“knot”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈnoː.doː/, [änˈnoːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈno.do/, [äd̪ˈnɔːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

adnōdō (present infinitive adnōdāre, perfect active adnōdāvī, supine adnōdātum); first conjugation

  1. (of knots, shoots or suckers of vines) to cut off, back or away
