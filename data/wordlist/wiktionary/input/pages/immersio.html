Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English immersion), ultimately from Late Latin immersiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈimːersio/, [ˈimːe̞rˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): im‧mer‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

immersio

  1. immersion

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From immergō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /imˈmer.si.oː/, [ɪmˈmɛrs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /imˈmer.si.o/, [imˈmɛrsio]

Noun[edit]

immersiō f (genitive immersiōnis); third declension

  1. immersion

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
