Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ob- +‎ petō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈop.pe.toː/, [ˈɔpːɛt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈop.pe.to/, [ˈɔpːet̪o]

Verb[edit]

oppetō (present infinitive oppetere, perfect active oppetīvī or oppetiī, supine oppetītum); third conjugation

  1. to go to meet; to encounter
  2. to perish (encounter death)
