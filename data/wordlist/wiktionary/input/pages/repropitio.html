Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ propitio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.proˈpi.ti.oː/, [rɛprɔˈpɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.proˈpit.t͡si.o/, [reproˈpit̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

repropitiō (present infinitive repropitiāre, perfect active repropitiāvī, supine repropitiātum); first conjugation

  1. to propitiate again
