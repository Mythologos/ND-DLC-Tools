Esperanto[edit]

Noun[edit]

sepio (accusative singular sepion, plural sepioj, accusative plural sepiojn)

  1. squid

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈseː.pi.oː/, [ˈs̠eːpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈse.pi.o/, [ˈsɛːpio]

Verb[edit]

sēpiō (present infinitive sēpīre, perfect active sēpsī, supine sēptum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of saepiō
