Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ veho.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.u̯e.hoː/, [ˈreu̯e(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ve.o/, [ˈrɛːveo]

Verb[edit]

revehō (present infinitive revehere, perfect active revē̆xī, supine revectum); third conjugation

  1. to carry, bear or convey back
