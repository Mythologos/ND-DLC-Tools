Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ glōrificō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.ɡloːˈri.fi.koː/, [kɔŋɡɫ̪oːˈrɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.ɡloˈri.fi.ko/, [koŋɡloˈriːfiko]

Verb[edit]

conglōrificō (present infinitive conglōrificāre, perfect active conglōrificāvī, supine conglōrificātum); first conjugation

  1. to glorify with others
