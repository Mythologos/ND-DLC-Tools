Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pri.moː/, [ˈäpːrɪmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pri.mo/, [ˈäd̪primo]

Verb[edit]

adprimō (present infinitive adprimere, perfect active adpressī, supine adpressum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of apprimō (“to press close to”)
