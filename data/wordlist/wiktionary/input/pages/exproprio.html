Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ proprius +‎ -ō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈspro.pri.oː/, [ɛkˈs̠prɔprioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈspro.pri.o/, [ekˈsprɔːprio]

Verb[edit]

expropriō (present infinitive expropriāre, perfect active expropriāvī, supine expropriātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) to expropriate
