Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ relinquō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.reˈlin.kʷoː/, [d̪eːrɛˈlʲɪŋkʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.reˈlin.kwo/, [d̪ereˈliŋkwo]

Verb[edit]

dērelinquō (present infinitive dērelinquere, perfect active dērelīquī, supine dērelictum); third conjugation

  1. to forsake, abandon, discard or desert
  2. to bequeath
