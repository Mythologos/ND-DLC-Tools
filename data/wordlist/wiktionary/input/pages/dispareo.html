Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ pāreō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈpaː.re.oː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈpäːreoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈpa.re.o/, [d̪isˈpäːreo]

Verb[edit]

dispāreō (present infinitive dispārēre, perfect active dispāruī, supine dispāritum); second conjugation

  1. to vanish, disappear
