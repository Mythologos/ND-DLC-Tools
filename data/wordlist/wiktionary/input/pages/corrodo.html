Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

corrodo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of corrodere

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conrōdō

Etymology[edit]

From con- (“completely”) +‎ rōdō (“I gnaw”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /korˈroː.doː/, [kɔrˈroːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /korˈro.do/, [korˈrɔːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

corrōdō (present infinitive corrōdere, perfect active corrōsī, supine corrōsum); third conjugation

  1. to gnaw (to pieces)
