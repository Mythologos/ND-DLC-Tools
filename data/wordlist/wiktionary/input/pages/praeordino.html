Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ ordino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈoːr.di.noː/, [präe̯ˈoːrd̪ɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈor.di.no/, [preˈɔrd̪ino]

Verb[edit]

praeōrdinō (present infinitive praeōrdināre, perfect active praeōrdināvī, supine praeōrdinātum); first conjugation

  1. to preordain
