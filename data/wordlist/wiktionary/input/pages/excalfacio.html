Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ calfaciō.

Verb[edit]

excalfaciō (present infinitive excalfacere, supine excalfactum); third conjugation iō-variant, no perfect stem, irregular passive voice

  1. to warm, heat[1]
