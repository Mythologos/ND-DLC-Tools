English[edit]

Chemical element
Am
Previous: plutonium (Pu)
Next: curium (Cm)

Etymology[edit]

From Americ(as) +‎ -ium, by analogy with europium.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: ămərĭs'ēəm, IPA(key): /ˌæməˈɹɪsiəm/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)

Noun[edit]

americium (usually uncountable, plural americiums)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
americium
Wikipedia
  2. The chemical element (symbol Am) with an atomic number of 95.
