Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From valē (“farewell”) +‎ dīcō (“I say”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯a.leˈdiː.koː/, [u̯äɫ̪ɛˈd̪iːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /va.leˈdi.ko/, [väleˈd̪iːko]

Verb[edit]

valedīcō (present infinitive valedīcere, perfect active valedīxī, supine valedictum); third conjugation, irregular short imperative

  1. to bid farewell
  2. to give a valediction
