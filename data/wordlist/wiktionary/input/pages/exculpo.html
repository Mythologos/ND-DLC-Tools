Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

exculpo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of exculpar

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈskul.poː/, [ɛkˈs̠kʊɫ̪poː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈskul.po/, [ekˈskulpo]

Verb[edit]

exculpō (present infinitive exculpere, perfect active exculpsī, supine exculptum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exsculpō
