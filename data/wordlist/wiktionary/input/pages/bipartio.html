Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From bi- +‎ partio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /biˈpar.ti.oː/, [bɪˈpärt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /biˈpar.t͡si.o/, [biˈpärt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

bipartiō (present infinitive bipartīre, perfect active bipartīvī, supine bipartītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to divide in two
  2. to bisect
