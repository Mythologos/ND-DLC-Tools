Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • rejiciō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /rei̯ˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [rɛi̯ˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /rejˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [rejˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

reiiciō (present infinitive reiicere, perfect active reiēcī, supine reiectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of reiciō
