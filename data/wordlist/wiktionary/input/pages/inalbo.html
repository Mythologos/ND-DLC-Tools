Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /iˈnal.bo/
  • Rhymes: -albo
  • Hyphenation: i‧nàl‧bo

Verb[edit]

inalbo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of inalbare

Anagrams[edit]

  • Alboni, Libano, albino, bolina

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ albō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnal.boː/, [ɪˈnäɫ̪boː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈnal.bo/, [iˈnälbo]

Verb[edit]

inalbō (present infinitive inalbāre, perfect active inalbāvī, supine inalbātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to make white, whiten
    Synonyms: albō, albicō, (Late Latin) exalbō
  2. (transitive) to lighten
