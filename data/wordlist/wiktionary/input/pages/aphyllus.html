Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek ἄφῠλλος (áphullos, “leafless”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈpʰyl.lus/, [äˈpʰʏlːʲʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈfil.lus/, [äˈfilːus]

Adjective[edit]

aphyllus (feminine aphylla, neuter aphyllum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (New Latin, botany) leafless, aphyllous
