Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈɡuː.noː/, [d̪eːˈɡuːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈɡu.no/, [d̪eˈɡuːno]

Verb[edit]

dēgūnō (present infinitive dēgūnāre, perfect active dēgūnāvī, supine dēgūnātum); first conjugation

  1. to taste
  2. to glance at
  3. to try
  4. to test
