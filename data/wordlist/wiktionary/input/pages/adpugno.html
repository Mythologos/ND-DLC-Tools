Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • appugnō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈpuɡ.noː/, [äpˈpʊŋnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈpuɲ.ɲo/, [äd̪ˈpuɲːo]

Verb[edit]

adpugnō (present infinitive adpugnāre, perfect active adpugnāvī, supine adpugnātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of appugnō
