Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • abligūrriō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ab.liˈɡuː.ri.oː/, [äblʲɪˈɡuːrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ab.liˈɡu.ri.o/, [äbliˈɡuːrio]

Verb[edit]

abligūriō (present infinitive abligūrīre, perfect active abligūrīvī or abligūriī, supine abligūrītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of abligūrriō (“to lick away; to squander”)
