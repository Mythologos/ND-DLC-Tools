Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbal.tʰi.kus/, [ˈbäɫ̪t̪ʰɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈbal.ti.kus/, [ˈbäl̪t̪ikus]

Adjective[edit]

balthicus (feminine balthica, neuter balthicum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (relational) Baltic
