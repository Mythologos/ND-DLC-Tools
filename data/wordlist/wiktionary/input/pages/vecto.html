Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯ek.toː/, [ˈu̯ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvek.to/, [ˈvɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

vectō (present infinitive vectāre, perfect active vectāvī, supine vectātum); first conjugation

  1. to bear, carry, convey
