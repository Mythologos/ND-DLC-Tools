Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.troːˈrum.poː/, [ɪn̪t̪roːˈrʊmpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.troˈrum.po/, [in̪t̪roˈrumpo]

Verb[edit]

intrōrumpō (present infinitive intrōrumpere, perfect active intrōrūpī, supine intrōruptum); third conjugation

  1. to break in
