Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English symmetry), ultimately from Latin symmetria. Contains the suffix -metria.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsymːetriɑ/, [ˈs̠ymːe̞t̪ˌriɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -iɑ
  • Syllabification(key): sym‧met‧ri‧a

Noun[edit]

symmetria

  1. symmetry

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek συμμετρία (summetría).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /symˈme.tri.a/, [s̠ʏmˈmɛt̪riä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /simˈme.tri.a/, [simˈmɛːt̪riä]

Noun[edit]

symmetria f (genitive symmetriae); first declension

  1. symmetry, proportion

Declension[edit]
