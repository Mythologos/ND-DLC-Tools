Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯o.luks/, [ˈu̯ɔɫ̪ʊks̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvo.luks/, [ˈvɔːluks]

Proper noun[edit]

Volux m sg (genitive Volucis); third declension

  1. The son of Bocchus, king of Mauretania

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun, singular only.
