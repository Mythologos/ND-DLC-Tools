Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ante- +‎ mittō (“to send”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /an.teˈmit.toː/, [än̪t̪ɛˈmɪt̪ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /an.teˈmit.to/, [än̪t̪eˈmit̪ːo]

Verb[edit]

antemittō (present infinitive antemittere, perfect active antemīsī, supine antemissum); third conjugation

  1. (transitive, rare) to send ahead
