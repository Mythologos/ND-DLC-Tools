Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ɡe.roː/, [ˈäɡːɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.d͡ʒe.ro/, [ˈädː͡ʒero]

Etymology 1[edit]

From ad- +‎ gerō (“bear, carry”).

Verb[edit]

adgerō (present infinitive adgerere, perfect active adgessī, supine adgestum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of aggerō
