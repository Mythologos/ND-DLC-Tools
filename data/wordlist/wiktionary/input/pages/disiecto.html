Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈi̯ek.toː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈi̯ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈjek.to/, [d̪isˈjɛkt̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

From disiciō +‎ -tō.

Verb[edit]

disiectō (present infinitive disiectāre, perfect active disiectāvī, supine disiectātum); first conjugation

  1. to scatter, disperse
