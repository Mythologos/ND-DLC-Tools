Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.kʰo.oː/, [ˈɪŋkʰoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.ko.o/, [ˈiŋkoː]

Verb[edit]

inchoō (present infinitive inchoāre, perfect active inchoāvī, supine inchoātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of incohō
