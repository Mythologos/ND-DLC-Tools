Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.pe.toː/, [ˈs̠ʊpːɛt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.pe.to/, [ˈsubpet̪o]

Verb[edit]

subpetō (present infinitive subpetere, perfect active subpetīvī or subpetiī, supine subpetītum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of suppetō
