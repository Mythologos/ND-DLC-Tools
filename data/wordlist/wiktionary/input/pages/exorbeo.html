Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • exsorbeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsor.be.oː/, [ɛkˈs̠ɔrbeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsor.be.o/, [eɡˈzɔrbeo]

Verb[edit]

exorbeō (present infinitive exorbēre, perfect active exorbuī, supine exorptum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exsorbeō
