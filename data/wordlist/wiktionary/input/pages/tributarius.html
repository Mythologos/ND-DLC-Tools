Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From tribūtum (“tax, tribute”) +‎ -ārius.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /tri.buːˈtaː.ri.us/, [t̪rɪbuːˈt̪äːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tri.buˈta.ri.us/, [t̪ribuˈt̪äːrius]

Adjective[edit]

tribūtārius (feminine tribūtāria, neuter tribūtārium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (relational) tribute
