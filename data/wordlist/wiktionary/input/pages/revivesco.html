Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.u̯iːˈu̯eːs.koː/, [reu̯iːˈu̯eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.viˈves.ko/, [reviˈvɛsko]

Verb[edit]

revīvēscō (present infinitive revīvēscere, perfect active revīxī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of revīvīscō
