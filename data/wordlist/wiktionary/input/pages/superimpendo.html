Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ impendo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.pe.rimˈpen.doː/, [s̠ʊpɛrɪmˈpɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.pe.rimˈpen.do/, [superimˈpɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

superimpendō (present infinitive superimpendere, perfect active superimpendī, supine superimpēnsum); third conjugation

  1. to spend or exhaust anything
