Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • saepēs

Etymology[edit]

Monophthongized 'rustic' counterpart to saepēs. From Proto-Indo-European *seh₂ip- (“to cram, fence”).

Noun[edit]

sēpēs f (genitive sēpis); third declension

  1. hedge, fence

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (i-stem).
