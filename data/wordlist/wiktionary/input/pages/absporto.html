Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈspor.toː/, [äpˈs̠pɔrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈspor.to/, [äbˈspɔrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

absportō (present infinitive absportāre, perfect active absportāvī, supine absportātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of asporto
