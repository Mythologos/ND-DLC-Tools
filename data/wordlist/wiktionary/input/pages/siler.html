Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unknown.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsi.ler/, [ˈs̠ɪɫ̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsi.ler/, [ˈsiːler]

Noun[edit]

siler n (genitive sileris); third declension

  1. the spindle tree (Euonymus)
  2. a kind of brook-willow

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (neuter, imparisyllabic non-i-stem).
