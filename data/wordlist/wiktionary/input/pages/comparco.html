Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈpar.koː/, [kɔmˈpärkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈpar.ko/, [komˈpärko]

Verb[edit]

comparcō (present infinitive comparcere, perfect active comparsī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of compercō
