Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- (“above, on”) +‎ fulgeō (“I shine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈful.ɡe.oː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈfʊɫ̪ɡeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈful.d͡ʒe.o/, [superˈful̠ʲd͡ʒeo]

Verb[edit]

superfulgeō (present infinitive superfulgēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (transitive) to shine forth over
