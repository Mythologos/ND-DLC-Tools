Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

morsico

  1. first-person singular present indicative of morsicare

Anagrams[edit]

  • Morisco

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From morsus +‎ -icō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈmor.si.koː/, [ˈmɔrs̠ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈmor.si.ko/, [ˈmɔrsiko]

Verb[edit]

morsicō (present infinitive morsicāre, perfect active morsicāvī, supine morsicātum); first conjugation (post-classical)

  1. to bite
  2. to press one's lips together (as in kissing)
