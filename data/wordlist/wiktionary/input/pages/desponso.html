Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From de- +‎ spōnsō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːsˈpon.soː/, [d̪eːs̠ˈpõːs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /desˈpon.so/, [d̪esˈpɔnso]

Verb[edit]

dēspōnsō (present infinitive dēspōnsāre, perfect active dēspōnsāvī, supine dēspōnsātum); first conjugation

  1. to betroth
