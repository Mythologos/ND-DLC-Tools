Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /opˈpan.doː/, [ɔpˈpän̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /opˈpan.do/, [opˈpän̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

oppandō (present infinitive oppandere, perfect active oppandī, supine oppānsum); third conjugation

  1. to spread or stretch out

Conjugation[edit]
