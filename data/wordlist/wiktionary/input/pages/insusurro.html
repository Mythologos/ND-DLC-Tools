Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.suˈsur.roː/, [ĩːs̠ʊˈs̠ʊrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.suˈsur.ro/, [insuˈs̬urːo]

Verb[edit]

īnsusurrō (present infinitive īnsusurrāre, perfect active īnsusurrāvī, supine īnsusurrātum); first conjugation

  1. to insinuate
  2. to suggest
  3. to whisper
