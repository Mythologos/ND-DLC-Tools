Italian[edit]

Noun[edit]

barbio m (plural barbi)

  1. barbel (fish)

Anagrams[edit]

  • rabbio, robbia

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From barba (“beard”) +‎ -iō.

Verb[edit]

barbiō (present infinitive barbīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (Late Latin) to grow one's beard
