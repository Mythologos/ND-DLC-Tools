Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cōnquinō

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ inquinō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈin.kʷi.noː/, [koˈɪŋkʷɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈin.kwi.no/, [koˈiŋkwino]

Verb[edit]

coinquinō (present infinitive coinquināre, perfect active coinquināvī, supine coinquinātum); first conjugation

  1. to defile, pollute or contaminate all over
