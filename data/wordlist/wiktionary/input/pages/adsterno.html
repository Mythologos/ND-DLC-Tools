Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈser.u̯i.oː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɛru̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈser.vi.o/, [äd̪ˈsɛrvio]

Verb[edit]

adsternō (present infinitive adsternere, perfect active adstrāvī, supine adstrātum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of asternō
