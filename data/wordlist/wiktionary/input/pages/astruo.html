Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ struō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈas.tru.oː/, [ˈäs̠t̪ruoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈas.tru.o/, [ˈäst̪ruo]

Verb[edit]

astruō (present infinitive astruere, perfect active astrūxī, supine astrūctum); third conjugation

  1. to build near or to a thing, to erect
  2. to build on, heap; build an additional structure
