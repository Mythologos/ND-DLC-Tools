Indonesian[edit]

Indonesian Wikipedia has an article on:
analis
Wikipedia id

Etymology[edit]

From Dutch analist.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈanalɪs]
  • Hyphenation: ana‧lis

Noun[edit]

analis (first-person possessive analisku, second-person possessive analismu, third-person possessive analisnya)

  1. analyst

Derived terms[edit]

  • analis kredit
  • analis pangkalan data
  • analis sistem komputer
 

Related terms[edit]

  • analisis
 

Further reading[edit]

  • “analis” in Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia, Jakarta: Language Development and Fostering Agency — Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology of the Republic Indonesia, 2016.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ānus +‎ -ālis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aːˈnaː.lis/, [äːˈnäːlʲɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈna.lis/, [äˈnäːlis]

Adjective[edit]

ānālis (neuter ānāle); third-declension two-termination adjective (New Latin)

  1. (New Latin) anal
  2. (New Latin) ringed (as a taxonomic epithet)
