English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Croatia
Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Croätia

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Medieval Latin Croātia, equivalent to modern Croat +‎ -ia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˌkɹəʊˈeɪ.ʃə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /ˌkɹoʊˈeɪ.ʃə/, /-ʃi.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Croatia

  2. A country on the Balkan Peninsula in Southeast Europe. Official name: Republic of Croatia. Capital and largest city: Zagreb. Part of Yugoslavia until 1991.

Related terms[edit]

  • Croat
  • Croatian
