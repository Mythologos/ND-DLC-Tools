Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ sternō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈster.noː/, [pɛrˈs̠t̪ɛrnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈster.no/, [perˈst̪ɛrno]

Verb[edit]

persternō (present infinitive persternere, perfect active perstrāvī, supine perstrātum); third conjugation

  1. to level or stretch something out evenly
  2. to pave all over
