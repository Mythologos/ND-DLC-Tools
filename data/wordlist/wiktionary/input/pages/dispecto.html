Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From dispiciō +‎ -tō.

Verb[edit]

dispectō (present infinitive dispectāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to consider, contemplate, examine
  2. to perceive, discern, discover
