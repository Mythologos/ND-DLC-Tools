Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈkom.mo.doː/, [äkˈkɔmːɔd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈkom.mo.do/, [äd̪ˈkɔmːod̪o]

Verb[edit]

adcommodō (present infinitive adcommodāre, perfect active adcommodāvī, supine adcommodātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of accommodō
