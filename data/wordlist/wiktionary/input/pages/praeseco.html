Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ seco.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈprae̯.se.koː/, [ˈpräe̯s̠ɛkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpre.se.ko/, [ˈprɛːs̬eko]

Verb[edit]

praesecō (present infinitive praesecāre, perfect active praesecuī, supine praesectum or praesecātum); first conjugation

  1. to cut off in front
