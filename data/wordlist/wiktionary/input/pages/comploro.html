Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ plōrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈploː.roː/, [kɔmˈpɫ̪oːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈplo.ro/, [komˈplɔːro]

Verb[edit]

complōrō (present infinitive complōrāre, perfect active complōrāvī, supine complōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to bewail, lament together loudly or violently
