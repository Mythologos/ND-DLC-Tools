Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • afformīdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.forˈmiː.doː/, [ät̪fɔrˈmiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.forˈmi.do/, [äd̪forˈmiːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

adformīdō (present infinitive adformīdāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of afformīdō
