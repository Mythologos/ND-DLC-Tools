Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯i̯ˈi̯uː.di.koː/, [präe̯i̯ˈi̯uːd̪ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pra.eˈju.di.ko/, [präeˈjuːd̪iko]

Verb[edit]

praejūdicō (present infinitive praejūdicāre, perfect active praejūdicāvī, supine praejūdicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of praeiūdicō
