Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

cōnus (“cone”) +‎ -oīdeus

Adjective[edit]

cōnoīdeus (feminine cōnoīdea, neuter cōnoīdeum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (New Latin) cone-shaped, conical
