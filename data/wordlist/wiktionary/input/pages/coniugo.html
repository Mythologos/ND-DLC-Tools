Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɔ.nju.ɡo/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɔnjuɡo
  • Hyphenation: cò‧niu‧go

Verb[edit]

coniugo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of coniugare

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conjugō

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ iugō (“I join, yoke”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.i̯u.ɡoː/, [ˈkɔni̯ʊɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.ju.ɡo/, [ˈkɔnjuɡo]

Verb[edit]

coniugō (present infinitive coniugāre, perfect active coniugāvī, supine coniugātum); first conjugation

  1. to unite, especially in marriage
