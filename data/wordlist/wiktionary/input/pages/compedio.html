Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈpe.di.oː/, [kɔmˈpɛd̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈpe.di.o/, [komˈpɛːd̪io]

Verb[edit]

compediō (present infinitive compedīre, perfect active compedīvī, supine compedītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to shackle, fetter
