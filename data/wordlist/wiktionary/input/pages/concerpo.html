Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ carpō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈker.poː/, [kɔŋˈkɛrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈt͡ʃer.po/, [kon̠ʲˈt͡ʃɛrpo]

Verb[edit]

concerpō (present infinitive concerpere, perfect active concerpsī, supine concerptum); third conjugation

  1. to tear to pieces
  2. to pluck off
  3. to abuse, revile, censure
