English[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • minæ

Noun[edit]

minae

  1. plural of mina

Anagrams[edit]

  • -amine, I mean, Maine, amine, anime, animé, maine, manie

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈmi.nae̯/, [ˈmɪnäe̯]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈmi.ne/, [ˈmiːne]

Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Italic *(eks)menā (“projection”), *menā, from Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to stand out”). See Latin minor, mōns, mentum.

Noun[edit]

minae f pl (genitive minārum); first declension

  1. projecting points, pinnacles, battlements, parapets
  2. (figuratively) threats, menaces
Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, plural only.
