Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • pejūrō

Etymology[edit]

From iūrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /pei̯ˈi̯uː.roː/, [pɛi̯ˈi̯uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /peˈju.ro/, [peˈjuːro]

Verb[edit]

peiūrō (present infinitive peiūrāre, perfect active peiūrāvī, supine peiūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to lie
  2. to swear false oath
  3. to swear falsely
