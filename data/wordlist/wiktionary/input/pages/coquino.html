Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From coquīnus +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈkʷiː.noː/, [kɔˈkʷiːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈkwi.no/, [koˈkwiːno]

Verb[edit]

coquīnō (present infinitive coquīnāre, perfect active coquīnāvī, supine coquīnātum); first conjugation

  1. to cook (professionally)
