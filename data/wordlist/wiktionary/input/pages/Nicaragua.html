Etymology[edit]

Native town name Nicarao + Spanish agua (“water”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˌnɪk.əɹˈæɡ.ju.ə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /ˌnɪk.əˈɹɑ.ɡwə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Nicaragua

  2. A country in Central America. Official name: Republic of Nicaragua.

Related terms[edit]

  • Nicaraguan
  • Nicaragua wood

Spanish[edit]

Spanish Wikipedia has an article on:
Nicaragua
Wikipedia es

Etymology[edit]

Unknown. One theory is that it was coined by Spanish colonists based upon the name of the local chief at that time, Classical Nahuatl Nicarao (“Nicarao”) + agua (“water”). Another is that it may have meant ‘surrounded by water’ in an indigenous language. (This could either be a reference to its two large freshwater lakes, Lake Nicaragua and Lake Managua, or to the fact that it bounded on the east and the west by oceans.)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /nikaˈɾaɡwa/ [ni.kaˈɾa.ɣ̞wa]
  • Rhymes: -aɡwa
