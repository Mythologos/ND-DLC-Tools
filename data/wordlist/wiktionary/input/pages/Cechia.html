Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈt͡ʃɛ.kja/
  • Rhymes: -ɛkja
  • Hyphenation: Cè‧chia

Proper noun[edit]

Cechia f

  1. (rare) Czech Republic, Czechia (a country in Central Europe)
    Synonym: (formal, common) Repubblica Ceca

Pronunciation[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

Cechia f sg (genitive Cechiae); first declension
