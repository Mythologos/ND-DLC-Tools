Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈtin.ɡʷoː/, [ɪn̪ˈt̪ɪŋɡʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈtin.ɡwo/, [in̪ˈt̪iŋɡwo]

Verb[edit]

intinguō (present infinitive intinguere, perfect active intīnxī, supine intīnctum); third conjugation

  1. to plunge in
  2. to steep or soak
  3. to colour (with cosmetics)
