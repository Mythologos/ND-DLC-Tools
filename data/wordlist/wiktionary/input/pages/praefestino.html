Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ festino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.fesˈtiː.noː/, [präe̯fɛs̠ˈt̪iːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.fesˈti.no/, [prefesˈt̪iːno]

Verb[edit]

praefestīnō (present infinitive praefestīnāre, perfect active praefestīnāvī, supine praefestīnātum); first conjugation

  1. to be overhasty
  2. to hasten past
