Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnark.toː/, [ɪˈnärkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈnark.to/, [iˈnärkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

inarctō (present infinitive inarctāre, perfect active inarctāvī, supine inarctātum); first conjugation

  1. to circumscribe or limit
