Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.kreːˈbeːs.koː/, [pɛrkreːˈbeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.kreˈbes.ko/, [perkreˈbɛsko]

Verb[edit]

percrēbēscō (present infinitive percrēbēscere, perfect active percrēbuī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of percrēbrēscō
    • 1832, Gregory XVI, Mirari Vos:
      Institutis enim exemploque praeceptorum, corruptis adolescentium animis, ingens religionis clades, morumque perversitas teterrima percrebuit.
