Latin[edit]

Latin Wikipedia has an article on:
servitudo
Wikipedia la

Etymology[edit]

From servus (“servile”) +‎ -tūdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ser.u̯iˈtuː.doː/, [s̠ɛru̯ɪˈt̪uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ser.viˈtu.do/, [serviˈt̪uːd̪o]

Noun[edit]

servitūdō f (genitive servitūdinis); third declension

  1. (rare) servitude, slavery
    Synonyms: servitūs, servitium
    Antonym: lībertās
