Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈi̯u.e.roː/, [äd̪ˈi̯uɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈju.e.ro/, [äd̪ˈjuːero]

Verb[edit]

adjuerō (present infinitive adjuerāre, perfect active adjuerāvī, supine adjuerātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of adiuvo
