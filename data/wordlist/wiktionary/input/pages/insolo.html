Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the phrase in sōlem ("into the sun") +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈsoː.loː/, [ĩːˈs̠oːɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈso.lo/, [inˈsɔːlo]

Verb[edit]

īnsōlō (present infinitive īnsōlāre, perfect active īnsōlāvī, supine īnsōlātum); first conjugation

  1. to insolate, to expose to the sun
