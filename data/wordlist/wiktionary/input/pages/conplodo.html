Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈploː.doː/, [kɔmˈpɫ̪oːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈplo.do/, [konˈplɔːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

conplōdō (present infinitive conplōdere, perfect active conplōsī, supine conplōsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of complōdō
