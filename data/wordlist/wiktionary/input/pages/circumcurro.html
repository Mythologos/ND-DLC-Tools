Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- (“circum-”) +‎ currō (“I run”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kunˈkur.roː/, [kɪrkʊŋˈkʊrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kunˈkur.ro/, [t͡ʃirkuŋˈkurːo]

Verb[edit]

circumcurrō (present infinitive circumcurrere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to run around
