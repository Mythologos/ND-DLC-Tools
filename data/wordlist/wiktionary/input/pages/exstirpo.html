Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • extirpō

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ stirps +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈstir.poː/, [ɛkˈs̠t̪ɪrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈstir.po/, [ekˈst̪irpo]

Verb[edit]

exstirpō (present infinitive exstirpāre, perfect active exstirpāvī, supine exstirpātum); first conjugation

  1. to uproot, root out
    Synonym: ērādīcō
  2. to eradicate, extirpate
