Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ko.o.perˈtuː.ra/, [koɔpɛrˈt̪uːrä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ko.o.perˈtu.ra/, [koːperˈt̪uːrä]

Etymology 1[edit]

coopertum +‎ -tūra

Noun[edit]

coopertūra f (genitive coopertūrae); first declension

  1. (Late Latin) covering, veil
    Synonym: vestīmentum
