Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ab- +‎ habeō (“have”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈhi.be.oː/, [äbˈ(ɦ)ɪbeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈbi.be.o/, [äˈbiːbeo]

Verb[edit]

abhibeō (present infinitive abhibēre, perfect active abhibuī, supine abhibitum); second conjugation

  1. (transitive) to keep off, keep away, keep at a distance
