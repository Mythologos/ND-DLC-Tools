Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prō- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈpun.ɡoː/, [proːˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈpun.ɡo/, [proˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

prōpungō (present infinitive prōpungere, perfect active prōpūnxī, supine prōpūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to prick in front, to puncture in front
