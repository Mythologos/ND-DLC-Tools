Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sufˈfuː.ror/, [s̠ʊfˈfuːrɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sufˈfu.ror/, [sufˈfuːror]

Verb[edit]

suffūror (present infinitive suffūrārī, perfect active suffūrātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to filch (steal secretly)
