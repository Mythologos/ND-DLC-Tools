Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From novus +‎ -ellus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /noˈu̯el.lus/, [noˈu̯ɛlːʲʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /noˈvel.lus/, [noˈvɛlːus]

Adjective[edit]

novellus (feminine novella, neuter novellum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (for living things) new, young, fresh
  2. novel, newly made
