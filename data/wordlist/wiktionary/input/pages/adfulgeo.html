Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • affulgeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈful.ɡe.oː/, [ät̪ˈfʊɫ̪ɡeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈful.d͡ʒe.o/, [äd̪ˈful̠ʲd͡ʒeo]

Verb[edit]

adfulgeō (present infinitive adfulgēre, perfect active adfulsī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of affulgeō
    • 9 CE, Ovid, The Ibis 209–210:
      Nōn Venus adfulsit, nōn illā Iuppiter hōrā,
           Lūnaque nōn aptō Sōlque fuēre locō.
      Venus didn't shine, Jupiter not at that time,
           neither the Moon nor the Sun were at the right place.
