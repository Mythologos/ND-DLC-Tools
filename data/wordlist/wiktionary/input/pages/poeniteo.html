Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /poe̯ˈni.te.oː/, [poe̯ˈnɪt̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /peˈni.te.o/, [peˈniːt̪eo]

Verb[edit]

poeniteō (present infinitive poenitēre, perfect active poenituī, future participle poenitūrus); second conjugation, no supine stem except in the future active participle, impersonal in the passive

  1. Alternative form of paeniteō
    In singulīs āctiōnibus tē ipse interrogā: Quōmodo haec mihi habet? Num eius mē poenitēbit?
    (please add an English translation of this usage example)
    In each of your actions, ask yourself: How does it appear to me? Am I going to repent it?
