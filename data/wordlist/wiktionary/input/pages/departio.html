Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- (“away from”) +‎ partiō (“part, divide”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈpar.ti.oː/, [d̪eːˈpärt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈpar.t͡si.o/, [d̪eˈpärt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

dēpartiō (present infinitive dēpartīre, perfect active dēpartīvī, supine dēpartītum); fourth conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to divide
