Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dis- (“in all directions”) +‎ sēminō (“I plant”, “I sow”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈseː.mi.noː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈs̠eːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈse.mi.no/, [d̪isˈsɛːmino]

Verb[edit]

dissēminō (present infinitive dissēmināre, perfect active dissēmināvī, supine dissēminātum); first conjugation

  1. to broadcast, disseminate
