Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /deˈlu.do/
  • Rhymes: -udo
  • Hyphenation: de‧lù‧do

Verb[edit]

deludo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of deludere

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ lūdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈluː.doː/, [d̪eːˈɫ̪uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈlu.do/, [d̪eˈluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

dēlūdō (present infinitive dēlūdere, perfect active dēlūsī, supine dēlūsum); third conjugation

  1. to deceive or dupe
  2. to mock
