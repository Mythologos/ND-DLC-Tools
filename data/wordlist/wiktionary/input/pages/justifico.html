Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i̯uːsˈti.fi.koː/, [i̯uːs̠ˈt̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /jusˈti.fi.ko/, [jusˈt̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

jūstificō (present infinitive jūstificāre, perfect active jūstificāvī, supine jūstificātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iūstifico
