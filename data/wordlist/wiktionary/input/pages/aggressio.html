Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑɡresːio/, [ˈɑ̝ɡre̞s̠ˌs̠io̞]
  • IPA(key): /ˈɑɡːresːio/, [ˈɑ̝ɡːre̞s̠ˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): agg‧res‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

aggressio

  1. aggression

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From aggredior (“advance, approach”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aɡˈɡres.si.oː/, [äɡˈɡrɛs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aɡˈɡres.si.o/, [äɡˈɡrɛsːio]

Noun[edit]

aggressiō f (genitive aggressiōnis); third declension

  1. advancing, approaching
  2. approaching, addressing
  3. aggression, attack
    Synonyms: impressiō, impetus, invāsiō, assultus, incursiō, oppugnātiō, incursus, appetītus, petītiō, ictus, occursio, concursus, vīs, procella
