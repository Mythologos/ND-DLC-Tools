Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • dēglūciō
  • dēglūtiō

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ gluttiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈɡlut.ti.oː/, [d̪eːˈɡɫ̪ʊt̪ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈɡlut.ti.o/, [d̪eˈɡlut̪ːio]

Verb[edit]

dēgluttiō (present infinitive dēgluttīre, perfect active dēgluttīvī or dēgluttiī, supine dēgluttītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to swallow down
  2. to overwhelm or abolish
