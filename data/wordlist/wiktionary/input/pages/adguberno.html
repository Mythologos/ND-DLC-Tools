Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • aggubernō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.ɡuˈber.noː/, [äɡːʊˈbɛrnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.ɡuˈber.no/, [äd̪ɡuˈbɛrno]

Verb[edit]

adgubernō (present infinitive adgubernāre, perfect active adgubernāvī, supine adgubernātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of aggubernō
