Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Probably from Ancient Greek πᾰνδῑ́ᾱ (pandī́ā), a substantive from πᾰνδῖος (pandîos, “all-divine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /panˈdiː.a/, [pän̪ˈd̪iːä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /panˈdi.a/, [pän̪ˈd̪iːä]

Noun[edit]

pandīa f (genitive pandīae); first declension

  1. A kind of precious stone

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
