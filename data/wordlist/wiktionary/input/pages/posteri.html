Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From English poster.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈposteri/, [ˈpo̞s̠te̞ri]
  • Rhymes: -osteri
  • Syllabification(key): pos‧te‧ri

Noun[edit]

posteri

  1. informational poster

Italian[edit]

Noun[edit]

posteri m pl (plural only)

  1. posterity
  2. descendants

Anagrams[edit]

  • esporti, estirpo, estirpò, petrosi, pistore, portesi, potersi, protesi, ripesto, ripestò, riposte, spietro, spietrò, stiperò, storpie

Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

posterī m pl (genitive posterōrum); second declension (plural only)

  1. descendants

Adjective[edit]

posterī

  1. inflection of posterus:
    1. nominative/vocative masculine plural
    2. genitive masculine/neuter singular

Welsh[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From poster (“poster”) +‎ -i.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (North Wales) IPA(key): /pɔsˈdɛri/, [pʰɔsˈtɛri]
  • (South Wales) IPA(key): /pɔsˈdeːri/, [pʰɔsˈteːri], /pɔsˈdɛri/, [pʰɔsˈtɛri]

Noun[edit]

posteri

  1. plural of poster
