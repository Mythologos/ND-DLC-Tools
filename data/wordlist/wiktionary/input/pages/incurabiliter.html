Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

incūrābilis +‎ -ter

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.kuː.raːˈbi.li.ter/, [ɪŋkuːräːˈbɪlʲɪt̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.ku.raˈbi.li.ter/, [iŋkuräˈbiːlit̪er]

Adverb[edit]

incūrābiliter (not comparable)

  1. (Late Latin) incurably; irremediably
    Synonym: irremediābiliter
