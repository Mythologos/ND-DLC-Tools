Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ sorbeō (“suck in, swallow”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈsor.be.oː/, [pɛrˈs̠ɔrbeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈsor.be.o/, [perˈsɔrbeo]

Verb[edit]

persorbeō (present infinitive persorbēre, perfect active persorbuī, supine persorptum); second conjugation

  1. to suck or drink up, absorb
