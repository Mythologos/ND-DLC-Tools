Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cirrhātus

Etymology[edit]

From cirrus (“ringlet, tendril”) +‎ -ātus.

Adjective[edit]

cirrātus (feminine cirrāta, neuter cirrātum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. curled, having ringlets
  2. (New Latin) having tendrils or cirri
