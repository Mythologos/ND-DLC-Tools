Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ad- +‎ rādō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈraː.doː/, [äd̪ˈräːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈra.do/, [äd̪ˈräːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

adrādō (present infinitive adrādere, perfect active adrāsī, supine adrāsum); third conjugation

  1. to scrape, cut short, shave, lop off
