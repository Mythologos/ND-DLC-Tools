Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ob.stu.peˈfa.ki.oː/, [ɔps̠t̪ʊpɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ob.stu.peˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [obst̪upeˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

obstupefaciō (present infinitive obstupefacere, perfect active obstupefēcī, supine obstupefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to astonish, amaze, astound, stupefy
  2. to be stunned, amazed, paralysed
