Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /feˈbriː.ki.toː/, [fɛˈbriːkɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /feˈbri.t͡ʃi.to/, [feˈbriːt͡ʃit̪o]

Verb[edit]

febrīcitō (present infinitive febrīcitāre, perfect active febrīcitāvī, supine febrīcitātum); first conjugation

  1. to have a fever, be feverish
