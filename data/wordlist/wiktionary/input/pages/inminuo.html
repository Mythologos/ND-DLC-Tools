Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈmi.nu.oː/, [ɪmˈmɪnuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈmi.nu.o/, [inˈmiːnuo]

Verb[edit]

inminuō (present infinitive inminuere, perfect active inminuī, supine inminūtum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of imminuō
