Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

in- +‎ ārdēscō (“to kindle, catch fire”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i.naːrˈdeːs.koː/, [ɪnäːrˈd̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /i.narˈdes.ko/, [inärˈd̪ɛsko]

Verb[edit]

inārdēscō (present infinitive inārdēscere, perfect active inārsī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (transitive) to kindle, catch fire, burn, glow
