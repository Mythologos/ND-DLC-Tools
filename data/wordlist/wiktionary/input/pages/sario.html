Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsa.ri.oː/, [ˈs̠ärioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsa.ri.o/, [ˈsäːrio]

Verb[edit]

sariō (present infinitive sarīre, perfect active saruī or sarīvī, supine sarītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of sarriō
