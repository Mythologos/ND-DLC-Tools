Verb[edit]

Latin[edit]

Adjective[edit]

mōrigerō

  1. dative/ablative masculine/neuter singular of mōrigerus

Verb[edit]

mōrigerō (present infinitive mōrigerāre, perfect active mōrigerāvī, supine mōrigerātum); first conjugation, no passive

  1. Alternative form of mōrigeror
