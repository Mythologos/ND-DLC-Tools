Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈi̯un.ɡoː/, [d̪iːˈi̯ʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈjun.ɡo/, [d̪iˈjuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

dīiungō (present infinitive dīiungere, perfect active dīiūnxī, supine dīiūnctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of disiungō
