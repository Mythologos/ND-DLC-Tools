English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Tanzania
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

Blend of Tanganyika +‎ Zanzibar +‎ -ia, combining the names of the former countries that merged to form Tanzania in 1964.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌtænzəˈniːə/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -iːə
    • Audio (Southern England)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Tanzania

  2. A country in East Africa. Official name: United Republic of Tanzania.

Hypernyms[edit]

  • Subsahara
