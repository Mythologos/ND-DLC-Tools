Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • fēteō
  • foeteō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfae̯.te.oː/, [ˈfäe̯t̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfe.te.o/, [ˈfɛːt̪eo]

Verb[edit]

faeteō (present infinitive faetēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of fēteō

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect or supine forms.
