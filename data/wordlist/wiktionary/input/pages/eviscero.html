Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

eviscero

  1. first-person singular present indicative of eviscerare

Anagrams[edit]

  • esercivo, scoverei

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ vīscera (“entrails”) +‎ -ō (verb-forming suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈu̯iːs.ke.roː/, [eːˈu̯iːs̠kɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈviʃ.ʃe.ro/, [eˈviʃːero]

Verb[edit]

ēvīscerō (present infinitive ēvīscerāre, supine ēvīscerātum); first conjugation, no perfect stem

  1. (transitive) to disembowel, eviscerate
  2. (transitive) to mangle, tear to pieces
  3. (transitive, figuratively) to squander
