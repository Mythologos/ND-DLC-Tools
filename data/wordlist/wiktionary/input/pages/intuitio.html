Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From intueor +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.tuˈi.ti.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪uˈɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.tuˈit.t͡si.o/, [in̪t̪uˈit̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

intuitiō f (genitive intuitiōnis); third declension (Medieval Latin)

  1. the act of seeing
  2. inspection, examination, scrutiny
  3. knowing, understanding
  4. intuition
