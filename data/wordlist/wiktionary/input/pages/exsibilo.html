Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- (“out”) +‎ sībilō (“hiss”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsiː.bi.loː/, [ɛkˈs̠iːbɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsi.bi.lo/, [eɡˈziːbilo]

Verb[edit]

exsībilō (present infinitive exsībilāre, perfect active exsībilāvī, supine exsībilātum); first conjugation

  1. to hiss out or forth
