Czech[edit]

Czech Wikipedia has an article on:
Troja
Wikipedia cs

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈtroja]
  • Hyphenation: Tro‧ja

Proper noun[edit]

Troja f (related adjective trojánský or trojský, demonym Trojan or Trójan, female demonym Trójanka)

  1. Alternative form of Trója: Troy (an ancient city in far northwestern Asia Minor, in modern Turkey)
  2. Troja (an area in Prague, in the Czech Republic)

Declension[edit]

Declension of Troja (sg-only mixed feminine)
singular
nominative Troja
genitive Troji, Troje
dative Troje, Troji
accusative Troju
vocative Trojo
locative Troje, Troji
instrumental Trojou

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtroː.i̯a/, [ˈt̪roːi̯ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtro.ja/, [ˈt̪rɔːjä]

Proper noun[edit]

Trōja f sg (genitive Trōjae); first declension

  1. Alternative spelling of Trōia

Declension[edit]
