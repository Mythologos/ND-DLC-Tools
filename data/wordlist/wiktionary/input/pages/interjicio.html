Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [in̪t̪erˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

interjiciō (present infinitive interjicere, perfect active interjēcī, supine interjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of intericiō
