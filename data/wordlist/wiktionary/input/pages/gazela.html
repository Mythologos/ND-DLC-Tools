Polish[edit]

Polish Wikipedia has an article on:
gazela
Wikipedia pl

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from French gazelle, from Old French gazel, from Arabic غَزَال (ḡazāl).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɡaˈzɛ.la/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛla
  • Syllabification: ga‧ze‧la

Noun[edit]

gazela f

  1. gazelle, antelope of the genus Gazella

Declension[edit]

Declension of gazela
singular plural
nominative gazela gazele
genitive gazeli gazel/gazeli
dative gazeli gazelom
accusative gazelę gazele
instrumental gazelą gazelami
locative gazeli gazelach
vocative gazelo gazele
