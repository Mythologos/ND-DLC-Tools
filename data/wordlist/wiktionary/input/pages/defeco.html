Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /deˈfɛ.ko/
  • Rhymes: -ɛko
  • Hyphenation: de‧fè‧co

Verb[edit]

defeco

  1. first-person singular present indicative of defecare

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.fe.koː/, [ˈd̪eːfɛkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.fe.ko/, [ˈd̪ɛːfeko]

Verb[edit]

dēfecō (present infinitive dēfecāre, perfect active dēfecāvī, supine dēfecātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēfaecō
