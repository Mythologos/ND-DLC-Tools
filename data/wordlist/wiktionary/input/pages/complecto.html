Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈplek.toː/, [kɔmˈpɫ̪ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈplek.to/, [komˈplɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

complectō (present infinitive complectere, perfect active complectī, supine complexum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of complector
