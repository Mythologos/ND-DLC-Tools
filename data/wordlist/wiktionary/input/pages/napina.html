Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

napi- +‎ -na

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnɑpinɑ/, [ˈnɑ̝pinɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑpinɑ
  • Syllabification(key): na‧pi‧na

Noun[edit]

napina

  1. grumble, complaining

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From nāpus (“turnip”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /naːˈpiː.na/, [näːˈpiːnä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /naˈpi.na/, [näˈpiːnä]

Noun[edit]

nāpīna f (genitive nāpīnae); first declension

  1. a turnip field

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
