Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

somnio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of somniar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

somnium (“a dream”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsom.ni.oː/, [ˈs̠ɔmnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsom.ni.o/, [ˈsɔmnio]

Noun[edit]

somnio

  1. ablative/dative singular of somnium

Verb[edit]

somniō (present infinitive somniāre, perfect active somniāvī, supine somniātum); first conjugation

  1. to dream
  2. to daydream, think idly
  3. to talk idly
