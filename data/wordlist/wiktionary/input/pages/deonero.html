Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ onerō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deˈo.ne.roː/, [d̪eˈɔnɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈo.ne.ro/, [d̪eˈɔːnero]

Verb[edit]

deonerō (present infinitive deonerāre, perfect active deonerāvī, supine deonerātum); first conjugation

  1. to unload, disburden
  2. (New Latin) to download
