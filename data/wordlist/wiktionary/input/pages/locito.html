Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈlo.ki.toː/, [ˈɫ̪ɔkɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈlo.t͡ʃi.to/, [ˈlɔːt͡ʃit̪o]

Verb[edit]

locitō (present infinitive locitāre, perfect active locitāvī, supine locitātum); first conjugation

  1. to let or hire out
