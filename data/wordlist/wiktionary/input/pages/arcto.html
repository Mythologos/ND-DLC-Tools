Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈark.toː/, [ˈärkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈark.to/, [ˈärkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

arctō (present infinitive arctāre, perfect active arctāvī, supine arctātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of artō
