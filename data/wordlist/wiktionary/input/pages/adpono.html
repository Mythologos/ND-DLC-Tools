Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈpoː.noː/, [äpˈpoːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈpo.no/, [äd̪ˈpɔːno]

Verb[edit]

adpōnō (present infinitive adpōnere, perfect active adposuī, supine adpositum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of appōnō
