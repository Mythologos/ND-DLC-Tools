Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From trans +‎ pōnō (“place, put”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /tranˈspoː.noː/, [t̪rä̃ːˈs̠poːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tranˈspo.no/, [t̪ränˈspɔːno]

Verb[edit]

trānspōnō (present infinitive trānspōnere, perfect active trānsposuī, supine trānspositum); third conjugation

  1. to place over or across
  2. to transfer
