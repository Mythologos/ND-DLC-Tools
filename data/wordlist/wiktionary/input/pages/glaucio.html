Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡlau̯.ki.oː/, [ˈɡɫ̪äu̯kioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈɡlau̯.t͡ʃi.o/, [ˈɡläːu̯t͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

glauciō (present infinitive glaucīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, of ewes) to bleat

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect is attested.
