Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • coerceō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈher.ke.oː/, [koˈ(ɦ)ɛrkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈer.t͡ʃe.o/, [koˈɛrt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

coherceō (present infinitive cohercēre, perfect active cohercuī, supine cohercitum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of coerceō
