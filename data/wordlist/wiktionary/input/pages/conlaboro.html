Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.laˈboː.roː/, [kɔnɫ̪äˈboːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.laˈbo.ro/, [konläˈbɔːro]

Verb[edit]

conlabōrō (present infinitive conlabōrāre, perfect active conlabōrāvī, supine conlabōrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of collabōrō
