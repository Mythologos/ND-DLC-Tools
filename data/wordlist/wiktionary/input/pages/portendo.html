  1. first-person singular present indicative of portendere

Anagrams[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From por- +‎ tendō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /porˈten.doː/, [pɔrˈt̪ɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /porˈten.do/, [porˈt̪ɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

portendō (present infinitive portendere, perfect active portendī, supine portentum); third conjugation

  1. to indicate, point out
  2. to predict, foretell, portend
    Synonyms: vāticinor, praesāgiō, praemoneō, moneō, praedīcō, canō
