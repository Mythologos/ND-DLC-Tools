Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Belgicus

Etymology[edit]

Belga m (“member of the Belgae”) or Belgae (“Belgae”) + -icus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbel.ɡi.kus/, [ˈbɛɫ̪ɡɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈbel.d͡ʒi.kus/, [ˈbɛl̠ʲd͡ʒikus]

Adjective[edit]

belgicus (feminine belgica, neuter belgicum); first/second-declension adjective (sometimes capitalised)

  1. Belgic
  2. (Modern day) Dutch
    1. Dutch language ((lingua) belgica)
