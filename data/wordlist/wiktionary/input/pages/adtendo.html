Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈten.doː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈten.do/, [äd̪ˈt̪ɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

adtendō (present infinitive adtendere, perfect active adtendī, supine adtentum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attendō
