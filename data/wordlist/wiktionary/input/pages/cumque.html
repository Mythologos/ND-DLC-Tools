Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cunque
  • quomque

Etymology[edit]

From cum +‎ -que (“each”). Compare with umquam.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkun.kʷe/, [ˈkʊŋkʷɛ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkum.kwe/, [ˈkumkwe]

Adverb[edit]

cumque (not comparable)

  1. however, whenever, howsoever, whensoever

Derived terms[edit]

  • quīcumque
  • quōcumque
