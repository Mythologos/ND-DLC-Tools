Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From semi- +‎ dormītō.

Verb[edit]

semidormītō (present infinitive semidormītāre, perfect active semidormītāvī, supine semidormītātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to be half-asleep
