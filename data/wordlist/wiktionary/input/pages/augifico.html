Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From augeō (“increase”) +‎ -ficō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /au̯ˈɡi.fi.koː/, [äu̯ˈɡɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /au̯ˈd͡ʒi.fi.ko/, [äu̯ˈd͡ʒiːfiko]

Verb[edit]

augificō (present infinitive augificāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to make bigger or larger, increase, augment
