Spanish[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /beˈneɾo/ [beˈne.ɾo]
  • Rhymes: -eɾo
  • Syllabification: ve‧ne‧ro

Etymology 1[edit]

From vena +‎ -ero.

Noun[edit]

venero m (plural veneros)

  1. spring
  2. (mining) vein
