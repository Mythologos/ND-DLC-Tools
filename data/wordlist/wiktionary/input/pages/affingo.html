Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adfingō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ fingō (“shape, fashion, form”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /afˈfin.ɡoː/, [äfˈfɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /afˈfin.ɡo/, [äfˈfiŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

affingō (present infinitive affingere, perfect active affīnxī, supine affictum); third conjugation

  1. to add to; to attach or affix
  2. to embellish
  3. to forge or counterfeit
