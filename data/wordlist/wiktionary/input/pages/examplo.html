Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- (“out”) +‎ amplus (“broad”) +‎ -ō (verb-forming suffix). Attested in the Liber Glossarum.[1]

Verb[edit]

examplō (present infinitive examplāre, perfect active examplāvī, supine examplātum); first conjugation (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. to broaden
