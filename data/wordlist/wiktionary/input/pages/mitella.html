Dutch[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin mitella.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /miˈtɛ.laː/
  • Audio(file)
  • Hyphenation: mi‧tel‧la

Noun[edit]

mitella f (plural mitella's, diminutive mitellaatje n)

  1. sling (hanging bandage)

Hypernyms[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mitra (“headband, turban”) +‎ -la (diminutive suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /miˈtel.la/, [mɪˈt̪ɛlːʲä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /miˈtel.la/, [miˈt̪ɛlːä]

Noun[edit]

mitella f (genitive mitellae); first declension

  1. (small) headband, turban
  2. (medicine) bandage

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
