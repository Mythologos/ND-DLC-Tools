Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i̯uːˈkun.doː/, [i̯uːˈkʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /juˈkun.do/, [juˈkun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

jūcundō (present infinitive jūcundāre, perfect active jūcundāvī, supine jūcundātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iūcundō
