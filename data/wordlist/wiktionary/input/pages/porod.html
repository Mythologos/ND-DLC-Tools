Czech[edit]

Czech Wikipedia has an article on:
porod
Wikipedia cs

Etymology[edit]

Deverbal from porodit.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈporot]

Noun[edit]

porod m inan

  1. birth, delivery (process of childbearing)

Serbo-Croatian[edit]

Serbo-Croatian Wikipedia has an article on:
porod
Wikipedia sh

Etymology[edit]

Deverbal from poroditi.

Noun[edit]

pòrod m (Cyrillic spelling по̀род)

  1. birth
    Synonym: rođénje
  2. delivery of a child
    Synonym: pȍrođāj
  3. children, progeny
    Synonym: pòtōmstvo
