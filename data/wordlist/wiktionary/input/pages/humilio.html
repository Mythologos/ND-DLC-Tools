Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

humilio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of humiliar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From humilis +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /huˈmi.li.oː/, [hʊˈmɪlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /uˈmi.li.o/, [uˈmiːlio]

Verb[edit]

humiliō (present infinitive humiliāre, perfect active humiliāvī, supine humiliātum); first conjugation

  1. to humble, to abase
  2. to humiliate
