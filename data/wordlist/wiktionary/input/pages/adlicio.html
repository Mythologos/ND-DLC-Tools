Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈli.ki.oː/, [älˈlʲɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈli.t͡ʃi.o/, [äd̪ˈliːt͡ʃio]
  • See the Pronunciation section of alliciō for discussion on the length of the vowel in the perfect stem (adlē̆x-).

Verb[edit]

adliciō (present infinitive adlicere, perfect active adlē̆xī, supine adlectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of alliciō
