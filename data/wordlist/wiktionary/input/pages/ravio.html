Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *rakja.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈrɑʋio/, [ˈrɑ̝ʋio̞]
  • Rhymes: -ɑʋio
  • Syllabification(key): ra‧vi‧o

Noun[edit]

ravio (dialectal)

  1. limb
  2. carcass of an animal

Anagrams[edit]

  • Viroa, arvio, raivo, varoi, viroa

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From rāvis (“hoarseness”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈraː.u̯i.oː/, [ˈräːu̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈra.vi.o/, [ˈräːvio]

Verb[edit]

rāviō (present infinitive rāvīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to talk oneself hoarse
