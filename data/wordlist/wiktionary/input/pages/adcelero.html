Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • accelerō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈke.le.roː/, [äkˈkɛɫ̪ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈt͡ʃe.le.ro/, [äd̪ˈt͡ʃɛːlero]

Verb[edit]

adcelerō (present infinitive adcelerāre, perfect active adcelerāvī, supine adcelerātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of accelerō (“to accelerate; to be quick”)
