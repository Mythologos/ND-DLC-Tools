Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ēiūrō

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ iūrō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.i̯e.roː/, [ˈeːi̯ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.je.ro/, [ˈɛːjero]

Verb[edit]

ēierō (present infinitive ēierāre, perfect active ēierāvī, supine ēierātum); first conjugation

  1. to abjure, resign, abdicate, renounce
