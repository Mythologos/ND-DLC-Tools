Proper noun[edit]

Flandria

  1. Flanders (a historical region of Western Europe)
  2. Flanders, County of Flanders (a historical county of Western Europe, of varying extent)
  3. Flanders (two provinces of Belgium, West Flanders and East Flanders)

Latin[edit]

The oldest mention of the historical region of Flanders dates back to 358 AD, when it was part of the Roman Empire as pagus Flandrensis.

Proper noun[edit]

Flandria f sg (genitive Flandriae); first declension (New Latin)

  1. Flanders (a historical region of Western Europe)
  2. Flanders, County of Flanders (a historical county of Western Europe, of varying extent)
  3. Flanders (two provinces of Belgium, West Flanders and East Flanders)
