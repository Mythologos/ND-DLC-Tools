Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

pedari +‎ -uus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpedɑrius/, [ˈpe̞dɑ̝ˌrius̠]
  • Rhymes: -ius
  • Syllabification(key): pe‧da‧ri‧us

Noun[edit]

pedarius (colloquial)

  1. pedophilia (the state of being a pedophile)

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /peˈdaː.ri.us/, [pɛˈd̪äːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /peˈda.ri.us/, [peˈd̪äːrius]

Adjective[edit]

pedārius (feminine pedāria, neuter pedārium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (relational) foot
  2. measuring a foot long
