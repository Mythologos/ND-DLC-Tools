Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.se.koː/, [ˈĩːs̠ɛkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.se.ko/, [ˈinseko]

Verb[edit]

īnsecō (present infinitive īnsecāre, perfect active īnsecuī, supine īnsectum); first conjugation

  1. to cut up or into
  2. to incise
  3. to dissect
