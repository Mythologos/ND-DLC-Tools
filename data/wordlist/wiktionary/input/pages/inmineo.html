Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈmi.ne.oː/, [ɪmˈmɪneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈmi.ne.o/, [inˈmiːneo]

Verb[edit]

inmineō (present infinitive inminēre, perfect active inminuī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of immineō
