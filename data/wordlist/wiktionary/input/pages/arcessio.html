Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /arˈkes.si.oː/, [ärˈkɛs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /arˈt͡ʃes.si.o/, [ärˈt͡ʃɛsːio]

Verb[edit]

arcessiō (present infinitive arcessīre, perfect active arcessīvī, supine arcessītum); fourth conjugation

  1. (rare, post-Classical) Alternative form of arcessō
