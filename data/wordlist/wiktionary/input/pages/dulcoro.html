Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dulcor (“sweetness”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /dulˈkoː.roː/, [d̪ʊɫ̪ˈkoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /dulˈko.ro/, [d̪ulˈkɔːro]

Verb[edit]

dulcōrō (present infinitive dulcōrāre, perfect active dulcōrāvī, supine dulcōrātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to sweeten
