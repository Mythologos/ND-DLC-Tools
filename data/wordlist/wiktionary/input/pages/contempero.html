Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /konˈtem.pe.ro/, (traditional) /konˈtɛm.pe.ro/[1]
  • Rhymes: -empero, (traditional) -ɛmpero
  • Hyphenation: con‧tém‧pe‧ro, (traditional) con‧tèm‧pe‧ro

Verb[edit]

contempero

  1. first-person singular present indicative of contemperare

Anagrams[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ temperō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈtem.pe.roː/, [kɔn̪ˈt̪ɛmpɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈtem.pe.ro/, [kon̪ˈt̪ɛmpero]

Verb[edit]

contemperō (present infinitive contemperāre, perfect active contemperāvī, supine contemperātum); first conjugation

  1. to moderate
  2. to temper (water-down a drink)
