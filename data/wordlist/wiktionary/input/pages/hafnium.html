English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
hafnium
Wikipedia
Chemical element
Hf
Previous: lutetium (Lu)
Next: tantalum (Ta)

Etymology[edit]

From Latin Hafnia (“Copenhagen”) +‎ -ium.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: hăf'nēəm, IPA(key): /ˈhæfniəm/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Noun[edit]

hafnium (uncountable)

  1. A chemical element (symbol Hf) with an atomic number of 72: a lustrous, silvery-grey tetravalent transition metal.

Synonyms[edit]

  • celtium (former name)

  • hafnium tetrahalide
