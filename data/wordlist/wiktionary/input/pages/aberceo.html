Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • abarceō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈber.ke.oː/, [äˈbɛrkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈber.t͡ʃe.o/, [äˈbɛrt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

aberceō (present infinitive abercēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of abarceō
