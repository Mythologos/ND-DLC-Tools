Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cūcūrriō

Etymology[edit]

Onomatopoeic.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kuːˈkuː.ri.oː/, [kuːˈkuːrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kuˈku.ri.o/, [kuˈkuːrio]

Verb[edit]

cūcūriō (present infinitive cūcūrīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, of roosters) to crow

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect is attested.
