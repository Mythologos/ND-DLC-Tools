Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Italic *potistāts. Equivalent to potis + -tās.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /poˈtes.taːs/, [pɔˈt̪ɛs̠t̪äːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /poˈtes.tas/, [poˈt̪ɛst̪äs]

Noun[edit]

potestās f (genitive potestātis); third declension

  1. power, ability
  2. mastery, control
  3. authority, jurisdiction
  4. dominion, political power
    Synonyms: dicio, imperium, arbitrium, auctōritās
  5. right, legal power
  6. (of a word) meaning
  7. possibility, opportunity
