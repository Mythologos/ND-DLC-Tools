Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unadapted borrowing from Latin vāde mēcum (literally “Go with me!”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /va.deˈmɛ.kum/
  • Rhymes: -ɛkum
  • Hyphenation: va‧de‧mè‧cum

Noun[edit]

vademecum m (invariable)

  1. vade mecum
    Synonyms: guida, manuale, prontuario, bignamino

Related terms[edit]

  • meco
