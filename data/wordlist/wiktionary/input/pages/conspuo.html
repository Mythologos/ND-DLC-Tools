Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ spuō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.spu.oː/, [ˈkõːs̠puoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.spu.o/, [ˈkɔnspuo]

Verb[edit]

cōnspuō (present infinitive cōnspuere, perfect active cōnspuī, supine cōnspūtum); third conjugation

  1. to spit upon or out (especially in contempt)
    Synonyms: spuō, spūtō
