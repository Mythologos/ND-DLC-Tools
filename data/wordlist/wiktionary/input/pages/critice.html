Noun[edit]

critice

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkri.ti.keː/, [ˈkrɪt̪ɪkeː]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkri.ti.t͡ʃe/, [ˈkriːt̪it͡ʃe]

Adverb[edit]

criticē (comparative criticius, superlative criticissimē)

  1. critically, decisively

Etymology 2[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkri.ti.ke/, [ˈkrɪt̪ɪkɛ]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkri.ti.t͡ʃe/, [ˈkriːt̪it͡ʃe]

Noun[edit]

critice

  1. vocative singular of criticus

Adjective[edit]

critice

  1. vocative masculine singular of criticus
