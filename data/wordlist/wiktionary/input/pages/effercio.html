Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • effarciō

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ farciō (“cram, stuff”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /efˈfer.ki.oː/, [ɛfˈfɛrkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /efˈfer.t͡ʃi.o/, [efˈfɛrt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

efferciō (present infinitive effercīre, perfect active effersī, supine effertum); fourth conjugation

  1. to stuff or cram full
