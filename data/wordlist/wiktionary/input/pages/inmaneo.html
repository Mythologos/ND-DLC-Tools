Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • immaneō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈma.ne.oː/, [ɪmˈmäneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈma.ne.o/, [inˈmäːneo]

Verb[edit]

inmaneō (present infinitive inmanēre, perfect active inmānsī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of immaneō
