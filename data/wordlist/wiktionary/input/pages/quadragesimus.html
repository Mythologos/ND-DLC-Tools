Etymology[edit]

From quadrāgintā (“forty”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kʷa.draːˈɡeː.si.mus/, [kʷäd̪räːˈɡeːs̠ɪmʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kwa.draˈd͡ʒe.si.mus/, [kwäd̪räˈd͡ʒɛːs̬imus]

Numeral[edit]

quadrāgēsimus (feminine quadrāgēsima, neuter quadrāgēsimum); first/second-declension numeral

  1. fortieth
