Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [d̪eːˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [d̪eˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

dējiciō (present infinitive dējicere, perfect active dējēcī, supine dējectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of dēiciō
