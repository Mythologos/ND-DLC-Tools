Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈpoː.toː/, [eːˈpoːt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈpo.to/, [eˈpɔːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

ēpōtō (present infinitive ēpōtāre, perfect active ēpōtāvī, supine ēpōtum); first conjugation

  1. to drink up, drain, quaff
