Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ volō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.u̯o.loː/, [ˈd̪eːu̯ɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.vo.lo/, [ˈd̪ɛːvolo]

Verb[edit]

dēvolō (present infinitive dēvolāre, perfect active dēvolāvī, supine dēvolātum); first conjugation

  1. to fly down or away
