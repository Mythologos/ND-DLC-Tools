Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈpin.ɡoː/, [ɪmˈpɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈpin.ɡo/, [inˈpiŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

inpingō (present infinitive inpingere, perfect active inpēgī, supine inpāctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of impingō
