Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • reconligō

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ colligō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈkol.li.ɡoː/, [rɛˈkɔlːʲɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈkol.li.ɡo/, [reˈkɔlːiɡo]

Verb[edit]

recolligō (present infinitive recolligere, perfect active recollēgī, supine recollēctum); third conjugation

  1. to recover, gather again, collect
  2. (reflexive) to pull oneself together, get ahold of oneself
