Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From st(ā) +‎ -bulum.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsta.bu.lum/, [ˈs̠t̪äbʊɫ̪ʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsta.bu.lum/, [ˈst̪äːbulum]

Noun[edit]

stabulum n (genitive stabulī); second declension

  1. dwelling, habitation
  2. stall, stable
  3. hut
  4. tavern, public house, hostelry
  5. brothel

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).
