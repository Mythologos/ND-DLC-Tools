Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈflek.si.lis/, [ˈfɫ̪ɛks̠ɪlʲɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈflek.si.lis/, [ˈflɛksilis]

Adjective[edit]

flexilis (neuter flexile); third-declension two-termination adjective

  1. pliant, pliable, supple, flexible
  2. bent, curved
