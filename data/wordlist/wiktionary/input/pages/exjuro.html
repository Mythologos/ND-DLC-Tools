Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eksˈi̯uː.roː/, [ɛks̠ˈi̯uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eksˈju.ro/, [eksˈjuːro]

Verb[edit]

exjūrō (present infinitive exjūrāre, perfect active exjūrāvī, supine exjūrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exiuro
