Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ legō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kirˈkum.le.ɡoː/, [kɪrˈkʊmɫ̪ɛɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃirˈkum.le.ɡo/, [t͡ʃirˈkumleɡo]

Verb[edit]

circumlegō (present infinitive circumlegere, perfect active circumlēgī, supine circumlēctum); third conjugation

  1. to sail around
