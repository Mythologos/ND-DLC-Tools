  • Hyphenation: ga‧zel‧la
  • Rhymes: -lɒ

Noun[edit]

gazella (plural gazellák)

  1. gazelle

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • gazela

Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from Arabic غَزَال (ḡazāl), probably via a Romance language, e.g. French gazelle.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡazˈzel.la/, [ɡäz̪ˈd̪͡z̪ɛlːʲä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡadˈd͡zel.la/, [ɡäd̪ˈd̪͡z̪ɛlːä]

Noun[edit]

gazella f (genitive gazellae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) gazelle
