Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈi̯a.ke.oː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈi̯äkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈja.t͡ʃe.o/, [superˈjäːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

superjaceō (present infinitive superjacēre, perfect active superjacuī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of superiaceō
