Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From decānus +‎ -ātus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /de.kaːˈnaː.tus/, [d̪ɛkäːˈnäːt̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.kaˈna.tus/, [d̪ekäˈnäːt̪us]

Noun[edit]

decānātus m (genitive decānātūs); fourth declension (Medieval Latin)

  1. deanship, office of a dean
  2. deanery, decanate
