Verb[edit]

excavo

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ cavō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.ska.u̯oː/, [ˈɛks̠käu̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.ska.vo/, [ˈɛkskävo]

Verb[edit]

excavō (present infinitive excavāre, perfect active excavāvī, supine excavātum); first conjugation

  1. to hollow out, excavate
