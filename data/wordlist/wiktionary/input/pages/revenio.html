Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- (“again, back”) +‎ veniō (“come”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈu̯e.ni.oː/, [reˈu̯ɛnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈve.ni.o/, [reˈvɛːnio]

Verb[edit]

reveniō (present infinitive revenīre, perfect active revēnī, supine reventum); fourth conjugation, impersonal in the passive

  1. to come back, return
    Synonyms: revertor, regredior
