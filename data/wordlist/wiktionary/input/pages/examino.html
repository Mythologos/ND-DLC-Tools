  • IPA(key): (Balearic) [əɡ.zəˈmi.no]

examino

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From exāmen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsaː.mi.noː/, [ɛkˈs̠äːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsa.mi.no/, [eɡˈzäːmino]

Verb[edit]

exāminō (present infinitive exāmināre, perfect active exāmināvī, supine exāminātum); first conjugation

  1. to weigh or consider
  2. to examine
