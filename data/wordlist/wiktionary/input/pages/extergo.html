Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • extergeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈster.ɡoː/, [ɛkˈs̠t̪ɛrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈster.ɡo/, [ekˈst̪ɛrɡo]

Verb[edit]

extergō (present infinitive extergere, perfect active extersī, supine extersum); third conjugation
