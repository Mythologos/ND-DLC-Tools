Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ culpō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈkul.poː/, [ɪŋˈkʊɫ̪poː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈkul.po/, [iŋˈkulpo]

Verb[edit]

inculpō (present infinitive inculpāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to blame, accuse
