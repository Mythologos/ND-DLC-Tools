Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ struo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈper.stru.oː/, [s̠ʊˈpɛrs̠t̪ruoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈper.stru.o/, [suˈpɛrst̪ruo]

Verb[edit]

superstruō (present infinitive superstruere, perfect active superstrūxī, supine superstrūctum); third conjugation

  1. to build over or upon
