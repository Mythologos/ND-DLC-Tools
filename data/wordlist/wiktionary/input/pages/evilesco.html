Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ vīlis (“worthless, cheap”) +‎ -ēscō. Predates vīlēscō by several centuries.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eː.u̯iːˈleːs.koː/, [eːu̯iːˈɫ̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /e.viˈles.ko/, [eviˈlɛsko]

Verb[edit]

ēvīlēscō (present infinitive ēvīlēscere, perfect active ēvīluī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive, rare) to become worthless, despicable
