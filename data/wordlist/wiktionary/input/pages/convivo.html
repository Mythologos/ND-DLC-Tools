Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ vīvō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈu̯iː.u̯oː/, [kɔnˈu̯iːu̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈvi.vo/, [koɱˈviːvo]

Verb[edit]

convīvō (present infinitive convīvere, perfect active convīxī, supine convīctum); third conjugation, impersonal in the passive

  1. to live with
