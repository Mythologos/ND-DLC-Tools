Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈi̯un.ɡoː/, [ɪnˈi̯ʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈjun.ɡo/, [inˈjuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

injungō (present infinitive injungere, perfect active injūnxī, supine injūnctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iniungō
