Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ cremō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.kre.moː/, [ˈkɔŋkrɛmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.kre.mo/, [ˈkɔŋkremo]

Verb[edit]

concremō (present infinitive concremāre, perfect active concremāvī, supine concremātum); first conjugation

  1. to burn up (consume by fire)
