Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- (“out”) +‎ nārrō (“I relate”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈnaːr.roː/, [eːˈnäːrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈnar.ro/, [eˈnärːo]

Verb[edit]

ēnārrō (present infinitive ēnārrāre, perfect active ēnārrāvī, supine ēnārrātum); first conjugation

  1. to explain in detail, to expound
