Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • attitulō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈti.tu.loː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɪt̪ʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈti.tu.lo/, [äd̪ˈt̪iːt̪ulo]

Verb[edit]

adtitulō (present infinitive adtitulāre, perfect active adtitulāvī, supine adtitulātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attitulō
