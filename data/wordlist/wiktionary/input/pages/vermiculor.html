Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯erˈmi.ku.lor/, [u̯ɛrˈmɪkʊɫ̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /verˈmi.ku.lor/, [verˈmiːkulor]

Verb[edit]

vermiculor (present infinitive vermiculārī, perfect active vermiculātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to be wormy, worm-eaten
