French[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /vɛʁ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Homophones: vair, vairs, verre, vers, vert, verts
  • Rhymes: -ɛʁ

Noun[edit]

verres m

  1. plural of verre

Verb[edit]

verres

  1. second-person singular present indicative/subjunctive of verrer

Anagrams[edit]

  • resver, revers, verser

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Italic *wersēn, from Proto-Indo-European *wers-, *wr̥s- (“male”). Cognates include Sanskrit वृषन् (vṛ́ṣan), वृष (vṛṣa), Ancient Greek ἄρσην (ársēn) and Lithuanian ver̃šis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯er.reːs/, [ˈu̯ɛrːeːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈver.res/, [ˈvɛrːes]

Noun[edit]

verrēs m (genitive verris); third declension

  1. boar, male swine

Declension[edit]
