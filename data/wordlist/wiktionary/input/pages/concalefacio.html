Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ calefaciō (“to warm”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.ka.leˈfa.ki.oː/, [kɔŋkäɫ̪ɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.ka.leˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [koŋkäleˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

concalefaciō (present infinitive concalefacere, perfect active concalefēcī, supine concalefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. (transitive) to warm thoroughly
