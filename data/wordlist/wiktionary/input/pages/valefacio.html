Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯a.leˈfa.ki.oː/, [u̯äɫ̪ɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /va.leˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [väleˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

valefaciō (present infinitive valefacere, perfect active valefēcī, supine valefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to say farewell

Conjugation[edit]

  • The verb has an irregular conjugation as for the passive voice.
