Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡluː.ti.oː/, [ˈɡɫ̪uːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈɡlut.t͡si.o/, [ˈɡlut̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

glūtiō (present infinitive glūtīre, perfect active glūtīvī or glūtiī, supine glūtītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of gluttiō
