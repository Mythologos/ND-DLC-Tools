Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ancient Greek τριάς (triás).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtri.as/, [ˈt̪riäs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtri.as/, [ˈt̪riːäs]

Noun[edit]

trias f (genitive triadis); third declension

  1. the number three, a triad
