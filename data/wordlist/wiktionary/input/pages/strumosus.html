Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

strūma (“struma, scrofulous tumor”) +‎ -ōsus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /struːˈmoː.sus/, [s̠t̪ruːˈmoːs̠ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /struˈmo.sus/, [st̪ruˈmɔːs̬us]

Adjective[edit]

strūmōsus (feminine strūmōsa, neuter strūmōsum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. strumous, having strumae
