English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Hebrew נֵ֤בֶל

Noun[edit]

nabla (plural nablas)

  1. A Hebrew stringed instrument.
  2. (mathematical analysis) The symbol ∇, used to denote the gradient operator.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (instrument): nebel (obsolete, possibly the same instrument)
  • (symbol): atled, del

Noun[edit]

nabla m (invariable)

  1. (mathematical analysis) nabla (the symbol ∇)
