Theodericus Rex: King Theoderic the Great of the Ostrogoths, the Goth who became the second Germanic King of the Kingdom of Italy, and a map of his united Gothic realms which grew to span a significant portion of the former western (Latin) Roman Empire in the 5th-6th centuries AD.
