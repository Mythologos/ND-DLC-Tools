French[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Homophones: capacita, capacitât

Verb[edit]

capacitas

  1. second-person singular past historic of capaciter

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From capāx +‎ -tās.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kaˈpaː.ki.taːs/, [käˈpäːkɪt̪äːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kaˈpa.t͡ʃi.tas/, [käˈpäːt͡ʃit̪äs]

Noun[edit]

capācitās f (genitive capācitātis); third declension

  1. capability; capacity
    Synonyms: ops, fortitūdō, efficācia, valētūdō
  2. comprehension

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
