Etymology 1[edit]

From Byzantine Greek Ἀλβανία (Albanía). Doublet of Albany.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK, US) IPA(key): /ælˈbeɪ.ni.ə/, /ɔːlˈbeɪ.ni.ə/
  • Audio (GA)(file)
  • Rhymes: -eɪniə

Proper noun[edit]

Albania

  2. A country in Southeast Europe. Official name: Republic of Albania. Capital and largest city: Tirana.
    • 2006 April 27, Sylvia Moosmüller, Theodor Granser, “The spread of Standard Albanian: An illustration based on an analysis of vowels”, in Language Variation and Change, volume 18, number 2, Cambridge University Press, →DOI:
