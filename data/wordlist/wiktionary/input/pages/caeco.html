Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkae̯.koː/, [ˈkäe̯koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈt͡ʃe.ko/, [ˈt͡ʃɛːko]

Etymology 1[edit]

From caecus +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

caecō (present infinitive caecāre, perfect active caecāvī, supine caecātum); first conjugation

  1. to blind
  2. to obscure
