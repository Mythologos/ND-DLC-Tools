Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adsiccō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ siccō (“make dry”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈsik.koː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɪkːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈsik.ko/, [äsˈsikːo]

Verb[edit]

assiccō (present infinitive assiccāre, perfect active assiccāvī, supine assiccātum); first conjugation

  1. to make dry, dry, dry up, dry out
