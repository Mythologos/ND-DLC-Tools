Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From varius +‎ agō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯aˈri.e.ɡoː/, [u̯äˈriɛɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /vaˈri.e.ɡo/, [väˈriːeɡo]

Verb[edit]

variegō (present infinitive variegāre, perfect active variegāvī, supine variegātum); first conjugation

  1. to variegate
  2. to be variegated
