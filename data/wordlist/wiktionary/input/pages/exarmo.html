Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ armō (“arm”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsar.moː/, [ɛkˈs̠ärmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsar.mo/, [eɡˈzärmo]

Verb[edit]

exarmō (present infinitive exarmāre, perfect active exarmāvī, supine exarmātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to disarm
  2. (transitive, nautical) to unrig
