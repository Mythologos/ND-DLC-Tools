Latin[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

Chroātia f sg (genitive Chroātiae); first declension

  1. Alternative spelling of Croātia (“Croatia (a country in Southeast Europe, in the Balkans)”)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.
