Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Latin scrība. Doublet of scrivano.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈskri.ba/
  • Rhymes: -iba
  • Hyphenation: scrì‧ba

Noun[edit]

scriba m (plural scribi)

  1. (archaic) scribe, amanuensis
  2. (derogatory) hack journalist
  3. (historical) scribe, copyist

Related terms[edit]

  • scrivano

Anagrams[edit]

  • sbraci, scabri

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

scrībō +‎ -a (agent noun)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈskriː.ba/, [ˈs̠kriːbä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈskri.ba/, [ˈskriːbä]

Noun[edit]

scrība m (genitive scrībae); first declension

  1. writer, scribe, secretary, clerk

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
