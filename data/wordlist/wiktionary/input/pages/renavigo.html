Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ nāvigō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈnaː.u̯i.ɡoː/, [rɛˈnäːu̯ɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈna.vi.ɡo/, [reˈnäːviɡo]

Verb[edit]

renāvigō (present infinitive renāvigāre, perfect active renāvigāvī, supine renāvigātum); first conjugation

  1. (intransitive) to sail back; to return by sea
  2. (transitive) to recross (a river)
