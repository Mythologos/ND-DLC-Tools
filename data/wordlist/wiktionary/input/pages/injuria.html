English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin iniuria. Doublet of injury.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɪnˈdʒʊəɹi.ə/

Noun[edit]

injuria (plural injuriae or injuriæ)

  1. (law) injury; invasion of another's rights

Related terms[edit]

  • crimen injuria
  • damnum absque injuria
  • injuria sine damno
