Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From olfactor (“smeller, sniffer”), derived from olfaciō (“I smell”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ol.fakˈtoː.ri.us/, [ɔɫ̪fäkˈt̪oːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ol.fakˈto.ri.us/, [olfäkˈt̪ɔːrius]

Adjective[edit]

olfactōrius (feminine olfactōria, neuter olfactōrium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Of or pertaining to smell or to one who smells.
