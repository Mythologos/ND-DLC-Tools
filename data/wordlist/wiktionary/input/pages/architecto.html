Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ar.kʰiˈtek.toː/, [ärkʰɪˈt̪ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ar.kiˈtek.to/, [ärkiˈt̪ɛkt̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

From architectus +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

architectō (present infinitive architectāre, perfect active architectāvī, supine architectātum); first conjugation

  1. to build
