Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [äbˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [äbˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

abjiciō (present infinitive abjicere, perfect active abjēcī, supine abjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of abiciō
