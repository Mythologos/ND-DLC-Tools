Latin[edit]

Verb[edit]

perlūstrō (present infinitive perlūstrāre, perfect active perlūstrāvī, supine perlūstrātum); first conjugation

  1. to go over, wander, traverse
    Synonyms: errō, pervagor, peragrō, vagor, discurrō, pererrō, lūstrō, pālor
  2. to visit, see, search, explore
  3. to consider, examine, observe, survey, look at
    Synonyms: aspiciō, lūstrō, recēnseō, circumspiciō, cōnspiciō, obeō, īnspiciō, arbitror, cōnsīderō, reputō, exsequor
  4. to purify
    Synonym: lūstrō
