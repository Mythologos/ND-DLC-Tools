Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ olfaciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.bolˈfa.ki.oː/, [s̠ʊbɔɫ̪ˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.bolˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [subolˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

subolfaciō (present infinitive subolfacere, perfect active subolfēcī, supine subolfactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. (literal, transitive) to smell out, scent
  2. (figuratively, transitive) to detect, perceive
