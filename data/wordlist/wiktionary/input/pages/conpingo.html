Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈpin.ɡoː/, [kɔmˈpɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈpin.ɡo/, [konˈpiŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

conpingō (present infinitive conpingere, perfect active conpēgī, supine conpāctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of compingō
