Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- (“circum-”) +‎ aedificō (“I build”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.ku.mae̯ˈdi.fi.koː/, [kɪrkʊmäe̯ˈd̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.ku.meˈdi.fi.ko/, [t͡ʃirkumeˈd̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

circumaedificō (present infinitive circumaedificāre, perfect active circumaedificāvī, supine circumaedificātum); first conjugation

  1. to build around
