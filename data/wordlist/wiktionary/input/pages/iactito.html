Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • jactitō

Etymology[edit]

From iactō (“I boast, brag”) +‎ -itō (frequentative suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯ak.ti.toː/, [ˈi̯äkt̪ɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈjak.ti.to/, [ˈjäkt̪it̪o]

Verb[edit]

iactitō (present infinitive iactitāre, perfect active iactitāvī, supine iactitātum); first conjugation

  1. (as remarks) to throw, to fling
  2. to boast, to brag
  3. to utter or mention
