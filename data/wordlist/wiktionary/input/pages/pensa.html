Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central) [ˈpɛn.sə]
  • IPA(key): (Balearic) [ˈpən.sə]
  • IPA(key): (Valencian) [ˈpen.sa]

Etymology 1[edit]

Deverbal from pensar.

Noun[edit]

pensa f (plural penses)

  1. thought (the facility to think)
  2. thought (the act of thinking)

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

pensa

  1. inflection of pensar:
    1. third-person singular present indicative
    2. second-person singular imperative

Further reading[edit]

  • “pensa” in Diccionari de la llengua catalana, segona edició, Institut d’Estudis Catalans.

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From pensum (“weighed out”).

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpen.sa/, [ˈpẽːs̠ä]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpen.sa/, [ˈpɛnsä]

Noun[edit]

pensa f (genitive pensae); first declension

  1. a day's provisions; ration for a day.
Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpen.sa/, [ˈpẽːs̠ä]

Pronunciation[edit]

Verb[edit]
