Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /babˈtiz.zoː/, [bäpˈt̪ɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /babˈtid.d͡zo/, [bäbˈt̪id̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

babtizō (present infinitive babtizāre, perfect active babtizāvī, supine babtizātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of baptizō
