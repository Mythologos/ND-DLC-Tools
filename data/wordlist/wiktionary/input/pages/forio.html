Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Possibly from foria (“diarrhoea”), possibly from foris (“door; opening, entrance”).

Verb[edit]

foriō (present infinitive forīre, perfect active foriī); fourth conjugation, no supine stem

  1. to evacuate the intestine
  2. to defecate, shit
