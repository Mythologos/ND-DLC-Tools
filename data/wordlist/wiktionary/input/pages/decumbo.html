Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ *cumbō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈkum.boː/, [d̪eːˈkʊmboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈkum.bo/, [d̪eˈkumbo]

Verb[edit]

dēcumbō (present infinitive dēcumbere, perfect active dēcubuī); third conjugation, no supine stem, impersonal in the passive

  1. to lie down or recline
  2. to fall (in a fight)
