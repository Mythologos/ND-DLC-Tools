Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ vomō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.u̯o.moː/, [ˈeːu̯ɔmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.vo.mo/, [ˈɛːvomo]

Verb[edit]

ēvomō (present infinitive ēvomere, perfect active ēvomuī, supine ēvomitum); third conjugation

  1. to vomit up
