Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈpe.di.oː/, [kɔmˈpɛd̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈpe.di.o/, [konˈpɛːd̪io]

Verb[edit]

conpediō (present infinitive conpedīre, perfect active conpedīvī, supine conpedītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of compediō
