Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈnuː.tri.oː/, [eːˈnuːt̪rioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈnu.tri.o/, [eˈnuːt̪rio]

Verb[edit]

ēnūtriō (present infinitive ēnūtrīre, perfect active ēnūtrīvī or ēnūtriī, supine ēnūtrītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to nurture or rear (offspring)
  2. to promote
