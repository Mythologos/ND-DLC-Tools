Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /resˈkriː.boː/, [rɛs̠ˈkriːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /resˈkri.bo/, [resˈkriːbo]

Verb[edit]

rescrībō (present infinitive rescrībere, perfect active rescrīpsī, supine rescrīptum); third conjugation

  1. to write back in reply
    Is ad me rescripsit.
    He wrote to me again.
