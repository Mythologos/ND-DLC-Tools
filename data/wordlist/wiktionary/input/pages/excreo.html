Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.oː/, [ˈɛks̠kreoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.o/, [ˈɛkskreo]

Verb[edit]

excreō (present infinitive excreāre, perfect active excreāvī, supine excreātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exscreō
