Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnae̯.kʷoː/, [ɪˈnäe̯kʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈne.kwo/, [iˈnɛːkwo]

Verb[edit]

inaequō (present infinitive inaequāre, perfect active inaequāvī, supine inaequātum); first conjugation

  1. to make equal, level or even
