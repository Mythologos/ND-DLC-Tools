English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /laɪˈbɪəɹ.i.ə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /laɪˈbɪɹ.i.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪəɹiə, -ɪɹiə

Etymology 1[edit]

From Latin līber (“free”) + -ia (“place”).

Proper noun[edit]

Liberia

  2. A country in West Africa. Official name: Republic of Liberia.

Etymology 2[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

Liberia

  1. The provincial capital of Guanacaste, Costa Rica.
