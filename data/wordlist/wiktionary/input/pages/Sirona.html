Proper noun[edit]

Sirona

  1. (Celtic mythology) A Celtic goddess of healing associated with healing springs.

Anagrams[edit]

  • Rianos, Rosina, Sonrai, Sorani, Soranî, norias, oirans, raions, rasion, sornai
