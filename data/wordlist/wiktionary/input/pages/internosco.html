Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ nōscō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈnoːs.koː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈnoːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈnos.ko/, [in̪t̪erˈnɔsko]

Verb[edit]

internōscō (present infinitive internōscere, perfect active internōvī, supine internōtum); third conjugation

  1. to distinguish between
  2. to pick out or tell apart
  3. to discern
