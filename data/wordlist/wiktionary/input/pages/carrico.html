Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • carcō

Etymology[edit]

From carrus +‎ -icō. First attested in the Lex Salica.[1]

Verb[edit]

carricō (present infinitive carricāre, perfect active carricāvī, supine carricātum); first conjugation (Late Latin)

  1. to load
