Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bomˈbar.da/
  • Rhymes: -arda
  • Hyphenation: bom‧bàr‧da

Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from French bombarde.

Noun[edit]

bombarda f (plural bombarde)

  1. bombard
  2. (nautical, historical) a small sailing ship armed with mortars
  3. (music) bombardon
Derived terms[edit]
  • bombardare
  • bombardella
  • bombardiero
  • bombardino
  • bombardone
