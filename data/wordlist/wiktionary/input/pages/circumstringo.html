Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ stringō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kumˈstrin.ɡoː/, [kɪrkũːˈs̠t̪rɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kumˈstrin.ɡo/, [t͡ʃirkumˈst̪riŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

circumstringō (present infinitive circumstringere, perfect active circumstrīnxī, supine circumstrictum); third conjugation

  1. to press around, tighten around, compress around
