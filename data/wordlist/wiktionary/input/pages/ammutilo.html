Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • admutilō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /amˈmu.ti.loː/, [ämˈmʊt̪ɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /amˈmu.ti.lo/, [ämˈmuːt̪ilo]

Verb[edit]

ammutilō (present infinitive ammutilāre, perfect active ammutilāvī, supine ammutilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of admutilō
