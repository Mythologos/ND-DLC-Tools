Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • vēndō
  • vēnundō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯eːˈnun.doː/, [u̯eːˈnʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /veˈnun.do/, [veˈnun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

vēnumdō (present infinitive vēnumdare, perfect active vēnumdedī, supine vēnumdatum); first conjugation, irregular

  1. Alternative form of vēndō
