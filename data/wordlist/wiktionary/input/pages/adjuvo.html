Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.i̯u.u̯oː/, [ˈäd̪i̯uː̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ju.vo/, [ˈäd̪juvo]

Verb[edit]

adjuvō (present infinitive adjuvāre, perfect active adjūvī, supine adjūtum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of adiuvō
