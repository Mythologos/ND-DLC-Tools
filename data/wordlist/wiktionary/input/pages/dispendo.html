Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /diˈspɛn.do/
  • Rhymes: -ɛndo
  • Hyphenation: di‧spèn‧do

Verb[edit]

dispendo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of dispendere

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From dis- (“asunder”) +‎ pendō (“I weigh”).

Verb[edit]

dispendō (present infinitive dispendere, perfect active dispendī, supine dispēnsum); third conjugation

  1. to dispense, distribute

Etymology 2[edit]

From dis- (“asunder”) +‎ pandō (“spread”).

Alternative forms[edit]

  • dispandō
  • dispennō

Verb[edit]

dispendō (present infinitive dispendere, perfect active dispendī, supine dispessum); third conjugation

  1. to spread out, to stretch out, to extend, to expand
