Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

remigo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of remigare

Anagrams[edit]

  • emigro, emigrò, gremio, regimo, regimò

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From rēmus (“oar”) +‎ -igō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈreː.mi.ɡoː/, [ˈreːmɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.mi.ɡo/, [ˈrɛːmiɡo]

Verb[edit]

rēmigō (present infinitive rēmigāre, perfect active rēmigāvī, supine rēmigātum); first conjugation

  1. (nautical) to row
