Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈpuːr.ɡoː/, [proːˈpuːrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈpur.ɡo/, [proˈpurɡo]

Verb[edit]

prōpūrgō (present infinitive prōpūrgāre, perfect active prōpūrgāvī, supine prōpūrgātum); first conjugation

  1. to cleanse beforehand
