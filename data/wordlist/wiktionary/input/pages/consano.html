Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ sānō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈsaː.noː/, [kõːˈs̠äːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈsa.no/, [konˈsäːno]

Verb[edit]

cōnsānō (present infinitive cōnsānāre, perfect active cōnsānāvī, supine cōnsānātum); first conjugation

  1. to heal, cure
