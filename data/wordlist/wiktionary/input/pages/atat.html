Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.tat/, [ˈät̪ät̪]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.tat/, [ˈäːt̪ät̪]

Interjection[edit]

atat

  1. Alternative form of attat

Tagalog[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Standard Tagalog) IPA(key): /ʔaˈtat/, [ʔɐˈtat]
  • Rhymes: -at
  • Syllabification: a‧tat

Adjective[edit]

atát (Baybayin spelling ᜀᜆᜆ᜔)

  1. (colloquial) very impatient; extremely eager (to do something)
  2. Alternative form of at-at: stammering

Noun[edit]

atát (Baybayin spelling ᜀᜆᜆ᜔)

  1. (colloquial) very impatient person

Derived terms[edit]

  • atat na atat
 

Further reading[edit]

  • Zorc, R. David; San Miguel, Rachel (1993) Tagalog Slang Dictionary, Manila: De La Salle University Press, →ISBN, page 7

Pronunciation[edit]

Pronoun[edit]

atat
