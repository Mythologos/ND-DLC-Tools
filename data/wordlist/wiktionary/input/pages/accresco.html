Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

accresco

  1. first-person singular present indicative of accrescere

Anagrams[edit]

  • scaccerò, scoccare, scoccerà

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adcrēscō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ crēscō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /akˈkreːs.koː/, [äkˈkreːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /akˈkres.ko/, [äkˈkrɛsko]

Verb[edit]

accrēscō (present infinitive accrēscere, perfect active accrēvī, supine accrētum); third conjugation, no passive

  1. to grow, increase
    Synonyms: adolēscō, crēscō, excrēscō, olēscō, glīscō
    Antonym: dēcrēscō
