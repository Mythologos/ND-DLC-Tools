Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

scarpino

  1. first-person singular present indicative of scarpinare

Anagrams[edit]

  • Scarponi, scarponi, sincarpo

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Uncertain. Found in Late and Vulgar Latin.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈskar.pi.noː/, [ˈs̠kärpɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈskar.pi.no/, [ˈskärpino]

Verb[edit]

scarpinō (present infinitive scarpināre, perfect active scarpināvī, supine scarpinātum); first conjugation

  1. to scrape, scratch, grate
