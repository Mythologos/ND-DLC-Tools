Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /mɒŋˈɡəʊli.ə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /mɑːŋˈɡoʊli.ə/

Proper noun[edit]

Mongolia

  2. A country in Eastern Asia.
  3. Region in East Asia, including Inner Mongolia and Outer Mongolia, sometimes also including Buryatia, Tuva, Altay.
