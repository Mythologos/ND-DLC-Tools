Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /koˈɔp.to/
  • Rhymes: -ɔpto
  • Hyphenation: co‧òp‧to

Verb[edit]

coopto

  1. first-person singular present indicative of cooptare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ optō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈop.toː/, [koˈɔpt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈop.to/, [koˈɔpt̪o]

Verb[edit]

cooptō (present infinitive cooptāre, perfect active cooptāvī, supine cooptātum); first conjugation

  1. to choose, elect
  2. to co-opt
