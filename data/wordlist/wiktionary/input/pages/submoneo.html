Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • summoneō

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ moneō (“warn, advise”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈmo.ne.oː/, [s̠ʊmˈmɔneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈmo.ne.o/, [subˈmɔːneo]

Verb[edit]

submoneō (present infinitive submonēre, perfect active submonuī, supine submonitum); second conjugation

  1. to remind or advise privily, give a hint
