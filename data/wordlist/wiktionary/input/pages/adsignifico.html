Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assignificō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.siɡˈni.fi.koː/, [äs̠ːɪŋˈnɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.siɲˈɲi.fi.ko/, [äd̪siɲˈɲiːfiko]

Verb[edit]

adsignificō (present infinitive adsignificāre, perfect active adsignificāvī, supine adsignificātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assignificō
