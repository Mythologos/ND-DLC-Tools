Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯i̯ˈi̯a.ke.oː/, [präe̯i̯ˈi̯äkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pra.eˈja.t͡ʃe.o/, [präeˈjäːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

praejaceō (present infinitive praejacēre, perfect active praejacuī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of praeiaceō
