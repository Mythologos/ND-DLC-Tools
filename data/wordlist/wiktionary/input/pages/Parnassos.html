English[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

Parnassos

  1. Alternative form of Parnassus

Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Παρνασσός (Parnassós).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɑrnɑsːos/, [ˈpɑ̝rnɑ̝s̠ːo̞s̠]
  • Rhymes: -ɑrnɑsːos
  • Syllabification(key): Par‧nas‧sos

Proper noun[edit]

Parnassos

  1. Parnassus (mountain in Greece)
  2. Parnassus (home of poetry)
