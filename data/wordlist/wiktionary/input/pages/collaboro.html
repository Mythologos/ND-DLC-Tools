Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

collaboro

  1. first-person singular present indicative of collaborare

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conlabōrō

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ labōrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kol.laˈboː.roː/, [kɔlːʲäˈboːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kol.laˈbo.ro/, [kolːäˈbɔːro]

Verb[edit]

collabōrō (present infinitive collabōrāre, perfect active collabōrāvī, supine collabōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to work together with others; to collaborate
