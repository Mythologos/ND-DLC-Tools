Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /pʰiˈlo.so.pʰor/, [pʰɪˈɫ̪ɔs̠ɔpʰɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /fiˈlo.so.for/, [fiˈlɔːs̬ofor]

Verb[edit]

philosophor (present infinitive philosophārī, perfect active philosophātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to philosophize
