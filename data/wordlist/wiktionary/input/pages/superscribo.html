Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ scrībō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈskriː.boː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈs̠kriːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈskri.bo/, [superˈskriːbo]

Verb[edit]

superscrībō (present infinitive superscrībere, perfect active superscrīpsī, supine superscrīptum); third conjugation

  1. to write over or upon: to superscribe
