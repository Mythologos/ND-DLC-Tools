Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ fīnītus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.fiːˈniː.tus/, [ĩːfiːˈniːt̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.fiˈni.tus/, [iɱfiˈniːt̪us]

Adjective[edit]

īnfīnītus (feminine īnfīnīta, neuter īnfīnītum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. boundless, unlimited, endless
  2. infinite
