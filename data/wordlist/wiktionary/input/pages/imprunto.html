Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Vulgar Latin *imprūmūtō. Attested in the eighth-century Reichenau Glossary.

Verb[edit]

imprūntō (present infinitive imprūntāre, perfect active imprūntāvī, supine imprūntātum); first conjugation (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. to borrow, lend
