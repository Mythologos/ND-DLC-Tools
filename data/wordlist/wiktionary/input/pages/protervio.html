Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From protervus +‎ -iō after lascīviō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proˈter.u̯i.oː/, [prɔˈt̪ɛru̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈter.vi.o/, [proˈt̪ɛrvio]

Verb[edit]

proterviō (present infinitive protervīre, perfect active protervīvī, supine protervītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to be bold or shameless
