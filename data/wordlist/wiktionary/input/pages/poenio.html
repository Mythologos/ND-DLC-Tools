Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpoe̯.ni.oː/, [ˈpoe̯nioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpe.ni.o/, [ˈpɛːnio]

Verb[edit]

poeniō (present infinitive poenīre, perfect active poenīvī or poeniī, supine poenītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of puniō
