Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • franchiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /franˈkʰiː.soː/, [fräŋˈkʰiːs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /franˈki.so/, [fräŋˈkiːs̬o]

Verb[edit]

franchīsō (present infinitive franchīsāre, perfect active franchīsāvī, supine franchīsātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) to enfranchise
