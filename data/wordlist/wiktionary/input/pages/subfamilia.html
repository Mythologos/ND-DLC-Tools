Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sub.faˈmi.li.a/, [s̠ʊfːäˈmɪlʲiä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sub.faˈmi.li.a/, [subfäˈmiːliä]

Noun[edit]

subfamilia f (genitive subfamiliae); first declension

  1. subfamily

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Case Singular Plural
Nominative subfamilia subfamiliae
Genitive subfamiliae subfamiliārum
Dative subfamiliae subfamiliīs
Accusative subfamiliam subfamiliās
Ablative subfamiliā subfamiliīs
Vocative subfamilia subfamiliae

Spanish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ familia.

Noun[edit]

subfamilia f (plural subfamilias)

  1. (taxonomy) subfamily
