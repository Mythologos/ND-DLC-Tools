Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From genū (“knee”) +‎ flectō (“bend”), literally "I bend the knee".

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡe.nuːˈflek.toː/, [ɡɛnuːˈfɫ̪ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /d͡ʒe.nuˈflek.to/, [d͡ʒenuˈflɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

genūflectō (present infinitive genūflectere, perfect active genūflexī, supine genūflexum); third conjugation

  1. (intransitive) to kneel down, to bend the knee, to genuflect
