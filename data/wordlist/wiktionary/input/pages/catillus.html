Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From catīnus (“food-vessel”) +‎ -lus (diminutive suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kaˈtiːl.lus/, [käˈt̪iːlːʲʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kaˈtil.lus/, [käˈt̪ilːus]

Noun[edit]

catīllus m (genitive catīllī); second declension

  1. small bowl, dish, or plate

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun.
