Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ofˈfu.sko/
  • Rhymes: -usko
  • Hyphenation: of‧fù‧sco

Verb[edit]

offusco

  1. first-person singular present indicative of offuscare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ob- +‎ fuscō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ofˈfus.koː/, [ɔfˈfʊs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ofˈfus.ko/, [ofˈfusko]

Verb[edit]

offuscō (present infinitive offuscāre, supine offuscātum); first conjugation, no perfect stem (Ecclesiastical Latin)

  1. to darken, obscure
  2. to vilify, degrade
