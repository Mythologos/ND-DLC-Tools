Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin lāmentum.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /laːˈmen.toː/, [ɫ̪äːˈmɛn̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /laˈmen.to/, [läˈmɛn̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

lāmentō (present infinitive lāmentāre, perfect active lāmentāvī, supine lāmentātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to lament, bewail
