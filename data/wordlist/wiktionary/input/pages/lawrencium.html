English[edit]

Chemical element
Lr
Previous: nobelium (No)
Next: rutherfordium (Rf)

Etymology[edit]

From Lawrence +‎ -ium; named for Ernest Lawrence.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: lərĕn'sēəm, IPA(key): /ləˈɹɛnsiəm/
  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

lawrencium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
lawrencium
Wikipedia
  1. A transuranic chemical element (symbol Lr, formerly Lw) with atomic number 103.

Synonyms[edit]

  • unniltrium

Related terms[edit]

  • lawrencate
  • Lawrence
