Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From auctus (“increased, enlarged”) +‎ -ficō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /au̯kˈti.fi.koː/, [äu̯kˈt̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /au̯kˈti.fi.ko/, [äu̯kˈt̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

auctificō (present infinitive auctificāre, supine auctificātum); first conjugation, no perfect stem

  1. to increase, enlarge
  2. (religion, of sacrifices) to honor by offerings
