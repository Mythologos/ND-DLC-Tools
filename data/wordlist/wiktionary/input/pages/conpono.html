Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.po.noː/, [ˈkɔmpɔnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.po.no/, [ˈkɔnpono]

Verb[edit]

conponō (present infinitive conponere, perfect active conposuī, supine conpositum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of compōnō
