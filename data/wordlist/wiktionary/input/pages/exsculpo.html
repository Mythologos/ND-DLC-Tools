Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • exculpō

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ scalpo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈskul.poː/, [ɛkˈs̠kʊɫ̪poː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈskul.po/, [ekˈskulpo]

Verb[edit]

exsculpō (present infinitive exsculpere, perfect active exsculpsī, supine exsculptum); third conjugation

  1. to dig out or carve out
  2. to elicit or extort
  3. to erase
