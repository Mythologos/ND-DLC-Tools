English[edit]

Noun[edit]

chrisma

  1. plural of chrismon

Anagrams[edit]

  • Hamrics, archism, charism

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • crisma

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek χρῖσμᾰ (khrîsma, “unction”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkʰriːs.ma/, [ˈkʰriːs̠mä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkris.ma/, [ˈkrizmä]

Noun[edit]

chrīsma n (genitive chrīsmatis); third declension

  1. (Ecclesiastical Latin) anointing, unction

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (neuter, imparisyllabic non-i-stem).
