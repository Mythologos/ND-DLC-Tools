Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • immulgeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈmul.ɡe.oː/, [ɪmˈmʊɫ̪ɡeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈmul.d͡ʒe.o/, [inˈmul̠ʲd͡ʒeo]

Verb[edit]

inmulgeō (present infinitive inmulgēre, perfect active inmulsī, supine inmulsum); second conjugation, limited passive

  1. Alternative form of immulgeō

Conjugation[edit]

  • This verb has only limited passive conjugation; only third-person passive forms are attested in surviving sources.
