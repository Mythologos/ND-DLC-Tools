Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From hīc. See also hūc.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /hink/, [hɪŋk]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ink/, [iŋk]

Adverb[edit]

hinc (not comparable)

  1. hence, from this place.
  2. henceforth.
  3. from this side, on this side, here
    Synonym: citrā
    Antonyms: ultrā, ultrō
  4. because of this, from this cause.
    Synonyms: eō, ideō
  5. next, afterwards
