Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ siccō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsik.koː/, [ɛkˈs̠ɪkːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsik.ko/, [eɡˈzikːo]

Verb[edit]

exsiccō (present infinitive exsiccāre, perfect active exsiccāvī, supine exsiccātum); first conjugation

  1. to dry up
  2. to empty (a bottle)
