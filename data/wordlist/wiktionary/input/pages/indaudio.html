Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈdau̯.di.oː/, [ɪn̪ˈd̪äu̯d̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈdau̯.di.o/, [in̪ˈd̪äːu̯d̪io]

Verb[edit]

indaudiō (present infinitive indaudīre, perfect active indaudīvī or indaudiī, supine indaudītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of inaudiō
