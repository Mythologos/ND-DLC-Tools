Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ determino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.deːˈter.mi.noː/, [präe̯d̪eːˈt̪ɛrmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.deˈter.mi.no/, [pred̪eˈt̪ɛrmino]

Verb[edit]

praedēterminō (present infinitive praedētermināre, perfect active praedētermināvī, supine praedēterminātum); first conjugation

  1. to fix beforehand; to predetermine
