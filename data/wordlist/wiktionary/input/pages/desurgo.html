Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ surgō (“to get up”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈsur.ɡoː/, [d̪eːˈs̠ʊrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈsur.ɡo/, [d̪eˈs̬urɡo]

Verb[edit]

dēsurgō (present infinitive dēsurgere); third conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, rare) to rise
  2. (intransitive) to (rise in order to) defecate
