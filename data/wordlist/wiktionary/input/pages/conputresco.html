Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.puˈtreːs.koː/, [kɔmpʊˈt̪reːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.puˈtres.ko/, [konpuˈt̪rɛsko]

Verb[edit]

conputrēscō (present infinitive conputrēscere, perfect active conputruī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of computrēscō
