Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ stillō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈstiːl.loː/, [pɛrˈs̠t̪iːlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈstil.lo/, [perˈst̪ilːo]

Verb[edit]

perstīllō (present infinitive perstīllāre, perfect active perstīllāvī, supine perstīllātum); first conjugation

  1. to drip, leak
