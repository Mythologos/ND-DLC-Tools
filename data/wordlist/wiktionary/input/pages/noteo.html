Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈnoː.te.oː/, [ˈnoːt̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈno.te.o/, [ˈnɔːt̪eo]

Verb[edit]

nōteō (present infinitive nōtēre, perfect active nōtuī, supine nōtitum); second conjugation

  1. to notify
