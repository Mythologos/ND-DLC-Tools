Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From coquus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈkʷiː.nus/, [kɔˈkʷiːnʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈkwi.nus/, [koˈkwiːnus]

Adjective[edit]

coquīnus (feminine coquīna, neuter coquīnum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. cook's
  2. of or pertaining to the kitchen
