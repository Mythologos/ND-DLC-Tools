Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

exfolio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of exfoliar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ folium +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eksˈfo.li.oː/, [ɛkfˈfɔlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eksˈfo.li.o/, [eksˈfɔːlio]

Verb[edit]

exfoliō (present infinitive exfoliāre, perfect active exfoliāvī, supine exfoliātum); first conjugation

  1. (intransitive) to strip of leaves
