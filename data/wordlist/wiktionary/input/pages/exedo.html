Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- (“out of, out from”) +‎ edō (“I eat”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.se.doː/, [ˈɛks̠ɛd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.se.do/, [ˈɛksed̪o]

Verb[edit]

exedō (present infinitive exedere, perfect active exēdī, supine exēsum); third conjugation

  1. to eat up, devour, consume
  2. to corrode
