Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ discō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈdis.koː/, [d̪eːˈd̪ɪs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈdis.ko/, [d̪eˈd̪isko]

Verb[edit]

dēdiscō (present infinitive dēdiscere, perfect active dēdidicī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to unlearn, forget
