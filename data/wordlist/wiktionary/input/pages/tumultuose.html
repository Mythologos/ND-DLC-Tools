Italian[edit]

Adjective[edit]

tumultuose

  1. feminine plural of tumultuoso

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

Inflected form.

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /tu.mul.tuˈoː.se/, [t̪ʊmʊɫ̪t̪uˈoːs̠ɛ]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tu.mul.tuˈo.se/, [t̪umul̪t̪uˈɔːs̬e]

Adjective[edit]

tumultuōse

  1. vocative masculine singular of tumultuōsus

Etymology 2[edit]

tumultuōsus +‎ -ē

Adverb[edit]

tumultuōse (comparative tumultuosius, superlative tumultuosissime)

  1. tumultuously, in confusion, disorderly
  2. in panic
