Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

vivifico

  1. first-person singular present indicative of vivificare

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯iːˈu̯i.fi.koː/, [u̯iːˈu̯ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /viˈvi.fi.ko/, [viˈviːfiko]

Verb[edit]

vīvificō (present infinitive vīvificāre, perfect active vīvificāvī, supine vīvificātum); first conjugation

  1. to vivify (to give life or vigor)
