Proper noun[edit]

Plexippus m

  1. A taxonomic genus within the family Salticidae – certain jumping spiders.

Hypernyms[edit]

  • (genus): Eukaryota – superkingdom; Animalia – kingdom; Bilateria – subkingdom; Protostomia – infrakingdom; Ecdysozoa – superphylum; Arthropoda – phylum; Chelicerata – subphylum; Arachnida – class; Araneae – order; Opisthothelae - suborder; Araneomorphae - infraorder; Neocribellatae - taxon; Entelegynae - series; Dionycha - section; Salticoidea - superfamily; Salticidae - family; Plexippinae - subfamily; Plexippini - tribe

Etymology[edit]

Ancient Greek Πλήξιππος (Plḗxippos)

Proper noun[edit]

Plexippus

  1. One of the 50 sons of Aegyptus (“a king of Egypt”), the twin brother of Danaus, who had 50 daughters.
