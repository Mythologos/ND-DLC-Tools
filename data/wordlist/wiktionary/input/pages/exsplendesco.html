Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ splendēscō (“to begin to shine”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ek.splenˈdeːs.koː/, [ɛks̠pɫ̪ɛn̪ˈd̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.splenˈdes.ko/, [eksplen̪ˈd̪ɛsko]

Verb[edit]

exsplendēscō (present infinitive exsplendēscere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to shine out
  2. (intransitive) to be eminent, be distinguished
