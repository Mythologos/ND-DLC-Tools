Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

laqueō

  1. dative/ablative singular of laqueus

Verb[edit]

laqueō (present infinitive laqueāre, perfect active laqueāvī, supine laqueātum); first conjugation

  1. to noose, entangle, ensnare
