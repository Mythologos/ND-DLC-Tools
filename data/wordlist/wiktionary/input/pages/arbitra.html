  • Rhymes: -itra

Adjective[edit]

arbitra (accusative singular arbitran, plural arbitraj, accusative plural arbitrajn)

  1. arbitrary

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈar.bi.tra/, [ˈärbɪt̪rä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈar.bi.tra/, [ˈärbit̪rä]

Etymology 1[edit]

arbiter (“witness”) +‎ -a

Noun[edit]

arbitra f (genitive arbitrae, masculine arbiter); first declension

  1. a female witness
Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

See the etymology of the corresponding lemma form.

Verb[edit]

arbitrā

  1. second-person singular present active imperative of arbitrō
