Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈlek.toː/, [proːˈɫ̪ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈlek.to/, [proˈlɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

prōlectō (present infinitive prōlectāre, perfect active prōlectāvī, supine prōlectātum); first conjugation

  1. to allure or entice
