Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • injectō

Etymology[edit]

From iniectus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈi̯ek.toː/, [ɪnˈi̯ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈjek.to/, [inˈjɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

iniectō (present infinitive iniectāre, perfect active iniectāvī, supine iniectātum); first conjugation

  1. to apply, lay on
