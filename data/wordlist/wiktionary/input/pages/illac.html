Interlingua[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From French là, Italian là, Spanish allá, and Portuguese lá, ultimately from Latin illac.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈil.lak/

Adverb[edit]

illac

  1. there (at that place)

Synonyms[edit]

  • ibi

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Adverb from the ablative ("illāc (viā)") of illic.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ilˈlaːk/, [ɪlˈlʲäːk]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ilˈlak/, [ilˈläk]

Adverb[edit]

illāc (not comparable)

  1. that way, that side, there, along that path, in that direction, over there
