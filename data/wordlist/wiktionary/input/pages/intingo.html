Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /inˈtin.ɡo/
  • Rhymes: -inɡo
  • Hyphenation: in‧tìn‧go

Verb[edit]

intingo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of intingere

Anagrams[edit]

  • Tignino

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ tingō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈtin.ɡoː/, [ɪn̪ˈt̪ɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈtin.ɡo/, [in̪ˈt̪iŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

intingō (present infinitive intingere, perfect active intīnxī, supine intīnctum); third conjugation

  1. to dip, plunge in
  2. to soak
  3. to saturate
