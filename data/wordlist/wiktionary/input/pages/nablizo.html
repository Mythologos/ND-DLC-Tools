Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek ναβλίζω (nablízō).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /naˈbliz.zoː/, [näˈblʲɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /naˈblid.d͡zo/, [näˈblid̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

nablizō (present infinitive nablizāre, perfect active nablizāvī, supine nablizātum); first conjugation

  1. to play the psaltery
