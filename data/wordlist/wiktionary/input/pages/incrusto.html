Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈkruːs.toː/, [ɪŋˈkruːs̠t̪oː] or IPA(key): /inˈkrus.toː/, [ɪŋˈkrʊs̠t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈkrus.to/, [iŋˈkrust̪o]

Verb[edit]

incrū̆stō (present infinitive incrū̆stāre, perfect active incrū̆stāvī, supine incrū̆stātum); first conjugation

  1. to encrust
