Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈfiː.ɡoː/, [ät̪ˈfiːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈfi.ɡo/, [äd̪ˈfiːɡo]

Verb[edit]

adfīgō (present infinitive adfīgere, perfect active adfīxī, supine adfīxum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of affīgō
