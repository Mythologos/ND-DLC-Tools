Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

erodo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of erodere

Anagrams[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ rōdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈroː.doː/, [eːˈroːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈro.do/, [eˈrɔːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

ērōdō (present infinitive ērōdere, perfect active ērōsī, supine ērōsum); third conjugation

  1. to gnaw away; corrode
