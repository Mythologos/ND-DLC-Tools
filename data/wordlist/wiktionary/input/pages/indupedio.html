Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • impedio

Etymology[edit]

From indu- +‎ pēs.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.duˈpe.di.oː/, [ɪn̪d̪ʊˈpɛd̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.duˈpe.di.o/, [in̪d̪uˈpɛːd̪io]

Verb[edit]

indupediō (present infinitive indupedīre, perfect active indupedīvī or indupediī, supine indupedītum); fourth conjugation

  1. (Archaic) hinder, impede
