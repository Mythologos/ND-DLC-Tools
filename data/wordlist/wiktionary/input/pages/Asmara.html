Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Tigrinya ኣስመራ (ʾasmära).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑsmɑrɑ/, [ˈɑ̝s̠mɑ̝rɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑsmɑrɑ
  • Syllabification(key): As‧ma‧ra

Proper noun[edit]

Asmara

  1. Asmara (the capital city of Eritrea)

Declension[edit]

The internal locative cases (inessive, illative and elative) are used with this place name when referring to a location; for example, "in Asmara" is Asmarassa.
