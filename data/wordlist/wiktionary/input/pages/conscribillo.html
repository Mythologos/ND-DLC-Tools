Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From cōnscrībō +‎ -illō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.skriːˈbil.loː/, [kõːs̠kriːˈbɪlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.skriˈbil.lo/, [konskriˈbilːo]

Verb[edit]

cōnscrībillō (present infinitive cōnscrībillāre, perfect active cōnscrībillāvī, supine cōnscrībillātum); first conjugation

  1. (rare) to scribble or scrawl over or upon
