Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ scindō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈskin.doː/, [kõːˈs̠kɪn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈʃin.do/, [kon̠ʲˈʃin̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

cōnscindō (present infinitive cōnscindere, perfect active cōnscidī, supine cōnscissum); third conjugation

  1. to tear or rend to pieces
