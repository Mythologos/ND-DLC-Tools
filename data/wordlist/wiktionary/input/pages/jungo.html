Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯un.ɡoː/, [ˈi̯ʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈjun.ɡo/, [ˈjuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

jungō (present infinitive jungere, perfect active jūnxī, supine jūnctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iungō
