Asturian[edit]

Adjective[edit]

cucio

  1. neuter of cuciu

Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

cucio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of cucire

Anagrams[edit]

  • -uccio, ciuco, cuoci, uccio

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈku.ki.oː/, [ˈkʊkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈku.t͡ʃi.o/, [ˈkuːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

cuciō (present infinitive cucīre, perfect active cucīvī); fourth conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to cry like a rooster
