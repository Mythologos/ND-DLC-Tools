Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈpin.ɡoː/, [kɔmˈpɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈpin.ɡo/, [komˈpiŋɡo]

Etymology 1[edit]

From con- +‎ pangō.

Verb[edit]

compingō (present infinitive compingere, perfect active compēgī, supine compāctum); third conjugation

  1. to attach
  2. to bind or join together
  3. to construct
  4. to confine
  5. to conceal

Etymology 2[edit]

From con- +‎ pingō.

Verb[edit]

compingō (present infinitive compingere, perfect active compinxī, supine compictum); third conjugation

  1. to paint over; to disguise, cover
