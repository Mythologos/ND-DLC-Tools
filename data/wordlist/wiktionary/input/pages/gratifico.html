Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡraːˈti.fi.koː/, [ɡräːˈt̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡraˈti.fi.ko/, [ɡräˈt̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

grātificō (present infinitive grātificāre, perfect active grātificāvī, supine grātificātum); first conjugation

  1. to oblige, gratify
