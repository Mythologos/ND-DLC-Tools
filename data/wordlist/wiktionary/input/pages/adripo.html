Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • arrīpō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to”) +‎ rīpa (“shore, bank”) +‎ -ō (verb-forming suffix). Attested from 822 CE.

Verb[edit]

adrīpō (present infinitive adrīpāre, perfect active adrīpāvī, supine adrīpātum); first conjugation (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. to land, come ashore
