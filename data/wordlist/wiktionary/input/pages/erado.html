Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

erado

  1. first-person singular present indicative of eradere

Anagrams[edit]

  • eroda

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex +‎ radō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈraː.doː/, [eːˈräːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈra.do/, [eˈräːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

ērādō (present infinitive ērādere, perfect active ērāsī, supine ērāsum); third conjugation

  1. to scrape away, pare
  2. to abolish, eradicate, remove
  3. to erase, delete
