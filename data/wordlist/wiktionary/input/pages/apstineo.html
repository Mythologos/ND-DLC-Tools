Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • abstineō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /apˈsti.ne.oː/, [äpˈs̠t̪ɪneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /apˈsti.ne.o/, [äpˈst̪iːneo]

Verb[edit]

apstineō (present infinitive apstinēre, perfect active apstinuī, supine apstentum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of abstineō
