Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ tracto.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈtrak.toː/, [rɛˈt̪räkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈtrak.to/, [reˈt̪räkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

retractō (present infinitive retractāre, perfect active retractāvī, supine retractātum); first conjugation

  1. to undertake again
  2. to withdraw, refuse, decline
  3. to retract
