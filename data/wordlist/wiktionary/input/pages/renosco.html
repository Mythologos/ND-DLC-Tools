Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈnoːs.koː/, [rɛˈnoːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈnos.ko/, [reˈnɔsko]

Verb[edit]

renōscō (present infinitive renōscere, perfect active renōvī, supine renōtum); third conjugation

  1. to recognise
