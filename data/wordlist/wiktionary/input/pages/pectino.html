Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From pecten (“comb”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpek.ti.noː/, [ˈpɛkt̪ɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpek.ti.no/, [ˈpɛkt̪ino]

Verb[edit]

pectinō (present infinitive pectināre, perfect active pectināvī, supine pectinātum); first conjugation

  1. to comb or card
