Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯ak.toː/, [ˈi̯äkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈjak.to/, [ˈjäkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

jactō (present infinitive jactāre, perfect active jactāvī, supine jactātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iactō

Portuguese[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Hyphenation: jac‧to

Noun[edit]

jacto m (plural jactos)

  1. Pre-reform spelling (until Brazil 1943/Portugal 1990) of jato. Still used in countries where the agreement hasn't come into effect; may occur as a sporadic misspelling.

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

jacto

  1. first-person singular present indicative of jactar
