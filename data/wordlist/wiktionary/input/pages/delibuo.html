Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈli.bu.oː/, [d̪eːˈlʲɪbuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈli.bu.o/, [d̪eˈliːbuo]

Verb[edit]

dēlibuō (present infinitive dēlibuere, perfect active dēlibuī, supine dēlibūtum); third conjugation

  1. to besmear
  2. to anoint
