Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsus.ki.toː/, [ɛkˈs̠ʊs̠kɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsuʃ.ʃi.to/, [eɡˈzuʃːit̪o]

Verb[edit]

exsuscitō (present infinitive exsuscitāre, perfect active exsuscitāvī, supine exsuscitātum); first conjugation

  1. to awaken
