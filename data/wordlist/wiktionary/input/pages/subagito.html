Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈba.ɡi.toː/, [s̠ʊˈbäɡɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈba.d͡ʒi.to/, [suˈbäːd͡ʒit̪o]

Verb[edit]

subagitō (present infinitive subagitāre, perfect active subagitāvī, supine subagitātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of subigitō
