Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ fleō (“weep”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdif.fle.oː/, [ˈd̪ɪfːɫ̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈdif.fle.o/, [ˈd̪ifːleo]

Verb[edit]

diffleō (present infinitive difflēre, perfect active difflēvī, supine difflētum); second conjugation, no passive

  1. (transitive, very rare, of the eyes) to weep away or out
