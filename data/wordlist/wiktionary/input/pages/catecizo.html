Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ka.teːˈkiz.zoː/, [kät̪eːˈkɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ka.teˈt͡ʃid.d͡zo/, [kät̪eˈt͡ʃid̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

catēcizō (present infinitive catēcizāre, perfect active catēcizāvī, supine catēcizātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of catēchīzō
