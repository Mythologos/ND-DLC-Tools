Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mors (“death”) +‎ -ficō (“-fy”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /morˈti.fi.koː/, [mɔrˈt̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /morˈti.fi.ko/, [morˈt̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

mortificō (present infinitive mortificāre, perfect active mortificāvī, supine mortificātum); first conjugation

  1. to kill or mortify
  2. to destroy
