Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdiː.i̯u.ɡoː/, [ˈd̪iːi̯ʊɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈdi.ju.ɡo/, [ˈd̪iːjuɡo]

Verb[edit]

dījugō (present infinitive dījugāre, perfect active dījugāvī, supine dījugātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative spelling of dīiugō
