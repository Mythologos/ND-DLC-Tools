Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • praeiaciō

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ iaciō (“throw, hurl”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [präe̯ˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [preˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

praeiciō (present infinitive praeicere, perfect active praeiēcī, supine praeiectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to throw or cast up in front
  2. (figuratively) to cast up, object, utter reproachfully
