Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /apˈprɛn.do/
  • Rhymes: -ɛndo
  • Hyphenation: ap‧prèn‧do

Verb[edit]

apprendo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of apprendere

Anagrams[edit]

  • propenda

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adprehendō
  • apprehendō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /apˈpreːn.doː/, [äpˈpreːn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /apˈpren.do/, [äpˈprɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

apprēndō (present infinitive apprēndere, perfect active apprēndī, supine apprēnsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of apprehendō
