Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /nəˈmɪbi.ə/
  • Rhymes: -ɪbiə
  • Audio (Canada)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Namibia

  2. A country in Southern Africa. Official name: Republic of Namibia. Capital: Windhoek.
