Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): par‧ti‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

partitio

  1. (computing) partition

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From partiō (“share, part; divide”) +‎ -tiō, from pars (“part, piece”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /parˈtiː.ti.oː/, [pärˈt̪iːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /parˈtit.t͡si.o/, [pärˈt̪it̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

partītiō f (genitive partītiōnis); third declension

  1. The act of sharing or parting; partition, division, distribution.
  2. (philosophy and rhetoric) A logical division into parts or heads, classification, partition.
