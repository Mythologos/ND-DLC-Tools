Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ linō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kirˈkum.li.noː/, [kɪrˈkʊmlʲɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃirˈkum.li.no/, [t͡ʃirˈkumlino]

Verb[edit]

circumlinō (present infinitive circumlinere, perfect active circumlevī, supine circumlitum); third conjugation

  1. to smear / anoint all over
