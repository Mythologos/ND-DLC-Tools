Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ūnus (“one”) +‎ latus (“side”) +‎ -ālis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /uː.ni.la.teˈraː.lis/, [uːnɪɫ̪ät̪ɛˈräːlʲɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /u.ni.la.teˈra.lis/, [unilät̪eˈräːlis]

Adjective[edit]

ūnilaterālis (neuter ūnilaterāle); third-declension two-termination adjective

  1. (New Latin) unilateral, one-sided
