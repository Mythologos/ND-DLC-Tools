Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpla.d͡ʒo/
  • Rhymes: -adʒo
  • Hyphenation: plà‧gio

Etymology 1[edit]

From Latin plagium.

Noun[edit]

plagio m (plural plagi)

  1. plagiarism
  2. plagiarized work
  3. subjugation
  4. conquest
Synonyms[edit]
  • copiatura
  • imitazione
  • sottomissione
  • assoggettamento
  • soggiogamento
Related terms[edit]
  • plagiare
  • plagiario

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

plagium (“kidnapping”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpla.ɡi.oː/, [ˈpɫ̪äɡioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpla.d͡ʒi.o/, [ˈpläːd͡ʒio]

Verb[edit]

plagiō (present infinitive plagiāre, perfect active plagiāvī, supine plagiātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, Late Latin) to steal (a person), kidnap
