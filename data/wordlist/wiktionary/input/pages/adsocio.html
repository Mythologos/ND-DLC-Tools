Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • associō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈso.ki.oː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɔkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈso.t͡ʃi.o/, [äd̪ˈsɔːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

adsociō (present infinitive adsociāre, perfect active adsociāvī, supine adsociātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of associō
