Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ signo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈsiɡ.noː/, [s̠ʊpˈs̠ɪŋnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈsiɲ.ɲo/, [subˈsiɲːo]

Verb[edit]

subsignō (present infinitive subsignāre, perfect active subsignāvī, supine subsignātum); first conjugation

  1. to undersign
  2. to subscribe or register
  3. to pledge (by signing)
