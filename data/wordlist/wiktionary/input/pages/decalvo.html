Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ calvus +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈkal.u̯oː/, [d̪eːˈkäɫ̪u̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈkal.vo/, [d̪eˈkälvo]

Verb[edit]

dēcalvō (present infinitive dēcalvāre, perfect active dēcalvāvī, supine dēcalvātum); first conjugation

  1. to make (or become) bald
