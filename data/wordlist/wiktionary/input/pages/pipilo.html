Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

  • From the taxonomic name of the genus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpipilo/, [ˈpipilo̞]
  • Rhymes: -ipilo
  • Syllabification(key): pi‧pi‧lo

Noun[edit]

pipilo

  1. towhee (bird of the genus Pipilo)

Verb[edit]
