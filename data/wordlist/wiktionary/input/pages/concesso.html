Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /konˈt͡ʃɛs.so/
  • Rhymes: -ɛsso
  • Hyphenation: con‧cès‧so

Participle[edit]

concesso (feminine concessa, masculine plural concessi, feminine plural concesse)

  1. past participle of concedere

Anagrams[edit]

  • concosse, sconosce

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ cessō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈkes.soː/, [kɔŋˈkɛs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈt͡ʃes.so/, [kon̠ʲˈt͡ʃɛsːo]

Verb[edit]

concessō (present infinitive concessāre, perfect active concessāvī, supine concessātum); first conjugation

  1. to cease or desist temporarily, rest
