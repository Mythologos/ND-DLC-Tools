Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • apparō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pa.roː/, [ˈäpːäroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pa.ro/, [ˈäd̪päro]

Verb[edit]

adparō (present infinitive adparāre, perfect active adparāvī, supine adparātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of apparō
