Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deˈku.ri.oː/, [d̪ɛˈkʊrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈku.ri.o/, [d̪eˈkuːrio]

Etymology 1[edit]

From decuria +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

decuriō (present infinitive decuriāre, perfect active decuriāvī, supine decuriātum); first conjugation

  2. to divide into decuriae

Etymology 2[edit]

From decuriō +‎ -ō.

Noun[edit]

decuriō m (genitive decuriōnis); third declension

  1. decurion
  2. foreman

Third-declension noun.
