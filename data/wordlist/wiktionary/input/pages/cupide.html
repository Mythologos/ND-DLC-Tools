French[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin cupidus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ky.pid/
  • Audio(file)

Adjective[edit]

cupide (plural cupides)

  1. greedy

Related terms[edit]

  • cupidité

Further reading[edit]

  • “cupide”, in Trésor de la langue française informatisé [Digitized Treasury of the French Language], 2012.

Latin[edit]

Adverb[edit]

cupidē (comparative cupidius, superlative cupidissimē)

  1. eagerly, particularly

Adjective[edit]

cupide

  1. vocative masculine singular of cupidus

References[edit]

  • “cupide”, in Charlton T. Lewis and Charles Short (1879) A Latin Dictionary, Oxford: Clarendon Press
  • cupide in Gaffiot, Félix (1934) Dictionnaire illustré latin-français, Hachette.
