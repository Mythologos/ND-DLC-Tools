Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

See immurmurō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈmur.mu.roː/, [ɪmˈmʊrmʊroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈmur.mu.ro/, [inˈmurmuro]

Verb[edit]

inmurmurō (present infinitive inmurmurāre, perfect active inmurmurāvī, supine inmurmurātum); first conjugation

  1. (intransitive) Alternative form of immurmurō
  2. (transitive) Alternative form of immurmurō
