Icelandic[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unclear.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɛtla/
  • Rhymes: -ɛtla

Noun[edit]

mella f (genitive singular mellu, nominative plural mellur)

  1. whore, hooker
    Synonym: vændiskona

Etymology[edit]

From mel (“honey”).

mella
