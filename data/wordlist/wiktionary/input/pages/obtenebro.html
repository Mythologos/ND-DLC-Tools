Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈte.ne.broː/, [ɔpˈt̪ɛnɛbroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈte.ne.bro/, [obˈt̪ɛːnebro]

Verb[edit]

obtenebrō (present infinitive obtenebrāre, perfect active obtenebrāvī, supine obtenebrātum); first conjugation

  1. to darken
  2. to obscure
