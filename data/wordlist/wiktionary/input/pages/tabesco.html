Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From tābeō +‎ -scō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /taːˈbeːs.koː/, [t̪äːˈbeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /taˈbes.ko/, [t̪äˈbɛsko]

Verb[edit]

tābēscō (present infinitive tābēscere, perfect active tābuī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to gradually melt or dissolve
  2. to dwindle away, waste away
