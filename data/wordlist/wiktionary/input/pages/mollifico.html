Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mollis (“soft”) +‎ faciō (“I do, I make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /molˈli.fi.ko/, [mɔlˈlʲɪfɪkɔ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /molˈli.fi.ko/, [molˈliːfiko]

Verb[edit]

mollificō (present infinitive mollificāre, perfect active mollificāvī, supine mollificātum); first conjugation

  1. to make soft, mollify
