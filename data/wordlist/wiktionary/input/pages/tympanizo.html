Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek τυμπανίζω (tumpanízō, “to play the drum”). See tympanum.

Verb[edit]

tympanizō (present infinitive tympanizāre, perfect active tympanizāvī, supine tympanizātum); first conjugation

  1. to play a drum, beat a drum
