Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ plantō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /imˈplan.toː/, [ɪmˈpɫ̪än̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /imˈplan.to/, [imˈplän̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

implantō (present infinitive implantāre, perfect active implantāvī, supine implantātum); first conjugation

  1. to implant, put in, add
