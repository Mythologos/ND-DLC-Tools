Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- (intensive prefix) +‎ pūrus (“pure”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈpuː.roː/, [d̪eːˈpuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈpu.ro/, [d̪eˈpuːro]

Verb[edit]

dēpūrō (present infinitive dēpūrāre, perfect active dēpūrāvī, supine dēpūrātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, Medieval Latin, literally, figuratively) to purify, cleanse
