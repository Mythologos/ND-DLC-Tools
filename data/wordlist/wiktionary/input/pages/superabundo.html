Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ abundo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.pe.raˈbun.doː/, [s̠ʊpɛräˈbʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.pe.raˈbun.do/, [superäˈbun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

superabundō (present infinitive superabundāre, perfect active superabundāvī, supine superabundātum); first conjugation

  1. to be very abundant; to superabound
