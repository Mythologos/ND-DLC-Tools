Hungarian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin plebeius (“plebeian”), from plebs (“common people”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈplɛbɛjuʃ]
  • Hyphenation: ple‧be‧jus
  • Rhymes: -uʃ

Adjective[edit]

plebejus (comparative plebejusabb, superlative legplebejusabb)

  1. plebeian

Noun[edit]

plebejus (plural plebejusok)

  1. plebeian
