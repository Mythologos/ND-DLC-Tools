Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- (“off, away”) +‎ gerō (“carry, wear”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.ɡe.roː/, [ˈd̪eːɡɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.d͡ʒe.ro/, [ˈd̪ɛːd͡ʒero]

Verb[edit]

dēgerō (present infinitive dēgerere, perfect active dēgessī, supine dēgestum); third conjugation

  1. (rare) to carry away, carry off
