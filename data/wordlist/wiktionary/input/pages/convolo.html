Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /konˈvo.lo/
  • Rhymes: -olo
  • Hyphenation: con‧vó‧lo

Verb[edit]

convolo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of convolare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ volō (“I fly”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.u̯o.loː/, [ˈkɔnu̯ɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.vo.lo/, [ˈkɔɱvolo]

Verb[edit]

convolō (present infinitive convolāre, perfect active convolāvī, supine convolātum); first conjugation

  1. to fly or flock together with others
