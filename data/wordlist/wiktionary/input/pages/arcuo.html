Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

arcuo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of arcuare

Anagrams[edit]

  • cuora, rauco

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From arcus (“bow”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈar.ku.oː/, [ˈärkuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈar.ku.o/, [ˈärkuo]

Verb[edit]

arcuō (present infinitive arcuāre, perfect active arcuāvī, supine arcuātum); first conjugation

  1. to make in the form of a bow, bend or curve like a bow
