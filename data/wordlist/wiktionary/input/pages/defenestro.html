Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē (“out of; down from”) +‎ fenestra (“window”) +‎ -ō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.feˈnes.troː/, [d̪eːfɛˈnɛs̠t̪roː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.feˈnes.tro/, [d̪efeˈnɛst̪ro]

Verb[edit]

dēfenestrō (present infinitive dēfenestrāre, perfect active dēfenestrāvī, supine dēfenestrātum); first conjugation

  1. (New Latin, humorous) to defenestrate
