Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sessus +‎ -itō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈses.si.toː/, [ˈs̠ɛs̠ːɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈses.si.to/, [ˈsɛsːit̪o]

Verb[edit]

sessitō (present infinitive sessitāre, perfect active sessitāvī, supine sessitātum); first conjugation

  1. to sit for long
