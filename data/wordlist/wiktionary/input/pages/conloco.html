Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.lo.koː/, [ˈkɔnɫ̪ɔkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.lo.ko/, [ˈkɔnloko]

Verb[edit]

conlocō (present infinitive conlocāre, perfect active conlocāvī, supine conlocātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of collocō
