Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ salūtō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.saˈluː.toː/, [kõːs̠äˈɫ̪uːt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.saˈlu.to/, [konsäˈluːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

cōnsalūtō (present infinitive cōnsalūtāre, perfect active cōnsalūtāvī, supine cōnsalūtātum); first conjugation

  1. to greet, salute cordially; hail
    Synonym: salūtō
