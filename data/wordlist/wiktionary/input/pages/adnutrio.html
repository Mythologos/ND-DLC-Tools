Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈnuː.tri.oː/, [änˈnuːt̪rioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈnu.tri.o/, [äd̪ˈnuːt̪rio]

Verb[edit]

adnūtriō (present infinitive adnūtrīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of annūtriō
