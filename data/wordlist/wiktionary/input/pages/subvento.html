Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ ventō.

Verb[edit]

subventō (present infinitive subventāre, perfect active subventāvī, supine subventātum); first conjugation

  1. to bring aid, rescue
  2. to come quickly to assistance
