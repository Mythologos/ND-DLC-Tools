Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.suˈpiː.noː/, [rɛs̠ʊˈpiːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.suˈpi.no/, [res̬uˈpiːno]

Verb[edit]

resupīnō (present infinitive resupīnāre, perfect active resupīnāvī, supine resupīnātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to bend, pull back
  2. (transitive) to knock down flat
