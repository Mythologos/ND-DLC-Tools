Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ascendō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“[up] to”) +‎ scandō (“climb”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsken.doː/, [äs̠(ː)kɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈʃen.do/, [äd̪ˈʃɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

adscendō (present infinitive adscendere, perfect active adscendī, supine adscēnsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of ascendō (“to climb up; to rise”)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (climb up): conscendō, ēscendō, īnscendō, scandō
  • (rise): orior, prōdeō, surgō
