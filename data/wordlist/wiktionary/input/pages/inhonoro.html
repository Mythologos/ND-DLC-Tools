Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.hoˈnoː.roː/, [ɪn(ɦ)ɔˈnoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /i.noˈno.ro/, [inoˈnɔːro]

Verb[edit]

inhonōrō (present infinitive inhonōrāre, perfect active inhonōrāvī, supine inhonōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to dishonour
