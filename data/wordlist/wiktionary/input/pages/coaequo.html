Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ aequō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈae̯.kʷoː/, [koˈäe̯kʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈe.kwo/, [koˈɛːkwo]

Verb[edit]

coaequō (present infinitive coaequāre, perfect active coaequāvī, supine coaequātum); first conjugation

  1. to equalize
  2. to equate
