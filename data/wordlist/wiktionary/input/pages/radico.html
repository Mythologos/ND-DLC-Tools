Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

radico

  1. first-person singular present indicative of radicare

Anagrams[edit]

  • -cardio, Coardi, Ocrida, Orcadi, cardio-, cordai, dacrio-, darico, dorica, ricado, rodica

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From rādīx (“root”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /raːˈdiː.koː/, [räːˈd̪iːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /raˈdi.ko/, [räˈd̪iːko]

Verb[edit]

rādīcō (present infinitive rādīcāre, perfect active rādīcāvī, supine rādīcātum); first conjugation

  1. to take root
