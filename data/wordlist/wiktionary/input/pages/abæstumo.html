Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈbae̯s.tu.moː/, [äˈbäe̯s̠t̪ʊmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈbes.tu.mo/, [äˈbɛst̪umo]

Verb[edit]

abæstumō (present infinitive abæstumāre, perfect active abæstumāvī, supine abæstumātum); first conjugation

  1. (Old Latin) Alternative form of abaestumō
