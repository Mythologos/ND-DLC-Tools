Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From agricola (“farmer”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈɡri.ko.lor/, [äˈɡrɪkɔɫ̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈɡri.ko.lor/, [äˈɡriːkolor]

Verb[edit]

agricolor (present infinitive agricolārī, perfect active agricolātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to cultivate land, farm, pursue agriculture
