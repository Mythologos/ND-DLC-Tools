Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From spatha (“blade”) +‎ -āceus from Ancient Greek σπάθη (spáthē).

Adjective[edit]

spathāceus (feminine spathācea, neuter spathāceum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (New Latin, botany) furnished with or enclosed by a spathe; resembling a spathe
