Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

depravo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of depravare

Anagrams[edit]

  • pervado, predavo, proveda

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ prāvus +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈpraː.u̯oː/, [d̪eːˈpräːu̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈpra.vo/, [d̪eˈpräːvo]

Verb[edit]

dēprāvō (present infinitive dēprāvāre, perfect active dēprāvāvī, supine dēprāvātum); first conjugation

  1. to pervert, distort or disfigure
  2. to seduce, corrupt or deprave
