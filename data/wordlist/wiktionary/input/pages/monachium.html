Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /moˈna.kʰi.um/, [mɔˈnäkʰiʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /moˈna.ki.um/, [moˈnäːkium]

Proper noun[edit]

Monachium n sg (genitive Monachiī or Monachī); second declension

  1. Alternative form of Monacum

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter), with locative, singular only.
