Interlingua[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /kal.tse.a/

Noun[edit]

calcea (plural calceas)

  1. sock
  2. stocking

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From calce- +‎ -a (feminine ending). Attested from 800 CE in the works of Theodulf of Orléans.[1]

Noun[edit]

calcea f (genitive calceae); first declension (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. stocking (? presumably)
Declension[edit]
