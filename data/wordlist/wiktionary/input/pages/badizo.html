Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /baˈdiz.zoː/, [bäˈd̪ɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /baˈdid.d͡zo/, [bäˈd̪id̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

badizō (present infinitive badizāre, perfect active badizāvī, supine badizātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of badissō
