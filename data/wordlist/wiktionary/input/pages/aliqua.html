Latin[edit]

Adverb[edit]

aliquā (not comparable)

  1. somehow
  2. to some extent

Pronoun[edit]

aliqua

  1. inflection of aliquis:
    1. feminine nominative singular
    2. neuter nominative/accusative plural

Pronoun[edit]

aliquā

  1. feminine ablative singular of aliquis
