Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈka.ma.roː/, [ˈkämäroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈka.ma.ro/, [ˈkäːmäro]

Etymology 1[edit]

Verb[edit]

camarō (present infinitive camarāre, perfect active camarāvī, supine camarātum); first conjugation
