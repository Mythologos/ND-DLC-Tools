Noun[edit]

democratia (plural democratias)

  1. democracy

Related terms[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the Ancient Greek δημοκρᾰτῐ́ᾱ (dēmokratíā, “democracy”, “popular government”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.moˈkra.ti.a/, [d̪eːmɔˈkrät̪iä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.moˈkrat.t͡si.a/, [d̪emoˈkrät̪ː͡s̪iä]

Noun[edit]

dēmocratia f (genitive dēmocratiae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) democracy

Declension[edit]
