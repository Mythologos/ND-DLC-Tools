Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English inhibition), ultimately from Latin inhibitiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈinhibitio/, [ˈinhiˌbit̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -itio
  • Syllabification(key): in‧hi‧bi‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

inhibitio

  1. inhibition

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

inhibeō +‎ -tiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.hiˈbi.ti.oː/, [ɪn(ɦ)ɪˈbɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /i.niˈbit.t͡si.o/, [iniˈbit̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

inhibitiō f (genitive inhibitiōnis); third declension

  1. restraint
  2. inhibition

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
