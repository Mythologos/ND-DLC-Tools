Etymology 1[edit]

From Latin Juba, variant of Iuba.

Proper noun[edit]

Juba

  1. (historical) A male given name from Latin, notably borne by kings of Numidia.
Related terms[edit]
  • Cordoba

Juba

  2. Alternative form of Jubba, a river in Ethiopia and Somalia.

Proper noun[edit]
