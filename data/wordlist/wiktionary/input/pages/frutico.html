Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From frutic- +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfru.ti.koː/, [ˈfrʊt̪ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfru.ti.ko/, [ˈfruːt̪iko]

Verb[edit]

fruticō (present infinitive fruticāre, perfect active fruticāvī, supine fruticātum); first conjugation

  1. to put forth shoots
  2. to sprout
