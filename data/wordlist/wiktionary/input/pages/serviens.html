French[edit]

Adjective[edit]

serviens

  1. masculine plural of servien

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Present active participle of serviō (“serve, be a slave to”)

Participle[edit]

serviēns (genitive servientis); third-declension one-termination participle

  1. serving, (as a servant or slave)
  2. being devoted to, subject to

Noun[edit]

serviēns m (genitive servientis); third declension

  1. a sergeant
  2. a servant
