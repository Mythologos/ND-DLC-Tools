Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek ὁμολογέω (homologéō).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /hoˈmo.lo.ɡoː/, [hɔˈmɔɫ̪ɔɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈmo.lo.ɡo/, [oˈmɔːloɡo]

Verb[edit]

homologō (present infinitive homologāre, perfect active homologāvī, supine homologātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) to homologate
