Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- (“out”) +‎ ululō (“howl, cry”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.lu.loː/, [ɛkˈs̠ʊɫ̪ʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.lu.lo/, [eɡˈzuːlulo]

Verb[edit]

exululō (present infinitive exululāre, perfect active exululāvī, supine exululātum); first conjugation

  1. to howl out
  2. to call by howling
