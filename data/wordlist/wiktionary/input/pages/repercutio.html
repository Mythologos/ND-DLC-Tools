Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ percutiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.perˈku.ti.oː/, [rɛpɛrˈkʊt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.perˈkut.t͡si.o/, [reperˈkut̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

repercutiō (present infinitive repercutere, perfect active repercussī, supine repercussum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to rebound, reflect
  2. to reverberate, resound
