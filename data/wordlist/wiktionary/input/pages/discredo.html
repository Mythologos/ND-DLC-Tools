Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dis- +‎ crēdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈkreː.doː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈkreːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈkre.do/, [d̪isˈkrɛːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

discrēdō (present infinitive discrēdere, perfect active discrēdidī, supine discrēditum); third conjugation

  1. (transitive, Late Latin) to be incredulous towards, to don't believe (in)
