Etymology[edit]

From Xhosa Mandela.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /mænˈdɛlə/

Proper noun[edit]

Mandela

  1. A surname from Xhosa, notably borne by Nelson Mandela.

Derived terms[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /manˈdeː.la/, [män̪ˈd̪eːɫ̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /manˈde.la/, [män̪ˈd̪ɛːlä]

Proper noun[edit]

Mandēla f sg (genitive Mandēlae); first declension

  1. a small town in the Sabines, near the Digentia

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, with locative, singular only.
