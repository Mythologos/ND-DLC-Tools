Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /inˈkon.tra/
  • Rhymes: -ontra
  • Hyphenation: in‧cón‧tra

Etymology 1[edit]

From Late Latin incontrā.

Adverb[edit]

incontra

  1. (archaic, poetic) Alternative form of incontro

Etymology 2[edit]

From incontrare.

Verb[edit]

incontra

  1. inflection of incontrare:
    1. third-person singular present indicative
    2. second-person singular imperative

Anagrams[edit]

  • trancino, trincano

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in + contrā (“against”, “opposite to”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈkon.traː/, [ɪŋˈkɔn̪t̪räː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈkon.tra/, [iŋˈkɔn̪t̪rä]

Adverb[edit]

incontrā (not comparable)

  1. (Late Latin) towards

Descendants[edit]

  • Italian: incontra, incontro
