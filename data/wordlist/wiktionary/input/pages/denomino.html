Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ nōminō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈnoː.mi.noː/, [d̪eːˈnoːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈno.mi.no/, [d̪eˈnɔːmino]

Verb[edit]

dēnōminō (present infinitive dēnōmināre, perfect active dēnōmināvī, supine dēnōminātum); first conjugation

  1. to name, designate, nominate
