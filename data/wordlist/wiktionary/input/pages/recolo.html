Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ko.loː/, [ˈrɛkɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ko.lo/, [ˈrɛːkolo]

Verb[edit]

recolō (present infinitive recolere, perfect active recoluī, supine recultum); third conjugation

  1. to till or cultivate again
  2. to renew
  3. to reconsider
  4. to recall or reflect upon
  5. to contemplate
