Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ roto.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kirˈkum.ro.toː/, [kɪrˈkʊmrɔt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃirˈkum.ro.to/, [t͡ʃirˈkumrot̪o]

Verb[edit]

circumrotō (present infinitive circumrotāre, perfect active circumrotāvī, supine circumrotātum); first conjugation

  1. to turn in a circle
