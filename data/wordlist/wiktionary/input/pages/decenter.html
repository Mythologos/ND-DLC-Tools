English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

de- +‎ center

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes: -ɛntə(ɹ)

Verb[edit]

decenter (third-person singular simple present decenters, present participle decentering, simple past and past participle decentered)

  1. Alternative spelling of decentre

Anagrams[edit]

  • centered, decentre

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

decens +‎ -ter

Adverb[edit]

decenter (comparative decentius, no superlative)

  1. seemly, decently, properly

References[edit]

  • “decenter”, in Charlton T. Lewis (1891) An Elementary Latin Dictionary, New York: Harper & Brothers
