Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin capillaris, itself derived from Latin capillus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ka.pilˈla.re/
  • Rhymes: -are
  • Hyphenation: ca‧pil‧là‧re

Adjective[edit]

capillare (plural capillari)

  1. capillary
  2. detailed, extensive
  3. vast, widespread

Derived terms[edit]

  • capillarmente

Noun[edit]

capillare m (plural capillari)

  1. (anatomy) capillary

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ka.pilˈlaː.re/, [käpɪlˈlʲäːrɛ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ka.pilˈla.re/, [käpilˈläːre]

Noun[edit]

capillāre n (genitive capillāris); third declension

  1. pomatum, pomade

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (neuter, “pure” i-stem).
