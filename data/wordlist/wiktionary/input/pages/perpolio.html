Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ polio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈpo.li.oː/, [pɛrˈpɔlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈpo.li.o/, [perˈpɔːlio]

Verb[edit]

perpoliō (present infinitive perpolīre, perfect active perpolīvī, supine perpolītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to polish thoroughly
  2. to put finishing touches to
