Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ temerō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈte.me.roː/, [kɔn̪ˈt̪ɛmɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈte.me.ro/, [kon̪ˈt̪ɛːmero]

Verb[edit]

contemerō (present infinitive contemerāre, perfect active contemerāvī, supine contemerātum); first conjugation

  1. (rare) to stain, pollute, defile
