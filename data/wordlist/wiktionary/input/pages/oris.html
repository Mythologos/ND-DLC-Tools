Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central, Balearic, Valencian) [ˈo.ɾis]

Verb[edit]

oris

  1. second-person singular present subjunctive of orar

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • ōris: (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈoː.ris/, [ˈoːrɪs̠]
  • ōris: (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈo.ris/, [ˈɔːris]
  • ōrīs: (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈoː.riːs/, [ˈoːriːs̠]
  • ōrīs: (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈo.ris/, [ˈɔːris]

Etymology 1[edit]

Contraction of auris.

Noun[edit]

ōris f (genitive ōris); third declension

  1. Alternative spelling of auris.
