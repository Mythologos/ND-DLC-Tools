Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ro.ɡoː/, [ˈäd̪rɔɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ro.ɡo/, [ˈäd̪roɡo]

Verb[edit]

adrogō (present infinitive adrogāre, perfect active adrogāvī, supine adrogātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of arrogō
