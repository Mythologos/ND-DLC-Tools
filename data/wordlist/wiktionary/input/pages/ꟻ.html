ꟻ U+A7FB, &#43003;
LATIN EPIGRAPHIC LETTER REVERSED F
← ꟺ
[U+A7FA] 		Latin Extended-D ꟼ →
[U+A7FC]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

  • A reversed F, recto 'F' standing for filius (“son”).

Noun[edit]

ꟻ f (indeclinable)

  1. An abbreviation for filia (“daughter”) or femina (“wife”).
