Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈfun.doː/, [ɔfˈfʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈfun.do/, [obˈfun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

obfundō (present infinitive obfundere, perfect active obfūdī, supine obfūsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of offundo
