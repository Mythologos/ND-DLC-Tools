Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.pe.roː/, [ɛkˈs̠ʊpɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.pe.ro/, [eɡˈzuːpero]

Verb[edit]

exuperō (present infinitive exuperāre, perfect active exuperāvī, supine exuperātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exsuperō
