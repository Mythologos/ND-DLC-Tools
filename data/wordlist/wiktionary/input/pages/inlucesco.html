Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • inlūcīscō, illūcīscō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.luːˈkeːs.koː/, [ɪnɫ̪uːˈkeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.luˈt͡ʃes.ko/, [inluˈt͡ʃɛsko]

Verb[edit]

inlūcēscō (present infinitive inlūcēscere, perfect active inlūxī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of illūcēscō

Conjugation[edit]

  • The third principal part is shared with inlūceō.
