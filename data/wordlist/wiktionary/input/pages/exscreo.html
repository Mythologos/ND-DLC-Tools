Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • excreo

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ screō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.oː/, [ˈɛks̠kreoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.o/, [ˈɛkskreo]

Verb[edit]

exscreō (present infinitive exscreāre, perfect active exscreāvī, supine exscreātum); first conjugation

  1. to cough up or out
