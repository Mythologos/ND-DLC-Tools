Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ induo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.peˈrin.du.oː/, [s̠ʊpɛˈrɪn̪d̪uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.peˈrin.du.o/, [supeˈrin̪d̪uo]

Verb[edit]

superinduō (present infinitive superinduere, perfect active superinduī, supine superindūtum); third conjugation

  1. to put on over other clothes
