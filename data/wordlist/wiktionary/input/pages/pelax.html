Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpe.laːks/, [ˈpɛɫ̪äːks̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpe.laks/, [ˈpɛːläks]

Noun[edit]

pelāx m (genitive pelācis); third declension

  1. Alternative spelling of pilax

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.

Nominative pelāx pelācēs

Accusative pelācem pelācēs

Northern Kurdish[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • — բալախ[1]

Etymology[edit]

From Armenian բալախ (balax), dialectal փալախ (pʿalax).

Noun[edit]

pelax ?[2]

  1. sedge
