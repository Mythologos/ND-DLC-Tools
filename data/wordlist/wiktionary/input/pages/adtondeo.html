Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • attondeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈton.de.oː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɔn̪d̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈton.de.o/, [äd̪ˈt̪ɔn̪d̪eo]

Verb[edit]

adtondeō (present infinitive adtondēre, perfect active adtondī, supine adtōnsum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attondeō
