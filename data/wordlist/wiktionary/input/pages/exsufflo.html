Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsuf.floː/, [ɛkˈs̠ʊfːɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsuf.flo/, [eɡˈzufːlo]

Verb[edit]

exsufflō (present infinitive exsufflāre, perfect active exsufflāvī, supine exsufflātum); first conjugation

  1. to blow at, upon or away
