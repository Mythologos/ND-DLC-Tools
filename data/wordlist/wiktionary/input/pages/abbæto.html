Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aːbˈbae̯.toː/, [äːbˈbäe̯t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈbe.to/, [äbˈbɛːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

ābbætō (present infinitive ābbætere); third conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of ābaetō
