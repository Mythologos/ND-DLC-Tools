Translingual[edit]

Symbol[edit]

TR

  1. (international standards) ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code for Turkey.
    Synonym: TUR (alpha-3)

English[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

TR

  1. (US politics) Initialism of Theodore/Teddy Roosevelt, 26th US president.
  2. Abbreviation of Tripura, a state of India.

Alternative forms[edit]

  • T.R., T. R.

Noun[edit]

TR (plural TRs)

  1. Initialism of technical report; a draft technical report (usage from ISO)
  2. (sports) Initialism of track record.
    Coordinate terms: CR, NR, WR
  3. (biochemistry) Initialism of thioredoxin reductase.
    Synonym: TrxR

Anagrams[edit]

  • R/T, RT, rt

Latin[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

TR

  1. (money) Abbreviation of Augusta Treverorum, Trier

Romanian[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

TR

  1. Abbreviation of Teleorman, a county in Romania.
