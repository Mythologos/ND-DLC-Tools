Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i̯u.u̯eˈneːs.koː/, [i̯uː̯ɛˈneːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ju.veˈnes.ko/, [juveˈnɛsko]

Verb[edit]

juvenēscō (present infinitive juvenēscere, perfect active juvenuī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of iuvenēscō
