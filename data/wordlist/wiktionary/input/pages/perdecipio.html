Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ decipio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.deːˈki.pi.oː/, [pɛrd̪eːˈkɪpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.deˈt͡ʃi.pi.o/, [perd̪eˈt͡ʃiːpio]

Verb[edit]

perdēcipiō (present infinitive perdēcipere, perfect active perdēcēpī, supine perdēceptum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to deceive utterly
