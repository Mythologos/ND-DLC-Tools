Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈnek.toː/, [s̠ʊbˈnɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈnek.to/, [subˈnɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

subnectō (present infinitive subnectere, perfect active subnexuī, supine subnexum); third conjugation

  1. to bind or tie under or beneath
  2. (figuratively, in speaking) to add, subjoin

Conjugation[edit]

  • The third principal part may be subnexī or subnexuī.
