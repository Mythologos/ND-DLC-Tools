Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ suppono.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.supˈpoː.noː/, [präe̯s̠ʊpˈpoːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.supˈpo.no/, [pres̬upˈpɔːno]

Verb[edit]

praesuppōnō (present infinitive praesuppōnere, perfect active praesupposuī, supine praesuppositum); third conjugation

  1. to presuppose
