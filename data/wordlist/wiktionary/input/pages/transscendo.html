Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From trans +‎ scandō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /tranˈsken.doː/, [t̪rä̃ːˈs̠kɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tranˈʃen.do/, [t̪rän̠ʲˈʃɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

trānsscendō (present infinitive trānsscendere, perfect active trānsscendī, supine trānsscēnsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of trānscendō
