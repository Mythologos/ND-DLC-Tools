Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯a.ku.eːˈfa.ki.oː/, [u̯äkueːˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /va.ku.eˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [väkueˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

vacuēfaciō (present infinitive vacuēfacere, perfect active vacuēfēcī, supine vacuēfactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to empty, evacuate
