Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.ɡra.u̯oː/, [ˈɪŋɡräu̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.ɡra.vo/, [ˈiŋɡrävo]

Verb[edit]

ingravō (present infinitive ingravāre, perfect active ingravāvī, supine ingravātum); first conjugation

  1. to weigh down
    Synonyms: gravō, aggravō
  2. to render worse, aggravate
    Synonyms: gravō, aggravō, onerō
  3. to oppress
    Synonyms: gravō, aggravō, premō, opprimō
