English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Manitoba
Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Manitobah (historical, rare)

Etymology[edit]

From Ojibwe ᒪᓂᑐᐸᐦ or Cree ᒪᓂᑐᐘᐳᐤ (manitowapow, “spirit strait”), referring to the Narrows of Lake Manitoba. Compare English manitou, Ojibwe manidoo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˌmæn.ɪˈtəʊ.bə/
  • (Canada, US) IPA(key): /ˌmænɪˈtoʊbə/
  • Hyphenation: Mani‧toba
  • Audio (CA)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Manitoba

  1. A province in Western Canada.

Synonyms[edit]

  • Province of Manitoba
  • MB
  • Man, Man.

Derived terms[edit]

  • Manitoba Escarpment
  • Manitoba flour
  • Manitoba maple (“ash-leaved maple”)
  • Manitoba wolf
  • Manitoban
  • (historical) Republic of Manitoba, Republic of Manitobah

Related terms[edit]

  • Lake Manitoba
