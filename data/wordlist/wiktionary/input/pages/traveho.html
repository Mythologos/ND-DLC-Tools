Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtraː.u̯e.hoː/, [ˈt̪räːu̯e(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtra.ve.o/, [ˈt̪räːveo]

Verb[edit]

trāvehō (present infinitive trāvehere, perfect active trāvē̆xī, supine trāvectum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of trānsvehō
