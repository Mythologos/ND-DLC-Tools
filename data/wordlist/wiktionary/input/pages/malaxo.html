Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ancient Greek μαλάσσω (malássō, “to soften”), from μαλακός (malakós, “soft”).

Verb[edit]

malaxō (present infinitive malaxāre, perfect active malaxāvī, supine malaxātum); first conjugation

  1. to soften, to knead
