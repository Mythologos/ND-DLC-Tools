Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈmiː.ma/, [ˈmiːmä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈmi.ma/, [ˈmiːmä]

Noun[edit]

mīma f (genitive mīmae); first declension

  1. mime actress, mime actor (female)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
