Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtran.su̯e.hoː/, [ˈt̪rä̃ːs̠u̯e(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtran.swe.o/, [ˈt̪ränsweo]

Verb[edit]

trānsvehō (present infinitive trānsvehere, perfect active trānsvē̆xī, supine trānsvectum); third conjugation

  1. to carry, bear, convey across or over
  2. to transport
    Synonyms: asportō, trānsportō, trānsferō, ēvehō, auferō, efferō
  3. to pass by
