Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conpluor

Etymology[edit]

Passive forms of compluit.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkom.plu.or/, [ˈkɔmpɫ̪uɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkom.plu.or/, [ˈkɔmpluor]

Verb[edit]

compluor (present infinitive compluī, perfect active complūtus sum); third conjugation, deponent

  1. (Late Latin) to be rained upon
