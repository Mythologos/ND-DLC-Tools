Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /inˈsu.do/
  • Rhymes: -udo
  • Hyphenation: in‧sù‧do

Verb[edit]

insudo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of insudare

Anagrams[edit]

  • dunosi, sudino

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

in- +‎ sūdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈsuː.doː/, [ĩːˈs̠uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈsu.do/, [inˈsuːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

insūdō (present infinitive insūdāre, perfect active insūdāvī, supine insūdātum); first conjugation

  1. (poetic, post-classical) to sweat in, to sweat on, to sweat at
