Interlingua[edit]

Noun[edit]

aranea (plural araneas)

  1. spider

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Either from Ancient Greek ἀράχνη (arákhnē) or from the same source.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈraː.ne.a/, [äˈräːneä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈra.ne.a/, [äˈräːneä]

Noun[edit]

arānea f (genitive arāneae); first declension

  1. spider
    Synonym: arāneus
  2. spider's web, cobweb
    Synonym: arāneum
  3. (figuratively) threads similar to spiders' webs

Declension[edit]
