Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈkal.ke.oː/, [d̪eːˈkäɫ̪keoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈkal.t͡ʃe.o/, [d̪eˈkäl̠ʲt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

dēcalceō (present infinitive dēcalceāre, perfect active dēcalceāvī, supine dēcalceātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēcalciō
