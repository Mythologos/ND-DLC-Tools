Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • pejerō
  • periūrō
  • perjūrō

Etymology[edit]

(This etymology is missing or incomplete. Please add to it, or discuss it at the Etymology scriptorium.)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpei̯.i̯e.roː/, [ˈpɛi̯ːɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpe.je.ro/, [ˈpɛːjero]

Verb[edit]

peierō (present infinitive peierāre, perfect active peierāvī, supine peierātum); first conjugation

  1. to swear a false oath
  2. to perjure oneself
