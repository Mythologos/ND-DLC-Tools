English[edit]

Chemical element
Po
Previous: bismuth (Bi)
Next: astatine (At)

Etymology[edit]

From New Latin polonium, from Medieval Latin Polonia (“Poland”), named after Marie Curie's homeland.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /pəˈləʊni.əm/
  • (US) enPR: pəlōʹnēəm, IPA(key): /pəˈloʊni.əm/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)
  • Rhymes: -əʊniəm

Noun[edit]

polonium (usually uncountable, plural poloniums)

  1. A rare, highly radioactive chemical element (symbol Po) with atomic number 84.

Hypernyms[edit]

  • chalcogen

Derived terms[edit]

  • eka-polonium
  • polane
  • polonide
  • polonium-210
  • polonium hydride

Related terms[edit]

  • Poland
