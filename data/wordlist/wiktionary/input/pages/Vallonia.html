Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From vallis (“a valley”, “a vale”) +‎ -ōnia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯alˈloː.ni.a/, [u̯älˈlʲoːniä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /valˈlo.ni.a/, [välˈlɔːniä]

Proper noun[edit]

Vallōnia f sg (genitive Vallōniae); first declension

  1. (Roman mythology) The goddess of valleys

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.
