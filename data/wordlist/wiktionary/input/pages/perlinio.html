Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈli.ni.oː/, [pɛrˈlʲɪnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈli.ni.o/, [perˈliːnio]

Verb[edit]

perliniō (present infinitive perlinīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of perlinō
