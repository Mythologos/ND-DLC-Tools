Esperanto[edit]

Noun[edit]

reago (accusative singular reagon, plural reagoj, accusative plural reagojn)

  1. reaction

Related terms[edit]

  • reagi

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- (“back, again”) +‎ agō (“do, make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.a.ɡoː/, [ˈreäɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.a.ɡo/, [ˈrɛːäɡo]

Verb[edit]

reagō (present infinitive reagere, perfect active reēgī, supine reāctum); third conjugation

  1. (Late Latin, Medieval Latin) to act upon again, to do again
  2. (New Latin, sciences) to react
