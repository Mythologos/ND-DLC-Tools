Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈproː.se.koː/, [ˈproːs̠ɛkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpro.se.ko/, [ˈprɔːs̬eko]

Verb[edit]

prōsecō (present infinitive prōsecāre, perfect active prōsecuī, supine prōsectum); first conjugation

  1. to cut off or away
  2. to sacrifice
