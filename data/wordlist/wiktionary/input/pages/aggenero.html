Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adgenerō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ generō (“produce, create, beget”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aɡˈɡe.ne.roː/, [äɡˈɡɛnɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈd͡ʒe.ne.ro/, [ädˈd͡ʒɛːnero]

Verb[edit]

aggenerō (present infinitive aggenerāre, perfect active aggenerāvī, supine aggenerātum); first conjugation

  1. to produce, create or beget in addition to
