Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ plicō (“fold”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.pli.koː/, [ˈd̪eːplʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.pli.ko/, [ˈd̪ɛːpliko]

Verb[edit]

dēplicō (present infinitive dēplicāre, perfect active dēplicāvī, supine dēplicātum); first conjugation

  1. to unfold
  2. to explain
