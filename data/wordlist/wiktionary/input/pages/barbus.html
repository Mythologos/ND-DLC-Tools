French[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /baʁ.by/
  • Rhymes: -y

Adjective[edit]

barbus

  1. masculine plural of barbu

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From barba (“beard”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbar.bus/, [ˈbärbʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈbar.bus/, [ˈbärbus]

Noun[edit]

barbus m (genitive barbī); second declension

  1. barbel

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun.

Case Singular Plural
Nominative barbus barbī
Genitive barbī barbōrum
Dative barbō barbīs
Accusative barbum barbōs
Ablative barbō barbīs
Vocative barbe barbī
