Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • dogana, dovana

Etymology[edit]

From Arabic دِيوَان (dīwān, “administrative office”), from Persian دیوان (divân, “customs, divan”).

Noun[edit]

doana f (genitive doanae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) A customs duty.

Declension[edit]
