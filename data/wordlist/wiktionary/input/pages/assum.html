Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈas.sum/, [ˈäs̠ːʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈas.sum/, [ˈäsːum]

Verb[edit]

assum (present infinitive adesse, perfect active affuī, future participle affutūrus); irregular conjugation, irregular, no passive, no supine stem except in the future active participle

  1. Alternative form of adsum
