Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Τύρος (Túros).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈtyros/, [ˈt̪yro̞s̠]
  • Rhymes: -yros
  • Syllabification(key): Ty‧ros

Proper noun[edit]

Tyros

  1. Tyre (an ancient city-state in Phoenicia)
  2. Tyre (a port city, the capital of Tyre district, Lebanon)

Latin[edit]
