  • IPA(key): /ˈkrɛ.pi.to/
  • Rhymes: -ɛpito
  • Hyphenation: crè‧pi‧to

Noun[edit]

crepito m (plural crepiti)

  1. crack

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From crepō +‎ -tō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkre.pi.toː/, [ˈkrɛpɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkre.pi.to/, [ˈkrɛːpit̪o]

Verb[edit]

crepitō (present infinitive crepitāre, perfect active crepitāvī, supine crepitātum); first conjugation

  1. to rattle, clatter, crackle, etc.
  2. to chatter, mumble
