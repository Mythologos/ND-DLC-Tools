Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From verbum (“word”) +‎ gerō (“carry, bear”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯erˈbi.ɡe.roː/, [u̯ɛrˈbɪɡɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /verˈbi.d͡ʒe.ro/, [verˈbiːd͡ʒero]

Verb[edit]

verbigerō (present infinitive verbigerāre, perfect active verbigerāvī, supine verbigerātum); first conjugation

  1. to talk, chat; dispute
