Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From rīvālis +‎ -icius.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /riː.u̯aːˈli.ki.us/, [riːu̯äːˈlʲɪkiʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ri.vaˈli.t͡ʃi.us/, [riväˈliːt͡ʃius]

Adjective[edit]

rīvālicius (feminine rīvālicia, neuter rīvālicium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Of or relating to those who make use of the same brook.
