Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /zinˈzi.lu.loː/, [d̪͡z̪ɪn̪ˈd̪͡z̪ɪɫ̪ʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /d͡zinˈd͡zi.lu.lo/, [d̪͡z̪inˈd̪͡z̪iːlulo]

Verb[edit]

zinzilulō (present infinitive zinzilulāre, perfect active zinzilulāvī, supine zinzilulātum); first conjugation

  1. to chirp
