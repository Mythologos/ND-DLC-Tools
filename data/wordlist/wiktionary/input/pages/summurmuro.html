Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • submurmurō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sumˈmur.mu.roː/, [s̠ʊmˈmʊrmʊroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sumˈmur.mu.ro/, [sumˈmurmuro]

Verb[edit]

summurmurō (present infinitive summurmurāre, perfect active summurmurāvī, supine summurmurātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of submurmurō
