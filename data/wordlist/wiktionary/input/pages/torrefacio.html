Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From torreō +‎ faciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /tor.reˈfa.ki.oː/, [t̪ɔrːɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tor.reˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [t̪orːeˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

torrefaciō (present infinitive torrefacere, perfect active torrefēcī, supine torrefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to parch
