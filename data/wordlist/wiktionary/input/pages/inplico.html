Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.pli.koː/, [ˈɪmplʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.pli.ko/, [ˈinpliko]

Verb[edit]

inplicō (present infinitive inplicāre, perfect active inplicāvī, supine inplicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of implicō
