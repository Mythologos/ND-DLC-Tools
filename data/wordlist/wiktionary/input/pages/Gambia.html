English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
The Gambia
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

From the name of the Gambia River. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɡæm.bi.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Gambia or the Gambia

  1. A river in West Africa.
  3. A country in West Africa. Official name: Republic of The Gambia.

Usage notes[edit]

The country is referred to with the definite article the. The capitalization of the article when it occurs not at the beginning of a sentence varies.
