Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ aedifico.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.pe.rae̯ˈdi.fi.koː/, [s̠ʊpɛräe̯ˈd̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.pe.reˈdi.fi.ko/, [supereˈd̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

superaedificō (present infinitive superaedificāre, perfect active superaedificāvī, supine superaedificātum); first conjugation

  1. to build over or upon
