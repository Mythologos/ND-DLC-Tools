Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • obtineō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /opˈti.ne.oː/, [ɔpˈt̪ɪneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /opˈti.ne.o/, [opˈt̪iːneo]

Verb[edit]

optineō (present infinitive optinēre, perfect active optinuī, supine optentum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative spelling of obtineō
