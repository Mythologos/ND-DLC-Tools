Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtemptō, adtentō, attentō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /atˈtemp.toː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɛmpt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /atˈtemp.to/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɛmpt̪o]

Verb[edit]

attemptō (present infinitive attemptāre, perfect active attemptāvī, supine attemptātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attentō
