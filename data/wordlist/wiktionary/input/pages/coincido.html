Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ incidō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈin.ki.doː/, [koˈɪŋkɪd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈin.t͡ʃi.do/, [koˈin̠ʲt͡ʃid̪o]

Verb[edit]

coincidō (present infinitive coincidere, perfect active coincidī, supine coincāsum); third conjugation

  1. to coincide
    Synonym: concurro
