Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • impertiō
  • inpertiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /imˈpar.ti.oː/, [ɪmˈpärt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /imˈpar.t͡si.o/, [imˈpärt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

impartiō (present infinitive impartīre, perfect active impartīvī or impartiī, supine impartītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of impertiō
