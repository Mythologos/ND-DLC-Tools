Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central, Balearic) [kunˈsum]
  • IPA(key): (Valencian) [konˈsum]

Etymology 1[edit]

Deverbal from consumir.

Noun[edit]

consum m (plural consums)

  1. consumption (the act of eating, drinking, or using)
  2. consumption (the amount consumed)
Derived terms[edit]
  • consumisme
  • consumista

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

consum

  1. inflection of consumir:
    1. third-person singular present indicative
    2. second-person singular imperative

Latin[edit]

Verb[edit]

consum

  1. see coest

Usage notes[edit]

This is the headword given in some dictionaries. However, this form does not occur, as the verb is impersonal.
