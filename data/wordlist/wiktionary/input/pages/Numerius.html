Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From numerus (“number”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /nuˈme.ri.us/, [nʊˈmɛriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /nuˈme.ri.us/, [nuˈmɛːrius]

Adjective[edit]

numerius (feminine numeria, neuter numerium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Of or belonging to a number; numeral.
