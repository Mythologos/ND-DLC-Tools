Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.miˈni.ku.loː/, [äd̪mɪˈnɪkʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.miˈni.ku.lo/, [äd̪miˈniːkulo]

Etymology 1[edit]

From adminiculum (“prop, support; assistance”) +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

adminiculō (present infinitive adminiculāre, perfect active adminiculāvī, supine adminiculātum); first conjugation

  1. to prop up, support
