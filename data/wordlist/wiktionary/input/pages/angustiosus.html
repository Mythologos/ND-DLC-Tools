Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

angustia +‎ -ōsus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /an.ɡusˈti.o.sus/, [äŋɡʊs̠ˈt̪iɔs̠ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /an.ɡusˈti.o.sus/, [äŋɡusˈt̪iːos̬us]

Adjective[edit]

angustiōsus (feminine angustiōsa, neuter angustiōsum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (Late Latin, Medieval Latin) scared, anxious
