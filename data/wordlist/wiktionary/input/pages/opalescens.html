Latin[edit]

Adjective[edit]

opalēscēns (genitive opalēscentis); third-declension one-termination adjective

  1. (New Latin) opalescent

Usage notes[edit]

  • Used almost exclusively as a taxonomic epithet and thus not normally in inflected forms other than the nominative singular.
