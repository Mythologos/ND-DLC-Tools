Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adsternō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ sternō (“spread”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈter.noː/, [äs̠ˈt̪ɛrnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈter.no/, [äsˈt̪ɛrno]

Verb[edit]

asternō (present infinitive asternere, perfect active astrāvī, supine astrātum); third conjugation

  1. to prostrate oneself, lie prone
