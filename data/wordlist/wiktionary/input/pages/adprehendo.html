Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • apprehendō
  • apprendō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.preˈhen.doː/, [äpːreˈ(ɦ)ɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.preˈen.do/, [äd̪preˈɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

adprehendō (present infinitive adprehendere, perfect active adprehendī, supine adprehēnsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of apprehendō
