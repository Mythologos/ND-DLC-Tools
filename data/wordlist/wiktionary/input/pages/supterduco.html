Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sup.terˈduː.koː/, [s̠ʊpt̪ɛrˈd̪uːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sup.terˈdu.ko/, [supt̪erˈd̪uːko]

Verb[edit]

supterdūcō (present infinitive supterdūcere, perfect active supterdūxī, supine supterductum); third conjugation, irregular short imperative

  1. Alternative form of subterdūcō
