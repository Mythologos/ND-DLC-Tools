Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • vīnāceus, vīnāceum

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯iːˈnaː.ke.a/, [u̯iːˈnäːkeä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /viˈna.t͡ʃe.a/, [viˈnäːt͡ʃeä]

Noun[edit]

vīnācea f (genitive vīnāceae); first declension

  1. skin or husk of a grape

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
