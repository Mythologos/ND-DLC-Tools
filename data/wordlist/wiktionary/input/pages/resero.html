Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

resero

  1. third-person plural past historic of rendere

Anagrams[edit]

  • ersero

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.se.roː/, [ˈrɛs̠ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.se.ro/, [ˈrɛːs̬ero]
  • Hyphenation: re‧se‧rō

Verb[edit]

reserō (present infinitive reserāre, perfect active reserāvī, supine reserātum); first conjugation

  1. to unlock, open
  2. to disclose, reveal

Spanish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From res +‎ -ero.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /reˈseɾo/ [reˈse.ɾo]
  • Rhymes: -eɾo
  • Syllabification: re‧se‧ro

Noun[edit]

resero m (plural reseros, feminine resera, feminine plural reseras)

  1. cattle farmer
