Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.fre.toː/, [ˈt̪rä̃ːfːrɛt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.fre.to/, [ˈt̪ränsfret̪o]

Verb[edit]

trānsfretō (present infinitive trānsfretāre, perfect active trānsfretāvī, supine trānsfretātum); first conjugation

  1. to cross a strait
  2. to pass over the sea
  3. to ferry (across)
