Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From inspicere.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈspek.toː/, [ĩːˈs̠pɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈspek.to/, [inˈspɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

īnspectō (present infinitive īnspectāre, perfect active īnspectāvī, supine īnspectātum); first conjugation

  1. to observe, look at
    Synonyms: videō, intueor, īnspiciō, suspiciō, tueor, cōnspicor, vīsō, spectō, speciō, invīsō
