Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • commoneō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈmo.ne.oː/, [kɔmˈmɔneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈmo.ne.o/, [konˈmɔːneo]

Verb[edit]

conmoneō (present infinitive conmonēre, perfect active conmonuī, supine conmonitum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of commoneō
