Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Swedish holme.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈholmi/, [ˈho̞lmi]
  • Rhymes: -olmi
  • Syllabification(key): Hol‧mi

Proper noun[edit]

Holmi

  1. a Finnish surname

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Holmoe

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Ὅλμοι (Hólmoi).

Proper noun[edit]

Holmī m pl (genitive Holmōrum); second declension

  1. A coastal town in Cilicia

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun, with locative, plural only.
