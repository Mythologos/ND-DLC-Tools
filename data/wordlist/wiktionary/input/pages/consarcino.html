Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈsar.ki.noː/, [kõːˈs̠ärkɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈsar.t͡ʃi.no/, [konˈsärt͡ʃino]

Verb[edit]

cōnsarcinō (present infinitive cōnsarcināre, perfect active cōnsarcināvī, supine cōnsarcinātum); first conjugation

  1. to sew, stitch or patch together
