Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ neo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈper.ne.oː/, [ˈpɛrneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈper.ne.o/, [ˈpɛrneo]

Verb[edit]

perneō (present infinitive pernēre, perfect active pernēvī, supine pernētum); second conjugation

  1. to spin out (or to the end)
