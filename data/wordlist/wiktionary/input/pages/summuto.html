Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • submūtō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sumˈmuː.toː/, [s̠ʊmˈmuːt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sumˈmu.to/, [sumˈmuːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

summūtō (present infinitive summūtāre, perfect active summūtāvī, supine summūtātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of submūtō (“to exchange, interchange”)
