Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

ditiō f (genitive ditiōnis); third declension

  1. Alternative form of dicio

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.

Welsh[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Middle English enditen, from Old French enditer.

Verb[edit]

ditio (first-person singular present ditiaf)

  1. (law) to indict
