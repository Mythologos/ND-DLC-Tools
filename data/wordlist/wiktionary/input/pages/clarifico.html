Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From clārus (“clear”) +‎ -ficō (“make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /klaːˈri.fi.koː/, [kɫ̪äːˈrɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /klaˈri.fi.ko/, [kläˈriːfiko]

Verb[edit]

clārificō (present infinitive clārificāre, perfect active clārificāvī, supine clārificātum); first conjugation

  1. to make illustrious or famous
