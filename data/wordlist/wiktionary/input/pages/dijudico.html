Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈi̯uː.di.koː/, [d̪iːˈi̯uːd̪ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈju.di.ko/, [d̪iˈjuːd̪iko]

Verb[edit]

dījūdicō (present infinitive dījūdicāre, perfect active dījūdicāvī, supine dījūdicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dīiūdicō
