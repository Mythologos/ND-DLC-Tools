Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ek.speˈtis.soː/, [ɛks̠pɛˈt̪ɪs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.speˈtis.so/, [ekspeˈt̪isːo]

Verb[edit]

expetissō (present infinitive expetissere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of expetessō
