Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈlan.t͡ʃo/
  • Rhymes: -antʃo
  • Hyphenation: làn‧cio

Etymology 1[edit]

From lanciare.

Noun[edit]

lancio m (plural lanci)

  1. throw, drop, shot
Derived terms[edit]
Derived terms
  • lanciatore
  • lancio del disco
  • lancio del giavellotto
  • lancio del martello
  • lancio del peso

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

lancio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of lanciare

Anagrams[edit]

  • Alcino, Laconi, Nicola, alcino, calino, clonai, colina

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈlan.ki.oː/, [ˈɫ̪äŋkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈlan.t͡ʃi.o/, [ˈlän̠ʲt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

lanciō (present infinitive lanciāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. medieval spelling of lanceō
