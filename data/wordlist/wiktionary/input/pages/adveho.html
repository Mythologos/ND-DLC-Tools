Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • arvehō (old form)

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ vehō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.u̯e.hoː/, [ˈäd̪u̯e(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ve.o/, [ˈäd̪veo]

Verb[edit]

advehō (present infinitive advehere, perfect active advē̆xī, supine advectum); third conjugation

  1. to bring to
  2. to import
