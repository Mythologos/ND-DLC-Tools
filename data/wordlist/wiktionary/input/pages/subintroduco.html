Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ intrōdūcō

Verb[edit]

subintrōdūcō (present infinitive subintrōdūcere, perfect active subintrōdūxī, supine subintrōductum); third conjugation, irregular short imperative

  1. to introduce in secret
