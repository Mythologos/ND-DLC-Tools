Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ glūtiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈɡluː.ti.oː/, [d̪eːˈɡɫ̪uːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈɡlut.t͡si.o/, [d̪eˈɡlut̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

dēglūtiō (present infinitive dēglūtīre, perfect active dēglūtīvī or dēglūtiī, supine dēglūtītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēgluttiō
