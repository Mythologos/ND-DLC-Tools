Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /stiˈpɛn.djo/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɛndjo
  • Hyphenation: sti‧pèn‧dio

Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Latin stīpendium (“military pay, stipend”).

Noun[edit]

stipendio m (plural stipendi)

  1. salary, pay, earnings, stipend
    Synonyms: paga, salario
Related terms[edit]
  • stipendiare
  • stipendiato

Etymology 2[edit]

Verb[edit]

stipendio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of stipendiare

Anagrams[edit]

  • depistino, diponesti

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /stiːˈpen.di.oː/, [s̠t̪iːˈpɛn̪d̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /stiˈpen.di.o/, [st̪iˈpɛn̪d̪io]

Etymology 1[edit]

From stīpendium +‎ -ō. Compare stīpendior.

Verb[edit]

stīpendiō (present infinitive stīpendiāre, perfect active stīpendiāvī); first conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (Renaissance Latin) to hire as a mercenary [1347]
