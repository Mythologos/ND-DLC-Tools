Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • accurrō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈkur.roː/, [äkˈkʊrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈkur.ro/, [äd̪ˈkurːo]

Verb[edit]

adcurrō (present infinitive adcurrere, perfect active adcurrī or adcucurrī, supine adcursum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of accurrō
