Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ābujō
  • abbuiō
  • abbujō

Etymology[edit]

From Old Italian buio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aːˈbuː.i̯oː/, [äːˈbuːi̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈbu.jo/, [äˈbuːjo]

Verb[edit]

ābuiō (present infinitive ābuiāre, perfect active ābuiāvī, supine ābuiātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) to darken
