Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

connumero

  1. first-person singular present indicative of connumerare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ numerō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈnu.me.roː/, [kɔnˈnʊmɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈnu.me.ro/, [konˈnuːmero]

Verb[edit]

connumerō (present infinitive connumerāre, perfect active connumerāvī, supine connumerātum); first conjugation

  1. to number or count with others
  2. to reckon among
