Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- (“before”) +‎ fugiō (“flee”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈfu.ɡi.oː/, [präe̯ˈfʊɡioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈfu.d͡ʒi.o/, [preˈfuːd͡ʒio]

Verb[edit]

praefugiō (present infinitive praefugere, perfect active praefūgī); third conjugation iō-variant, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to flee before
