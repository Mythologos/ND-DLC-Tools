English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin dīvīsiō (“division”). Doublet of division.

Noun[edit]

divisio (plural divisiones)

  1. (biology, taxonomy) A category in the classification of organisms, ranking below regnum and above classis.
    Synonyms: division, phylum

  • Syllabification(key): di‧vi‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

divisio

  1. (taxonomy, rare) Synonym of pääjakso (“phylum”).

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dīvidō (“divide”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈu̯iː.si.oː/, [d̪iːˈu̯iːs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈvi.si.o/, [d̪iˈviːs̬io]

Noun[edit]

dīvīsiō f (genitive dīvīsiōnis); third declension

  1. division
  2. distribution
