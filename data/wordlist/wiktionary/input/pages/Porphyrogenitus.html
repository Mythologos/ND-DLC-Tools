English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˌpɔːfɪɹəˈdʒɛnɪtəs/

Noun[edit]

porphyrogenitus (plural porphyrogeniti)

  1. Alternative form of porphyrogenite

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Porphyrogenitus

Etymology[edit]

Partial calque of Ancient Greek πορφυρογέννητος (porphurogénnētos).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /por.pʰy.roˈɡe.ni.tus/, [pɔrpʰʏrɔˈɡɛnɪt̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /por.fi.roˈd͡ʒe.ni.tus/, [porfiroˈd͡ʒɛːnit̪us]

Adjective[edit]

porphyrogenitus (feminine porphyrogenita, neuter porphyrogenitum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (Medieval Latin) born to the purple, porphyrogenite
