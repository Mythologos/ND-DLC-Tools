Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ tribuo

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈtri.bu.oː/, [rɛˈt̪rɪbuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈtri.bu.o/, [reˈt̪riːbuo]

Verb[edit]

retribuō (present infinitive retribuere, perfect active retribuī, supine retribūtum); third conjugation

  1. to give back, return or repay (money)
  2. to recompense
