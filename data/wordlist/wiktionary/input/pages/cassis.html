English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Noun[edit]

cassis (usually uncountable, plural cassises)

  1. The blackcurrant plant, Ribes nigrum; the flavor of its berries
  2. A liqueur made from these berries, especially crÃ¨me de cassis.
