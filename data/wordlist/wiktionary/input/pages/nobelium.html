English[edit]

Chemical element
No
Previous: mendelevium (Md)
Next: lawrencium (Lr)

Etymology[edit]

Nobel +‎ -ium, after Alfred Nobel.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: nōbĕl'ēəm, IPA(key): /noʊˈbɛliəm/; enPR: nōbēl'ēəm, IPA(key): /noʊˈbiːliəm/
  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

nobelium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
nobelium
Wikipedia
  1. a transuranic chemical element (symbol No) with an atomic number of 102.

Synonyms[edit]

  • unnilbium

Related terms[edit]
