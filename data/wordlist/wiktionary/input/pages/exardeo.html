Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ ārdeō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsar.de.oː/, [ɛkˈs̠ärd̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsar.de.o/, [eɡˈzärd̪eo]

Verb[edit]

exardeō (present infinitive exardēre, perfect active exarsī, supine exarsum); second conjugation

  1. to burn
  2. to be inflamed
