Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From fidicen +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /fiˈdi.ki.noː/, [fɪˈd̪ɪkɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /fiˈdi.t͡ʃi.no/, [fiˈd̪iːt͡ʃino]

Verb[edit]

fidicinō (present infinitive fidicināre, perfect active fidicināvī, supine fidicinātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to play the lute or lyre
