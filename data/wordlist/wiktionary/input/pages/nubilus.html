Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From nūbēs (“cloud”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈnuː.bi.lus/, [ˈnuːbɪɫ̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈnu.bi.lus/, [ˈnuːbilus]

Adjective[edit]

nūbilus (feminine nūbila, neuter nūbilum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. cloudy, overcast; cloud-bringing
  2. dark, gloomy, dim
  3. (figuratively) troubled, confused, beclouded
  4. (figuratively) sad, gloomy, melancholy
