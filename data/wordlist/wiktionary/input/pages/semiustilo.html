Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /seː.miˈus.ti.loː/, [s̠eːmiˈʊs̠t̪ɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /se.miˈus.ti.lo/, [semiˈust̪ilo]

Verb[edit]

sēmiustilō (present infinitive sēmiustilāre, perfect active sēmiustilāvī, supine sēmiustilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of sēmustulō
