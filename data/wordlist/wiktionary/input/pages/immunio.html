Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • inmūniō

Verb[edit]

immūniō (present infinitive immūnīre, perfect active immūnīvī); fourth conjugation, no supine stem

  1. to fortify
    Synonyms: firmō, cōnfirmō, mūniō, fortificō
