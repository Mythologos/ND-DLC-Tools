Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈmoe̯.ni.oː/, [kɔmˈmoe̯nioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈme.ni.o/, [komˈmɛːnio]

Verb[edit]

commoeniō (present infinitive commoenīre, perfect active commoenīvī, supine commoenītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of commūniō
