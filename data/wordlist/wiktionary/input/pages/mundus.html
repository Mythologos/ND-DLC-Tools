Adjective[edit]

mundus (feminine munda, neuter mundum, comparative mundior, superlative mundissimus, adverb munditer); first/second-declension adjective

  1. clean, pure; neat
  2. nice, fine, elegant, sophisticated
  3. decorated, adorned
  4. pure (trait)
