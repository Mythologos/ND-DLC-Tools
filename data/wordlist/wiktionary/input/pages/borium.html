Dutch[edit]

Dutch Wikipedia has an article on:
borium
Wikipedia nl

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English borium. Coined by Humphry Davy.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈboːriʏm/
  • Audio(file)
  • Hyphenation: bo‧ri‧um
  • Rhymes: -oːriʏm
  • Homophone: bohrium

Noun[edit]

borium n (uncountable)

  1. The metalloid element boron

Synonyms[edit]

  • boor n

Related terms[edit]

  • borax
