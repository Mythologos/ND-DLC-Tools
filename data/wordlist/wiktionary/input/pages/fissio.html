Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English fission), ultimately from Latin fissiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈfisːio/, [ˈfis̠ːio̞]
  • Rhymes: -isːio
  • Syllabification(key): fis‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

fissio

  1. (physics) fission

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From findō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfis.si.oː/, [ˈfɪs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfis.si.o/, [ˈfisːio]

Noun[edit]

fissiō f (genitive fissiōnis); third declension

  1. The act of breaking up or dividing.

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
