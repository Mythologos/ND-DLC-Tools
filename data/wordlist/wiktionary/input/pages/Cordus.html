Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkor.dus/, [ˈkɔrd̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkor.dus/, [ˈkɔrd̪us]

Adjective[edit]

cordus (feminine corda, neuter cordum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative form of chordus

Declension[edit]
