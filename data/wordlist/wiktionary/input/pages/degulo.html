Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ gula (“throat, gullet”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.ɡu.loː/, [ˈd̪eːɡʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.ɡu.lo/, [ˈd̪ɛːɡulo]

Verb[edit]

dēgulō (present infinitive dēgulāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (archaic, rare) to devour, consume
