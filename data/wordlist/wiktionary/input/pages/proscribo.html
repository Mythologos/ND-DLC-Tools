Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prō- +‎ scrībō (“write”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːsˈkriː.boː/, [proːs̠ˈkriːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /prosˈkri.bo/, [prosˈkriːbo]

Verb[edit]

prōscrībō (present infinitive prōscrībere, perfect active prōscrīpsī, supine prōscrīptum); third conjugation

  1. to advertise, proclaim
  2. to publish
  3. to proscribe, ban
  4. to outlaw, banish
