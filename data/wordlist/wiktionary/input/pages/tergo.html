Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈtɛr.ɡo/
  • Rhymes: -ɛrɡo
  • Hyphenation: tèr‧go

Etymology 1[edit]

From Latin tergum.

Noun[edit]

tergo m (plural terghi)

  1. back
  2. reverse (coin or medal)

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the corresponding lemma form.

Verb[edit]

tergo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of tergere

Anagrams[edit]

  • greto
