Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • eja
  • heia

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈei̯.a/, [ˈɛi̯ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈei̯.a/, [ˈɛi̯ä]

Interjection[edit]

eia

  1. aha! come now! come on! (expressing delight, playful remonstrance, encouragement)
