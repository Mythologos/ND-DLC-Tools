Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • abbuiō
  • abbujō

Etymology[edit]

From Old Italian buio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aːˈbuː.i̯oː/, [äːˈbuːi̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈbu.jo/, [äˈbuːjo]

Verb[edit]

ābujō (present infinitive ābujāre, perfect active ābujāvī, supine ābujātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of ābuiō
