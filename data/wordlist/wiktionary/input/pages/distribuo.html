Esperanto[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From distribui +‎ -o.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [distriˈbuo]
  • Audio:
    (file)
  • Rhymes: -uo
  • Hyphenation: dis‧tri‧bu‧o

Noun[edit]

distribuo (accusative singular distribuon, plural distribuoj, accusative plural distribuojn)

  1. distribution
    Synonyms: disvastiĝo, disdono

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ tribuō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈtri.bu.oː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈt̪rɪbuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈtri.bu.o/, [d̪isˈt̪riːbuo]

Verb[edit]

distribuō (present infinitive distribuere, perfect active distribuī, supine distribūtum); third conjugation

  1. to divide or distribute
    Synonyms: partiō, tribuō, dispēnsō, cōnsociō, participō, discrībō, dīvidō, largior, compartior
  2. to assign
    Synonyms: dēlēgō, dēsignō, assignō, mandō, dēmandō, tribuō, īnstituō, impertiō, elēgō, appōnō, prōdō, cōnsociō, ōrdinō, attribuō, discrībō, addīcō
