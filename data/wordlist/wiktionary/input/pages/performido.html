Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.forˈmiː.doː/, [pɛrfɔrˈmiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.forˈmi.do/, [perforˈmiːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

performīdō (present infinitive performīdāre, perfect active performīdāvī, supine performīdātum); first conjugation

  1. to dread greatly, fear greatly
