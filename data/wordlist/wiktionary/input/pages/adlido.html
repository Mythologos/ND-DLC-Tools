Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈliː.doː/, [älˈlʲiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈli.do/, [äd̪ˈliːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

adlīdō (present infinitive adlīdere, perfect active adlīsī, supine adlīsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of allīdō
