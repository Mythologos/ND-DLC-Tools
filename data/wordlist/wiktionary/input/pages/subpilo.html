Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈpiː.loː/, [s̠ʊpˈpiːɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈpi.lo/, [subˈpiːlo]

Verb[edit]

subpīlō (present infinitive subpīlāre, perfect active subpīlāvī, supine subpīlātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative spelling of suppīlō
