Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ mingō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈmin.ɡoː/, [kɔmˈmɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈmin.ɡo/, [komˈmiŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

commingō (present infinitive commingere, perfect active comminxī, supine commīnctum); third conjugation

  1. to pollute, defile
