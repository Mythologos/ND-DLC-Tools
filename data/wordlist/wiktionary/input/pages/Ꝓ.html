Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Ꝓ

Letter[edit]

ꝓ (lower case, upper case Ꝓ)

  1. (Medieval Latin) Scribal abbreviation of pro. or por, usually in initial position.

Preposition[edit]

ꝓ

  1. (Medieval Latin, siglum) Scribal abbreviation of pro.
