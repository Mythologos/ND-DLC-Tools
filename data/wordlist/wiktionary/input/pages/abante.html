Tagalog[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Spanish avante (“ahead”), from Catalan avant, from Late Latin ab ante.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Standard Tagalog) IPA(key): /ʔaˈbante/, [ʔɐˈban.tɛ]
  • Audio:(file)
  • Rhymes: -ante
  • Syllabification: a‧ban‧te

Noun[edit]

abante (Baybayin spelling ᜀᜊᜈ᜔ᜆᜒ)

  1. movement forward; advance
  2. advantage in scoring; lead (over an opponent)
  3. progress; development; advance
  4. sufficiency (as of stock or supply)

Derived terms[edit]

  • abentihan
  • iabante

  • paabantihin
  • paabantihin

Related terms[edit]

  • abanse
 

Interjection[edit]

abante (Baybayin spelling ᜀᜊᜈ᜔ᜆᜒ)

  1. forward!; proceed!; go ahead!
    Synonyms: lakad, sige, tuloy
