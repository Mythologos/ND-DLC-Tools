Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ agitō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈa.ɡi.toː/, [koˈäɡɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈa.d͡ʒi.to/, [koˈäːd͡ʒit̪o]

Verb[edit]

coagitō (present infinitive coagitāre, perfect active coagitāvī, supine coagitātum); first conjugation

  1. to shake together
