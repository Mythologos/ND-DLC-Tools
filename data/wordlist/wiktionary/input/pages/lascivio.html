Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From lascīvus (“wanton, playful, frisky”) +‎ -iō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /lasˈkiː.u̯i.oː/, [ɫ̪äs̠ˈkiːu̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /laʃˈʃi.vi.o/, [läʃˈʃiːvio]

Verb[edit]

lascīviō (present infinitive lascīvīre, perfect active lascīviī, supine lascīvītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to frolic, sport, be frisky
