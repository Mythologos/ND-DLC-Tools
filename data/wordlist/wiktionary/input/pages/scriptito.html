Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From scrībō (“write”) +‎ -titō (frequentative suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈskriːp.ti.toː/, [ˈs̠kriːpt̪ɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈskrip.ti.to/, [ˈskript̪it̪o]

Verb[edit]

scrīptitō (present infinitive scrīptitāre, perfect active scrīptitāvī, supine scrīptitātum); first conjugation

  1. to write (often)
