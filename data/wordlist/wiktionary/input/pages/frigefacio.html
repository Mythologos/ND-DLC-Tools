Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From frīgeō (“I am cold”) +‎ faciō (“I make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /friː.ɡeˈfa.ki.oː/, [friːɡɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /fri.d͡ʒeˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [frid͡ʒeˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

frīgefaciō (present infinitive frīgefacere, perfect active frīgefēcī, supine frīgefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to make cold, to cool
