Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

transformo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of transformar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From trāns- +‎ fōrmō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈfoːr.moː/, [t̪rä̃ːfˈfoːrmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈfor.mo/, [t̪ränsˈfɔrmo]

Verb[edit]

trānsfōrmō (present infinitive trānsfōrmāre, perfect active trānsfōrmāvī, supine trānsfōrmātum); first conjugation

  1. to transform (change in shape)
    Synonym: vertō
