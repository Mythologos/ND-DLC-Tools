Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ runcō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈrun.koː/, [eːˈrʊŋkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈrun.ko/, [eˈruŋko]

Verb[edit]

ēruncō (present infinitive ēruncāre, perfect active ēruncāvī, supine ēruncātum); first conjugation

  1. to weed out
