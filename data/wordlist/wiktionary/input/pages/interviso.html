Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ vīsō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈu̯iː.soː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈu̯iːs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈvi.so/, [in̪t̪erˈviːs̬o]

Verb[edit]

intervīsō (present infinitive intervīsere, perfect active intervīsī, supine intervīsum); third conjugation

  1. to look at
  2. to visit
