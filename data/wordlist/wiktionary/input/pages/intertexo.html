Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

inter- +‎ texō (“to weave, plait”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈtek.soː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈt̪ɛks̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈtek.so/, [in̪t̪erˈt̪ɛkso]

Verb[edit]

intertexō (present infinitive intertexere, perfect active intertexuī, supine intertextum); third conjugation

  1. (transitive) to intertwine, interweave, intersperse
