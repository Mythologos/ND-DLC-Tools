Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /laːˈter.na/, [ɫ̪äːˈt̪ɛrnä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /laˈter.na/, [läˈt̪ɛrnä]

Noun[edit]

lāterna f (genitive lāternae); first declension

  1. Alternative form of lanterna (“lantern, lamp; torch”)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Turkish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Italian lanterna.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [laˈteɾnɑ]
  • Hyphenation: la‧ter‧na

Noun[edit]

laterna (definite accusative laternayı, plural laternalar)

  1. A kind of organ in the shape of a chest, played by spinning its small handle.
