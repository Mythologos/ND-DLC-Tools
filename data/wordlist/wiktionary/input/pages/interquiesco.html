Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

inter- +‎ quiēscō (“to rest”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.ter.kʷiˈeːs.koː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrkʷiˈeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.ter.kwiˈes.ko/, [in̪t̪erkwiˈɛsko]

Verb[edit]

interquiēscō (present infinitive interquiēscere, perfect active interquiēvī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to rest briefly, pause between tasks
