Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ pilōsus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sub.piˈloː.sus/, [s̠ʊpːɪˈɫ̪oːs̠ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sub.piˈlo.sus/, [subpiˈlɔːs̬us]

Adjective[edit]

subpilōsus (feminine subpilōsa, neuter subpilōsum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. somewhat hairy
