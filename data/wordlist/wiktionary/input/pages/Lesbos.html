English[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Lesvos

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Λέσβος (Lésbos).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˈlɛz.bɒs/

Proper noun[edit]

Lesbos

  1. An island in Greece, in the northeastern Aegean Sea.

Synonyms[edit]

  • Mytilene
