Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ mānō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈmaː.noː/, [eːˈmäːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈma.no/, [eˈmäːno]

Verb[edit]

ēmānō (present infinitive ēmānāre, perfect active ēmānāvī, supine ēmānātum); first conjugation

  1. to flow out; arise or emanate (from)
  2. to become known
