  • Rhymes: -esːio
  • Syllabification(key): ses‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

sessio

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sedeō (“sit”) +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈses.si.oː/, [ˈs̠ɛs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈses.si.o/, [ˈsɛsːio]

Noun[edit]

sessiō f (genitive sessiōnis); third declension

  1. a sitting
  2. a seat, place to sit
  3. an idle sitting, loitering
  4. a discussion session
