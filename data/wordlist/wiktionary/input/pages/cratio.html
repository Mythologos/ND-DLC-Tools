Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From crātis +‎ -iō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkraː.ti.oː/, [ˈkräːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkrat.t͡si.o/, [ˈkrät̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

crātiō (present infinitive crātīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to harrow
