Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈpun.ɡoː/, [pɛrˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈpun.ɡo/, [perˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

perpungō (present infinitive perpungere, perfect active perpūnxī, supine perpūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to pierce through
