Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From splendeō (“I shine, glitter”) +‎ faciō (“I do, make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /splenˈdi.fi.koː/, [s̠pɫ̪ɛn̪ˈd̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /splenˈdi.fi.ko/, [splen̪ˈd̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

splendificō (present infinitive splendificāre, perfect active splendificāvī, supine splendificātum); first conjugation

  1. to brighten, make bright, illumine
