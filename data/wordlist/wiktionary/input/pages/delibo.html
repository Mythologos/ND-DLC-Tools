Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

delibo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of delibare

Anagrams[edit]

  • bolide, deboli

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ lībō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈliː.boː/, [d̪eːˈlʲiːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈli.bo/, [d̪eˈliːbo]

Verb[edit]

dēlībō (present infinitive dēlībāre, perfect active dēlībāvī, supine dēlībātum); first conjugation

  1. to take off or away (a little)
  2. to diminish
