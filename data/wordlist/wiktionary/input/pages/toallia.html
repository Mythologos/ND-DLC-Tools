Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • toacula, toacla, tuala, toalia, tuella, *toaillia

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Frankish *þwahlijā, *þwahilu (“towel, washcloth”).[1][2]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /toˈal.li.a/, [t̪oˈälːʲiä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /toˈal.li.a/, [t̪oˈälːiä]

Noun[edit]

toallia f (genitive toalliae); first declension[3]

  1. (Medieval Latin) towel, washcloth
    Synonym: mantele

Declension[edit]
