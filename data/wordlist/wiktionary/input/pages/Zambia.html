Etymology[edit]

From "Zambezi". See Zambezi at Wikipedia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈzæmbiə/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)
  • Audio (Zambia)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Zambia

  2. A country in Southern Africa. Official name: Republic of Zambia. Formerly called Northern Rhodesia.
