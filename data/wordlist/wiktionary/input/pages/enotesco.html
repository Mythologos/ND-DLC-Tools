Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ notēscō (“to become known”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eː.noˈteːs.koː/, [eːnɔˈt̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /e.noˈtes.ko/, [enoˈt̪ɛsko]

Verb[edit]

ēnotēscō (present infinitive ēnotēscere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to become widely known
