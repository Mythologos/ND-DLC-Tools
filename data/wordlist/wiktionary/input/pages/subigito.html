Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈbi.ɡi.toː/, [s̠ʊˈbɪɡɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈbi.d͡ʒi.to/, [suˈbiːd͡ʒit̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

From subigō +‎ -itō.

Verb[edit]

subigitō (present infinitive subigitāre, perfect active subigitāvī, supine subigitātum); first conjugation

  1. to sexually arouse by caressing
