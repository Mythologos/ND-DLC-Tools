Alternative forms[edit]

  • Grœnlandia, Grönlandia

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡroe̯nˈlan.di.a/, [ɡroe̯nˈɫ̪än̪d̪iä]

Proper noun[edit]

Groenlandia f sg (genitive Groenlandiae); first declension

  1. (New Latin) Greenland (a large self-governing island and dependent territory of Denmark, in North America)
