Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈreː.ti.oː/, [ɪnˈreːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈret.t͡si.o/, [inˈrɛt̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

inrētiō (present infinitive inrētīre, perfect active inrētīvī or inrētiī, supine inrētītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of irrētiō
