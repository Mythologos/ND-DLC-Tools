Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see French révision), ultimately from Latin revīsiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈreʋisio/, [ˈre̞ʋiˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): re‧vi‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

revisio

  1. revision

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From revideō (“to see”) +‎ -iō (feminine abstract noun foming suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈu̯iː.si.oː/, [reˈu̯iːs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈvi.si.o/, [reˈviːs̬io]

Noun[edit]

revīsiō f (genitive revīsiōnis); third declension

  1. a seeing again

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
