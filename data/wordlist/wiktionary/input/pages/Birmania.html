Italian[edit]

Italian Wikipedia has an article on:
Birmania
Wikipedia it

Etymology[edit]

Ultimately derived from Burmese ဗမာ (ba.ma), colloquial form of မြန်မာ (mranma).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /birˈma.nja/
  • Rhymes: -anja
  • Hyphenation: Bir‧mà‧nia

Proper noun[edit]

Birmania f

  1. Myanmar (a country in Southeast Asia)

Derived terms[edit]

  • birmano

Anagrams[edit]

  • Marabini
