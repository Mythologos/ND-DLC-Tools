Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • praejudico

Etymology[edit]

prae- +‎ iūdicō (“I judge, pass judgement”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈi̯uː.di.koː/, [präe̯ˈi̯uːd̪ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈju.di.ko/, [preˈjuːd̪iko]

Verb[edit]

praeiūdicō (present infinitive praeiūdicāre, perfect active praeiūdicāvī, supine praeiūdicātum); first conjugation

  1. to prejudge
