Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Swedish provision, ultimately from Latin prōvīsiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈproʋisio/, [ˈpro̞ʋiˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): pro‧vi‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

provisio

  1. commission (fee charged by an agent or broker for carrying out a transaction)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

prōvideō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈu̯iː.si.oː/, [proːˈu̯iːs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈvi.si.o/, [proˈviːs̬io]

Noun[edit]

prōvīsiō f (genitive prōvīsiōnis); third declension

  1. foreknowledge
  2. foresight
  3. precaution

Third-declension noun.
