Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈluː.doː/, [älˈlʲuːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈlu.do/, [äd̪ˈluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

adlūdō (present infinitive adlūdere, perfect active adlūsī, supine adlūsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of allūdō
