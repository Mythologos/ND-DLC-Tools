Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

exaspero

  1. first-person singular present indicative of exasperar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ asperō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsas.pe.roː/, [ɛkˈs̠äs̠pɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsas.pe.ro/, [eɡˈzäspero]

Verb[edit]

exasperō (present infinitive exasperāre, perfect active exasperāvī, supine exasperātum); first conjugation

  1. to roughen
  2. to irritate, provoke, exasperate
