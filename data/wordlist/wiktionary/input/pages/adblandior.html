Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ad- +‎ blandior

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈblan.di.or/, [äd̪ˈbɫ̪än̪d̪iɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈblan.di.or/, [äd̪ˈblän̪d̪ior]

Verb[edit]

adblandior (present infinitive adblandīrī, perfect active adblanditus sum); fourth conjugation, deponent

  1. Alternative form of ēblandior
