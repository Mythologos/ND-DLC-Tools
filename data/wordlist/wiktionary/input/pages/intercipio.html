Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ capiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈki.pi.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈkɪpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈt͡ʃi.pi.o/, [in̪t̪erˈt͡ʃiːpio]

Verb[edit]

intercipiō (present infinitive intercipere, perfect active intercēpī, supine interceptum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to intercept
  2. to interrupt, cut off
  3. to rob, steal
