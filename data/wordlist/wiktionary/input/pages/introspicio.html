Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.troːsˈpi.ki.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪roːs̠ˈpɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.trosˈpi.t͡ʃi.o/, [in̪t̪rosˈpiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

intrōspiciō (present infinitive intrōspicere, perfect active intrōspexī, supine intrōspectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to look at, into or upon
  2. to examine, inspect
