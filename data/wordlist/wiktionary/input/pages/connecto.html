Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈnek.toː/, [kɔnˈnɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈnek.to/, [konˈnɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

connectō (present infinitive connectere, perfect active connexuī, supine connexum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of cōnectō
