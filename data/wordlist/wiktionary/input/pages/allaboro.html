Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adlabōrō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ labōrō (“work”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /al.laˈboː.roː/, [älːʲäˈboːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /al.laˈbo.ro/, [älːäˈbɔːro]

Verb[edit]

allabōrō (present infinitive allabōrāre, perfect active allabōrāvī, supine allabōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to labor or toil at something
  2. (with dative) to add to with labor or pains
