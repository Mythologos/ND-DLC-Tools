Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtractō
  • attractō
  • attrectō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈtrek.toː/, [ät̪ˈt̪rɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈtrek.to/, [äd̪ˈt̪rɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

adtrectō (present infinitive adtrectāre, perfect active adtrectāvī, supine adtrectātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attrectō
