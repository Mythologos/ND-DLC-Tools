Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • asservō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈser.u̯oː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɛru̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈser.vo/, [äd̪ˈsɛrvo]

Verb[edit]

adservō (present infinitive adservāre, perfect active adservāvī, supine adservātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of asservō
