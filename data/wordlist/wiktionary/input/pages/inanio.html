Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inānis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnaː.ni.oː/, [ɪˈnäːnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈna.ni.o/, [iˈnäːnio]

Verb[edit]

ināniō (present infinitive inānīre, perfect active inānīvī or ināniī, supine inānītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to empty (out) or evacuate
