Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cafaea, cafaeum; cafēum
  • caffaea, caffaeum; caffēa, caffēum
  • coffaea, coffaeum; coffēa, coffēum

Etymology[edit]

Compare Coffea.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kaˈfeː.a/, [käˈfeːä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kaˈfe.a/, [käˈfɛːä]

Noun[edit]

cafēa f (genitive cafēae); first declension

  1. (New Latin) coffee

Romanian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ottoman Turkish قهوه (kahve), from Arabic قَهْوَة (qahwa). Akin to French café.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /kaˈfe̯a/
  • Rhymes: -a
  • Hyphenation: ca‧fea
  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

cafea f (plural cafele)

  1. (uncountable) coffee
  2. (countable) serving of coffee
