Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈru.ti.lo/
  • Rhymes: -utilo
  • Hyphenation: rù‧ti‧lo

Noun[edit]

rutilo m (plural rutili)

  1. rutile (mineral)
  2. roach (fish)

Anagrams[edit]

  • tuorli

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From rutilus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈru.ti.loː/, [ˈrʊt̪ɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈru.ti.lo/, [ˈruːt̪ilo]

Verb[edit]

rutilō (present infinitive rutilāre, perfect active rutilāvī, supine rutilātum); first conjugation

  1. to redden

Alternative forms[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

 
  • (Brazil) IPA(key): /ʁuˈt͡ʃi.lu/ [huˈt͡ʃi.lu]
  • (Brazil) IPA(key): /ʁuˈt͡ʃi.lu/ [huˈt͡ʃi.lu]
    • (Rio de Janeiro) IPA(key): /ʁuˈt͡ʃi.lu/ [χuˈt͡ʃi.lu]

Noun[edit]

rutilo m (plural rutilos)

  1. intense shine, brightness
  2. (mineralogy) rutile (mineral composed primarily of titanium dioxide)
