Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

Adverbial accusative of aliquis (“someone, something”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.li.kʷid/, [ˈälʲɪkʷɪd̪]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.li.kwid/, [ˈäːlikwid̪]

Adverb[edit]

aliquid (not comparable)

  1. somewhat, to some extent

Etymology 2[edit]

See the etymology of the corresponding lemma form.

Pronoun[edit]

aliquid

  1. neuter nominative/accusative singular of aliquis
