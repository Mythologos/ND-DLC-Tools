Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ bulliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈbul.li.oː/, [rɛˈbʊlːʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈbul.li.o/, [reˈbulːio]

Verb[edit]

rebulliō (present infinitive rebullīre, perfect active rebullīvī or rebulliī); fourth conjugation, no supine stem

  1. to bubble up
