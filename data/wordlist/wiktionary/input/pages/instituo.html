Verb[edit]

ƒ´nstitu≈ç (present infinitive ƒ´nstituere, perfect active ƒ´nstituƒ´, supine ƒ´nstit≈´tum); third conjugation

  3. to make (something) a habit, practice, or custom
  4. to build, construct
