Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈkom.mo.doː/, [ɪŋˈkɔmːɔd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈkom.mo.do/, [iŋˈkɔmːod̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

From in- +‎ commodō.

Verb[edit]

incommodō (present infinitive incommodāre, perfect active incommodāvī, supine incommodātum); first conjugation

  1. to inconvenience
