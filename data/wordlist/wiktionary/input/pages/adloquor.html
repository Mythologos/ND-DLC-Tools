Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • alloquor

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.lo.kʷor/, [ˈälːʲɔkʷɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.lo.kwor/, [ˈäd̪lokwor]

Verb[edit]

adloquor (present infinitive adloquī, perfect active adlocūtus sum); third conjugation, deponent

  1. Alternative form of alloquor
