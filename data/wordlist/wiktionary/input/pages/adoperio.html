Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ operio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.doˈpe.ri.oː/, [äd̪ɔˈpɛrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.doˈpe.ri.o/, [äd̪oˈpɛːrio]

Verb[edit]

adoperiō (present infinitive adoperīre, perfect active adoperuī, supine adopertum); fourth conjugation

  1. to cover up or over
    Synonyms: vēlō, dissimulō, occultō, indūcō, operiō, obnūbō, occulō, condō, recondō, verrō, cooperiō, obruō, nūbō, tegō, abscondō, abdō, premō, opprimō, comprimō, obvolvō, prōtegō, mergō
    Antonyms: adaperiō, aperiō, patefaciō
