Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English nutrition), ultimately from Latin nūtrītiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnutritio/, [ˈnut̪riˌt̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): nut‧ri‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

nutritio

  1. nutrition

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /nuːˈtriː.ti.oː/, [nuːˈt̪riːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /nuˈtrit.t͡si.o/, [nuˈt̪rit̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

nūtrītiō f (genitive nūtrītiōnis); third declension

  1. nutrition

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.

Related terms[edit]

  • nūtrīciō

Adjective[edit]

nūtrītiō

  1. dative/ablative masculine/neuter singular of nūtrītius
