Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ honestō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ko.hoˈnes.toː/, [ko(ɦ)ɔˈnɛs̠t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ko.oˈnes.to/, [koːˈnɛst̪o]

Verb[edit]

cohonestō (present infinitive cohonestāre, perfect active cohonestāvī, supine cohonestātum); first conjugation

  1. to honour, grace
