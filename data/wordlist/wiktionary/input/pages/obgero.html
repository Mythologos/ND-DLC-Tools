Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈob.ɡe.roː/, [ˈɔbɡɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈob.d͡ʒe.ro/, [ˈɔbd͡ʒero]

Verb[edit]

obgerō (present infinitive obgerere, perfect active obgessī, supine obgestum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of oggerō
