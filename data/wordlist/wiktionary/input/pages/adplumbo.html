Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • applumbō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈplum.boː/, [äpˈpɫ̪ʊmboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈplum.bo/, [äd̪ˈplumbo]

Verb[edit]

adplumbō (present infinitive adplumbāre, perfect active adplumbāvī, supine adplumbātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of applumbō
