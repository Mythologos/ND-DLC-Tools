Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ peiōrō. Attested in the Lex Frisionum.[1]

Verb[edit]

impeiōrō (present infinitive impeiōrāre, perfect active impeiōrāvī, supine impeiōrātum); first conjugation (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. to worsen
