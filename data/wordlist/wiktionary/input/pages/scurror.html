Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

scurra (“buffoon”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈskur.ror/, [ˈs̠kʊrːɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈskur.ror/, [ˈskurːor]

Verb[edit]

scurror (present infinitive scurrārī, perfect active scurrātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to play the buffoon
