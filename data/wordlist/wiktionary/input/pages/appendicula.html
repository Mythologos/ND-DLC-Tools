English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the genus name.

Noun[edit]

appendicula (plural appendiculas)

  1. Any orchid of the genus Appendicula; stream orchids.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From appendix +‎ -ula.

Noun[edit]

appendicula f (genitive appendiculae); first declension

  1. Diminutive of appendix
  2. small appendage

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
