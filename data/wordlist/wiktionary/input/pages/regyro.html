Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • regīrō

Etymology[edit]

re- (“again”) +‎ gȳrō (“turn around”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈɡyː.roː/, [rɛˈɡyːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈd͡ʒi.ro/, [reˈd͡ʒiːro]

Verb[edit]

regȳrō (present infinitive regȳrāre, perfect active regȳrāvī, supine regȳrātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to turn about again, to wheel around
  2. (Medieval Latin, transitive, intransitive) to turn round and round, rotate
