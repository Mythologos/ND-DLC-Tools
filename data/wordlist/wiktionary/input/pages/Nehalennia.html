Proper noun[edit]

Nehalennia f

  1. A taxonomic genus within the family Coenagrionidae – certain damselflies.

Proper noun[edit]

Nehalennia f (genitive Nehalenniae); first declension

  1. (mythology) A Celtic or Germanic goddess.
