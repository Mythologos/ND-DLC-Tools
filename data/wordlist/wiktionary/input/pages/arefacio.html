Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ārfaciō (contraction)

Etymology[edit]

From āreō +‎ faciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aː.reˈfa.ki.oː/, [äːrɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.reˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [äreˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

ārefaciō (present infinitive ārefacere, perfect active ārefēcī, supine ārefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to dry (up)
  2. to wither
