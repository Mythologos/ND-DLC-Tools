Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Latin Juppiter.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈjupːiter/, [ˈjupːit̪e̞r]
  • Rhymes: -upːiter
  • Syllabification(key): Jup‧pi‧ter

Proper noun[edit]

Juppiter

  1. (Roman mythology) Jupiter (Roman god)
    Synonym: Jupiter

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯up.pi.ter/, [ˈi̯ʊpːɪt̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈjup.pi.ter/, [ˈjupːit̪er]

Proper noun[edit]

Juppiter m (genitive Jovis); third declension

  1. Alternative spelling of Iuppiter

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
