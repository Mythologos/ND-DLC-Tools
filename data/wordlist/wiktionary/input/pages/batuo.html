Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbaː.tu.oː/, [ˈbäːt̪uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈba.tu.o/, [ˈbäːt̪uo]

Verb[edit]

bātuō (present infinitive bātuere, perfect active bātuī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative spelling of battuō (“to beat, strike”)
