Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • approperō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈpro.pe.roː/, [äpˈprɔpɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈpro.pe.ro/, [äd̪ˈprɔːpero]

Verb[edit]

adproperō (present infinitive adproperāre, perfect active adproperāvī, supine adproperātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of approperō
