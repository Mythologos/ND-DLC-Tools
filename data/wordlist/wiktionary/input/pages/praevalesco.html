Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From praevaleō +‎ -esco.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.u̯aˈleːs.koː/, [präe̯u̯äˈɫ̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.vaˈles.ko/, [preväˈlɛsko]

Verb[edit]

praevalēscō (present infinitive praevalēscere); third conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive) I become or grow very strong
