Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯i̯ˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [präe̯i̯ˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pra.eˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [präeˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

praejiciō (present infinitive praejicere, perfect active praejēcī, supine praejectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of praeiciō
