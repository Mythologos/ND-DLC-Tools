Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ trucīdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.truˈkiː.doː/, [kɔn̪t̪rʊˈkiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.truˈt͡ʃi.do/, [kon̪t̪ruˈt͡ʃiːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

contrucīdō (present infinitive contrucīdāre, perfect active contrucīdāvī, supine contrucīdātum); first conjugation

  1. to hew or cut to pieces
