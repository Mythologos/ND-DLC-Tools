Interjection[edit]

ehem

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈe.hem/, [ˈe(ɦ)ɛ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.em/, [ˈɛːem]

Interjection[edit]

ehem

  1. ha! so! (expressing surprise)
