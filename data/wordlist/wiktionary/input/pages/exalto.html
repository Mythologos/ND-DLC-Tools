Galician[edit]

Verb[edit]

exalto

  1. first-person singular present indicative of exaltar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ altus (“high”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsal.toː/, [ɛkˈs̠äɫ̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsal.to/, [eɡˈzäl̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

exaltō (present infinitive exaltāre, perfect active exaltāvī, supine exaltātum); first conjugation

  1. to exalt or elevate
  2. to praise
  3. to deepen
