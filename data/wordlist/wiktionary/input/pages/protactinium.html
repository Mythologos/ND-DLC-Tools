English[edit]

Chemical element
Pa
Previous: thorium (Th)
Next: uranium (U)

Etymology[edit]

proto- +‎ actinium.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˌpɹəʊ.tækˈtɪn.i.əm/
  • (US) enPR: prō"tăktĭ'nēəm, IPA(key): /ˌpɹoʊ.tækˈtɪn.i.əm/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Noun[edit]

protactinium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
protactinium
Wikipedia
  1. A chemical element (symbol Pa) with atomic number 91: a dense, silvery-gray actinide metal.

Usage notes[edit]

It was called protoactinium until 1949. Then the name was shortened.

Synonyms[edit]
