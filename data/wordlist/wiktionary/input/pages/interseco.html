Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

interseco

  1. first-person singular present indicative of intersecare

Anagrams[edit]

  • certosine, cestinerò, consterei, cortinese, noceresti, recensito, rinoceste, sconterei, secretino

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ secō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈter.se.koː/, [ɪn̪ˈt̪ɛrs̠ɛkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈter.se.ko/, [in̪ˈt̪ɛrseko]

Verb[edit]

intersecō (present infinitive intersecāre, perfect active intersecuī, supine intersectum); first conjugation

  1. to cut apart, separate, divide
  2. to intersect
