Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ quaerō (“seek”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈskʷiː.roː/, [ɛkˈs̠kʷiːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eksˈkwi.ro/, [eksˈkwiːro]

Verb[edit]

exquīrō (present infinitive exquīrere, perfect active exquīsīvī, supine exquīsītum); third conjugation

  1. to seek out, search for or hunt up
  2. to inquire into
