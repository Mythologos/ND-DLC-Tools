Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From pastinum.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpas.ti.noː/, [ˈpäs̠t̪ɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpas.ti.no/, [ˈpäst̪ino]

Verb[edit]

pastinō (present infinitive pastināre, perfect active pastināvī, supine pastinātum); first conjugation

  1. to dig and level the ground in preparation for planting
