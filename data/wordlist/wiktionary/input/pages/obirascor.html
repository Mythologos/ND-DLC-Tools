Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ob- +‎ īrāscor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /o.biːˈraːs.kor/, [ɔbiːˈräːs̠kɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /o.biˈras.kor/, [obiˈräskor]

Verb[edit]

obīrāscor (present infinitive obīrāscī, perfect active obīrātus sum); third conjugation, deponent

  1. to become enraged against someone or something (+ dative)
    Synonym: īrāscor
