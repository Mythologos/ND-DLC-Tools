Catalan[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *vojevoda.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central) [bujˈβɔ.ðə]
  • IPA(key): (Balearic) [vojˈvɔ.ðə]
  • IPA(key): (Valencian) [vojˈvɔ.ða]

Noun[edit]

voivoda m (plural voivodes)

  1. voivode

Derived terms[edit]

  • voivodat

Related terms[edit]

  • voivodina
  • Voivodina

Further reading[edit]

  • “voivoda” in Diccionari de la llengua catalana, segona edició, Institut d’Estudis Catalans.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Slavic *vojevoda.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯oi̯.u̯o.da/, [ˈu̯ɔi̯u̯ɔd̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvoj.vo.da/, [ˈvɔjvod̪ä]

Noun[edit]

voivoda m (genitive voivodae); first declension

  1. voivode
