Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

immodestus (“immoderate”) +‎ -ē

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /im.moˈdes.teː/, [ɪmːɔˈd̪ɛs̠t̪eː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /im.moˈdes.te/, [imːoˈd̪ɛst̪e]

Adverb[edit]

immodestē (comparative immodestius, superlative immodestissimē)

  1. immoderately, shamelessly
