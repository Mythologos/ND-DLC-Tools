Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /opˈtin.ɡoː/, [ɔpˈt̪ɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /opˈtin.ɡo/, [opˈt̪iŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

optingō (present infinitive optingere, perfect active optigī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of obtingō
