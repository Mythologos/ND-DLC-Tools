Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [äd̪ˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [äd̪ˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

adjiciō (present infinitive adjicere, perfect active adjēcī, supine adjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of adiciō
