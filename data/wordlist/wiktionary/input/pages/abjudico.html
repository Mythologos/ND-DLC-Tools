Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈi̯uː.di.koː/, [äbˈi̯uːd̪ɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈju.di.ko/, [äbˈjuːd̪iko]

Verb[edit]

abjūdicō (present infinitive abjūdicāre, perfect active abjūdicāvī, supine abjūdicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of abiūdicō
