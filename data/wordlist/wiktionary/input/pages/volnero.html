Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯ol.ne.roː/, [ˈu̯ɔɫ̪nɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvol.ne.ro/, [ˈvɔlnero]

Verb[edit]

volnerō (present infinitive volnerāre, perfect active volnerāvī, supine volnerātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of vulnerō
