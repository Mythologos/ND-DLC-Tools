Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ metō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.me.toː/, [ˈd̪eːmɛt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.me.to/, [ˈd̪ɛːmet̪o]

Verb[edit]

dēmetō (present infinitive dēmetere, perfect active dēmessuī, supine dēmessum); third conjugation

  1. to reap, cut or mow
  2. to cut down
  3. to pick (fruit)
  4. to gather
  5. to shear
