Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • agrī cultūra

Etymology[edit]

ager (“field, soil”) +‎ cultūra (“cultivation”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.ɡri.kulˈtuː.ra/, [äɡrɪkʊɫ̪ˈt̪uːrä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.ɡri.kulˈtu.ra/, [äɡrikul̪ˈt̪uːrä]

Noun[edit]

agricultūra f (genitive agricultūrae); first declension

  1. agriculture, farming

Declension[edit]
