Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdis.si.koː/, [ˈd̪ɪs̠ːɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈdis.si.ko/, [ˈd̪isːiko]

Verb[edit]

dissicō (present infinitive dissicāre, perfect active dissicāvī, supine dissicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dissecō
