Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /fi.deːˈi̯u.be.oː/, [fɪd̪eːˈi̯ʊbeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /fi.deˈju.be.o/, [fid̪eˈjuːbeo]

Verb[edit]

fidējubeō (present infinitive fidējubēre, perfect active fidējussī, supine fidējussum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of fideiubeō
