Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

super- +‎ lūceō (“shine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈluː.ke.oː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈɫ̪uːkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈlu.t͡ʃe.o/, [superˈluːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

superlūceō (present infinitive superlūcēre); second conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (Late Latin) to shine
