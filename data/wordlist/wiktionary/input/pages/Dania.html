Dania

Proper noun[edit]

Dania

  1. The former name and present-day synonym of Dania Beach, Florida, renamed in 1998.
  2. A town in Vavoua Department, Haut-Sassandra Region, Ivory Coast.

Latin[edit]

Latin Wikipedia has an article on:
Dania

Etymology[edit]

From Dānī (“Danes”) +‎ -ia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdaː.ni.a/, [ˈd̪äːniä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈda.ni.a/, [ˈd̪äːniä]

Proper noun[edit]

Dānia f sg (genitive Dāniae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) Denmark

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.
