Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ trahō (“I drag”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kirˈkun.tra.hoː/, [kɪrˈkʊn̪t̪rä(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃirˈkun.tra.o/, [t͡ʃirˈkun̪t̪räo]

Verb[edit]

circumtrahō (present infinitive circumtrahere, perfect active circumtrāxī, supine circumtractum); third conjugation

  1. to draw, drag or pull around
