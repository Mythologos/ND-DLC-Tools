English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Guinea
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Portuguese Guiné, which is of uncertain origin.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɡɪniː/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪni

Proper noun[edit]

Guinea

  1. (historical) The coastal region of West Africa between Morocco and the Congo, particularly the north shore of the Gulf of Guinea.
  3. A country in West Africa. Official name: Republic of Guinea.
  4. (derogatory US slang, ethnic slur) Someone of Italian descent in the United States.

Hyponyms[edit]

  • (historical region): Upper Guinea, Lower Guinea; Gold Coast, Pepper Coast, Grain Coast, Slave Coast, Ivory Coast
