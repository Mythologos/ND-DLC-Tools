Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈlo.re.tʰoː/, [ˈɫ̪ɔrɛt̪ʰoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈlo.re.to/, [ˈlɔːret̪o]

Verb[edit]

lorethō (present infinitive lorethāre); first conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, of rams) to bleat

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect is attested.
