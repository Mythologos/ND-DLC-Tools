Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • subfulciō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sufˈful.ki.oː/, [s̠ʊfˈfʊɫ̪kioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sufˈful.t͡ʃi.o/, [sufˈful̠ʲt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

suffulciō (present infinitive suffulcīre, perfect active suffulsī, supine suffultum); fourth conjugation

  1. to prop underneath, prop up, support
