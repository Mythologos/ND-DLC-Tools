Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From faber.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfa.bri.oː/, [ˈfäbrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfa.bri.o/, [ˈfäːbrio]

Verb[edit]

fabriō (present infinitive fabrīre, perfect active fabrīvī); fourth conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to make, prepare
