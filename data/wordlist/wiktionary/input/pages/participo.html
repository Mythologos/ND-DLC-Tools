Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From particeps (“sharing”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /parˈti.ki.poː/, [pärˈt̪ɪkɪpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /parˈti.t͡ʃi.po/, [pärˈt̪iːt͡ʃipo]

Verb[edit]

participō (present infinitive participāre, perfect active participāvī, supine participātum); first conjugation

  1. to partake of, participate in
  2. to share
    Synonyms: partiō, distribuō, tribuō, cōnsociō, discrībō, dīvidō, compartior, impertiō
  3. to impart
    Synonym: contribuō
