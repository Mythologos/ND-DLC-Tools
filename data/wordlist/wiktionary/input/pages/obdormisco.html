Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

obdormiō +‎ -scō

Verb[edit]

obdormīscō (present infinitive obdormīscere, perfect active obdormīvī, supine obdormītum); third conjugation, no passive

  1. to fall asleep
    Synonyms: dormiō, obdormiō, dormītō, obdormītō, cubō
    Antonyms: expergīscor, vigilō
