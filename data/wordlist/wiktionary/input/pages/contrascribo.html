Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From contrā- +‎ scrībō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.traːˈskriː.boː/, [kɔn̪t̪räːˈs̠kriːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.traˈskri.bo/, [kon̪t̪räˈskriːbo]

Verb[edit]

contrāscrībō (present infinitive contrāscrībere, perfect active contrāscrīpsī, supine contrāscrīptum); third conjugation

  1. (transitive) to countersign
