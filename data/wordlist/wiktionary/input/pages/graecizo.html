Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Graecus +‎ -izō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡrae̯ˈkiz.zoː/, [ɡräe̯ˈkɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡreˈt͡ʃid.d͡zo/, [ɡreˈt͡ʃid̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

graecizō (present infinitive graecizāre, perfect active graecizāvī, supine graecizātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) Alternative form of graecissō (“to act like the Greeks do; to speak Greek”)
