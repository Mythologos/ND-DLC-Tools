Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • alleviō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈle.u̯i.oː/, [älˈlʲeu̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈle.vi.o/, [äd̪ˈlɛːvio]

Verb[edit]

adleviō (present infinitive adleviāre, perfect active adleviāvī, supine adleviātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of alleviō
