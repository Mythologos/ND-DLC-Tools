Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • recuperō

Etymology[edit]

From recipio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈki.pe.roː/, [rɛˈkɪpɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈt͡ʃi.pe.ro/, [reˈt͡ʃiːpero]

Verb[edit]

reciperō (present infinitive reciperāre, perfect active reciperāvī, supine reciperātum); first conjugation

  1. to get again, regain, recover
    Synonyms: recipiō, revocō
    Antonyms: reddō, remittō
  2. to revive, restore
