Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.te.roː/, [ˈät̪ːɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.te.ro/, [ˈäd̪t̪ero]

Verb[edit]

adterō (present infinitive adterere, perfect active adtrīvī, supine adtrītum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of atterō
