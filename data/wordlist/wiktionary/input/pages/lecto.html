Interlingua[edit]

Noun[edit]

lecto (plural lectos)

  1. bed

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From legō (“I read, gather”) +‎ -tō (frequentative suffix).

Verb[edit]

lēctō (present infinitive lēctāre, perfect active lēctāvī, supine lēctātum); first conjugation

  1. (rare) Synonym of lēctitō
