Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /oˈbau̯.di.oː/, [ɔˈbäu̯d̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈbau̯.di.o/, [oˈbäːu̯d̪io]

Verb[edit]

obaudiō (present infinitive obaudīre, perfect active obaudīvī); fourth conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (Late Latin) Alternative form of oboediō
