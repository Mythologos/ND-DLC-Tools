Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sordeō (“I am dirty”) +‎ -scō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sorˈdeːs.koː/, [s̠ɔrˈd̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sorˈdes.ko/, [sorˈd̪ɛsko]

Verb[edit]

sordēscō (present infinitive sordēscere, perfect active sorduī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to become dirty

Conjugation[edit]

  • The third principal part is shared with sordeō.
