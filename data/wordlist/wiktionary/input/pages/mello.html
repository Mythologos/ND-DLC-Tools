English[edit]

Noun[edit]

mello (plural mellos)

  1. (marching band) Short for mellophone.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mel (“honey”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈmel.loː/, [ˈmɛlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈmel.lo/, [ˈmɛlːo]

Verb[edit]

mellō (present infinitive mellāre, perfect active mellāvī, supine mellātum); first conjugation

  1. to make or collect honey
