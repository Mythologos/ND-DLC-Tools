Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /tranˈspi.ki.oː/, [t̪rä̃ːˈs̠pɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /tranˈspi.t͡ʃi.o/, [t̪ränˈspiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

trānspiciō (present infinitive trānspicere); third conjugation iō-variant, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (rare) to look or see through

Conjugation[edit]

  • This verb is very rare.
  • Post-Classical trānspexit occurs very rarely.
