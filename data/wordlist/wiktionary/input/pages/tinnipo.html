Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtin.ni.poː/, [ˈt̪ɪnːɪpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtin.ni.po/, [ˈt̪inːipo]

Verb[edit]

tinnipō (present infinitive tinnipāre); first conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, of ospreys) to cry

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect is attested.
