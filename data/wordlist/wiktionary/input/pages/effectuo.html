Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From effectus +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

effectuō (present infinitive effectuāre, perfect active effectuāvī, supine effectuātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin, New Latin) effect, bring about
