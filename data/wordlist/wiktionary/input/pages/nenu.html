Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

nenä +‎ -u

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnenu/, [ˈne̞nu]
  • Rhymes: -enu
  • Syllabification(key): ne‧nu

Noun[edit]

nenu (colloquial, childish)

  1. nose
