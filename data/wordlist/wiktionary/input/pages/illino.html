Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • illiniō, inlinō, inliniō

Etymology[edit]

in- +‎ linō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈil.li.noː/, [ˈɪlːʲɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈil.li.no/, [ˈilːino]

Verb[edit]

illinō (present infinitive illinere, perfect active illēvī, supine illitum); third conjugation

  1. to anoint, appoint
