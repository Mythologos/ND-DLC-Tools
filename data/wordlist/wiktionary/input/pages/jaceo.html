Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯a.ke.oː/, [ˈi̯äkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈja.t͡ʃe.o/, [ˈjäːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

jaceō (present infinitive jacēre, perfect active jacuī, supine jacitum); second conjugation, no passive

  1. Alternative form of iaceō
