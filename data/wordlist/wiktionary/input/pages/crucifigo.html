Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From crux +‎ fīgō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kru.kiˈfiː.ɡoː/, [krʊkɪˈfiːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kru.t͡ʃiˈfi.ɡo/, [krut͡ʃiˈfiːɡo]

Verb[edit]

crucifīgō (present infinitive crucifīgere, perfect active crucifīxī, supine crucifīxum); third conjugation

  1. to crucify, fix on a cross
