Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ante- +‎ volō (“fly”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /anˈte.u̯o.loː/, [än̪ˈt̪eu̯ɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /anˈte.vo.lo/, [än̪ˈt̪ɛːvolo]

Verb[edit]

antevolō (present infinitive antevolāre, perfect active antevolāvī); first conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to fly before
