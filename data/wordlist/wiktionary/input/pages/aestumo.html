Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • æstumō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈae̯s.tu.moː/, [ˈäe̯s̠t̪ʊmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈes.tu.mo/, [ˈɛst̪umo]

Verb[edit]

aestumō (present infinitive aestumāre, perfect active aestumāvī, supine aestumātum); first conjugation

  1. (Old Latin) Alternative form of aestimō
