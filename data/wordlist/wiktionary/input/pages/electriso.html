Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈlek.tri.soː/, [eːˈɫ̪ɛkt̪rɪs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈlek.tri.so/, [eˈlɛkt̪ris̬o]

Verb[edit]

ēlectrisō (present infinitive ēlectrisāre, perfect active ēlectrisāvī, supine ēlectrisātum); first conjugation

  1. to charge (with electricity)
