Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /efˈfi.d͡ʒo/
  • Rhymes: -idʒo
  • Hyphenation: ef‧fì‧gio

Verb[edit]

effigio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of effigiare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From effigiēs (“likeness, portrait”) +‎ -ō (1st-conjugation verbal suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /efˈfi.ɡi.oː/, [ɛfˈfɪɡioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /efˈfi.d͡ʒi.o/, [efˈfiːd͡ʒio]

Verb[edit]

effigiō (present infinitive effigiāre, perfect active effigiāvī, supine effigiātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, Late Latin) to portray
