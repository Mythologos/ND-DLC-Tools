Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ surgō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈsur.ɡoː/, [ĩːˈs̠ʊrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈsur.ɡo/, [inˈsurɡo]

Verb[edit]

īnsurgō (present infinitive īnsurgere, perfect active īnsurrēxī, supine īnsurrēctum); third conjugation, limited passive

  1. (intransitive) to rise up (against)
