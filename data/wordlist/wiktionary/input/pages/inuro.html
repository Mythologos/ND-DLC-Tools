Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈnuː.roː/, [ɪˈnuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈnu.ro/, [iˈnuːro]

Verb[edit]

inūrō (present infinitive inūrere, perfect active inussī, supine inustum); third conjugation

  1. to burn (in, off or away)
