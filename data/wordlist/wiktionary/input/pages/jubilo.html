Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯uː.bi.loː/, [ˈi̯uːbɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈju.bi.lo/, [ˈjuːbilo]

Verb[edit]

jūbilō (present infinitive jūbilāre, perfect active jūbilāvī, supine jūbilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iūbilō
