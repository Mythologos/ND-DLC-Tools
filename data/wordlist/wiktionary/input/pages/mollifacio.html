Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mollis (“soft, mild, tender”) + faciō (“make, do”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /mol.liˈfa.ki.oː/, [mɔlːʲɪˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /mol.liˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [molːiˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

mollifaciō (present infinitive mollifacere, perfect active mollifēcī, supine mollifactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to loosen, release
