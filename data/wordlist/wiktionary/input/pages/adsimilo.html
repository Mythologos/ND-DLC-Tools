Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsi.mi.loː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɪmɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈsi.mi.lo/, [äd̪ˈsiːmilo]

Verb[edit]

adsimilō (present infinitive adsimilāre, perfect active adsimilāvī, supine adsimilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assimulō
