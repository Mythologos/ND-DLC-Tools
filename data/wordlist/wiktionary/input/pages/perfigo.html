Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

per- (“through”) +‎ fīgō (“I pierce”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈfiː.ɡoː/, [pɛrˈfiːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈfi.ɡo/, [perˈfiːɡo]

Verb[edit]

perfīgō (present infinitive perfīgere, perfect active perfīxī, supine perfīxum); third conjugation

  1. to pierce through, transfix
