Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aːrˈfa.ki.oː/, [äːrˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /arˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [ärˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

ārfaciō (present infinitive ārfacere, perfect active ārfēcī, supine ārfactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. Alternative form of ārefaciō
