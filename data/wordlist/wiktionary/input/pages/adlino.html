Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.li.noː/, [ˈälːʲɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.li.no/, [ˈäd̪lino]

Verb[edit]

adlinō (present infinitive adlinere, perfect active adlēvī, supine adlitum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of allinō
