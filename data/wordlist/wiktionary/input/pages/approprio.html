Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

approprio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of appropriarsi

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ propriō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /apˈpro.pri.oː/, [äpˈprɔprioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /apˈpro.pri.o/, [äpˈprɔːprio]

Verb[edit]

appropriō (present infinitive appropriāre, perfect active appropriāvī, supine appropriātum); first conjugation

  1. to make one's own, appropriate
