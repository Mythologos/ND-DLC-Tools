Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /suˈbor.di.no/
  • Rhymes: -ordino
  • Hyphenation: su‧bór‧di‧no

Verb[edit]

subordino

  1. first-person singular present indicative of subordinare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ ordino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈboːr.di.noː/, [s̠ʊˈboːrd̪ɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈbor.di.no/, [suˈbɔrd̪ino]

Verb[edit]

subōrdinō (present infinitive subōrdināre, perfect active subōrdināvī, supine subōrdinātum); first conjugation

  1. to subordinate
