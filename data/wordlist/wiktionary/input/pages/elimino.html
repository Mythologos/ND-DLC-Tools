Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ēx- +‎ līmen +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈliː.mi.noː/, [eːˈlʲiːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈli.mi.no/, [eˈliːmino]

Verb[edit]

ēlīminō (present infinitive ēlīmināre, perfect active ēlīmināvī, supine ēlīminātum); first conjugation

  1. to carry out of doors
  2. to eliminate
  3. to blab
