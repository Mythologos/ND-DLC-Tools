Esperanto[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From persisti +‎ -o.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [perˈsisto]
  • Rhymes: -isto
  • Hyphenation: per‧sis‧to

Noun[edit]

persisto (uncountable, accusative persiston)

  1. persistence

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ sistō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈsis.toː/, [pɛrˈs̠ɪs̠t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈsis.to/, [perˈsist̪o]

Verb[edit]

persistō (present infinitive persistere, perfect active perstitī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to persist, continue steadfastly
  2. (intransitive) to remain
