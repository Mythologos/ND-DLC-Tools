Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈpar.ɡoː/, [äs̠ˈpärɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈpar.ɡo/, [äsˈpärɡo]

Verb[edit]

aspargō (present infinitive aspargere, perfect active asparsī, supine asparsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of aspergō

Portuguese[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

 
  • (Brazil) IPA(key): /asˈpaʁ.ɡu/ [asˈpaɦ.ɡu]
  • (Brazil) IPA(key): /asˈpaʁ.ɡu/ [asˈpaɦ.ɡu]
    • (São Paulo) IPA(key): /asˈpaɾ.ɡu/
    • (Rio de Janeiro) IPA(key): /aʃˈpaʁ.ɡu/

Noun[edit]

aspargo m (plural aspargos)

  1. Brazilian Portuguese standard spelling of espargo.
