Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ stimulō (“to urge on”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsti.mu.loː/, [ɛkˈs̠t̪ɪmʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsti.mu.lo/, [ekˈst̪iːmulo]

Verb[edit]

exstimulō (present infinitive exstimulāre, perfect active exstimulāvī, supine exstimulātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, literally) to goad on
  2. (transitive) to provoke, instigate
