Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • minūriō

Etymology[edit]

Related to μινυρίζω (minurízō).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /miˈnur.ri.oː/, [mɪˈnʊrːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /miˈnur.ri.o/, [miˈnurːio]

Verb[edit]

minurriō (present infinitive minurrīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (of small birds) to twitter, to chirp
