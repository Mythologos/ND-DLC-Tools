Crimean Tatar[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowing from Latin abbreviatura.

Noun[edit]

abbreviatura

  1. abbreviature, abbreviation

Declension[edit]

Declension of abbreviatura
singular plural
nominative abbreviatura abbreviaturalar
genitive abbreviaturanıñ abbreviaturalarnıñ
dative abbreviaturağa abbreviaturalarğa
accusative abbreviaturanı abbreviaturalarnı
locative abbreviaturada abbreviaturalarda
ablative abbreviaturadan abbreviaturalardan

Synonyms[edit]

  • qısqartma (more common)

References[edit]

Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ab.bre.vjaˈtu.ra/
  • Rhymes: -ura
  • Hyphenation: ab‧bre‧via‧tù‧ra

Noun[edit]

abbreviatura f (plural abbreviature)

  1. initialism

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

abbreviō +‎ -tūra

Pronunciation 1[edit]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ab.bre.vi.aˈtu.ra/, [äbːreviäˈt̪uːrä]
Noun[edit]

abbreviātūra f (genitive abbreviātūrae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) abbreviature, abbreviation
Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Ablative abbreviātūrā abbreviātūrīs

Pronunciation 2[edit]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ab.bre.vi.aˈtu.ra/, [äbːreviäˈt̪uːrä]
Noun[edit]

abbreviātūrā f

  1. ablative singular of abbreviātūra

Etymology 2[edit]

Participle[edit]

abbreviātūra

  1. inflection of abbreviātūrus:
    1. nominative/vocative feminine singular
    2. nominative/accusative/vocative neuter plural

Participle[edit]

abbreviātūrā

  1. ablative feminine singular of abbreviātūrus
