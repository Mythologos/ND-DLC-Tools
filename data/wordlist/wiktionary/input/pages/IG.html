English[edit]

Phrase[edit]

IG

  1. (Internet slang, text messaging) Initialism of I guess.

Noun[edit]

IG (plural IGs)

  1. Initialism of inspector general.
    Coordinate term: OIG

Translations[edit]

inspector general
  • French: BIG (IG's Office)

Proper noun[edit]

IG

  1. Abbreviation of Instagram.
    • 2018, Nicki Minaj, "Barbie Tingz", Queen (Target version):
      Rap bitches tell their team: "Make em like Barbie" / Had to come off IG so they can't stalk me
    • 2020, Moneybagg Yo, “Match My Fly”, in Time Served:
      One day I was lurkin' on IG, thirsty, full of the purple
    • 2021, “On Me”, in Parallel World, performed by Cadence Weapon:
      Face ID on me, white programmer so they don’t see it’s me / Got fans on me, IG story, know where I be
  2. (epigraphy) Latin IG.

Adjective[edit]

IG (not comparable)

  1. (video games, slang) Abbreviation of in-game.
    Antonym: IRL

Anagrams[edit]

  • G. I., G.I., GI, gi

Latin[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

IG

  1. Initialism of Inscriptiones Graecae. Greek Inscriptions: a collection of ancient Greek inscriptions.

Related terms[edit]

  • SEG

Spanish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from English Instagram, abbreviated.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈiɡ/ [ˈiɣ̞]
  • Rhymes: -iɡ
  • Syllabification: IG

Proper noun[edit]

IG

  1. (informal) Instagram

Noun[edit]

IG m (plural IGs)

  1. (informal) a user account belonging to Instagram.
  2. (informal) a message sent using Instagram.

IG
