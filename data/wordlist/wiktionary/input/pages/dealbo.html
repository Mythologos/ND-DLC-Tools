Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ albō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deˈal.boː/, [d̪eˈäɫ̪boː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈal.bo/, [d̪eˈälbo]

Verb[edit]

dealbō (present infinitive dealbāre, perfect active dealbāvī, supine dealbātum); first conjugation

  1. to whitewash
  2. to plaster, parget
  3. to purify, cleanse
