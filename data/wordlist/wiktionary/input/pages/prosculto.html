Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prosˈkul.toː/, [prɔs̠ˈkʊɫ̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /prosˈkul.to/, [prosˈkul̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

proscultō (present infinitive proscultāre, perfect active proscultāvī, supine proscultātum); first conjugation

  1. to peer into, to examine
