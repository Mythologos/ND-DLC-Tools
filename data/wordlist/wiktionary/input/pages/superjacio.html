Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈi̯a.ki.oː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈi̯äkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈja.t͡ʃi.o/, [superˈjäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

superjaciō (present infinitive superjacere, perfect active superjēcī, supine superjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of superiaciō
