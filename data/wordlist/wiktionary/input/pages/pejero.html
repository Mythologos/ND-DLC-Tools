Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpei̯.i̯e.roː/, [ˈpɛi̯ːɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpe.je.ro/, [ˈpɛːjero]

Verb[edit]

pejerō (present infinitive pejerāre, perfect active pejerāvī, supine pejerātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of peierō
