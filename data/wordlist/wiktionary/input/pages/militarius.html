Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mīles (“soldier”) +‎ -ārius.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /miː.liˈtaː.ri.us/, [miːlʲɪˈt̪äːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /mi.liˈta.ri.us/, [miliˈt̪äːrius]

Adjective[edit]

mīlitārius (feminine mīlitāria, neuter mīlitārium, adverb mīlitāriē); first/second-declension adjective

  1. soldierlike, military
