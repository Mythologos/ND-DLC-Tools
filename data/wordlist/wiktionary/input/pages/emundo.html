Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ mundo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈmun.doː/, [eːˈmʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈmun.do/, [eˈmun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

ēmundō (present infinitive ēmundāre, perfect active ēmundāvī, supine ēmundātum); first conjugation

  1. to clean or cleanse thoroughly
