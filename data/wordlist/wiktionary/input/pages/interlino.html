Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ lino.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈter.li.noː/, [ɪn̪ˈt̪ɛrlʲɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈter.li.no/, [in̪ˈt̪ɛrlino]

Verb[edit]

interlinō (present infinitive interlinere, perfect active interlēvī, supine interlitum); third conjugation

  1. to daub, besmear or anoint between
  2. to falsify by erasing
