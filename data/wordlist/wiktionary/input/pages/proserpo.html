Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prō- (“forward”) +‎ serpō (“creep, crawl”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈser.poː/, [proːˈs̠ɛrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈser.po/, [proˈs̬ɛrpo]

Verb[edit]

prōserpō (present infinitive prōserpere, perfect active prōserpsī, supine prōserptum); third conjugation, no passive

  1. to creep forward
  2. to creep along, to crawl along
