English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
vigoro
Wikipedia

Noun[edit]

vigoro (uncountable)

  1. a team sport with elements of cricket, played mainly by women in Australia

Anagrams[edit]

  • Rovigo

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From vigorem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯iˈɡoː.roː/, [u̯ɪˈɡoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /viˈɡo.ro/, [viˈɡɔːro]

Verb[edit]

vigōrō (present infinitive vigōrāre, perfect active vigōrāvī, supine vigōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to animate, invigorate
