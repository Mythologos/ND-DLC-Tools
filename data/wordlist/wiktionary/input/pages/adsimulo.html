Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assimulō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsi.mu.loː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɪmʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈsi.mu.lo/, [äd̪ˈsiːmulo]

Verb[edit]

adsimulō (present infinitive adsimulāre, perfect active adsimulāvī, supine adsimulātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assimulō
