Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [ɪnˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [inˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

injiciō (present infinitive injicere, perfect active injēcī, supine injectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of iniciō
