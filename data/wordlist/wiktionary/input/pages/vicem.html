Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯i.kem/, [ˈu̯ɪkɛ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvi.t͡ʃem/, [ˈviːt͡ʃem]

Noun[edit]

vicem f

  1. accusative singular of vicis

Adverb[edit]

vicem (not comparable)

  1. in the place of
    in vicem ― mutually, reciprocally
  2. due to, because of, on behalf of
  3. in the manner of
