Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈplek.soː/, [kɔmˈpɫ̪ɛks̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈplek.so/, [konˈplɛkso]

Verb[edit]

conplexō (present infinitive conplexāre, perfect active conplexāvī, supine conplexātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of complexō
