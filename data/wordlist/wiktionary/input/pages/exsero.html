Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ serere (“to string together”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.se.roː/, [ˈɛks̠ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.se.ro/, [ˈɛksero]

Verb[edit]

exserō (present infinitive exserere, perfect active exseruī, supine exsertum); third conjugation

  1. to stretch or thrust forth or out
  2. to bare or uncover
