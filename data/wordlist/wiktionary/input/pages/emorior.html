Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ morior.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈmo.ri.or/, [eːˈmɔriɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈmo.ri.or/, [eˈmɔːrior]

Verb[edit]

ēmorior (present infinitive ēmorī, perfect active ēmortuus sum); third conjugation iō-variant, deponent

  1. to die or die off
  2. to die out
