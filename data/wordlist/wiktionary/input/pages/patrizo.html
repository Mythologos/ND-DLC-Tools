Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /paˈtriz.zoː/, [päˈt̪rɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /paˈtrid.d͡zo/, [päˈt̪rid̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

patrizō (present infinitive patrizāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of patrissō
