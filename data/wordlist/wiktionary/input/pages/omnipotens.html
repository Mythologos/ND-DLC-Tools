Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Compound of omni(s) (“all”) +‎ potēns (“powerful”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /omˈni.po.tens/, [ɔmˈnɪpɔt̪ẽːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /omˈni.po.tens/, [omˈniːpot̪ens]

Adjective[edit]

omnipotēns (genitive omnipotentis); third-declension one-termination adjective

  1. omnipotent, all-powerful, almighty
