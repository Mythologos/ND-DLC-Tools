English Wikipedia has an article on:
Saskatchewan
Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Saskatchouan (the river)

Etymology[edit]

From Cree ᑭᓯᐢᑳᒋᐘᓂ ᓰᐱᕀ (kisiskaaciwani siipiy, “swift-flowing river”)/kisiskāciwani-sīpiy.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /səˈskæt͡ʃ.əˌwɑːn/, /səˈskæt͡ʃ.ə.wən/
  • Audio (CAN)(file)
  • Audio (CAN)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Saskatchewan

  1. A river in Canada.
  2. Prairie province in western Canada (named after the river, which flows through it) which has Regina as its capital city.

Synonyms[edit]

  • Sask, Sask.
  • SSK
