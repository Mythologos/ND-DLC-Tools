Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /oˈbar.moː/, [ɔˈbärmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈbar.mo/, [oˈbärmo]

Verb[edit]

obarmō (present infinitive obarmāre, perfect active obarmāvī, supine obarmātum); first conjugation

  1. to arm
