Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • accanō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ canō (“sing”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈak.ki.noː/, [ˈäkːɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈat.t͡ʃi.no/, [ˈätː͡ʃino]

Verb[edit]

accinō (present infinitive accinere, perfect active accinuī, supine accentum); third conjugation, no passive

  1. to sing to
