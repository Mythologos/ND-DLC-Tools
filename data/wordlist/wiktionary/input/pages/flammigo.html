Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

flamma (“flame”) +‎ -igō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈflam.mi.ɡoː/, [ˈfɫ̪ämːɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈflam.mi.ɡo/, [ˈflämːiɡo]

Verb[edit]

flammigō (present infinitive flammigāre, perfect active flammigāvī, supine flammigātum); first conjugation

  1. to emit flame
