Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From pūrus +‎ -ficō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /puːˈri.fi.koː/, [puːˈrɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /puˈri.fi.ko/, [puˈriːfiko]

Verb[edit]

pūrificō (present infinitive pūrificāre, perfect active pūrificāvī, supine pūrificātum); first conjugation

  1. to purify (ritually)
  2. to clean or cleanse
