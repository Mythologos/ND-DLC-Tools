Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈspi.ki.oː/, [ˈs̠pɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈspi.t͡ʃi.o/, [ˈspiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

spiciō (present infinitive spicere, perfect active spexī, supine spectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of speciō
