Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ ruō (“to rush; throw down”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.ru.oː/, [ˈd̪eːruoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.ru.o/, [ˈd̪ɛːruo]

Verb[edit]

dēruō (present infinitive dēruere, perfect active dēruī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (transitive) to throw down
  2. (transitive) to take away, detract
  3. (intransitive) to fall down
