Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eu̯ˈroː.us/, [ɛu̯ˈroːʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eu̯ˈro.us/, [eu̯ˈrɔːus]

Adjective[edit]

eurōus (feminine eurōa, neuter eurōum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. eastern, oriental
