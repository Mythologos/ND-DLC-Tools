Ido[edit]

Noun[edit]

sepulto (plural sepulti)

  1. burial (act of burying)

Derived terms[edit]

  • sepulteyo
  • sepultar
  • sepultisto

Latin[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From sepultus +‎ -ō, thus a frequentative of sepeliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /seˈpul.toː/, [s̠ɛˈpʊɫ̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /seˈpul.to/, [seˈpul̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

sepultō (present infinitive sepultāre, perfect active sepultāvī, supine sepultātum); first conjugation

  1. to hold buried
