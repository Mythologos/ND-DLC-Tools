Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.spu.oː/, [ˈɛks̠puoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.spu.o/, [ˈɛkspuo]

Verb[edit]

expuō (present infinitive expuere, perfect active expuī, supine expūtum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exspuō
