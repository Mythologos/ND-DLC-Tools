Galician[edit]

Verb[edit]

retendo

  1. gerund of reter

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- (“back; again”) +‎ tendō (“stretch, strive for”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈten.doː/, [rɛˈt̪ɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈten.do/, [reˈt̪ɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

retendō (present infinitive retendere, perfect active retendī, supine retēnsum); third conjugation

  1. to unbend, slacken, relax
