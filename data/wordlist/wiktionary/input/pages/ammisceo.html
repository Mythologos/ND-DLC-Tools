Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • admisceō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /amˈmis.ke.oː/, [ämˈmɪs̠keoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /amˈmiʃ.ʃe.o/, [ämˈmiʃːeo]

Verb[edit]

ammisceō (present infinitive ammiscēre, perfect active ammiscuī, supine ammixtum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of admisceō

Conjugation[edit]

  • The fourth principal part may be ammixtum or ammistum.
