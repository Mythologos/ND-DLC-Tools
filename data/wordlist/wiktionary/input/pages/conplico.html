Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pli.koː/, [ˈkɔmplʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pli.ko/, [ˈkɔnpliko]

Verb[edit]

conplicō (present infinitive conplicāre, perfect active conplicāvī, supine conplicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of complicō
