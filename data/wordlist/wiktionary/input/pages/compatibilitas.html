Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From compatibilis +‎ -tās.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kom.pa.tiˈbi.li.taːs/, [kɔmpät̪ɪˈbɪlʲɪt̪äːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kom.pa.tiˈbi.li.tas/, [kompät̪iˈbiːlit̪äs]

Noun[edit]

compatibilitās f (genitive compatibilitātis); third declension

  1. (New Latin) compatibility
