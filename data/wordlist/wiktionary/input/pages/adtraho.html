Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • attrahō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.tra.hoː/, [ˈät̪ːrä(ɦ)oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.tra.o/, [ˈäd̪t̪räo]

Verb[edit]

adtrahō (present infinitive adtrahere, perfect active adtrāxī, supine adtractum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attrahō
