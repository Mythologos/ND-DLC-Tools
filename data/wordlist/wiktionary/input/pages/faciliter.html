French[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Italian facilitare.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /fa.si.li.te/
  • Audio(file)

Verb[edit]

faciliter

  1. (transitive) to facilitate

Latin[edit]

Adverb[edit]

faciliter (comparative facilius, superlative facilissimē)

  1. easily, without trouble or difficulty
    Synonym: facile
    Antonyms: difficilē, difficiliter, difficulter

References[edit]

  • “faciliter”, in Charlton T. Lewis and Charles Short (1879) A Latin Dictionary, Oxford: Clarendon Press
