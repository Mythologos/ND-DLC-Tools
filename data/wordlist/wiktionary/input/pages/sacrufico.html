Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /saˈkru.fi.koː/, [s̠äˈkrʊfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /saˈkru.fi.ko/, [säˈkruːfiko]

Verb[edit]

sacruficō (present infinitive sacruficāre, perfect active sacruficāvī, supine sacruficātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of sacrificō
