Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ sentio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈsen.ti.oː/, [pɛrˈs̠ɛn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈsen.t͡si.o/, [perˈsɛnt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

persentiō (present infinitive persentīre, perfect active persēnsī, supine persēnsum); fourth conjugation

  1. to perceive plainly
  2. to feel deeply
