Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ doceō (“teach”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈdo.ke.oː/, [s̠ʊbˈd̪ɔkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈdo.t͡ʃe.o/, [subˈd̪ɔːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

subdoceō (present infinitive subdocēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (rare) to teach additionally or as an assistant; act as an assistant teacher
