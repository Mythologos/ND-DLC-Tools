Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • heiulō, hejulō, heiulor, hejulor
  • ejulō

Etymology[edit]

From ei (“oh!”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈei̯.i̯u.loː/, [ˈɛi̯ːʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.ju.lo/, [ˈɛːjulo]

Verb[edit]

eiulō (present infinitive eiulāre, perfect active eiulāvī, supine eiulātum); first conjugation

  1. to wail, lament
