Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈu̯er.soː/, [s̠ʊbˈu̯ɛrs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈver.so/, [subˈvɛrso]

Verb[edit]

subversō (present infinitive subversāre, perfect active subversāvī, supine subversātum); first conjugation

  1. to overturn, overthrow, subvert
