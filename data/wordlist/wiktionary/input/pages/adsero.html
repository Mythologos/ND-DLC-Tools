Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.se.roː/, [ˈäs̠ːɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.se.ro/, [ˈäd̪sero]

Verb[edit]

adserō (present infinitive adserere, perfect active adsēvī, supine adsitum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of asserō
