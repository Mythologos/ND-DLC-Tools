Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dīmensus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈmen.si.oː/, [d̪iːˈmẽːs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈmen.si.o/, [d̪iˈmɛnsio]

Noun[edit]

dīmēnsiō f (genitive dīmēnsiōnis); third declension

  1. measuring (out)
  2. measurement
  3. dimension

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
