Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /zimˈba.bu.a/, [d̪͡z̪ɪmˈbäbuä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /d͡zimˈba.bu.a/, [d̪͡z̪imˈbäːbuä]

Proper noun[edit]

Zimbabua f sg (genitive Zimbabuae); first declension

  1. Zimbabwe (a country in Southern Africa)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.
