Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Provençal escac, from Persian شاه (šâh, “shah”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈskak.ki/
  • Rhymes: -akki
  • Hyphenation: scàc‧chi
  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

scacchi m

  1. plural of scacco
  2. (board games) chess

Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

scacchī m pl (genitive scacchōrum); second declension

  1. Alternative spelling of scaccī (“chess”)

Declension[edit]
