Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ audiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈbau̯.di.oː/, [s̠ʊˈbäu̯d̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈbau̯.di.o/, [suˈbäːu̯d̪io]

Verb[edit]

subaudiō (present infinitive subaudīre, perfect active subaudiī, supine subaudītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to understand
  2. to hear a little
