Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

expurgo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of expurgar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ pūrgō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈspuːr.ɡoː/, [ɛkˈs̠puːrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈspur.ɡo/, [ekˈspurɡo]

Verb[edit]

expūrgō (present infinitive expūrgāre, perfect active expūrgāvī, supine expūrgātum); first conjugation

  1. to purge, cleanse, purify; expurgate
  2. to exculpate, vindicate, justify, excuse
