Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prō- +‎ mulgeō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈmul.ɡoː/, [proːˈmʊɫ̪ɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈmul.ɡo/, [proˈmulɡo]

Verb[edit]

prōmulgō (present infinitive prōmulgāre, perfect active prōmulgāvī, supine prōmulgātum); first conjugation

  1. to publish, promulgate
