Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • apportō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈpor.toː/, [äpˈpɔrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈpor.to/, [äd̪ˈpɔrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

adportō (present infinitive adportāre, perfect active adportāvī, supine adportātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of apportō
