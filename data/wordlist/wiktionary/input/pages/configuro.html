Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

cōn- (“together”) +‎ figūrō (“figure”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.fiˈɡuː.roː/, [kõːfɪˈɡuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.fiˈɡu.ro/, [koɱfiˈɡuːro]

Verb[edit]

cōnfigūrō (present infinitive cōnfigūrāre, perfect active cōnfigūrāvī, supine cōnfigūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to mould, shape or fashion accordingly
