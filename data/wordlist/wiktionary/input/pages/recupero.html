Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /reˈku.pe.ro/
  • Rhymes: -upero
  • Hyphenation: re‧cù‧pe‧ro

Etymology 1[edit]

Deverbal from recuperare +‎ -o.

Noun[edit]

recupero m (plural recuperi)

  1. recovery
  2. salvage, rescue
  3. recycling, reclamation, reclaim
  4. making up, remedial
  5. rehabilitation (criminals etc.)
  6. (sports) replay
    minuti di recupero ― extra time
  7. retrieval
Related terms[edit]
  • recuperare

Etymology 2[edit]

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈku.pe.roː/, [rɛˈkʊpɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈku.pe.ro/, [reˈkuːpero]

Verb[edit]

recuperō (present infinitive recuperāre, perfect active recuperāvī, supine recuperātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of reciperō (“regain, restore, restore to health”)
