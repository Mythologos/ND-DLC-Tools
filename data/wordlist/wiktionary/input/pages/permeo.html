Anagrams[edit]

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

per- +‎ meō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈper.me.oː/, [ˈpɛrmeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈper.me.o/, [ˈpɛrmeo]

Verb[edit]

permeō (present infinitive permeāre, perfect active permeāvī, supine permeātum); first conjugation

  1. to pass through, traverse
  2. to penetrate, pervade
