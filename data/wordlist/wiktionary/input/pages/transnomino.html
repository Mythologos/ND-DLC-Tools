Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

trāns- +‎ nōminō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈnoː.mi.noː/, [t̪rä̃ːs̠ˈnoːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈno.mi.no/, [t̪ränzˈnɔːmino]

Verb[edit]

trānsnōminō (present infinitive trānsnōmināre, perfect active trānsnōmināvī, supine trānsnōminātum); first conjugation

  1. to rename
