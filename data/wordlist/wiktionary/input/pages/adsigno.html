Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assignō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsiɡ.noː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɪŋnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈsiɲ.ɲo/, [äd̪ˈsiɲːo]

Verb[edit]

adsignō (present infinitive adsignāre, perfect active adsignāvī, supine adsignātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assignō
