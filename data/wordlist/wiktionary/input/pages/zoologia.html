Etymology[edit]

Internationalism; compare English zoology, French zoologie, German Zoologie, ultimately from Ancient Greek ζῷον (zôion) + λόγος (lógos).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /zɔ.ɔˈlɔ.ɡja/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɔɡja
  • Syllabification: zo‧o‧lo‧gia

Noun[edit]

zoologia f

  1. (zoology) zoology (science that studies the animal kingdom)
