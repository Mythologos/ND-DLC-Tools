Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards, at”) +‎ arō (“plough”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.da.roː/, [ˈäd̪äroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.da.ro/, [ˈäːd̪äro]

Verb[edit]

adarō (present infinitive adarāre, perfect active adarāvī, supine adarātum); first conjugation

  1. to plough carefully
