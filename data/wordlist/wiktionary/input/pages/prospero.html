Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [prosˈpero]
  • Rhymes: -ero
  • Hyphenation: pros‧pe‧ro

Noun[edit]

prospero (uncountable, accusative prosperon)

  1. prosperity, success

Galician[edit]

Verb[edit]

prospero

Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈprɔ.spe.ro/
  • Rhymes: -ɔspero
  • Hyphenation: prò‧spe‧ro

Etymology 1[edit]

From Latin prosperus.

Adjective[edit]

prospero (feminine prospera, masculine plural prosperi, feminine plural prospere)

  1. flourishing, thriving
  2. prosperous, affluent
Derived terms[edit]
  • prosperamente
Related terms[edit]
  • prosperare
  • prosperità

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpros.pe.roː/, [ˈprɔs̠pɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpros.pe.ro/, [ˈprɔspero]

Etymology 1[edit]

From prosperus +‎ -ō.

Verb[edit]

prosperō (present infinitive prosperāre, perfect active prosperāvī, supine prosperātum); first conjugation

  1. to further (cause to succeed)
  2. to propitiate
