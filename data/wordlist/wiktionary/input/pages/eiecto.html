Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈi̯ek.toː/, [eːˈi̯ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈjek.to/, [eˈjɛkt̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

Participle[edit]

ēiectō

  1. dative/ablative masculine/neuter singular of ēiectus

Etymology 2[edit]

From ēiectus.

Verb[edit]

ēiectō (present infinitive ēiectāre, perfect active ēiectāvī, supine ēiectātum); first conjugation

  1. to cast out, throw out
