Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈpul.so/, [kɔmˈpʊɫ̪s̠ɔ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈpul.so/, [konˈpulso]

Verb[edit]

conpulsō (present infinitive conpulsāre, perfect active conpulsāvī, supine conpulsātum); first conjugation

  1. to batter, pound
  2. to be at war with
