Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

vacillo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of vacillare

Anagrams[edit]

  • Villaco, cavillo, cavillò

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unknown.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯aˈkil.loː/, [u̯äˈkɪlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /vaˈt͡ʃil.lo/, [väˈt͡ʃilːo]

Verb[edit]

vacillō (present infinitive vacillāre, perfect active vacillāvī, supine vacillātum); first conjugation

  1. to sway to and fro
  2. to stagger, reel, totter
  3. to waver, hesitate
  4. to vacillate
