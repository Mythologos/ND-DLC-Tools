Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From cēnō +‎ -itō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkeː.ni.toː/, [ˈkeːnɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈt͡ʃe.ni.to/, [ˈt͡ʃɛːnit̪o]

Verb[edit]

cēnitō (present infinitive cēnitāre, perfect active cēnitāvī, supine cēnitātum); first conjugation

  1. to dine often
