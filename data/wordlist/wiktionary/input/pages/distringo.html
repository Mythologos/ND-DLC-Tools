Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /diˈstrin.ɡo/
  • Rhymes: -inɡo
  • Hyphenation: di‧strìn‧go

Verb[edit]

distringo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of distringere

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ stringō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈtrin.ɡoː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈt̪rɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈtrin.ɡo/, [d̪isˈt̪riŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

distringō (present infinitive distringere, perfect active distrīnxī, supine districtum); third conjugation

  1. to stretch out or apart
  2. to detain
  3. to distract
