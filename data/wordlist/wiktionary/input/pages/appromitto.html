Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adpromitto

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ prōmittō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ap.proːˈmit.toː/, [äpːroːˈmɪt̪ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ap.proˈmit.to/, [äpːroˈmit̪ːo]

Verb[edit]

apprōmittō (present infinitive apprōmittere, perfect active apprōmīsī, supine apprōmissum); third conjugation

  1. to promise in addition, or to another
