Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • collūceō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈluː.ke.oː/, [kɔnˈɫ̪uːkeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈlu.t͡ʃe.o/, [konˈluːt͡ʃeo]

Verb[edit]

conlūceō (present infinitive conlūcēre, perfect active conlūxī); second conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of collūceō
