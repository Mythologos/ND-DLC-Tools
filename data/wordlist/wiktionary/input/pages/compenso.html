Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /komˈpɛn.so/
  • Rhymes: -ɛnso
  • Hyphenation: com‧pèn‧so

Etymology 1[edit]

From compensare.

Noun[edit]

compenso m (plural compensi)

  1. remuneration
  2. fee
    Synonym: corrispettivo
  3. reward

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ pēnsō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈpen.soː/, [kɔmˈpẽːs̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈpen.so/, [komˈpɛnso]

Verb[edit]

compēnsō (present infinitive compēnsāre, perfect active compēnsāvī, supine compēnsātum); first conjugation

  1. to balance, poise, weigh, offset (one thing against another)
  2. to compensate, make up for
