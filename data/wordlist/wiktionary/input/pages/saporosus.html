Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sapōr- (“taste”) +‎ -ōsus (“full of”, adjective).

Adjective[edit]

sapōrōsus (feminine sapōrōsa, neuter sapōrōsum); first/second-declension adjective (Late Latin)

  1. of good flavor
