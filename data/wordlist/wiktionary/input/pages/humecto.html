Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /huˈmek.toː/, [hʊˈmɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /uˈmek.to/, [uˈmɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

humectō (present infinitive humectāre, perfect active humectāvī, supine humectātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of ūmectō
