Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ sōpiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈsoː.pi.oː/, [kõːˈs̠oːpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈso.pi.o/, [konˈsɔːpio]

Verb[edit]

consōpiō (present infinitive consōpīre, perfect active consōpīvī, supine consōpītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to lull to sleep; to stupefy
  2. to make obsolete
