Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ad- (“to”) +‎ iugō (“bind, join”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.i̯u.ɡoː/, [ˈäd̪i̯ʊɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.ju.ɡo/, [ˈäd̪juɡo]

Verb[edit]

adjugō (present infinitive adjugāre, perfect active adjugāvī, supine adjugātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of adiugō
