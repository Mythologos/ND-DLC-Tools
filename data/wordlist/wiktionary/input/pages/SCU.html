English Wikipedia has an article on:
SCU
Wikipedia

Noun[edit]

SCU (plural SCU's or SCUs)

  1. Initialism of solitary confinement unit.

Synonyms[edit]

  • (solitary confinement unit): administrative segregation, ad-seg, block (UK), box, cooler (UK), hotbox, hole, lockdown, pound, security housing unit, SHU, solitary confinement, special handling unit

Proper noun[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Sacra Corona Unita

SCU

  1. (crime) Abbreviation of Sacra Corona Unita (“United Sacred Crown”), the Puglia mafia.
    Coordinate terms: Mafia, mafia, Italian mafia, Italian mob, Cosa Nostra, Camorra, 'Ndrangheta
