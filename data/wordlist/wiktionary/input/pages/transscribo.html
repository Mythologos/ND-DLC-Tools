Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈskriː.boː/, [t̪rä̃ːs̠(ː)kriːboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈskri.bo/, [t̪ränsˈskriːbo]

Verb[edit]

trānsscrībō (present infinitive trānsscrībere, perfect active trānsscrīpsī, supine trānsscrīptum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of trānscrībō
