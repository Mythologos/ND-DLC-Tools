Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ stimulō (“to urge on”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈsti.mu.loː/, [ĩːˈs̠t̪ɪmʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈsti.mu.lo/, [inˈst̪iːmulo]

Verb[edit]

īnstimulō (present infinitive īnstimulāre, perfect active īnstimulāvī, supine īnstimulātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to urge on, stimulate
