Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /taˈri.o.na/, [t̪äˈriɔnä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /taˈri.o.na/, [t̪äˈriːonä]

Proper noun[edit]

Tariona f sg (genitive Tarionae); first declension

  1. A town in Dalmatia situated near the course of the Tityus

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, with locative, singular only.
