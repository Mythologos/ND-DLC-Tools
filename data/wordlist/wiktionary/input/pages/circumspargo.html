Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ spargo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kumˈspar.ɡoː/, [kɪrkũːˈs̠pärɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kumˈspar.ɡo/, [t͡ʃirkumˈspärɡo]

Verb[edit]

circumspargō (present infinitive circumspargere, perfect active circumsparsī, supine circumsparsum); third conjugation

  1. to scatter, strew, sprinkle around
