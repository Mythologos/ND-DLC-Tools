Latin[edit]

Latin Wikipedia has an article on:
Olaus
Wikipedia la

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Old Norse Óláfr or Old Danish Olaf

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /oːˈlaː.us/, [oːˈɫ̪äːʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈla.us/, [oˈläːus]

Proper noun[edit]

Ōlāus m sg (genitive Ōlāī); second declension

  1. a male given name, equivalent to English Olaf

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun, singular only.

Case Singular
Nominative Ōlāus

Dative Ōlāō

Vocative Ōlāe
