Latin[edit]

Adjective[edit]

maura

  1. inflection of maurus:
    1. nominative/vocative feminine singular
    2. nominative/accusative/vocative neuter plural

Adjective[edit]

maurā

  1. ablative feminine singular of maurus

Noun[edit]

maura f (genitive maurae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) black nightshade (Solanum nigrum)
    Synonyms: sōlānum, ūva lupīna, ūva canīna, sōlāta, sōlātrum, strychnos, strignus, strignum, mīlleborbia
