Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ gelō (“freeze”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ɡe.loː/, [ˈrɛɡɛɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.d͡ʒe.lo/, [ˈrɛːd͡ʒelo]

Verb[edit]

regelō (present infinitive regelāre, perfect active regelāvī, supine regelātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to unfreeze, thaw, warm
