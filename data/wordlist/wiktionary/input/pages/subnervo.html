Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Ultimately from sub- +‎ nervus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈner.u̯oː/, [s̠ʊbˈnɛru̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈner.vo/, [subˈnɛrvo]

Verb[edit]

subnervō (present infinitive subnervāre, perfect active subnervāvī, supine subnervātum); first conjugation

  1. to hamstring
  2. to invalidate or refute
