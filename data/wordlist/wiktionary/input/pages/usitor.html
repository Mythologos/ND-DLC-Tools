Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Frequentative verb of Latin ūtor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈuː.si.tor/, [ˈuːs̠ɪt̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈu.si.tor/, [ˈuːs̬it̪or]

Verb[edit]

ūsitor (present infinitive ūsitārī, perfect active ūsitātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to use habitually
