Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adnumerō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /anˈnu.me.roː/, [änˈnʊmɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /anˈnu.me.ro/, [änˈnuːmero]

Verb[edit]

annumerō (present infinitive annumerāre, perfect active annumerāvī, supine annumerātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of adnumerō
