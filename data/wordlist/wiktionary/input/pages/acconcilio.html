Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards”) +‎ conciliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ak.konˈki.li.o/, [äkːɔŋˈkɪlʲiɔ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ak.konˈt͡ʃi.li.o/, [äkːon̠ʲˈt͡ʃiːlio]

Verb[edit]

acconciliō (present infinitive acconciliāre, perfect active acconciliāvī, supine acconciliātum); first conjugation

  1. to advise, suggest, recommend, counsel
