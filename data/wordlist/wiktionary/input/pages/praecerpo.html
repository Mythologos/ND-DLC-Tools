Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • praecarpo

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ carpo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈker.poː/, [präe̯ˈkɛrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈt͡ʃer.po/, [preˈt͡ʃɛrpo]

Verb[edit]

praecerpō (present infinitive praecerpere, perfect active praecerpsī, supine praecerptum); third conjugation

  1. to pluck, pick, harvest beforehand, or before the right time
  2. to lessen or diminish
