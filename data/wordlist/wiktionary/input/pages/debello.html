Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /deˈbɛl.lo/
  • Rhymes: -ɛllo
  • Hyphenation: de‧bèl‧lo

Verb[edit]

debello

  1. first-person singular present indicative of debellare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ bellō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈbel.loː/, [d̪eːˈbɛlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈbel.lo/, [d̪eˈbɛlːo]

Verb[edit]

dēbellō (present infinitive dēbellāre, perfect active dēbellāvī, supine dēbellātum); first conjugation

  1. to conquer, subdue
