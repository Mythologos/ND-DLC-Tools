Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

excorio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of excoriar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ corium

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsko.ri.oː/, [ɛkˈs̠kɔrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsko.ri.o/, [ekˈskɔːrio]

Verb[edit]

excoriō (present infinitive excoriāre, perfect active excoriāvī, supine excoriātum); first conjugation

  1. to skin (strip the skin from)
