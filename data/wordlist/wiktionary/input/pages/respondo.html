Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /resˈpondo/
  • Hyphenation: res‧pon‧do
  • Rhymes: -ondo

Noun[edit]

respondo (accusative singular respondon, plural respondoj, accusative plural respondojn)

  1. answer, reply, response

Derived terms[edit]

  • respondado
  • respondi
 

Galician[edit]

Verb[edit]

respondo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of responder

Ido[edit]

Noun[edit]

respondo (plural respondi)

  1. answer
    Antonym: questiono

Derived terms[edit]

  • respondar
  • respondizar
 

Italian[edit]

respondo

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Classical respondeō, with a change in verb class. Attested in the Mulomedicina Chironis.

Verb[edit]

respondō (present infinitive respondere, perfect active respondī or *respōnsī, supine respōnsum); third conjugation (Late Latin)

  1. Alternative form of respondeō (“reply”)
