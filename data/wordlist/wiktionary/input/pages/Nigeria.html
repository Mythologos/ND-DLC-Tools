English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Niger +‎ -ia, coined by British journalist Flora Shaw in 1897.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /naɪˈd͡ʒɪə.ɹɪə/
  • (General American) IPA(key): /naɪˈd͡ʒɪɹ.i.ə/
  • (Scotland) IPA(key): /naɪˈd͡ʒiː.ɹi.ə/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪəɹiə
  • Hyphenation: Ni‧ge‧ria
  • Rhymes: -ɪɹiə

Proper noun[edit]

Nigeria

  2. A country in West Africa, south of the country of Niger. Official name: Federal Republic of Nigeria. Capital: Abuja. Largest city: Lagos.
