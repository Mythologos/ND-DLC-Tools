English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ repo

Noun[edit]

subrepo (plural subrepos)

  1. (computing) Short for subrepository.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ rēpō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈreː.poː/, [s̠ʊbˈreːpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈre.po/, [subˈrɛːpo]

Verb[edit]

subrēpō (present infinitive subrēpere, perfect active subrēpsī, supine subrēptum); third conjugation, no passive

  1. Alternative form of surrēpō
