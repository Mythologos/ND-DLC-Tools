Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From nōminō +‎ -itō (frequentative suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /noːˈmi.ni.toː/, [noːˈmɪnɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /noˈmi.ni.to/, [noˈmiːnit̪o]

Verb[edit]

nōminitō (present infinitive nōminitāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (transitive) I name, term
