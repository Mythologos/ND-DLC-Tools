Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From squāma +‎ -ōsus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /skʷaːˈmoː.sus/, [s̠kʷäːˈmoːs̠ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /skwaˈmo.sus/, [skwäˈmɔːs̬us]

Adjective[edit]

squāmōsus (feminine squāmōsa, neuter squāmōsum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. scaly
