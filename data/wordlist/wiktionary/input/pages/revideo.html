Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ videō (“see, observe”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈu̯i.de.oː/, [reˈu̯ɪd̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈvi.de.o/, [reˈviːd̪eo]

Verb[edit]

revideō (present infinitive revidēre, perfect active revīdī, supine revīsum); second conjugation

  1. (transitive) to revisit somewhere
  2. (intransitive) to go to see again, pay another visit
