Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈfa.bu.lo/
  • Rhymes: -abulo
  • Hyphenation: fà‧bu‧lo

Verb[edit]

fabulo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of fabulare

Anagrams[edit]

  • bufalo

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfaː.bu.loː/, [ˈfäːbʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfa.bu.lo/, [ˈfäːbulo]

Verb[edit]

fābulō (present infinitive fābulāre, perfect active fābulāvī, supine fābulātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of fābulor
