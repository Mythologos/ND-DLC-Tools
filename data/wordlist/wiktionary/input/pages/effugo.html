Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈef.fu.ɡoː/, [ˈɛfːʊɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈef.fu.ɡo/, [ˈɛfːuɡo]

Verb[edit]

effugō (present infinitive effugāre, perfect active effugāvī, supine effugātum); first conjugation

  1. to put to flight
  2. to deter
