Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ rēte +‎ -iō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /irˈreː.ti.oː/, [ɪrˈreːt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /irˈret.t͡si.o/, [irˈrɛt̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

irrētiō (present infinitive irrētīre, perfect active irrētīvī or irrētiī, supine irrētītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to entangle, ensnare
