Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [s̠ʊbˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [subˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

subjiciō (present infinitive subjicere, perfect active subjēcī, supine subjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of subiciō
