Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /abˈne.ɡo/, /abˈnɛ.ɡo/, /ˈab.ne.ɡo/
  • Rhymes: -eɡo, -ɛɡo, -abneɡo
  • Hyphenation: ab‧né‧go, ab‧nè‧go, àb‧ne‧go

Verb[edit]

abnego

  1. first-person singular present indicative of abnegare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ab- (“from, away from”) +‎ negō (“I deny, refuse, say no”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈab.ne.ɡoː/, [ˈäbnɛɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈab.ne.ɡo/, [ˈäbneɡo]

Verb[edit]

abnegō (present infinitive abnegāre, perfect active abnegāvī, supine abnegātum); first conjugation

  1. to refuse, deny, decline, be unwilling
