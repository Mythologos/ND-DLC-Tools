Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯eːn.toː/, [ˈi̯eːn̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈjen.to/, [ˈjɛn̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

jēntō (present infinitive jēntāre, perfect active jēntāvī, supine jēntātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of iēntō
