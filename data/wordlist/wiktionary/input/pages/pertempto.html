Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ tempto.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈtemp.toː/, [pɛrˈt̪ɛmpt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈtemp.to/, [perˈt̪ɛmpt̪o]

Verb[edit]

pertemptō (present infinitive pertemptāre, perfect active pertemptāvī, supine pertemptātum); first conjugation

  1. to test, try, attempt thoroughly
