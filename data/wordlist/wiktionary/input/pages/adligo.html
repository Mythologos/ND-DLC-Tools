Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • alligō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.li.ɡoː/, [ˈälːʲɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.li.ɡo/, [ˈäd̪liɡo]

Verb[edit]

adligō (present infinitive adligāre, perfect active adligāvī, supine adligātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of alligō
