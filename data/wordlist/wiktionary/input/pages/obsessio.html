Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English obsession), ultimately from Latin obsessiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈobsesːio/, [ˈo̞bs̠e̞s̠ˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): ob‧ses‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

obsessio

  1. (psychology) obsession

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

obsideo +‎ -tio

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈses.si.oː/, [ɔpˈs̠ɛs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈses.si.o/, [obˈsɛsːio]

Noun[edit]

obsessiō f (genitive obsessiōnis); third declension

  1. blockade, siege
  2. obsession

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
