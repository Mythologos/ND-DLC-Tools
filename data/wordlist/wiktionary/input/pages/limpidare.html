Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

limpidus (“clear”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /limˈpi.da.re/, [lʲɪmˈpɪd̪ärɛ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /limˈpi.da.re/, [limˈpiːd̪äre]

Verb[edit]

limpidō (present infinitive limpidāre, perfect active limpidāvī, supine limpidātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to cleanse
