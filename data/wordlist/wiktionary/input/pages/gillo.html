  • Rhymes: -illo
  • Hyphenation: Gìl‧lo

Proper noun[edit]

  1. a male given name

Anagrams[edit]

  • logli

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Maybe from gillō (“cooler”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡil.loː/, [ˈɡɪlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈd͡ʒil.lo/, [ˈd͡ʒilːo]

Proper noun[edit]

Gillō m sg (genitive Gillōnis); third declension

  1. A Roman cognomen — famously held by:
    1. Quintus Fulvius Gillo, a Roman praetor

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun, singular only.

Case Singular
Nominative Gillō
Genitive Gillōnis
