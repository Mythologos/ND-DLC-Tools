Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ stagno.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /resˈtaɡ.no/, [rɛs̠ˈt̪äŋnɔ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /resˈtaɲ.ɲo/, [resˈt̪äɲːo]

Verb[edit]

restagnō (present infinitive restagnāre, perfect active restagnāvī, supine restagnātum); first conjugation

  1. to overflow, run over
