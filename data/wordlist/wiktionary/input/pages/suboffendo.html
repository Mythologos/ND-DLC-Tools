Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ offendō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.bofˈfen.doː/, [s̠ʊbɔfˈfɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.bofˈfen.do/, [subofˈfɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

suboffendō (present infinitive suboffendere, perfect active suboffendī, supine suboffēnsum); third conjugation

  1. to give some offence
