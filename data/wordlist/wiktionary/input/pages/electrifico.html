Galician[edit]

Verb[edit]

electrifico

  1. first-person singular present indicative of electrificar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ēlectrum +‎ -ficō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eː.lekˈtri.fi.koː/, [eːɫ̪ɛkˈt̪rɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /e.lekˈtri.fi.ko/, [elekˈt̪riːfiko]

Verb[edit]

ēlectrificō (present infinitive ēlectrificāre, perfect active ēlectrificāvī, supine ēlectrificātum); first conjugation

  1. (New Latin) to electrify
