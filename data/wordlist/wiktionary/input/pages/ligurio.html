Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /liˈɡuː.ri.oː/, [lʲɪˈɡuːrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /liˈɡu.ri.o/, [liˈɡuːrio]

Verb[edit]

ligūriō (present infinitive ligūrīre, perfect active ligūrīvī or ligūriī, supine ligūrītum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of ligurriō
