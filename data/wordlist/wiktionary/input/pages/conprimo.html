Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pri.moː/, [ˈkɔmprɪmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pri.mo/, [ˈkɔnprimo]

Verb[edit]

conprimō (present infinitive conprimere, perfect active conpressī, supine conpressum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of comprimō
