Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English dispersion), ultimately from Latin dispertiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈdispersio/, [ˈdis̠pe̞rˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): dis‧per‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

dispersio

  1. (optics) dispersion

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈper.si.oː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈpɛrs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈper.si.o/, [d̪isˈpɛrsio]

Noun[edit]

dispersiō f (genitive dispersiōnis); third declension

  1. scattering, dispersion
  2. destruction

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
