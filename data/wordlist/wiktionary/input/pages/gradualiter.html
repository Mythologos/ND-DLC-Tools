Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • gradāliter

Etymology[edit]

Adverb of graduālis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡra.duˈaː.li.ter/, [ɡräd̪uˈäːlʲɪt̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡra.duˈa.li.ter/, [ɡräd̪uˈäːlit̪er]

Adverb[edit]

graduāliter (not comparable)

  1. (Medieval Latin) gradually
