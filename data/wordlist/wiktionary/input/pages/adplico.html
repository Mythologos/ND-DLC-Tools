Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pli.koː/, [ˈäpːlʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pli.ko/, [ˈäd̪pliko]

Verb[edit]

adplicō (present infinitive adplicāre, perfect active adplicāvī or adplicuī, supine adplicātum or adplicitum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of applicō
