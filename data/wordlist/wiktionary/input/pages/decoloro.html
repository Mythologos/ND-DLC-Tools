Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ colōrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.koˈloː.roː/, [d̪eːkɔˈɫ̪oːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.koˈlo.ro/, [d̪ekoˈlɔːro]

Verb[edit]

dēcolōrō (present infinitive dēcolōrāre, perfect active dēcolōrāvī, supine dēcolōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to discolour, stain or deface
  2. to disgrace
  3. to corrupt
