Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈpuːr.ɡoː/, [rɛˈpuːrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈpur.ɡo/, [reˈpurɡo]

Verb[edit]

repūrgō (present infinitive repūrgāre, perfect active repūrgāvī, supine repūrgātum); first conjugation

  1. to cleanse again
