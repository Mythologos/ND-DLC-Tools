Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • dēpacīscor

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ pacīscor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.peˈkiːs.kor/, [d̪eːpɛˈkiːs̠kɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.peˈt͡ʃis.kor/, [d̪epeˈt͡ʃiskor]

Verb[edit]

dēpecīscor (present infinitive dēpecīscī, perfect active dēpectus sum); third conjugation, deponent

  1. to bargain for; to come to terms
