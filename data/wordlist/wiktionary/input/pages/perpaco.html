Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ paco.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈpaː.koː/, [pɛrˈpäːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈpa.ko/, [perˈpäːko]

Verb[edit]

perpācō (present infinitive perpācāre, perfect active perpācāvī, supine perpācātum); first conjugation

  1. to quiet or subdue completely
