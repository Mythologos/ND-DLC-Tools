Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /reˈt͡ʃin.ɡo/
  • Rhymes: -inɡo
  • Hyphenation: re‧cìn‧go

Verb[edit]

recingo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of recingere

Anagrams[edit]

  • cingerò

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ cingō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈkin.ɡoː/, [rɛˈkɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈt͡ʃin.ɡo/, [reˈt͡ʃiŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

recingō (present infinitive recingere, perfect active recinxī, supine recinctum); third conjugation

  1. to ungird, loosen, undo
  2. to gird again, refasten
