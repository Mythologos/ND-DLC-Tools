Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ volvo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈu̯ol.u̯oː/, [pɛrˈu̯ɔɫ̪u̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈvol.vo/, [perˈvɔlvo]

Verb[edit]

pervolvō (present infinitive pervolvere, perfect active pervolvī, supine pervolūtum); third conjugation

  1. to roll or tumble about
  2. to be very busy
