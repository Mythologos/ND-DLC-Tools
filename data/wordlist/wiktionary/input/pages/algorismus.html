Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Arabic الخَوَارِزْمِيّ (al-ḵawārizmiyy), the nisba of Persian mathematician Muhammad ibn Musa al-Khwarizmi.

Noun[edit]

algorismus m (genitive algorismī); second declension

  1. algorithm

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun.
