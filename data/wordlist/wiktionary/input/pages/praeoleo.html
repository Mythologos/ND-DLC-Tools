Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ oleō (“smell”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈo.le.oː/, [präe̯ˈɔɫ̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈo.le.o/, [preˈɔːleo]

Verb[edit]

praeoleō (present infinitive praeolēre); second conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to emit or exhale an odor beforehand
