Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • comparcō

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ parcō (“to spare, refrain”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈper.koː/, [kɔmˈpɛrkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈper.ko/, [komˈpɛrko]

Verb[edit]

compercō (present infinitive compercere, perfect active compersī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (transitive) to save, hoard
  2. (transitive) to refrain, abstain (+ infinitive)
