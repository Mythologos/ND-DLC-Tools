Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ fricō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈper.fri.koː/, [ˈpɛrfrɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈper.fri.ko/, [ˈpɛrfriko]

Verb[edit]

perfricō (present infinitive perfricāre, perfect active perfricuī, supine perfricātum or perfrictum); first conjugation

  1. to rub all over
  2. to put on a bold face (rub away blushes)
