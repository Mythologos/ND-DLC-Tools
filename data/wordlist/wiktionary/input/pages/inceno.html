Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ cēnō (“I dine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈkeː.noː/, [ɪŋˈkeːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈt͡ʃe.no/, [in̠ʲˈt͡ʃɛːno]

Verb[edit]

incēnō (present infinitive incēnāre, perfect active incēnāvī, supine incēnātum); first conjugation

  1. to dine there, dine in or at a place
