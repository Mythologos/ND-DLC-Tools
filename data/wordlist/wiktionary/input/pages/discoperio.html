Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /dis.koˈpe.ri.oː/, [d̪ɪs̠kɔˈpɛrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /dis.koˈpe.ri.o/, [d̪iskoˈpɛːrio]

Verb[edit]

discoperiō (present infinitive discoperīre, perfect active discoperuī, supine discopertum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of discooperiō
