English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Euboea
Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Eubœa (dated)

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Εὔβοια (Eúboia).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes: -iːə

IPA(key): /juːˈbiːə/

Proper noun[edit]

Euboea

  1. An island in Greece, the second-largest, just off the eastern coast of Boeotia, in the Aegean Sea.

Synonyms[edit]

  • Negropont (former name)
  • Ocha (former name)

Derived terms[edit]

  • Euboean
