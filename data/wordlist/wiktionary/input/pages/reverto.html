Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈu̯er.toː/, [reˈu̯ɛrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈver.to/, [reˈvɛrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

revertō (present infinitive revertere, perfect active revertī, supine reversum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of revertor
