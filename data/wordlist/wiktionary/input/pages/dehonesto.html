Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ honestō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /de.hoˈnes.toː/, [d̪e(ɦ)ɔˈnɛs̠t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.oˈnes.to/, [d̪eoˈnɛst̪o]

Verb[edit]

dehonestō (present infinitive dehonestāre, perfect active dehonestāvī, supine dehonestātum); first conjugation

  1. to disgrace, dishonor, disparage
