Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From appetō (“strive, desire, long for”) +‎ -essō (intensive verb forming suffix) or from ad- +‎ petessō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.peˈtis.soː/, [äpːɛˈt̪ɪs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.peˈtis.so/, [äd̪peˈt̪isːo]

Verb[edit]

adpetissō (present infinitive adpetissere, perfect active adpetissīvī or adpetissiī or adpetissī, supine adpetessītum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of appetissō
