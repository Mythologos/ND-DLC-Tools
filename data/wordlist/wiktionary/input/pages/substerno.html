Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ sternō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈster.noː/, [s̠ʊpˈs̠t̪ɛrnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈster.no/, [subˈst̪ɛrno]

Verb[edit]

substernō (present infinitive substernere, perfect active substrāvī, supine substrātum); third conjugation

  1. to strew, scatter, spread out or under
  2. to bestrew, bespread
  3. to yield, submit for examination, acceptance, etc.
