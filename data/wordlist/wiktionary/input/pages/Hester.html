English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (US) IPA(key): /ˈhɛstɚ/
  • Rhymes: -ɛstə(ɹ)

Etymology 1[edit]

From the Latin form of Esther.

Proper noun[edit]

Hester

  1. A female given name from Persian, used since the 17th century.
Related terms[edit]
  • Esther
  • pet forms: Hettie, Hetty

Etymology 2[edit]

Borrowed from Middle Low German hêster (“beech”).

Proper noun[edit]

Hester

  1. A surname from German.

Anagrams[edit]

  • Ehrets, Esther, ethers, there's, theres, threes

Dutch[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Perhaps a variant of Esther.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɦɛs.tər/
  • Audio(file)
  • Hyphenation: Hes‧ter

Proper noun[edit]

Hester f

  1. a female given name

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

(Classical) IPA(key): /ˈhes.ter/, [ˈhɛs̠t̪ɛr]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈes.ter/, [ˈɛst̪er]

Proper noun[edit]

Hester f (indeclinable)

  1. Alternative form of Esther
