Indonesian[edit]

Indonesian Wikipedia has an article on:
identitas
Wikipedia id

Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Latin identitās.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [i.dɛnˈti.tas]
  • Hyphenation: idèntitas

Noun[edit]

idèntitas (plural identitas-identitas, first-person possessive identitasku, second-person possessive identitasmu, third-person possessive identitasnya)

  1. identity.
    Synonym: jati diri
    kartu identitas ― identity card

  • idéntiti (Brunei, Malaysia, Singapore)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From idem n (“the same”) +‎ -tās, a calque of Ancient Greek ταὐτότης (tautótēs, “sameness”). Compare essentitās, essentiālitās.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈden.ti.taːs/, [ɪˈd̪ɛn̪t̪ɪt̪äːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈden.ti.tas/, [iˈd̪ɛn̪t̪it̪äs]

Noun[edit]

identitās f (genitive identitātis); third declension

  1. (Late Latin, philosophy, theology) sameness, identicalness, identity
    Synonyms: ūnitās, identitūdō
    Antonym: alteritās
    identitās nātūrae ― the identicalness of nature
    identitās fōrmalis et reālis ― formal and objective identity
