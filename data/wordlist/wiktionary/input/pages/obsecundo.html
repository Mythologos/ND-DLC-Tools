Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ob.seˈkun.doː/, [ɔps̠ɛˈkʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ob.seˈkun.do/, [obseˈkun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

obsecundō (present infinitive obsecundāre, perfect active obsecundāvī, supine obsecundātum); first conjugation

  1. to comply with
  2. to obey
