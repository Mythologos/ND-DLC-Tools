Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈnok.toː/, [pɛrˈnɔkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈnok.to/, [perˈnɔkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

pernoctō (present infinitive pernoctāre, perfect active pernoctāvī, supine pernoctātum); first conjugation

  1. to spend the night
