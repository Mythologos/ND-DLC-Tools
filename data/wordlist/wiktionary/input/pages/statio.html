Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From stō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsta.ti.oː/, [ˈs̠t̪ät̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈstat.t͡si.o/, [ˈst̪ät̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

statiō f (genitive statiōnis); third declension

  1. outpost, picket
  2. station
  3. watch
  4. a standing, standing firm, position

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
