Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈha.u̯e.oː/, [ˈhäu̯eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.ve.o/, [ˈäːveo]

Verb[edit]

haveō (present infinitive havēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of aveō
