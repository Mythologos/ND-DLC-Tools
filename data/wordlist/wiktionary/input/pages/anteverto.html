Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ante- +‎ vertō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /an.teˈu̯er.toː/, [än̪t̪eˈu̯ɛrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /an.teˈver.to/, [än̪t̪eˈvɛrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

antevertō (present infinitive antevertere, perfect active antevertī, supine anteversum); third conjugation

  1. to anticipate
  2. to prefer (place before)
    Synonyms: anteferō, praeoptō, praeferō, praepōnō, mālō
