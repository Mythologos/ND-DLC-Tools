Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.ri.ɡoː/, [ˈd̪eːrɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.ri.ɡo/, [ˈd̪ɛːriɡo]

Verb[edit]

dērigō (present infinitive dērigere, perfect active dērēxī, supine dērēctum); third conjugation

  1. to direct, steer, guide, align, point
  2. to straighten or level
  3. Alternative form of dīrigō
