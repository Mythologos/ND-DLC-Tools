Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ mōlior.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈmoː.li.oː/, [d̪eːˈmoːlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈmo.li.o/, [d̪eˈmɔːlio]

Verb[edit]

dēmōliō (present infinitive dēmōlīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to demolish
