Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mirus +‎ faciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /miːˈri.fi.koː/, [miːˈrɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /miˈri.fi.ko/, [miˈriːfiko]

Verb[edit]

mīrificō (present infinitive mīrificāre, perfect active mīrificāvī, supine mīrificātum); first conjugation

  1. (Late Latin) to exalt, magnify
