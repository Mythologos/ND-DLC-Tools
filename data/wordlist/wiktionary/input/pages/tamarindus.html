Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Arabic تَمْر هِنْدِيّ (tamr hindiyy).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ta.maˈrin.dus/, [t̪ämäˈrɪn̪d̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ta.maˈrin.dus/, [t̪ämäˈrin̪d̪us]

Noun[edit]

tamarindus m (genitive tamarindī); second declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) tamarind

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun.
