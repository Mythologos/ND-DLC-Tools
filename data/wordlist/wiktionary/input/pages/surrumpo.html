Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ rumpo

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /surˈrum.poː/, [s̠ʊrˈrʊmpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /surˈrum.po/, [surˈrumpo]

Verb[edit]

surrumpō (present infinitive surrumpere, perfect active surrūpī, supine surruptum); third conjugation

  1. to break (off) below
  2. to destroy
