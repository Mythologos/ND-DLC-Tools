Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ currō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈkur.roː/, [präe̯ˈkʊrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈkur.ro/, [preˈkurːo]

Verb[edit]

praecurrō (present infinitive praecurrere, perfect active praecurrī or praecucurrī, supine praecursum); third conjugation

  1. to run before
  2. to precede
  3. to surpass
