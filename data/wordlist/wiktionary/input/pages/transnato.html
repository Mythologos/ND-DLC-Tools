Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.na.toː/, [ˈt̪rä̃ːs̠nät̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.na.to/, [ˈt̪ränznät̪o]

Verb[edit]

trānsnatō (present infinitive trānsnatāre, perfect active trānsnatāvī, supine trānsnatātum); first conjugation

  1. to swim across or through
