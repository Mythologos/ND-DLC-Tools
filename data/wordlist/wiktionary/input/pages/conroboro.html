Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈroː.bo.roː/, [kɔnˈroːbɔroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈro.bo.ro/, [konˈrɔːboro]

Verb[edit]

conrōborō (present infinitive conrōborāre, perfect active conrōborāvī, supine conrōborātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of corrōborō
