Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English perversion), ultimately from Latin perversiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈperʋersio/, [ˈpe̞rʋe̞rˌs̠io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): per‧ver‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

perversio

  1. perversion

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈu̯er.si.oː/, [pɛrˈu̯ɛrs̠ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈver.si.o/, [perˈvɛrsio]

Noun[edit]

perversiō f (genitive perversiōnis); third declension

  1. inversion
  2. perversion

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
