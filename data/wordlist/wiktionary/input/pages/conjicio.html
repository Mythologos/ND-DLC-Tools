Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [kɔnˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [konˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

conjiciō (present infinitive conjicere, perfect active conjēcī, supine conjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of coniciō
