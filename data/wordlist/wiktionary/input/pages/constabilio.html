Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ stabiliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.staˈbi.li.oː/, [kõːs̠t̪äˈbɪlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.staˈbi.li.o/, [konst̪äˈbiːlio]

Verb[edit]

cōnstabiliō (present infinitive cōnstabilīre, perfect active cōnstabilīvī, supine cōnstabilītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to establish
  2. to confirm
  3. to strengthen
