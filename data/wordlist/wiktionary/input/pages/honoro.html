Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [hoˈnoro]
  • Audio:
    (file)
  • Rhymes: -oro
  • Hyphenation: ho‧no‧ro

Noun[edit]

honoro (accusative singular honoron, plural honoroj, accusative plural honorojn)

  1. honor

Ido[edit]

Noun[edit]

honoro (plural honori)

  1. honor (US), honour (UK)

Antonyms[edit]

  • deshonoro

Derived terms[edit]

  • honorumala (“honorary”)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From honor (“honor, repute”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /hoˈnoː.roː/, [hɔˈnoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈno.ro/, [oˈnɔːro]

Verb[edit]

honōrō (present infinitive honōrāre, perfect active honōrāvī, supine honōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to honor, respect
  2. to clothe with honor, decorate
