English[edit]

Chemical element
U
Previous: protactinium (Pa)
Next: neptunium (Np)

Etymology[edit]

After Uranus (the planet), +‎ -ium.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (US) enPR: ūrā'nēəm, IPA(key): /jʊˈɹeɪniəm/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -eɪniəm

Noun[edit]

uranium (countable and uncountable, plural uraniums)

  2. The element with atomic number 92 and symbol U: a radioactive silvery-grey metal in the actinide series.
