Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ru.beˈfa.ki.oː/, [rʊbɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ru.beˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [rubeˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

rubefaciō (present infinitive rubefacere, perfect active rubefēcī, supine rubefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to make red or ruddy; redden
