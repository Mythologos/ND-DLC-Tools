Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

exsudo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of exsudar

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • exūdō, ēsūdō

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ sūdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsuː.doː/, [ɛkˈs̠uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.do/, [eɡˈzuːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

exsūdō (present infinitive exsūdāre, perfect active exsūdāvī, supine exsūdātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to sweat out, to exude
