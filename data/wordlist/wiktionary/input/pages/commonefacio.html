Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From commoneō +‎ faciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kom.mo.neˈfa.ki.oː/, [kɔmːɔnɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kom.mo.neˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [komːoneˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

commonefaciō (present infinitive commonefacere, perfect active commonefēcī, supine commonefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to remind (forcibly), warn or admonish
