Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From cauma +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkau̯.moː/, [ˈkäu̯moː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkau̯.mo/, [ˈkäːu̯mo]

Verb[edit]

caumō (present infinitive caumāre, perfect active caumāvī); first conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (Late Latin) to rest during the heat
