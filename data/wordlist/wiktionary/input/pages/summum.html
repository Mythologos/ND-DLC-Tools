Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Noun use of neuter of summus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsum.mum/, [ˈs̠ʊmːʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsum.mum/, [ˈsumːum]

Noun[edit]

summum n (genitive summī); second declension

  1. top
  2. summit

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).

Case Singular Plural
Nominative summum summa
Genitive summī summōrum
Dative summō summīs
Accusative summum summa
Ablative summō summīs
Vocative summum summa

Descendants[edit]

  • Catalan: súmmum
  • Old French: som, somet
    • English: summit
    • French: sommet
  • Spanish: somo, súmmum

Adjective[edit]

summum

  1. inflection of summus:
    1. accusative masculine singular
    2. nominative/accusative/vocative neuter singular
