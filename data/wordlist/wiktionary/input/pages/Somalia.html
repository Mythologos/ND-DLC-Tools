English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /səˈmɑːliə/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɑːliə

Proper noun[edit]

Somalia

  2. A country in East Africa, in the Horn of Africa. Official name: Federal Republic of Somalia.
