Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From vannus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯an.noː/, [ˈu̯änːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvan.no/, [ˈvänːo]

Verb[edit]

vannō (present infinitive vannere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (pre-Classical) to fan, winnow
