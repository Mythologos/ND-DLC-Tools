Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

dilanio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of dilaniare

Anagrams[edit]

  • anolidi

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ laniō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈla.ni.oː/, [d̪iːˈɫ̪änioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈla.ni.o/, [d̪iˈläːnio]

Verb[edit]

dīlaniō (present infinitive dīlaniāre, perfect active dīlaniāvī, supine dīlaniātum); first conjugation

  1. to shred
  2. to rend asunder
