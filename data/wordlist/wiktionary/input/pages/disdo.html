Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdis.doː/, [ˈd̪ɪs̠d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈdis.do/, [ˈd̪izd̪o]

Verb[edit]

disdō (present infinitive disdere, perfect active disdidī, supine disditum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dīdō
