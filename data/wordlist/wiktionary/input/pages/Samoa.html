Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Samoan Sāmoa. See there for more.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (General Australian) IPA(key): /səˈmoʊə/

  • (Samoa) IPA(key): /saː.mo.a/
  • (UK) IPA(key): /səˈməʊ.ə/
  • (US) enPR: sə-mō'ə, IPA(key): /səˈmoʊ.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Samoa

  2. A country consisting of the western part of the Samoan archipelago in Polynesia, in Oceania. Official name: Independent State of Samoa. Capital and largest city: Apia. Formerly Western Samoa, and, before that, German Samoa. It is distinct from American Samoa.
  3. A census-designated place in Humboldt County, California, United States.
