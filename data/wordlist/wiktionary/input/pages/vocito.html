Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯o.ki.toː/, [ˈu̯ɔkɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvo.t͡ʃi.to/, [ˈvɔːt͡ʃit̪o]

Verb[edit]

vocitō (present infinitive vocitāre, perfect active vocitāvī, supine vocitātum); first conjugation

  1. to call or name
