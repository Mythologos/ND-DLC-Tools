English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
convector
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

convect +‎ -or

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio (US)(file)

Noun[edit]

convector (plural convectors)

  1. A space heater that transfers heat by convection; a radiator

Translations[edit]

space heater
  • French: convecteur (fr) m
  • Greek: θερμοπομπός (el) m (thermopompós)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ vector (“carrier, passenger”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈu̯ek.tor/, [kɔnˈu̯ɛkt̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈvek.tor/, [koɱˈvɛkt̪or]

Noun[edit]

convector m (genitive convectōris); third declension

  1. one who carries or brings together
  2. a fellow passenger

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
