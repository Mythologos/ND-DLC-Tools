Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯oi̯.u̯o.da/, [ˈu̯ɔi̯u̯ɔd̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvoj.vo.da/, [ˈvɔjvod̪ä]

Noun[edit]

vojvoda m (genitive vojvodae); first declension

  1. alternative spelling of voivoda (“voivode”)

Declension[edit]

Serbo-Croatian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Proto-Slavic *vojevoda.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʋǒjʋoda/
  • Hyphenation: voj‧vo‧da

Noun[edit]

vòjvoda m (Cyrillic spelling во̀јвода)

  1. duke (male ruler of a duchy)
