Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ flagito.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈflaː.ɡi.toː/, [rɛˈfɫ̪äːɡɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈfla.d͡ʒi.to/, [reˈfläːd͡ʒit̪o]

Verb[edit]

reflāgitō (present infinitive reflāgitāre, perfect active reflāgitāvī, supine reflāgitātum); first conjugation

  1. to demand again; to demand back
