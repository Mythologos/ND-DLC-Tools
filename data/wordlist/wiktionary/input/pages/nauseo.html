Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

nauseo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of nauseare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

See nausia, nausea.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈnau̯.se.oː/, [ˈnäu̯s̠eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈnau̯.se.o/, [ˈnäːu̯seo]

Verb[edit]

nauseō (present infinitive nauseāre, perfect active nauseāvī, supine nauseātum); first conjugation

  1. to feel seasick
  2. to be squeamish
