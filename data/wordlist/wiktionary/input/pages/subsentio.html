Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ sentiō (“feel, perceive”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈsen.ti.oː/, [s̠ʊpˈs̠ɛn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈsen.t͡si.o/, [subˈsɛnt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

subsentiō (present infinitive subsentīre, perfect active subsēnsī, supine subsēnsum); fourth conjugation

  1. to perceive, note, observe
