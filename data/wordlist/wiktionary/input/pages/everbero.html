Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ verberō (“to beat”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈu̯er.be.roː/, [eːˈu̯ɛrbɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈver.be.ro/, [eˈvɛrbero]

Verb[edit]

ēverberō (present infinitive ēverberāre, perfect active ēverberāvī, supine ēverberātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to strike violently, beat
