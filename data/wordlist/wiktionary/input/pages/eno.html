Etymology[edit]

From Japanese 円 (en, “yen, circle”). Compare Korean 엔 (en), Chinese 円 (yuán), French yen, Russian иена (ijena).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈeno]
  • Rhymes: -eno
  • Hyphenation: e‧no

Noun[edit]

eno (accusative singular enon, plural enoj, accusative plural enojn)

  1. (numismatics) yen

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ nō (“to swim”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.noː/, [ˈeːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.no/, [ˈɛːno]

Verb[edit]

ēnō (present infinitive ēnāre, perfect active ēnāvī, supine ēnātum); first conjugation

  1. (intransitive) to swim out, swim away, escape by swimming
  2. (intransitive) to swim across, sail across, etc.
