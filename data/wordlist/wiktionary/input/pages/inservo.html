Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- (“in”) +‎ servō (“save, preserve”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈser.u̯oː/, [ĩːˈs̠ɛru̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈser.vo/, [inˈsɛrvo]

Verb[edit]

inservō (present infinitive inservāre, perfect active inservāvī, supine inservātum); first conjugation

  1. to attend, observe
  2. to keep
