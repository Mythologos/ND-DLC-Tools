Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From trītūra.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /triːˈtuː.roː/, [t̪riːˈt̪uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /triˈtu.ro/, [t̪riˈt̪uːro]

Verb[edit]

trītūrō (present infinitive trītūrāre, perfect active trītūrāvī, supine trītūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to thresh
