Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈri.pi.oː/, [präe̯ˈrɪpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈri.pi.o/, [preˈriːpio]

Verb[edit]

praeripiō (present infinitive praeripere, perfect active praeripuī, supine praereptum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to snatch (before another)
  2. to forestall
