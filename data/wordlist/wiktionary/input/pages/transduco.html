Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈduː.koː/, [t̪rä̃ːs̠ˈd̪uːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈdu.ko/, [t̪ränzˈd̪uːko]

Verb[edit]

trānsdūcō (present infinitive trānsdūcere, perfect active trānsdūxī, supine trānsductum); third conjugation, irregular short imperative

  1. Alternative form of trādūcō
