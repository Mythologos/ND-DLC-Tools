Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • revīvēscō

Etymology[edit]

From revīvō +‎ -īscō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.u̯iːˈu̯iːs.koː/, [reu̯iːˈu̯iːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.viˈvis.ko/, [reviˈvisko]

Verb[edit]

revīvīscō (present infinitive revīvīscere, perfect active revīxī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to revive (be restored to life)
