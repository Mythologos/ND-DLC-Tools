Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Derived from Latin adpositio > adpositionem (from adpositum, past participle of adponere), via some of its descendants (e.g. Swedish adposition).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑdpositio/, [ˈɑ̝dpo̞ˌs̠it̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -itio
  • Syllabification(key): ad‧po‧si‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

adpositio

  1. (grammar) adposition

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.poˈsi.ti.oː/, [äpːɔˈs̠ɪt̪ioː]

Noun[edit]

adpositiō f (genitive adpositiōnis); third declension

  1. Alternative form of appositiō
