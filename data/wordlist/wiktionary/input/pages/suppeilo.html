Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /supˈpei̯.loː/, [s̠ʊpˈpɛi̯ɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /supˈpej.lo/, [supˈpɛjlo]

Verb[edit]

suppeilō (present infinitive suppeilāre, perfect active suppeilāvī, supine suppeilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative spelling of suppīlō
