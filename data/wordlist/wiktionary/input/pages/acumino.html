Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From acūmen (“sharpened point; sharpness, acuteness”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈkuː.mi.noː/, [äˈkuːmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈku.mi.no/, [äˈkuːmino]

Verb[edit]

acūminō (present infinitive acūmināre, perfect active acūmināvī, supine acūminātum); first conjugation

  1. to make pointed or sharp, sharpen
