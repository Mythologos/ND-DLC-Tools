Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈat.ti.ɡoː/, [ˈät̪ːɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈat.ti.ɡo/, [ˈät̪ːiɡo]

Verb[edit]

attigō (present infinitive attigere, perfect active attigī, supine attāctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attingō
