Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From astrum (“star”) +‎ faciō (“do, make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈtri.fi.koː/, [äs̠ˈt̪rɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈtri.fi.ko/, [äsˈt̪riːfiko]

Verb[edit]

astrificō (present infinitive astrificāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to produce or make stars
