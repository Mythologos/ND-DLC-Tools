Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈpun.ɡoː/, [rɛˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈpun.ɡo/, [reˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

repungō (present infinitive repungere, perfect active repūnxī, supine repūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to prick again, to goad again
