Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From amylum (“starch”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.my.loː/, [ˈämʏɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.mi.lo/, [ˈäːmilo]

Verb[edit]

amylō (present infinitive amylāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (transitive) to mix with starch
