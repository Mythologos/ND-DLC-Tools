Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • vadiō, guadiō, gagiō, gajō, gaviō, wadicō

Etymology[edit]

From wadium (“a contract”).

Verb[edit]

wadiō (present infinitive wadiāre, perfect active wadiāvī, supine wadiātum); first conjugation[1][2] (Medieval Latin)

  1. to contract
  2. to guarantee
  3. to pay a fine
  4. to mortgage
  5. to take hostage
