Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ calceō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈkul.ki.oː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈkʊɫ̪kioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈkul.t͡ʃi.o/, [d̪isˈkul̠ʲt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

disculciō (present infinitive disculciāre, perfect active disculciāvī, supine disculciātum); first conjugation

  1. to unshoe
  2. to remove the shoes
