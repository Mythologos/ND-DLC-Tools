Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- + hūmor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eks.huːˈmoː.roː/, [ɛks̠(ɦ)uːˈmoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.suˈmo.ro/, [eɡzuˈmɔːro]

Verb[edit]

exhūmōrō (present infinitive exhūmōrāre, perfect active exhūmōrāvī, supine exhūmōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to free from (injurious) humors
