Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • compleō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.ple.oː/, [ˈkɔmpɫ̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.ple.o/, [ˈkɔnpleo]

Verb[edit]

conpleō (present infinitive conplēre, perfect active conplēvī, supine conplētum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of compleō
