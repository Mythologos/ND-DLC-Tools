Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ terminō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈster.mi.noː/, [ɛkˈs̠t̪ɛrmɪnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈster.mi.no/, [ekˈst̪ɛrmino]

Verb[edit]

exterminō (present infinitive extermināre, perfect active extermināvī, supine exterminātum); first conjugation

  1. to expel, exile or banish
  2. to abolish, extirpate or destroy
