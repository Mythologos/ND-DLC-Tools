Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • attorqueō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈtor.kʷe.oː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɔrkʷeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈtor.kwe.o/, [äd̪ˈt̪ɔrkweo]

Verb[edit]

adtorqueō (present infinitive adtorquēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of attorqueō
