English[edit]

Chemical element
Np
Previous: uranium (U)
Next: plutonium (Pu)

Etymology[edit]

Neptune +‎ -ium, after the planet.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: nĕptū'nēəm, IPA(key): /nɛpˈtjuːniəm/; enPR: nĕpto͞o'nēəm, IPA(key): /nɛpˈtuːniəm/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)

Noun[edit]

neptunium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
neptunium
Wikipedia
  1. The transuranic chemical element with atomic number 93 and symbol Np.
