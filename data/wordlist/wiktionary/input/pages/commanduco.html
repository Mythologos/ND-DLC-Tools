Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ mandūcō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kom.manˈduː.koː/, [kɔmːän̪ˈd̪uːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kom.manˈdu.ko/, [komːän̪ˈd̪uːko]

Verb[edit]

commandūcō (present infinitive commandūcāre, perfect active commandūcāvī, supine commandūcātum); first conjugation

  1. to chew to pieces
