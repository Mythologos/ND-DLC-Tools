Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • vallecula (post-classical)

Etymology[edit]

From vallis (“a valley, vale”) +‎ -cula (diminutive suffix).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯alˈli.ku.la/, [u̯älˈlʲɪkʊɫ̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /valˈli.ku.la/, [välˈliːkulä]

Noun[edit]

vallicula f (genitive valliculae); first declension

  1. Diminutive of vallis: a glen, dell (little valley)
