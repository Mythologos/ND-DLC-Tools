Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [ɔbˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [obˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

objiciō (present infinitive objicere, perfect active objēcī, supine objectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of obicio
