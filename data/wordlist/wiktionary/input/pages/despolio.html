Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ spoliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːsˈpo.li.oː/, [d̪eːs̠ˈpɔlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /desˈpo.li.o/, [d̪esˈpɔːlio]

Verb[edit]

dēspoliō (present infinitive dēspoliāre, perfect active dēspoliāvī, supine dēspoliātum); first conjugation

  1. to rob, plunder or despoil
