Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adlicefaciō

Etymology[edit]

From alliciō +‎ faciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /al.li.keˈfa.ki.oː/, [älːʲɪkɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /al.li.t͡ʃeˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [älːit͡ʃeˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

allicefaciō (present infinitive allicefacere, perfect active allicefēcī, supine allicefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to allure, entice, seduce
