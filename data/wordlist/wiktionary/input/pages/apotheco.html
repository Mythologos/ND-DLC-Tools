Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From apothēca (“storehouse”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.poˈtʰeː.koː/, [äpɔˈt̪ʰeːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.poˈte.ko/, [äpoˈt̪ɛːko]

Verb[edit]

apothēcō (present infinitive apothēcāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to lay up in a storehouse
