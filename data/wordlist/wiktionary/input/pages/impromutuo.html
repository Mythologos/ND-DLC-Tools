Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ prōmūtuor. Attested in the Visigothic Code.[1]

Verb[edit]

imprōmūtuō (present infinitive imprōmūtuāre, perfect active imprōmūtuāvī, supine imprōmūtuātum); first conjugation (Early Medieval Latin)

  1. to lend
