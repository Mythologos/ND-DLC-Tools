German[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

Ammonium n (strong, genitive Ammoniums, no plural)

  1. (inorganic chemistry) ammonium

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /amˈmo.ni.um/, [ämˈmɔniʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /amˈmo.ni.um/, [ämˈmɔːnium]

Proper noun[edit]

Ammōnium n sg (genitive Ammōniī or Ammōnī); second declension

  1. (historical) Siwa Oasis (an oracle and town in southern Libya)

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter), with locative, singular only.
