Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From cohors +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈhor.toː/, [koˈ(ɦ)ɔrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈor.to/, [koˈɔrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

cohortō (present infinitive cohortāre, perfect active cohortāvī, supine cohortātum); first conjugation

  1. to encourage or exhort
