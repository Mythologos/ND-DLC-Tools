Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

hīrtus (“hairy”) +‎ -ellus (diminutive)

Adjective[edit]

hī̆rtellus (feminine hī̆rtella, neuter hī̆rtellum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (New Latin) having minuscule hairs
