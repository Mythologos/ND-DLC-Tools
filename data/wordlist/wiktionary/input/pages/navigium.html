Etymology[edit]

From nāvigō (“to sail”) +‎ -ium, from nāvis (“ship”).

Noun[edit]

nāvigium n (genitive nāvigiī or nāvigī); second declension

  1. vessel, ship, boat

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).
