Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈkiː.doː/, [äpˈkiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈt͡ʃi.do/, [äbˈt͡ʃiːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

abcīdō (present infinitive abcīdere, perfect active abcīdī, supine abcīsum); third conjugation

  1. (potentially erroneous) Alternative form of abscīdō
