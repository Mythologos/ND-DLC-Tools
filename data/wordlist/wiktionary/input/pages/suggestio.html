Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English suggestion), ultimately from Latin suggestiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsuɡːestio/, [ˈs̠uɡːe̞s̠ˌtio̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): sug‧ges‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

suggestio

  1. (psychology) suggestion

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suɡˈɡes.ti.oː/, [s̠ʊɡˈɡɛs̠t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sudˈd͡ʒes.ti.o/, [sudˈd͡ʒɛst̪io]

Noun[edit]

suggestiō f (genitive suggestiōnis); third declension

  1. addition
  2. suggestion, hint
