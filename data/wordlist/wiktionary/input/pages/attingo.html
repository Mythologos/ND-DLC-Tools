Anagrams[edit]

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtingō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ tangō (“touch”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /atˈtin.ɡoː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɪŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /atˈtin.ɡo/, [ät̪ˈt̪iŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

attingō (present infinitive attingere, perfect active attigī, supine attāctum); third conjugation

  1. to come in contact with, touch
  2. to assault; strike, attack
  3. (in eating) to touch, taste
  4. to approach, reach, arrive at
  5. to be contiguous to, lie near, border upon
  6. to touch, affect, interest, reach
  7. to mention slightly, touch upon
  8. to undertake, enter upon, engage in,
  9. to be similar; to belong, relate or appertain to, concern
