Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • approbō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pro.boː/, [ˈäpːrɔboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.pro.bo/, [ˈäd̪probo]

Verb[edit]

adprobō (present infinitive adprobāre, perfect active adprobāvī, supine adprobātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of approbō
