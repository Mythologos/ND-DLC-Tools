Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ sūgō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsuː.ɡoː/, [ɛkˈs̠uːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsu.ɡo/, [eɡˈzuːɡo]

Verb[edit]

exsūgō (present infinitive exsūgere, perfect active exsūxī, supine exsūctum); third conjugation

  1. to suck out
