Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ dispono.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.disˈpoː.noː/, [präe̯d̪ɪs̠ˈpoːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.disˈpo.no/, [pred̪isˈpɔːno]

Verb[edit]

praedispōnō (present infinitive praedispōnere, perfect active praedisposuī, supine praedispositum); third conjugation

  1. to dispose, distribute or arrange in advance
