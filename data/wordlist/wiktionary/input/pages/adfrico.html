Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • affricō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈad.fri.koː/, [ˈät̪frɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈad.fri.ko/, [ˈäd̪friko]

Verb[edit]

adfricō (present infinitive adfricāre, perfect active adfricuī, supine adfricātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of affricō
