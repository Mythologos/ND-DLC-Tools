French[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ka.pi.tal/
  • audio(file)

Noun[edit]

capitale f (plural capitales)

  1. capital, capital city
  2. capital letter
    Synonym: majuscule
    Antonym: minuscule

Derived terms[edit]

  • petite capitale

Adjective[edit]

capitale

  1. feminine singular of capital

Further reading[edit]

  • “capitale”, in Trésor de la langue française informatisé [Digitized Treasury of the French Language], 2012.

Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ka.piˈta.le/
  • Rhymes: -ale
  • Hyphenation: ca‧pi‧tà‧le

Etymology 1[edit]

Borrowed from Latin capitālis, derived from caput (“head”).

Adjective[edit]

capitale (plural capitali)

  1. (relational) head
    1. (law) capital
    2. (by extension) bitter
      Synonym: acerrimo
      nemico capitale ― bitter enemy
    3. (by extension) fierce
      Synonym: accanito
      odio capitale ― fierce hatred
  2. primary, prime, chief, main, capital
    Synonyms: essenziale, fondamentale, primario, vitale
Derived terms[edit]
  • capitalmente
  • peccato capitale
  • pena capitale
  • sette peccati capitali

Etymology 2[edit]

Noun use of the above adjective.

Noun[edit]

capitale f (plural capitali)

  1. capital city, capital
    la Capitale ― the Capital (= Rome)

Noun[edit]

capitale m (plural capitali)

  1. (economics) capital
Derived terms[edit]
  • capitalismo
  • capitalista
Related terms[edit]
  • capitalizzare
Descendants[edit]
  • → Dutch: kapitaal
    • → Indonesian: kapital
  • → German: Kapital, Capital
    • → Yiddish: קאַפּיטאַל (kapital)
  • → Piedmontese: capital

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

A nominalised form from capitālis.

Noun[edit]

capitāle n (genitive capitālis); third declension

  1. (Late Latin) capital crime
  2. (Late Latin) pillow
  3. (Medieval Latin, finance) capital (money)

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (neuter, “pure” i-stem).

Case Singular Plural
Nominative capitāle capitālia
Genitive capitālis capitālium
Dative capitālī capitālibus
Accusative capitāle capitālia
Ablative capitālī capitālibus
Vocative capitāle capitālia

Adjective[edit]

capitāle

  1. nominative/accusative/vocative neuter singular of capitālis
