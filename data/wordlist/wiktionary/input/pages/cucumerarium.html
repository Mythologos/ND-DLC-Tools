Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From cucumis (“cucumber”) +‎ -ārium (“place for”).

Noun[edit]

cucumerārium n (genitive cucumerāriī or cucumerārī); second declension

  1. cucumber patch or field

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).
