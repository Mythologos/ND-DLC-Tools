Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈspel.loː/, [äpˈs̠pɛlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈspel.lo/, [äbˈspɛlːo]

Verb[edit]

abspellō (present infinitive abspellere, perfect active abspulī, supine abspulsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of aspello
