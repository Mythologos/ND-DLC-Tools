Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ clīnō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈkliː.noː/, [rɛˈklʲiːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈkli.no/, [reˈkliːno]

Verb[edit]

reclīnō (present infinitive reclīnāre, perfect active reclīnāvī, supine reclīnātum); first conjugation

  1. to bend or lean back
  2. to recline
