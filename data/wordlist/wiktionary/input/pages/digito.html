Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

digito

  1. first-person singular present indicative of digitar

Interlingua[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin digitus.

Noun[edit]

digito (plural digitos)

  1. digit, (finger, or toe.)
  2. digit, (cipher, figure.)

Derived terms[edit]

Derived terms
  • digito anular
  • digito auricular
  • digito indice
  • digito medie

Pronunciation[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

Verb[edit]

digitō (present infinitive digitāre, perfect active digitāvī, supine digitātum); first conjugation
