Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From mangōn- +‎ -izō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /man.ɡoːˈniz.zoː/, [mäŋɡoːˈnɪz̪d̪͡z̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /man.ɡoˈnid.d͡zo/, [mäŋɡoˈnid̪ː͡z̪o]

Verb[edit]

mangōnizō (present infinitive mangōnizāre, perfect active mangōnizāvī, supine mangōnizātum); first conjugation

  1. to deck or adorn (wares for sale)
    Synonym: mangōnicō
