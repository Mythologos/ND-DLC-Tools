Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ gluttiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈɡlut.ti.oː/, [ɪŋˈɡɫ̪ʊt̪ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈɡlut.ti.o/, [iŋˈɡlut̪ːio]

Verb[edit]

ingluttiō (present infinitive ingluttīre, perfect active ingluttīvī, supine ingluttītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to swallow, gulp
