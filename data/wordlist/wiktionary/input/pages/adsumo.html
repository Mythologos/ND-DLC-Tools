Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsuː.moː/, [äs̠ˈs̠uːmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈsu.mo/, [äd̪ˈsuːmo]

Verb[edit]

adsūmō (present infinitive adsūmere, perfect active adsūmpsī, supine adsūmptum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assūmō
