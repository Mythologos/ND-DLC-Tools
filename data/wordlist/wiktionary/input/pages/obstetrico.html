Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ob.steˈtriː.koː/, [ɔps̠t̪ɛˈt̪riːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ob.steˈtri.ko/, [obst̪eˈt̪riːko]

Verb[edit]

obstetrīcō (present infinitive obstetrīcāre, perfect active obstetrīcāvī, supine obstetrīcātum); first conjugation

  1. to perform the office of a midwife, assist at childbirth
