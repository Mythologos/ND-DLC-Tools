Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ lassō (“to weary, tire”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈlas.soː/, [d̪eːˈɫ̪äs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈlas.so/, [d̪eˈläsːo]

Verb[edit]

dēlassō (present infinitive dēlassāre, perfect active dēlassāvī, supine dēlassātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to wear out, tire
