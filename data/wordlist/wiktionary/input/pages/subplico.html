Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.pli.koː/, [ˈs̠ʊpːlʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.pli.ko/, [ˈsubpliko]

Verb[edit]

subplicō (present infinitive subplicāre, perfect active subplicāvī, supine subplicātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of supplicō
