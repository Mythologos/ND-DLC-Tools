Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /roˈkʰeː.ta/, [rɔˈkʰeːt̪ä] or IPA(key): /ˈro.kʰe.ta/, [ˈrɔkʰɛt̪ä]

Noun[edit]

rochē̆ta f (genitive rochē̆tae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) rochet
  2. (Medieval Latin, New Latin, Contemporary Latin) rocket
