Chibcha[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /aβaɣo/

Noun[edit]

abago

  1. Part of maize that is given to someone as a thank you for the maize they helped to pick.

References[edit]

  • Gómez Aldana D. F., Análisis morfológico del Vocabulario 158 de la Biblioteca Nacional de Colombia. Grupo de Investigación Muysccubun. 2013.

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.ba.ɡoː/, [ˈäbäɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.ba.ɡo/, [ˈäːbäɡo]

Verb[edit]

abagō (present infinitive abagere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. Alternative form of abigō
