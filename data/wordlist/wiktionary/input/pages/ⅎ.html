Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Created by the Roman Emperor Claudius. Possibly inspired by the Greek digamma.

Letter[edit]

Ⅎ (lower case ⅎ)

  1. (obsolete) Used to represent the consonantal value of U/V (i.e. W/V).
