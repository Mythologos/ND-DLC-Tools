Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /proˈtru.do/
  • Rhymes: -udo
  • Hyphenation: pro‧trù‧do

Verb[edit]

protrudo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of protrudere

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

prō- +‎ trūdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈtruː.doː/, [proːˈt̪ruːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈtru.do/, [proˈt̪ruːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

prōtrūdō (present infinitive prōtrūdere, perfect active prōtrūsī, supine prōtrūsum); third conjugation

  1. to thrust or push forwards
  2. to protrude
