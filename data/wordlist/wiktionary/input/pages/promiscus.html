Catalan[edit]

Adjective[edit]

promiscus

  1. masculine plural of promiscu

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈmis.kus/, [proːˈmɪs̠kʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈmis.kus/, [proˈmiskus]

Adjective[edit]

prōmiscus (feminine prōmisca, neuter prōmiscum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative form of prōmiscuus
