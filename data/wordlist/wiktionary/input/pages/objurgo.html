Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈi̯uːr.ɡoː/, [ɔbˈi̯uːrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈjur.ɡo/, [obˈjurɡo]

Verb[edit]

objūrgō (present infinitive objūrgāre, perfect active objūrgāvī, supine objūrgātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of obiūrgō
