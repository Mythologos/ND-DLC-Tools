Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From frēnō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈfreː.noː/, [ĩːˈfreːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈfre.no/, [iɱˈfrɛːno]

Verb[edit]

īnfrēnō (present infinitive īnfrēnāre, perfect active īnfrēnāvī, supine īnfrēnātum); first conjugation

  1. to bridle, restrain, check
  2. to break in
