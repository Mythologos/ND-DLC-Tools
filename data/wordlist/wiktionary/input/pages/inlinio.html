Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈli.ni.oː/, [ɪnˈlʲɪnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈli.ni.o/, [inˈliːnio]

Verb[edit]

inliniō (present infinitive inlinīre, perfect active inlinīvī, supine inlinītum); fourth conjugation

  1. alternative form of illiniō
