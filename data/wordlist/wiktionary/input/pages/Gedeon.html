Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Ancient Greek Γεδεών (Gedeṓn), from Biblical Hebrew גִּדְעוֹן (Giḏʿṓn).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡe.de.oːn/, [ˈɡɛd̪eoːn]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈd͡ʒe.de.on/, [ˈd͡ʒɛːd̪eon]

Proper noun[edit]

Gedeōn m sg (genitive Gedeōnis); third declension

  1. Gideon

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun, singular only.
