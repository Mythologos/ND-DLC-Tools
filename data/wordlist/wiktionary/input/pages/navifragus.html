Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /naːˈu̯i.fra.ɡus/, [näːˈu̯ɪfräɡʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /naˈvi.fra.ɡus/, [näˈviːfräɡus]

Adjective[edit]

nāvifragus (feminine nāvifraga, neuter nāvifragum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative form of naufragus (“causing shipwreck”)

Usage notes[edit]

This alternative form is only attested in the active sense "causing shipwreck".
