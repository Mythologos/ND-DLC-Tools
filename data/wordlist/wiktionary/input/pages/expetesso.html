Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • expetissō

Etymology[edit]

ex- (intensive prefix) +‎ petessō (“strive for, pursue”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ek.speˈtes.soː/, [ɛks̠pɛˈt̪ɛs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.speˈtes.so/, [ekspeˈt̪ɛsːo]

Verb[edit]

expetessō (present infinitive expetessere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to desire, long for
