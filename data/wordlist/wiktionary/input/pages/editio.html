Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see French édition), ultimately from Latin ēditiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈeditio/, [ˈe̞diˌt̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): e‧di‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

editio

  1. edition

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Derived from ēdō (“I dispatch”) +‎ -tiō (“-ation”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈdi.ti.oː/, [eːˈd̪ɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈdit.t͡si.o/, [eˈd̪it̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

ēditiō f (genitive ēditiōnis); third declension

  1. publishing
  2. edition
  3. statement, representation
  4. exhibition

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
