Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ vestīgō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.u̯esˈtiː.ɡoː/, [pɛru̯ɛs̠ˈt̪iːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.vesˈti.ɡo/, [pervesˈt̪iːɡo]

Verb[edit]

pervestīgō (present infinitive pervestīgāre, perfect active pervestīgāvī, supine pervestīgātum); first conjugation

  1. to examine or investigate thoroughly
