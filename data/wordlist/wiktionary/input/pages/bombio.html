Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From bombus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbom.bi.oː/, [ˈbɔmbioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈbom.bi.o/, [ˈbɔmbio]

Verb[edit]

bombiō (present infinitive bombīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to buzz
