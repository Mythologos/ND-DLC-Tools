Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ argentum +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /de.arˈɡen.toː/, [d̪eärˈɡɛn̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.arˈd͡ʒen.to/, [d̪eärˈd͡ʒɛn̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

deargentō (present infinitive deargentāre, perfect active deargentāvī, supine deargentātum); first conjugation

  1. to deprive of money
  2. to plate with silver
