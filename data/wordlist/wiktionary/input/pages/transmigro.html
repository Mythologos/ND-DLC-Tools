Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

transmigro

  1. first-person singular present indicative of transmigrar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From trāns- +‎ migrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.mi.ɡroː/, [ˈt̪rä̃ːs̠mɪɡroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈtrans.mi.ɡro/, [ˈt̪ränzmiɡro]

Verb[edit]

trānsmigrō (present infinitive trānsmigrāre, perfect active trānsmigrāvī, supine trānsmigrātum); first conjugation

  1. to move house
  2. to transmigrate
