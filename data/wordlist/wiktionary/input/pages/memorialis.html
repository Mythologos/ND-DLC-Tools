Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From memoria +‎ -ālis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /me.mo.riˈaː.lis/, [mɛmɔriˈäːlʲɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /me.mo.riˈa.lis/, [memoriˈäːlis]

Adjective[edit]

memoriālis (neuter memoriāle); third-declension two-termination adjective

  1. of or pertaining to memory or remembrance; memorial
