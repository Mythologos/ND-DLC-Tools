Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ lūdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈluː.doː/, [ɪnˈɫ̪uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈlu.do/, [inˈluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

inlūdō (present infinitive inlūdere, perfect active inlūsī, supine inlūsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of illūdō
