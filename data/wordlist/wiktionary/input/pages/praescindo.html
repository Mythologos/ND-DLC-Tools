Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ scindō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯sˈkin.doː/, [präe̯s̠ˈkɪn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preʃˈʃin.do/, [preʃˈʃin̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

praescindō (present infinitive praescindere, perfect active praescidī, supine praescissum); third conjugation

  1. to cut or tear in front
