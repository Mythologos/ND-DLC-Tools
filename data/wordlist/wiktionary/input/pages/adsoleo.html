Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assoleō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈso.le.oː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɔɫ̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈso.le.o/, [äd̪ˈsɔːleo]

Verb[edit]

adsoleō (present infinitive adsolēre, perfect active adsoluī or adsolitus sum, supine adsolitum); second conjugation, optionally semi-deponent, no imperative, no future

  1. Alternative form of assoleō

Conjugation[edit]

  • Unlike most semi-deponent verbs, adsoleō has no future tense.
