Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈpreːn.doː/, [rɛˈpreːn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈpren.do/, [reˈprɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

reprēndō (present infinitive reprēndere, perfect active reprēndī, supine reprēnsum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of reprehendō
