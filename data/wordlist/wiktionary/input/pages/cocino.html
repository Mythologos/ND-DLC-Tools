Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From earlier coquīnō. Attested in the Pactus Legis Salicae.[1]

Verb[edit]

cocīnō (present infinitive cocīnāre, perfect active cocīnāvī, supine cocīnātum); first conjugation (Late Latin)

  1. to cook
