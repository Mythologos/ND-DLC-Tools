Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central, Balearic) [ˈar.dus]
  • IPA(key): (Valencian) [ˈaɾ.ðus]

Adjective[edit]

ardus

  1. masculine plural of ardu

Verb[edit]

ardus

  1. conditional of ardi

French[edit]

Adjective[edit]

ardus

  1. masculine plural of ardu

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Syncopic form of āridus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈar.dus/, [ˈärd̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈar.dus/, [ˈärd̪us]

Adjective[edit]

ārdus (feminine ārda, neuter ārdum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative form of āridus

Declension[edit]

First/second-declension adjective.
