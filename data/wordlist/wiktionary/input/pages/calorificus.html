Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From calor (“warmth, heat”) + faciō (“do, make”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ka.loːˈri.fi.kus/, [käɫ̪oːˈrɪfɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ka.loˈri.fi.kus/, [käloˈriːfikus]

Adjective[edit]

calōrificus (feminine calōrifica, neuter calōrificum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Causing or promoting warmth, warming or heating.
