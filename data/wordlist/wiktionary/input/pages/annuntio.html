Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adnūntiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /anˈnuːn.ti.oː/, [änˈnuːn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /anˈnun.t͡si.o/, [änˈnunt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

annūntiō (present infinitive annūntiāre, perfect active annūntiāvī, supine annūntiātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of adnūntiō
