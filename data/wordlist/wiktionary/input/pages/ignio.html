Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ignis (“fire”) +‎ -iō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈiɡ.ni.oː/, [ˈɪŋnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈiɲ.ɲi.o/, [ˈiɲːio]

Verb[edit]

igniō (present infinitive ignīre, perfect active ignīvī or igniī, supine ignītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to ignite, set on fire
