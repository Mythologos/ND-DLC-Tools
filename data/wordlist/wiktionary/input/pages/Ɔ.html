Dagbani[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɔ/

Letter[edit]

Ɔ (lower case ɔ)

  1. A letter of the Dagbani alphabet.

Ewe[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɔ/

Letter[edit]

Ɔ (lower case ɔ)

  1. A letter of the Ewe alphabet.

See also[edit]

  • (Latin-script letters) A a, B b, C c, D d, Ɖ ɖ, Dz dz, E e, Ɛ ɛ, F f, Ƒ ƒ, G g, Gb gb, Ɣ ɣ, H h, I i, K k, Kp kp, Q q, L l, M m, N n, Ny ny, Ŋ ŋ, O o, Ɔ ɔ, P p, R r, S s, T t, Ts ts, U u, V v, Ʋ ʋ, W w, X x, Y y, Z z

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

  • A reversed C, 'C' standing for Gaius.

Proper noun[edit]

Ɔ f (indeclinable)

  1. Praenominal abbreviation of Gaia.

Lingala[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɔ̀/

Letter[edit]

Ɔ (lower case ɔ)

  1. A letter of the Lingala alphabet, written in the Latin script.

Yucatec Maya[edit]

Letter[edit]

Ɔ (lowercase ɔ)

  1. (Colonial-era texts) The capital letter ɔ, representing /t͡sʼ/.
