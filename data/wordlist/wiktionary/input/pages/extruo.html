Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.stru.oː/, [ˈɛks̠t̪ruoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.stru.o/, [ˈɛkst̪ruo]

Verb[edit]

extruō (present infinitive extruere, perfect active extruxī, supine extructum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of exstruo
