Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ vinciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈu̯in.ki.oː/, [d̪eːˈu̯ɪŋkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈvin.t͡ʃi.o/, [d̪eˈvin̠ʲt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

dēvinciō (present infinitive dēvincīre, perfect active dēvīnxī, supine dēvīnctum); fourth conjugation

  1. to bind fast, tie up
  2. to subjugate
  3. to oblige, constrain
