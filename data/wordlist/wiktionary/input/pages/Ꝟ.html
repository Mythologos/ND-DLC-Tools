Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

ꝟ (upper case Ꝟ)

  1. (Medieval Latin) Scribal abbreviation of virgo 'virgin'.

Old Galician-Portuguese[edit]

Letter[edit]

ꝟ (upper case Ꝟ)

  1. (siglum) Scribal abbreviation of ver(V) as in conꝟsa (conversa 'conversation'), ꝟador (vereador 'town councillor')

Verb[edit]

ꝟ (upper case Ꝟ)

  1. (siglum) Scribal abbreviation of ver 'to see' and Scribal abbreviation of vir 'to come'.
