Czech[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈɪnfantɪt͡sɪda]

Noun[edit]

infanticida f

  1. infanticide (the murder of an infant or a newborn)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

īnfāns (“infant”) +‎ -cīda

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.fan.tiˈkiː.da/, [ĩːfän̪t̪ɪˈkiːd̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.fan.tiˈt͡ʃi.da/, [iɱfän̪t̪iˈt͡ʃiːd̪ä]

Noun[edit]

īnfanticīda m (genitive īnfanticīdae); first declension

  1. (Late Latin) a child-murderer, an infanticide

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Case Singular Plural
Nominative īnfanticīda īnfanticīdae
Genitive īnfanticīdae īnfanticīdārum

Accusative īnfanticīdam īnfanticīdās
Ablative īnfanticīdā īnfanticīdīs

Derived terms[edit]

  • īnfanticīdium
