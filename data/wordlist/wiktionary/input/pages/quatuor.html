English[edit]

Noun[edit]

quatuor (plural quatuors)

  1. (music) A quartet.

French[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unadapted borrowing from Latin quattuor (frequently occurs without doubling quatuor).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /kwa.tɥɔʁ/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɥɔ

Noun[edit]

quatuor m (plural quatuors)

  1. (music) quartet (composition)
  2. (music) quartet (group of musicians)
  3. (sexuality) foursome

Further reading[edit]

  • “quatuor”, in Trésor de la langue française informatisé [Digitized Treasury of the French Language], 2012.

Latin[edit]

Numeral[edit]
