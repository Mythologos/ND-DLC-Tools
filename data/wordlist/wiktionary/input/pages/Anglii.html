Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Plural of Anglus

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈan.ɡlii̯/, [ˈäŋɡlʲɪi̯]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈan.ɡlij/, [ˈäŋɡlij]

Proper noun[edit]

Angliī m pl (genitive Angliōrum); second declension

  1. the Angles

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun, plural only.
