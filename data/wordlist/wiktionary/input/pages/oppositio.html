Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Late Latin oppositiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈopːositio/, [ˈo̞pːo̞ˌs̠it̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -itio
  • Syllabification(key): op‧po‧si‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

oppositio

  1. (politics) opposition
  2. (astronomy) opposition

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • obpositio

Etymology[edit]

From oppōnō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /op.poˈsi.ti.oː/, [ɔpːɔˈs̠ɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /op.poˈsit.t͡si.o/, [opːoˈs̬it̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

oppositiō f (genitive oppositiōnis); third declension

  1. opposing, opposition
