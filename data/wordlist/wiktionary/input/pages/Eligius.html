Latin[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Eligius
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

From ēligere (“to pick, to choose from”) +‎ -ius (“-y: forming adjectives and names”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eˈli.ɡi.us/, [ɛˈlʲɪɡiʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈli.d͡ʒi.us/, [eˈliːd͡ʒius]

Proper noun[edit]

Eligius

  1. a male given name
