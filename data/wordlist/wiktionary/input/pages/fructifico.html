Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From frūctus +‎ -ficō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /fruːkˈti.fi.koː/, [fruːkˈt̪ɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /frukˈti.fi.ko/, [frukˈt̪iːfiko]

Verb[edit]

frūctificō (present infinitive frūctificāre, perfect active frūctificāvī, supine frūctificātum); first conjugation

  1. to bear fruit; to fructify
