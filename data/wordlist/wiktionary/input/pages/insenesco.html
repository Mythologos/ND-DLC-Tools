Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.seˈneːs.koː/, [ĩːs̠ɛˈneːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.seˈnes.ko/, [inseˈnɛsko]

Verb[edit]

īnsenēscō (present infinitive īnsenēscere, perfect active īnsenuī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive, with dative) to grow old in or at
