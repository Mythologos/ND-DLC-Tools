Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unknown. Perhaps a literary coinage based on the dialectal (primarily Tavastian) sense "cow abomasum".

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈhɑi̯mɑ/, [ˈhɑ̝i̯mɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑimɑ
  • Syllabification(key): hai‧ma

Noun[edit]

haima

  1. pancreas
