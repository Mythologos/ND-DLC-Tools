Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtrectō
  • attrāctō
  • attrectō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈtrak.toː/, [ät̪ˈt̪räkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈtrak.to/, [äd̪ˈt̪räkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

adtractō (present infinitive adtractāre, perfect active adtractāvī, supine adtractātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attrectō
