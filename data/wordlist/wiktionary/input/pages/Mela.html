Statistics[edit]

  • Mela is the 4820th (tied with 25 other surnames) most common surname in Finland, belonging to 151 individuals, according to February 2023 data from the Digital and Population Data Services Agency of Finland.

Galician[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Carmela: Carme +‎ -ela.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈmɛlɐ]

Proper noun[edit]

Mela f

  1. a diminutive of the female given name Carme

References[edit]

  • “Mela” in Tesouro informatizado da lingua galega. Santiago: ILG.
  • “Mela” in Dicionario de Dicionarios da lingua galega, SLI - ILGA 2006–2013.

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Mel. (abbreviation)

Etymology[edit]

(This etymology is missing or incomplete. Please add to it, or discuss it at the Etymology scriptorium.)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈme.la/, [ˈmɛɫ̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈme.la/, [ˈmɛːlä]

Proper noun[edit]

Mela m sg (genitive Melae); first declension

  1. A Roman masculine cognomen — famously held by:
    1. Pomponius Mela (ob. circa AD 45), the earliest Roman geographer
    2. Annius Mela (cos. AD 103), a Roman senator and consul
