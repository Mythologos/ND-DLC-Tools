Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē +‎ iūrō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈi̯uː.roː/, [d̪eːˈi̯uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈju.ro/, [d̪eˈjuːro]

Verb[edit]

dēiūrō (present infinitive dēiūrāre, perfect active dēiūrāvī, supine dēiūrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēierō
