Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central) [pɾu.nunˈsi.u]
  • IPA(key): (Balearic, Valencian) [pɾo.nunˈsi.o]

Verb[edit]

pronuncio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of pronunciar

Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /proˈnun.t͡ʃo/
  • Rhymes: -untʃo
  • Hyphenation: pro‧nùn‧cio

Verb[edit]

pronuncio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of pronunciare

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈnuːn.ki.oː/, [proːˈnuːŋkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈnun.t͡ʃi.o/, [proˈnun̠ʲt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

prōnūnciō (present infinitive prōnūnciāre, perfect active prōnūnciāvī, supine prōnūnciātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of prōnūntiō
