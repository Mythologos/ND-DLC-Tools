Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek σιμός (simós).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsiː.mus/, [ˈs̠iːmʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsi.mus/, [ˈsiːmus]

Adjective[edit]

sīmus (feminine sīma, neuter sīmum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. snub-nosed
  2. flattened, splayed

Verb[edit]

sīmus

  1. first-person plural present active subjunctive of sum
