Afrikaans[edit]

Noun[edit]

sepe

  1. plural of seep

Esperanto[edit]

Esperanto numbers (edit)
 ←  6 7 8  →

    Ordinal: sepa

Etymology[edit]

sep + -e

Adverb[edit]

sepe

  1. seventhly

Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

sēpe

  1. ablative singular of sēpēs

Adverb[edit]

sēpe (comparative sēpius, superlative sēpissimē)

  1. Alternative form of saepe

References[edit]

Narragansett[edit]

Noun[edit]
