Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad (“to”) +‎ dormiō (“sleep”). Attested in Caelius Aurelianus.[1]

Verb[edit]

addormiō (present infinitive addormīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem (Late Latin)

  1. to begin to sleep
  2. to fall asleep, go to sleep
