Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ vēndō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈu̯eːn.doː/, [d̪iːˈu̯eːn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈven.do/, [d̪iˈvɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

dīvēndō (present infinitive dīvēndere, perfect active dīvēndidī, supine dīvēnditum); third conjugation

  1. to retail (sell in small lots)
