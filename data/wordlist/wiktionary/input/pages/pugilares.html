Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /pu.ɡiˈlaː.reːs/, [pʊɡɪˈɫ̪äːreːs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pu.d͡ʒiˈla.res/, [pud͡ʒiˈläːres]

Noun[edit]

pugilārēs m pl (genitive pugilārium); third declension

  1. Alternative form of pugillārēs

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (i-stem), plural only.

pugilares

Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Latin pugillārēs.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /pu.ɡiˈla.rɛs/
  • Audio(file)
  • Rhymes: -arɛs
  • Syllabification: pu‧gi‧la‧res

Noun[edit]

pugilares m inan

  1. (dated) wallet
    Synonyms: pulares, portfel, portmonetka, podkówka
