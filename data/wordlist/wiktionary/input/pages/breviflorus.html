Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Scientific coinage: brevis +‎ flōs

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /bre.u̯iˈfloː.rus/, [breu̯ɪˈfɫ̪oːrʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /bre.viˈflo.rus/, [breviˈflɔːrus]

Adjective[edit]

breviflōrus (feminine breviflōra, neuter breviflōrum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (New Latin, taxonomy) short-flowered
