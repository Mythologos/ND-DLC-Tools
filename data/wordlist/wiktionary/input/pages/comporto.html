Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conportō

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ portō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /komˈpor.toː/, [kɔmˈpɔrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /komˈpor.to/, [komˈpɔrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

comportō (present infinitive comportāre, perfect active comportāvī, supine comportātum); first conjugation

  1. to carry, transport or convey
  2. to amass or collect
