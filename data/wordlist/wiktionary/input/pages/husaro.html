Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio(file)
  • IPA(key): [huˈsaro]
  • Rhymes: -aro
  • Hyphenation: hu‧sa‧ro

Noun[edit]

husaro (accusative singular husaron, plural husaroj, accusative plural husarojn)

  1. hussar

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • hussārō, huszārius, huszārō, huszszārus

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Hungarian huszár.

Noun[edit]

husārō m (genitive husārōnis); third declension

  1. (Medieval Latin, New Latin, Hungary) hussar

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
