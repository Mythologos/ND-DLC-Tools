Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prō- +‎ sentio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈsen.ti.oː/, [proːˈs̠ɛn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈsen.t͡si.o/, [proˈs̬ɛnt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

prōsentiō (present infinitive prōsentīre, perfect active prōsēnsī, supine prōsēnsum); fourth conjugation

  1. to see beforehand, to perceive beforehand
