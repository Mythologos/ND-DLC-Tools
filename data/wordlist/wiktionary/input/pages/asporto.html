Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /aˈspɔr.to/
  • Rhymes: -ɔrto
  • Hyphenation: a‧spòr‧to

Noun[edit]

asporto m (plural asporti)

  1. removal
  2. takeout (food purchased from a takeaway)

Derived terms[edit]

  • da asporto

Verb[edit]

asporto

  1. first-person singular present indicative of asportare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ab(s)- +‎ portō (“carry, bear”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈpor.toː/, [äs̠ˈpɔrt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈpor.to/, [äsˈpɔrt̪o]

Verb[edit]

asportō (present infinitive asportāre, perfect active asportāvī, supine asportātum); first conjugation

  1. to carry away or take off, remove; transport, train
    Synonyms: trānsportō, trānsferō, trānsvehō, ēvehō, auferō, efferō
