Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /i̯orˈda.ni.a/, [i̯ɔrˈd̪äniä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /jorˈda.ni.a/, [jorˈd̪äːniä]

Proper noun[edit]

Iordania f sg (genitive Iordaniae); first declension

  1. (New Latin) Jordan (a country in Western Asia, in the Middle East)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.
