Estonian[edit]

Noun[edit]

sappa

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Found in Late Latin, but of uncertain origin.

Noun[edit]

sappa f (genitive sappae); first declension

  1. a sort of mattock

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Etymology[edit]

Inherited from Sanskrit सर्प (sarpá). Cognate with Maharastri Prakrit 𑀲𑀧𑁆𑀧 (sappa).

Noun[edit]

sappa m

  1. snake
    Synonyms: ahi, uraga
