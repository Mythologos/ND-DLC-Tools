Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • admoneō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /amˈmo.ne.oː/, [ämˈmɔneoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /amˈmo.ne.o/, [ämˈmɔːneo]

Verb[edit]

ammoneō (present infinitive ammonēre, perfect active ammonuī, supine ammonitum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of admoneō
