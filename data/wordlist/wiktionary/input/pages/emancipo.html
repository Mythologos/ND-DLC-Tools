Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ mancipō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈman.ki.poː/, [eːˈmäŋkɪpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈman.t͡ʃi.po/, [eˈmän̠ʲt͡ʃipo]

Verb[edit]

ēmancipō (present infinitive ēmancipāre, perfect active ēmancipāvī, supine ēmancipātum); first conjugation

  1. to emancipate
  2. to alienate
