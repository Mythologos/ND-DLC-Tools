Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeks.fri.koː/, [ˈɛkfːrɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈeks.fri.ko/, [ˈɛksfriko]

Verb[edit]

exfricō (present infinitive exfricāre, perfect active exfrixī, supine exfricātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of effricō
