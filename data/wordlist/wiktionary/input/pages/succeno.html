Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ cēnō (“to dine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /sukˈkeː.noː/, [s̠ʊkˈkeːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /sutˈt͡ʃe.no/, [sutˈt͡ʃɛːno]

Verb[edit]

succēnō (present infinitive succēnāre, perfect active succēnāvī, supine succēnātum); first conjugation

  1. to dine below, underneath
