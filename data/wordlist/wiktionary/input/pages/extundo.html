Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ tundō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈstun.do/, [ɛkˈs̠t̪ʊn̪d̪ɔ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈstun.do/, [ekˈst̪un̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

extundō (present infinitive extundere, perfect active extudī, supine extūsum); third conjugation

  1. to beat out, strike out, force out
