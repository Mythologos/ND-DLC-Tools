Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adplumbō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ plumbō (“solder with lead”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /apˈplum.boː/, [äpˈpɫ̪ʊmboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /apˈplum.bo/, [äpˈplumbo]

Verb[edit]

applumbō (present infinitive applumbāre, perfect active applumbāvī, supine applumbātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to apply lead to, solder with lead
