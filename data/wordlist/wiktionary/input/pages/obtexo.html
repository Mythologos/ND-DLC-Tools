Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ob- +‎ texō (“to weave”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈtek.soː/, [ɔpˈt̪ɛks̠oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈtek.so/, [obˈt̪ɛkso]

Verb[edit]

obtexō (present infinitive obtexere, perfect active obtexuī); third conjugation, no supine stem, limited passive

  1. (transitive) to weave over, cover over
