Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.pe.doː/, [ˈrɛpɛd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.pe.do/, [ˈrɛːped̪o]

Verb[edit]

repedō (present infinitive repedāre, perfect active repedāvī, supine repedātum); first conjugation

  1. to turn back, retreat, recoil
