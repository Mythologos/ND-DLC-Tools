Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ pondero.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈpon.de.roː/, [präe̯ˈpɔn̪d̪ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈpon.de.ro/, [preˈpɔn̪d̪ero]

Verb[edit]

praeponderō (present infinitive praeponderāre, perfect active praeponderāvī, supine praeponderātum); first conjugation

  1. to preponderate (give more weight or influence to)
