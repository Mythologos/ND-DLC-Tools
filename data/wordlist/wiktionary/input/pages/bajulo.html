Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbai̯.i̯u.loː/, [ˈbäi̯ːʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈba.ju.lo/, [ˈbäːjulo]

Verb[edit]

bajulō (present infinitive bajulāre, perfect active bajulāvī, supine bajulātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of baiulō
