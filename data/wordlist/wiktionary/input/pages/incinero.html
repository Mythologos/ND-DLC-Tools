Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

incinero

  1. first-person singular present indicative of incinerar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Related to cinis (“ashes”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈki.ne.roː/, [ɪŋˈkɪnɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈt͡ʃi.ne.ro/, [in̠ʲˈt͡ʃiːnero]

Verb[edit]

incinerō (present infinitive incinerāre, perfect active incinerāvī, supine incinerātum); first conjugation

  1. to incinerate (burn to ashes)
