Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈpi.ɡri.toː/, [ˈpɪɡrɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpi.ɡri.to/, [ˈpiːɡrit̪o]

Verb[edit]

pigritō (present infinitive pigritāre, perfect active pigritāvī, supine pigritātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of pigritor
