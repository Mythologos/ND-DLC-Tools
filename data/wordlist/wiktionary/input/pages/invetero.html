Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ veterō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈu̯e.te.roː/, [ɪnˈu̯ɛt̪ɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈve.te.ro/, [iɱˈvɛːt̪ero]

Verb[edit]

inveterō (present infinitive inveterāre, perfect active inveterāvī, supine inveterātum); first conjugation

  1. to age (make old, grow old)
