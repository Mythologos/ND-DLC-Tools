English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (place): IPA(key): /ˈɡælə/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)
  • (apple): IPA(key): /ˈɡeɪlə/, /ˈɡælə/, /ˈɡɑːlə/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Gala

  1. (informal) Galashiels, a town in Scotland.

Related terms[edit]

  • Gala Water

Noun[edit]

Gala (plural Galas)

  1. A type of apple, a cross-pollination between Kidd's Orange Red and Golden Delicious.

See also[edit]

  • Gala apples on Wikipedia.Wikipedia

Anagrams[edit]

  • Gaal, agal, alga
