Maltese[edit]

Root
w-t-j
7 terms

Etymology[edit]

From Arabic وَطَّأَ (waṭṭaʔa).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈwɪt.ta/

Verb[edit]

witta (imperfect jwitti, past participle mwitti, verbal noun tutija)

  1. to level, smooth (a path)
  2. to tear down, raze (a structure etc.)
  3. to ease, facilitate (a situation, problem)
