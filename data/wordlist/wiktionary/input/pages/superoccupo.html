Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From super +‎ occupō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.peˈrok.ku.poː/, [s̠ʊpɛˈrɔkːʊpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.peˈrok.ku.po/, [supeˈrɔkːupo]

Verb[edit]

superoccupō (present infinitive superoccupāre, perfect active superoccupāvī, supine superoccupātum); first conjugation

  1. to take by surprise from above
