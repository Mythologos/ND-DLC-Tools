Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From hittus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈhit.ti.oː/, [ˈhɪt̪ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈit.ti.o/, [ˈit̪ːio]

Verb[edit]

hittiō (present infinitive hittīre); fourth conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (intransitive, of dogs) to murmur (especially during hunting)

Conjugation[edit]

No perfect is attested.
