English[edit]

Chemical element
Bh
Previous: seaborgium (Sg)
Next: hassium (Hs)

Etymology[edit]

From Bohr +‎ -ium, named after Niels Bohr.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio(file)
  • enPR: bôr'ēəm, IPA(key): /ˈbɔːɹiəm/
  • Rhymes: -ɔːɹiəm

Noun[edit]

bohrium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
bohrium
Wikipedia
  1. A transuranic chemical element (symbol Bh) with an atomic number of 107.

Synonyms[edit]

  • eka-rhenium (used before the element was discovered)
  • unnilseptium (used before the element was officially named)

Related terms[edit]

  • Bohr
  • bohrate
  • nielsbohrium
