Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • sufficiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈfi.ki.oː/, [s̠ʊfˈfɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈfi.t͡ʃi.o/, [subˈfiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

subficiō (present infinitive subficere, perfect active subfēcī, supine subfectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of sufficiō
