Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • jugō

Etymology[edit]

From iugum (“yoke”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi̯u.ɡoː/, [ˈi̯ʊɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈju.ɡo/, [ˈjuːɡo]

Verb[edit]

iugō (present infinitive iugāre, perfect active iugāvī, supine iugātum); first conjugation

  1. to join, yoke
