Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): pro‧fes‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

professio

  1. profession

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From profiteor.

Noun[edit]

professiō f (genitive professiōnis); third declension

  1. declaration, avowal, profession (act of professing)
  2. occupation, profession (professional employment)

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
