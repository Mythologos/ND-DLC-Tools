Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ honoro.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eks.hoˈnoː.roː/, [ɛks̠(ɦ)ɔˈnoːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.soˈno.ro/, [eɡzoˈnɔːro]

Verb[edit]

exhonōrō (present infinitive exhonōrāre, perfect active exhonōrāvī, supine exhonōrātum); first conjugation

  1. to dishonour, disrespect
  2. to despise
