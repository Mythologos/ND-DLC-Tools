English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Serbia
Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Servia (archaic)

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Medieval Latin Serbia, equivalent to modern Serb +‎ -ia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /ˈsɜː.bi.ə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /ˈsɝ.bi.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Serbia

  2. A country on the Balkan Peninsula in Southeast Europe. Official name: Republic of Serbia. Capital and largest city: Belgrade. Part of Yugoslavia until 1992, then part of Serbia and Montenegro until 2006.
  3. A region of Southeast Europe.
