Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ struo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈres.tru.oː/, [ˈrɛs̠t̪ruoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈres.tru.o/, [ˈrɛst̪ruo]

Verb[edit]

restruō (present infinitive restruere, perfect active restrūxī, supine restrūctum); third conjugation

  1. to restore
  2. to erect again
