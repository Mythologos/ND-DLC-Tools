Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

from con- +‎ sepeliō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kon.seˈpe.li.oː/, [kõːs̠ɛˈpɛlʲioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kon.seˈpe.li.o/, [konseˈpɛːlio]

Verb[edit]

cōnsepeliō (present infinitive cōnsepelīre, perfect active cōnsepelīvī, supine cōnsepultum); fourth conjugation

  1. to bury (or be buried) with others
