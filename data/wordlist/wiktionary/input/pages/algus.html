Noun[edit]

algus (genitive alguse, partitive algust)

  1. beginning

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Related to algeō.

Noun[edit]

algus m (genitive algūs); fourth declension

  1. the subjective feeling of the cold, coldness
  2. (Old Latin) coldness (uncomfortably so)
    Synonym: algor

Declension[edit]

Fourth-declension noun.
