Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈpun.ɡoː/, [kɔmˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈpun.ɡo/, [konˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

conpungō (present infinitive conpungere, perfect active conpūnxī, supine conpūnctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of compungō
