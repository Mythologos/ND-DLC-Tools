Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English absorption), ultimately from Latin absorptiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑbsorptio/, [ˈɑ̝bs̠o̞rpˌt̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): ab‧sorp‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

absorptio

  1. (chemistry, physics) absorption

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • absorbitiō

Etymology[edit]

From absorbeō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈsorp.ti.oː/, [äpˈs̠ɔrpt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈsorp.t͡si.o/, [äbˈsɔrpt̪͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

absorptiō f (genitive absorptiōnis); third declension

  1. (Late Latin) drink, beverage

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
