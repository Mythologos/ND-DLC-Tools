Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /lek.tuˈlaː.ri.us/, [ɫ̪ɛkt̪ʊˈɫ̪äːriʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /lek.tuˈla.ri.us/, [lekt̪uˈläːrius]

Adjective[edit]

lectulārius (feminine lectulāria, neuter lectulārium); first/second-declension adjective

  1. (relational) bed, couch
