Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- (“out of, from”) +‎ molō (“I grind, mill”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈeː.mo.loː/, [ˈeːmɔɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.mo.lo/, [ˈɛːmolo]

Verb[edit]

ēmolō (present infinitive ēmolere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to grind up
  2. to consume by grinding
