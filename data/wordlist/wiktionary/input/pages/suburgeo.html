Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • suburgueō

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ urgeō (“urge”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈbur.ɡe.oː/, [s̠ʊˈbʊrɡeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈbur.d͡ʒe.o/, [suˈburd͡ʒeo]

Verb[edit]

suburgeō (present infinitive suburgēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to drive or urge close to
