Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ findō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /difˈfin.doː/, [d̪ɪfˈfɪn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /difˈfin.do/, [d̪ifˈfin̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

diffindō (present infinitive diffindere, perfect active diffidī, supine diffissum); third conjugation

  1. to divide (cleave asunder)
