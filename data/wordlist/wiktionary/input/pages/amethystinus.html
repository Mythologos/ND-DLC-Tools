Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek ἀμεθῠ́στῐνος (amethústinos, “of amethyst”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.meˈtʰys.ti.nus/, [ämɛˈt̪ʰʏs̠t̪ɪnʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.meˈtis.ti.nus/, [ämeˈt̪ist̪inus]

Adjective[edit]

amethystinus (feminine amethystina, neuter amethystinum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. of the color of amethyst, amethyst green
  2. set or adorned with amethyst
