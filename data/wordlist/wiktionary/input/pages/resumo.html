Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ sūmō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈsuː.moː/, [rɛˈs̠uːmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈsu.mo/, [reˈs̬uːmo]

Verb[edit]

resūmō (present infinitive resūmere, perfect active resūmpsī, supine resūmptum); third conjugation

  1. to take again; take back
  2. to resume
  3. to recover
