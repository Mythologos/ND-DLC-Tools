Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈdul.koː/, [ɔbˈd̪ʊɫ̪koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈdul.ko/, [obˈd̪ulko]

Verb[edit]

obdulcō (present infinitive obdulcāre, perfect active obdulcāvī, supine obdulcātum); first conjugation

  1. to sweeten
