Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • muraena

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek μύραινα (múraina).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /muˈreː.na/, [mʊˈreːnä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /muˈre.na/, [muˈrɛːnä]

Noun[edit]

murēna f (genitive murēnae); first declension

  1. The moray eel or the lamprey, eaten as food.
  2. A black stripe, as found on the fish.

Declension[edit]
