Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From torculum +‎ -ō. Attested from the writings of Venantius Fortunatus.

Verb[edit]

torculō (present infinitive torculāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem (Late Latin)

  1. to press
