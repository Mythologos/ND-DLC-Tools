Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈfar.ki.oː/, [ĩːˈfärkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈfar.t͡ʃi.o/, [iɱˈfärt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

īnfarciō (present infinitive īnfarcīre, perfect active īnfarsī, supine īnfartum or īnfarctum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of īnferciō
