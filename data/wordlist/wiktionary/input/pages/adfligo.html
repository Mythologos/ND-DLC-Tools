Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • afflīgō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈfliː.ɡoː/, [ät̪ˈflʲiːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈfli.ɡo/, [äd̪ˈfliːɡo]

Verb[edit]

adflīgō (present infinitive adflīgere, perfect active adflīxī, supine adflīctum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of afflīgō
