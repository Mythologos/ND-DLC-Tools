Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

fāgus +‎ -eus.

Adjective[edit]

fāgeus (feminine fāgea, neuter fāgeum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. of beech; beechen
    Synonyms: fāginus, fāgineus
