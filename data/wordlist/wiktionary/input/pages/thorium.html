English[edit]

Chemical element
Th
Previous: actinium (Ac)
Next: protactinium (Pa)

Etymology[edit]

Thor +‎ -ium

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: thôr'ēəm, IPA(key): /ˈθɔːɹiəm/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɔːɹiəm

Noun[edit]

thorium (usually uncountable, plural thoriums)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
thorium
Wikipedia
  2. A chemical element (symbol Th) with atomic number 90: a weakly radioactive, malleable, moderately hard silvery metal that tarnishes black when exposed to air.

Synonyms[edit]

  • thorinum (former alternative form; obsolete)
