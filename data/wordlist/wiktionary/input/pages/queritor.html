Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkʷe.ri.tor/, [ˈkʷɛrɪt̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkwe.ri.tor/, [ˈkwɛːrit̪or]

Etymology 1[edit]

queror +‎ -tō

Verb[edit]

queritor (present infinitive queritārī, perfect active queritātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to complain
  2. to complain excessively
  3. to make a public outcry, cry out in protest
