Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Mariānus

Etymology[edit]

Marius +‎ -ānus

Adjective[edit]

mariānus (feminine mariāna, neuter mariānum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative form of Mariānus
