  1. plural of far

Azerbaijani[edit]

Other scripts
Cyrillic фарс
Abjad فارس

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [fɑrs]
  • Audio (Baku)(file)
  • Audio(file)

Noun[edit]

fars (definite accusative farsı, plural farslar)

  1. Persian (person)
    Farslar İran əhalisinin təqribən yarısını təşkil edirlər.
    Persians constitute around half of the Iranian population.
  2. (in izafet II compounds) Persian
    fars dili ― Persian language
    fars şairləri ― Persian poets

Danish[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From French farce (“stuffing”), from Late Latin farsa, from Latin farcīre (“to cram, stuff”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /fars/, [fɑːˀs]

Noun[edit]

fars c (singular definite farsen, plural indefinite farser)

  1. minced meat (bound with flour and eggs)
  2. forcemeat

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From fartus, participle of farcio.

Noun[edit]

fars f (genitive fartis); third declension

  1. stuffing
  2. (figuratively) mincemeat
