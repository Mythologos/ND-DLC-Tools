Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.paˈkiːs.kor/, [d̪eːpäˈkiːs̠kɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.paˈt͡ʃis.kor/, [d̪epäˈt͡ʃiskor]

Verb[edit]

dēpacīscor (present infinitive dēpacīscī, perfect active dēpactus sum); third conjugation, deponent

  1. Alternative form of dēpecīscor
