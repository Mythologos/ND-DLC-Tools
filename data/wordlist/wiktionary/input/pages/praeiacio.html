Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈi̯a.ki.oː/, [präe̯ˈi̯äkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈja.t͡ʃi.o/, [preˈjäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

praeiaciō (present infinitive praeiacere, perfect active praeiaēcī, supine praeiaectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of praeicio
