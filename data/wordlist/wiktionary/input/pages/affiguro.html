Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

affiguro

  1. first-person singular present indicative of affigurare

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adfigūrō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards, at”) +‎ figūrō (“form, fashion, shape”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /af.fiˈɡuː.roː/, [äfːɪˈɡuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /af.fiˈɡu.ro/, [äfːiˈɡuːro]

Verb[edit]

affigūrō (present infinitive affigūrāre, perfect active affigūrāvī, supine affigūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to form or fashion a word after an analogy of something else
