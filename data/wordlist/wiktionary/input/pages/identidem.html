Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From idem n (“the same”) + et + idem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈden.ti.dem/, [ɪˈd̪ɛn̪t̪ɪd̪ɛ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈden.ti.dem/, [iˈd̪ɛn̪t̪id̪em]

Adverb[edit]

identidem (not comparable)

  1. again and again, repeatedly, continually

Related terms[edit]

  • identitās
  • identicus
  • itidem
