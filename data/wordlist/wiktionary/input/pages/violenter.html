English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈvaɪələntə(ɹ)/

Adjective[edit]

violenter

  1. (nonstandard) comparative form of violent: more violent

Anagrams[edit]

  • lovertine

French[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the adjective violent.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /vjɔ.lɑ̃.te/
  • Audio(file)

Verb[edit]

violenter

  1. (transitive) to subject to violence, commit violence upon
  2. (transitive, criticized) to rape, sexually assault

Latin[edit]

Adverb[edit]

violenter (comparative violentius, superlative violentissimē)

  1. violently, impetuously, vehemently
