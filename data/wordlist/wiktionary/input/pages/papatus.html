Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

papattaa +‎ -us

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɑpɑtus/, [ˈpɑ̝pɑ̝t̪us̠]
  • Rhymes: -ɑpɑtus
  • Syllabification(key): pa‧pa‧tus

Noun[edit]

papatus (colloquial)

  1. spouting, rambling

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Frp, pāpa (“pope”) +‎ -ātus (forming abstract nouns).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /paˈpa.tus/, [päˈpäːt̪us]

Noun[edit]

pāpātus m (genitive pāpātūs); fourth declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) the office of pope, the papacy

Declension[edit]

Fourth-declension noun.
