Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • interjaciō

Etymology[edit]

From inter- +‎ iaciō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈi̯a.ki.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈi̯äkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈja.t͡ʃi.o/, [in̪t̪erˈjäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

interiaciō (present infinitive interiacere, perfect active interiēcī, supine interiectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to interpose, intersperse, intervene, intermingle
