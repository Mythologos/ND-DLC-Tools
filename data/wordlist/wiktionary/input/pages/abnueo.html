Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • abnuō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈnu.e.oː/, [äbˈnueoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈnu.e.o/, [äbˈnuːeo]

Verb[edit]

abnueō (present infinitive abnuēre, perfect active abnuī, supine abnuitum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of abnuō
