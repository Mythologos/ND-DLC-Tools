Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /eˈma.t͡ʃo/
  • Rhymes: -atʃo
  • Hyphenation: e‧mà‧cio

Verb[edit]

emacio

  1. first-person singular present indicative of emaciare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ maciēs (“leanness”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈma.ki.oː/, [eːˈmäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈma.t͡ʃi.o/, [eˈmäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

ēmaciō (present infinitive ēmaciāre, perfect active ēmaciāvī, supine ēmaciātum); first conjugation

  1. to emaciate, to make lean
