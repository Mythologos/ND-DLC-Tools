Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Γεώργιος (Geṓrgios).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡeˈoːr.ɡi.us/, [ɡeˈoːrɡiʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /d͡ʒeˈor.d͡ʒi.us/, [d͡ʒeˈɔrd͡ʒius]

Proper noun[edit]

Geōrgius m (genitive Geōrgiī or Geōrgī); second declension

  1. a male given name from Ancient Greek, equivalent to English George

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun.
