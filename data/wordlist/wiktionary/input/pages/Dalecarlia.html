English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From New Latin Dalecarlia.

Proper noun[edit]

Dalecarlia

  1. A province in central Sweden

Synonyms[edit]

  • Dalarna

Translations[edit]

province of Sweden

  • Latin: Dalecarlia f
  • Swedish: Dalarna (sv)

Latin[edit]

Latin Wikipedia has an article on:
Dalecarlia
Wikipedia la

Etymology[edit]

New Latin, Latinized from Old Swedish Dalarna.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /da.leˈkar.li.a/, [d̪äɫ̪ɛˈkärlʲiä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /da.leˈkar.li.a/, [d̪äleˈkärliä]

Proper noun[edit]

Dalecarlia f sg (genitive Dalecarliae); first declension

  1. Dalarna, province of Sweden

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun, singular only.

Case Singular
Nominative Dalecarlia
Genitive Dalecarliae
Dative Dalecarliae
Accusative Dalecarliam
Ablative Dalecarliā
Vocative Dalecarlia

Derived terms[edit]
