Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

vellico

  1. first-person singular present indicative of vellicare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From vellō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯el.li.koː/, [ˈu̯ɛlːʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvel.li.ko/, [ˈvɛlːiko]

Verb[edit]

vellicō (present infinitive vellicāre, perfect active vellicāvī, supine vellicātum); first conjugation

  1. to pluck, twitch, pinch, nip
  2. to suck (of bees)
