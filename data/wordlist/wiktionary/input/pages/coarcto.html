Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈark.toː/, [koˈärkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈark.to/, [koˈärkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

coarctō (present infinitive coarctāre, perfect active coarctāvī, supine coarctātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of coartō
