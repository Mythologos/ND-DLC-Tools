Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /leˈd͡ʒi.sta/
  • Rhymes: -ista
  • Hyphenation: le‧gì‧sta

Noun[edit]

legista m or f by sense (masculine plural legisti, feminine plural legiste)

  1. (law) jurist

Further reading[edit]

  • legista in Treccani.it – Vocabolario Treccani on line, Istituto dell'Enciclopedia Italiana

Anagrams[edit]

  • gelasti, legasti, sgelati, siglate, slegati

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From lēx (“law”) +‎ -ista (“-ist”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /leːˈɡis.ta/, [ɫ̪eːˈɡɪs̠t̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /leˈd͡ʒis.ta/, [leˈd͡ʒist̪ä]

Noun[edit]

lēgista m (genitive lēgistae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) one skilled in law, legist

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
