Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Possibly from vinum (“wine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯iːˈni.ki.us/, [u̯iːˈnɪkiʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /viˈni.t͡ʃi.us/, [viˈniːt͡ʃius]

Proper noun[edit]

Vīnicius m sg (genitive Vīniciī or Vīnicī); second declension

  1. a Roman nomen gentile, gens or "family name"

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun, singular only.
