Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pa.roː/, [ˈkɔmpäroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.pa.ro/, [ˈkɔnpäro]

Verb[edit]

conparō (present infinitive conparāre, perfect active conparāvī, supine conparātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of comparō
