Czech[edit]

Czech Wikipedia has an article on:
anachronismus
Wikipedia cs

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈanaxronɪzmus]

Noun[edit]

anachronismus m inan

  1. anachronism (a person or thing which seems to belong to a different time or period of time)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek ἀναχρονισμός (anakhronismós).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.na.kʰroˈnis.mus/, [änäkʰrɔˈnɪs̠mʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.na.kroˈnis.mus/, [änäkroˈnizmus]

Noun[edit]

anachronismus m (genitive anachronismī); second declension

  1. (New Latin) anachronism
