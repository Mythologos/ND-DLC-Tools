Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From victima.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯ik.ti.moː/, [ˈu̯ɪkt̪ɪmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvik.ti.mo/, [ˈvikt̪imo]

Verb[edit]

victimō (present infinitive victimāre, perfect active victimāvī, supine victimātum); first conjugation

  1. to offer for sacrifice
