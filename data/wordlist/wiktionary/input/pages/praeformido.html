Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯.forˈmiː.doː/, [präe̯fɔrˈmiːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pre.forˈmi.do/, [preforˈmiːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

praeformīdō (present infinitive praeformīdāre, perfect active praeformīdāvī, supine praeformīdātum); first conjugation

  1. to dread beforehand, fear beforehand
