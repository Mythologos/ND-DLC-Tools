Esperanto[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From imiti +‎ -o.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [iˈmito]
  • Rhymes: -ito
  • Hyphenation: i‧mi‧to

Noun[edit]

imito (accusative singular imiton, plural imitoj, accusative plural imitojn)

  1. imitation
    Synonym: imitado

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Rare active-voice form of the deponent verb imitor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈi.mi.toː/, [ˈɪmɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈi.mi.to/, [ˈiːmit̪o]

Verb[edit]

imitō (present infinitive imitāre, perfect active imitāvī, supine imitātum); first conjugation

  1. to imitate, copy, mimic
