English[edit]

Noun[edit]

sequestre (plural sequestres)

  1. Obsolete form of sequester.

Verb[edit]

sequestre (third-person singular simple present sequestres, present participle sequestring, simple past and past participle sequestred)

  1. Obsolete form of sequester.

Anagrams[edit]

  • requestes, sequester

Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

sequestre n (genitive sequestris); third declension

  1. Alternative form of sequestrum (“escrow”)

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun (neuter, “pure” i-stem).

Case Singular Plural
Nominative sequestre sequestria
Genitive sequestris sequestrium
Dative sequestrī sequestribus
Accusative sequestre sequestria
Ablative sequestrī sequestribus
Vocative sequestre sequestria
