  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): po‧si‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

positio

  1. position

Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin positiōnem.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /poˈzit.t͡sjo/
  • Rhymes: -ittsjo
  • Hyphenation: po‧sì‧tio

Noun[edit]

positio f (plural positiones)

  1. (linguistics) position (of a vowel)

Derived terms[edit]

  • positio debilis
  • positio fortis

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the supine stem of pōnō (“put, place”) +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /poˈsi.ti.oː/, [pɔˈs̠ɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /poˈsit.t͡si.o/, [poˈs̬it̪ː͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

positiō f (genitive positiōnis); third declension

  1. position, place
  2. framing
  3. theme
  4. planting (of crops)
  5. lie (of land)
  6. attitude
