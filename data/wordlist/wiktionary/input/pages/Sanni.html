Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Originally a diminutive of Aleksandra.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɑnːi/, [ˈs̠ɑ̝nːi]
  • Rhymes: -ɑnːi
  • Syllabification(key): San‧ni

Proper noun[edit]

Sanni

  1. a female given name

Statistics[edit]

  • Sanni is the 109th most common female given name in Finland, belonging to 7,257 female individuals (and as a middle name to 969 more), according to February 2023 data from the Digital and Population Data Services Agency of Finland.
