Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Borrowed from Latin capsula.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈka.psu.la/
  • Rhymes: -apsula
  • Hyphenation: cà‧psu‧la

Noun[edit]

capsula f (plural capsule)

  1. (botany, anatomy) capsule (all senses)
  2. bottle cap
  3. crown (in a tooth)
  4. pod, boll

Derived terms[edit]

  • incapsulare

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From capsa +‎ -ula.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkap.su.la/, [ˈkäps̠ʊɫ̪ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkap.su.la/, [ˈkäpsulä]

Noun[edit]

capsula f (genitive capsulae); first declension

  1. Diminutive of capsa (“box, chest”)

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
