Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsae̯.kʷoː/, [ɛkˈs̠äe̯kʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈse.kwo/, [eɡˈzɛːkwo]

Verb[edit]

exaequō (present infinitive exaequāre, perfect active exaequāvī, supine exaequātum); first conjugation

  1. to equalize
  2. to balance or compensate (for)
