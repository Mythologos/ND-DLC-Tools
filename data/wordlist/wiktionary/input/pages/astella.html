Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

astua +‎ -ella (frequentative aspect)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑstelːɑˣ/, [ˈɑ̝s̠te̞lːɑ̝(ʔ)]
  • Rhymes: -ɑstelːɑ
  • Syllabification(key): as‧tel‧la

Verb[edit]

astella

  1. to pace around, pace about

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From astula +‎ -la.

Noun[edit]

astella f (genitive astellae); first declension (Late Latin)

  1. little splinter

Descendants[edit]

  • Dalmatian:
    • sčela
  • Italo-Romance:
    • Italian: stella (“splinter”) (central dialects)
  • Gallo-Italic:
    • Lombard: stèla
    • Piedmontese: stèla
  • Gallo-Romance:
    • Franco-Provençal: ètèla
    • Old French: astelle
      • French: attelle
  • Occitano-Romance:

    • Occitan: estèla
  • Ibero-Romance:

    • Spanish: astilla
