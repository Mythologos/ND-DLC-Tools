Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Found in Late Latin. From follis +‎ -icō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfol.li.koː/, [ˈfɔlːʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfol.li.ko/, [ˈfɔlːiko]

Verb[edit]

follicō (present infinitive follicāre, perfect active follicāvī, supine follicātum); first conjugation

  1. to pant, blow, breathe
