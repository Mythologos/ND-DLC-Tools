Cebuano[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Hyphenation: ba‧u‧lo

Noun[edit]

baulo

  1. bluefin trevally (Caranx melampygus)
  2. bigeye trevally (Caranx sexfasciatus)

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the onomatopoeia "bau". Compare baubor.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈbau̯.loː/, [ˈbäu̯ɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈbau̯.lo/, [ˈbäːu̯lo]

Verb[edit]

baulō (present infinitive baulāre, perfect active baulāvī); first conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. (Medieval Latin) to bark
    Synonym: baubor
