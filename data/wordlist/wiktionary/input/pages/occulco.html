Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ob- +‎ calcō (“trample”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /okˈkul.koː/, [ɔkˈkʊɫ̪koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /okˈkul.ko/, [okˈkulko]

Verb[edit]

occulcō (present infinitive occulcāre, perfect active occulcāvī, supine occulcātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to tread down, trample
