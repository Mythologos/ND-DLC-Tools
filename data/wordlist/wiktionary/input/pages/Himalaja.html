Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Sanskrit हिमालय (himālaya).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈhimɑlɑjɑ/, [ˈhimɑ̝ˌlɑ̝jɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑjɑ
  • Syllabification(key): Hi‧ma‧la‧ja

Proper noun[edit]

Himalaja

  1. Himalaya (one mountain in the mountain range)
  2. Himalayas (mountain range)

 Himalaja on German Wikipedia

Alternative forms[edit]

  • Himalaya

Etymology[edit]

Learned borrowing from Sanskrit हिमालय (himālaya).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /hiˈma.la.ja/, (also) /hiˈmaː-/, /-laɪ̯.a/, (rare) /himaˈlaːja/
  • Audio(file)

Proper noun[edit]

der Himalaja m (proper noun, strong, usually definite, definite genitive des Himalaja or des Himalajas)

  1. Himalayas (a mountain range in south-central Asia)
