Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /anˈɡo.ri.oː/, [äŋˈɡɔrioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /anˈɡo.ri.o/, [äŋˈɡɔːrio]

Verb[edit]

angoriō (present infinitive angoriāre, perfect active angoriāvī, supine angoriātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of angāriō
