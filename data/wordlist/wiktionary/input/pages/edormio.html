Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ dormiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈdor.mi.oː/, [eːˈd̪ɔrmioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈdor.mi.o/, [eˈd̪ɔrmio]

Verb[edit]

ēdormiō (present infinitive ēdormīre, perfect active ēdormīvī or ēdormiī, supine ēdormītum); fourth conjugation, no passive

  1. to sleep out, away, off or through
