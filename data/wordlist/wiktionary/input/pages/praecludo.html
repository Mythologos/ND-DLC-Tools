Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

prae- +‎ clūdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈkluː.doː/, [präe̯ˈkɫ̪uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈklu.do/, [preˈkluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

praeclūdō (present infinitive praeclūdere, perfect active praeclūsī, supine praeclūsum); third conjugation

  1. to close or block
  2. to forbid access to
