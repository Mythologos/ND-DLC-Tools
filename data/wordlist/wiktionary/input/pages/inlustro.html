Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈluːs.troː/, [ɪnˈɫ̪uːs̠t̪roː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈlus.tro/, [inˈlust̪ro]

Verb[edit]

inlūstrō (present infinitive inlūstrāre, perfect active inlūstrāvī, supine inlūstrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of illūstrō
