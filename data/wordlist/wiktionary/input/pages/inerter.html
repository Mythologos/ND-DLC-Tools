French[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From inerte +‎ -er.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /i.nɛʁ.te/
  • Audio(file)

Verb[edit]

inerter

  1. to replace a dangerous gas with a safe one
  2. to make inert or safe (especially radioactive waste)

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈner.ter/, [ɪˈnɛrt̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈner.ter/, [iˈnɛrt̪er]

Adverb[edit]

inerter (comparative inertius, superlative inertissimē)

  1. inactively, unskillfully
