Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /daˈuː.ri.kus/, [d̪äˈuːrɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /daˈu.ri.kus/, [d̪äˈuːrikus]

Adjective[edit]

Daūricus (feminine Daūrica, neuter Daūricum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Alternative spelling of Dahūricus
