Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ūrō (“burn”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈus.tu.loː/, [ˈʊs̠t̪ʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈus.tu.lo/, [ˈust̪ulo]

Verb[edit]

ustulō (present infinitive ustulāre, perfect active ustulāvī, supine ustulātum); first conjugation

  1. to scorch or singe
  2. to burn up; consume with fire
