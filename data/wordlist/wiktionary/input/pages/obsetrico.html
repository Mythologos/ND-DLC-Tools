Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ob.seˈtriː.koː/, [ɔps̠ɛˈt̪riːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ob.seˈtri.ko/, [obseˈt̪riːko]

Verb[edit]

obsetrīcō (present infinitive obsetrīcāre, perfect active obsetrīcāvī, supine obsetrīcātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of obstētrīcō
