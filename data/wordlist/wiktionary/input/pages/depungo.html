Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From de- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈpun.ɡoː/, [d̪eːˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈpun.ɡo/, [d̪eˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

dēpungō (present infinitive dēpungere, perfect active dēpūnxī, supine dēpūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to mark off, to designate
