Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈsop.toː/, [ɛkˈs̠ɔpt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsop.to/, [eɡˈzɔpt̪o]

Verb[edit]

exoptō (present infinitive exoptāre, perfect active exoptāvī, supine exoptātum); first conjugation

  1. to long for (greatly desire)
