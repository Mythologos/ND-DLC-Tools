Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- (“before”) +‎ rogō (“ask; request”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈprae̯.ro.ɡoː/, [ˈpräe̯rɔɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈpre.ro.ɡo/, [ˈprɛːroɡo]

Verb[edit]

praerogō (present infinitive praerogāre, perfect active praerogāvī, supine praerogātum); first conjugation

  1. to ask before someone else, ask first
  2. to pay beforehand or in advance
