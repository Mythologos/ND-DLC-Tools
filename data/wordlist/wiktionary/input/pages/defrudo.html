Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈfruː.doː/, [d̪eːˈfruːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈfru.do/, [d̪eˈfruːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

dēfrūdō (present infinitive dēfrūdāre, perfect active dēfrūdāvī, supine dēfrūdātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēfraudō
