Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ coquo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ko.kʷoː/, [ˈrɛkɔkʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.ko.kwo/, [ˈrɛːkokwo]

Verb[edit]

recoquō (present infinitive recoquere, perfect active recoxī, supine recoctum); third conjugation

  1. to recook etc.
