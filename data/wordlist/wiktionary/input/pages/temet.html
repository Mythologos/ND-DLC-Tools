Hungarian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From the tem variant of the verb töm +‎ -et (causative suffix).[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈtɛmɛt]
  • Hyphenation: te‧met
  • Rhymes: -ɛt

Verb[edit]

temet

  1. (transitive) to bury, inter (a dead body or some object; optionally into something: -ba/-be)
