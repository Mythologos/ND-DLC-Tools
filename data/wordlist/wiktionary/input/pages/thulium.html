thulium

Previous: erbium (Er)

Etymology[edit]

Thule (a poetic name for Scandinavia) +‎ -ium

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: thū'lēəm, IPA(key): /ˈθjuːliəm/; enPR: tho͞o'lēəm, IPA(key): /ˈθuːliəm/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -uːliəm

Noun[edit]

thulium (uncountable)

  2. A metallic chemical element (symbol Tm) with atomic number 69: a fairly soft, easily workable metal with a bright silvery-gray lustre.

Derived terms[edit]

  • thulia
  • thulium oxide

Related terms[edit]

  • Thule
  • thulite
