Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /par.u̯iˈpen.doː/, [päru̯ɪˈpɛn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /par.viˈpen.do/, [pärviˈpɛn̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

parvipendō (present infinitive parvipendere, perfect active parvipendī, supine parvipēnsum); third conjugation

  1. to slight (pay little attention to)
