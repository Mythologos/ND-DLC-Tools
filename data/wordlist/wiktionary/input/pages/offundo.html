Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ofˈfun.doː/, [ɔfˈfʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ofˈfun.do/, [ofˈfun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

offundō (present infinitive offundere, perfect active offūdī, supine offūsum); third conjugation

  1. to pour or spread over, out, down etc.
