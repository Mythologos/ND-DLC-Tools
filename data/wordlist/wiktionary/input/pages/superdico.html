Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • supradico

Etymology[edit]

From super- +‎ dīcō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /su.perˈdiː.koː/, [s̠ʊpɛrˈd̪iːkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /su.perˈdi.ko/, [superˈd̪iːko]

Verb[edit]

superdīcō (present infinitive superdīcere, perfect active superdīxī, supine superdictum); third conjugation, irregular short imperative

  1. to say in addition
