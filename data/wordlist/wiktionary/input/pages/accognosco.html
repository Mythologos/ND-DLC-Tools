Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- +‎ cognōsco.

Verb[edit]

accognōscō (present infinitive accognōscere, perfect active accognōvī, supine accognitum); third conjugation (Late Latin)[1]

  1. to recognize, acknowledge
