Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈpun.ɡoː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈpun.ɡo/, [d̪isˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

dispungō (present infinitive dispungere, perfect active dispūnxī, supine dispūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to check off the credits and debits of an account
  2. to examine, revise an account
