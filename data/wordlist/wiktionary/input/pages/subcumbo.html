Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈkum.boː/, [s̠ʊpˈkʊmboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈkum.bo/, [subˈkumbo]

Verb[edit]

subcumbō (present infinitive subcumbere, perfect active subcubuī, supine subcubitum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of succumbō
