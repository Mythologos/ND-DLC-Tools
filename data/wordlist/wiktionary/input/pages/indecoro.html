Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈde.ko.roː/, [ɪn̪ˈd̪ɛkɔroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈde.ko.ro/, [in̪ˈd̪ɛːkoro]

Etymology 1[edit]

in- +‎ decorō (“to decorate, honour”)

Verb[edit]

indecorō (present infinitive indecorāre); first conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (transitive) to disgrace, disfigure
    Synonym: dēdecorō
