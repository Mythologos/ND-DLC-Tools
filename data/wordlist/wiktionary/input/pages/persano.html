Latin[edit]

Verb[edit]

persānō (present infinitive persānāre, perfect active persānāvī, supine persānātum); first conjugation

  1. Emphatic form of sānō: to heal thoroughly; to heal completely
    • c. 65 CE, Seneca the Younger, Epistulae morales ad Lucilium I.8.2:
      quae etiam si persanata non sunt
