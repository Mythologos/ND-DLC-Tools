Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ tergeō (“rub or wipe off, cleanse”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kunˈter.ɡe.oː/, [kɪrkʊn̪ˈt̪ɛrɡeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kunˈter.d͡ʒe.o/, [t͡ʃirkun̪ˈt̪ɛrd͡ʒeo]

Verb[edit]

circumtergeō (present infinitive circumtergēre); second conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to wipe around
