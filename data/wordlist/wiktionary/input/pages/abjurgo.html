Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /abˈi̯uːr.ɡoː/, [äbˈi̯uːrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /abˈjur.ɡo/, [äbˈjurɡo]

Verb[edit]

abjūrgō (present infinitive abjūrgāre, perfect active abjūrgāvī, supine abjūrgātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of abiūrgō
