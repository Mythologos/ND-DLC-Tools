Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ vigēscō (“I flourish, thrive”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deː.u̯iˈɡeːs.koː/, [d̪eːu̯ɪˈɡeːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.viˈd͡ʒes.ko/, [d̪eviˈd͡ʒɛsko]

Verb[edit]

dēvigēscō (present infinitive dēvigēscere, perfect active deviguī); third conjugation, no passive, no supine stem

  1. to lose one's vigor
