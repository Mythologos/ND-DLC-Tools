Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From advehō +‎ -tō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈu̯ek.toː/, [äd̪ˈu̯ɛkt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈvek.to/, [äd̪ˈvɛkt̪o]

Verb[edit]

advectō (present infinitive advectāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to carry, bring or convey to somewhere often; import
