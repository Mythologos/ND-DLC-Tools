Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ex- +‎ futuō (“fuck”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /efˈfu.tu.oː/, [ɛfˈfʊt̪uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /efˈfu.tu.o/, [efˈfuːt̪uo]

Verb[edit]

effutuō (present infinitive effutuere, perfect active effutuī, supine effutūtum); third conjugation

  1. (vulgar) to waste in debauchery
  2. (vulgar) to be shagged out
