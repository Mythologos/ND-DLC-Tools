Alternative forms[edit]

Proper noun[edit]

Nava (plural Navas)

  1. A town in central Asturias, in northern Spain
  2. A surname.

Etymology[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈnaː.u̯a/, [ˈnäːu̯ä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈna.va/, [ˈnäːvä]

Proper noun[edit]

Nāva m sg (genitive Nāvae); first declension

  1. The river Nahe, in Germany
