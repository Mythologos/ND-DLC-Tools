Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ colloco

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈkol.lo.koː/, [rɛˈkɔlːʲɔkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈkol.lo.ko/, [reˈkɔlːoko]

Verb[edit]

recollocō (present infinitive recollocāre, perfect active recollocāvī, supine recollocātum); first conjugation

  1. to replace
