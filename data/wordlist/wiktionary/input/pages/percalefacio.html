Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ calefacio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.ka.leˈfa.ki.oː/, [pɛrkäɫ̪ɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.ka.leˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [perkäleˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

percalefaciō (present infinitive percalefacere, perfect active percalefēcī, supine percalefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to warm or heat through or thoroughly
