Etymology[edit]

From New Latin metatarsus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌmɛtəˈtɑː(ɹ)səs/
  • Rhymes: -ɑː(ɹ)səs

Noun[edit]

metatarsus (plural metatarsi)

  1. The part of the foot between the toes and the ankle, especially its five bones.
    Synonym: instep

Coordinate terms[edit]

  • metacarpus
  • tarsus

Derived terms[edit]

  • metatarsal
  • metatarso- (metatars-)
