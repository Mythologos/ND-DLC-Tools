Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub- +‎ servio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈser.u̯i.oː/, [s̠ʊpˈs̠ɛru̯ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈser.vi.o/, [subˈsɛrvio]

Verb[edit]

subserviō (present infinitive subservīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem, impersonal in the passive

  1. (with dative) to serve; be subservient to
