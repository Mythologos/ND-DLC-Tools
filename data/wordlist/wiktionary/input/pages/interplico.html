Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈter.pli.koː/, [ɪn̪ˈt̪ɛrplʲɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈter.pli.ko/, [in̪ˈt̪ɛrpliko]

Verb[edit]

interplicō (present infinitive interplicāre, perfect active interplicāvī, supine interplicātum); first conjugation

  1. to fold or plait between, interweave; surround
