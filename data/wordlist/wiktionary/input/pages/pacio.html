Latin[edit]

Noun[edit]

paciō f (genitive paciōnis); third declension

  1. Alternative form of pactiō

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.

Welsh[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • paco

Etymology[edit]

From English pack +‎ -io.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpakjɔ/

Verb[edit]

pacio (first-person singular present paciaf)

  1. to pack
