Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From comitium +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /koˈmi.ti.oː/, [kɔˈmɪt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /koˈmit.t͡si.o/, [koˈmit̪ː͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

comitiō (present infinitive comitiāre, perfect active comitiāvī, supine comitiātum); first conjugation

  1. to go to the comitium
  2. to offer the sacrifice after which the comitia were held
