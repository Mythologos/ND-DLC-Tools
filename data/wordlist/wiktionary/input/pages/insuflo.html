Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.su.floː/, [ˈĩːs̠ʊfɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.su.flo/, [ˈinsuflo]

Verb[edit]

īnsuflō (present infinitive īnsuflāre, perfect active īnsuflāvī, supine īnsuflātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of īnsufflō
