Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

honor +‎ -ficō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ho.noːˈri.fi.koː/, [hɔnoːˈrɪfɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /o.noˈri.fi.ko/, [onoˈriːfiko]

Verb[edit]

honōrificō (present infinitive honōrificāre, perfect active honōrificāvī, supine honōrificātum); first conjugation

  1. to honour
