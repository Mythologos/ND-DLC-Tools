Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dē- +‎ serpō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈser.poː/, [d̪eːˈs̠ɛrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈser.po/, [d̪eˈs̬ɛrpo]

Verb[edit]

dēserpō (present infinitive dēserpere); third conjugation, no passive, no perfect or supine stem

  1. to creep down
    • Publius Papinius Statius, Thebais 6.586
