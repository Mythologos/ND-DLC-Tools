Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From aqua (“water”) +‎ -eus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈa.kʷe.us/, [ˈäkʷeʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈa.kwe.us/, [ˈäːkweus]

Adjective[edit]

aqueus (feminine aquea, neuter aqueum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. watery
  2. aqueous
