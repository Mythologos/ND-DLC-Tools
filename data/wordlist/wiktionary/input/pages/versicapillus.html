Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /u̯er.si.kaˈpil.lus/, [u̯ɛrs̠ɪkäˈpɪlːʲʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ver.si.kaˈpil.lus/, [versikäˈpilːus]

Adjective[edit]

versicapillus (feminine versicapilla, neuter versicapillum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. having hair that has turned grey, greying
