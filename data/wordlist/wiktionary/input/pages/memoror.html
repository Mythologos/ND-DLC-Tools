Ido[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /memoˈrɔr/

Verb[edit]

memoror

  1. future infinitive of memorar

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈme.mo.ror/, [ˈmɛmɔrɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈme.mo.ror/, [ˈmɛːmoror]

Verb[edit]

memoror

  1. first-person singular present passive indicative of memorō

Verb[edit]

memoror (present infinitive memorārī, perfect active memorātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to remember
  2. to be mindful of
