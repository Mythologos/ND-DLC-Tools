Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.moː/, [ˈɛks̠krɛmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈek.skre.mo/, [ˈɛkskremo]

Verb[edit]

excremō (present infinitive excremāre, perfect active excremāvī, supine excremātum); first conjugation

  1. to burn
