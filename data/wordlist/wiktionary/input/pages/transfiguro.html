Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /trans.fiˈɡuː.roː/, [t̪rä̃ːfːɪˈɡuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /trans.fiˈɡu.ro/, [t̪ränsfiˈɡuːro]

Verb[edit]

trānsfigūrō (present infinitive trānsfigūrāre, perfect active trānsfigūrāvī, supine trānsfigūrātum); first conjugation

  1. to transform, transfigure or metamorphose
