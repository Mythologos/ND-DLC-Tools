Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • murra, myrra

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈmyr.ra/, [ˈmʏrːä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈmir.ra/, [ˈmirːä]

Noun[edit]

myrrha f (genitive myrrhae); first declension

  1. Alternative form of murra

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.

Case Singular Plural
Nominative myrrha myrrhae
Genitive myrrhae myrrhārum
Dative myrrhae myrrhīs
Accusative myrrham myrrhās
Ablative myrrhā myrrhīs
Vocative myrrha myrrhae

Descendants[edit]

  • → Old English: myrre

      • English: myrrh

        • German: Myrrhe
  • → Polish: mirra

  1. Pre-reform spelling (until Brazil 1943/Portugal 1911) of mirra.
