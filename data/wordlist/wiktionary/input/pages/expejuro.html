Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ek.spei̯ˈi̯uː.roː/, [ɛks̠pɛi̯ˈi̯uːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.speˈju.ro/, [ekspeˈjuːro]

Verb[edit]

expejūrō (present infinitive expejūrāre, perfect active expejūrāvī, supine expejūrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of expeiūrō
