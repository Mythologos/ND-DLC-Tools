Latin[edit]

tonitrus

Alternative forms[edit]

  • tonitruum

Etymology[edit]

For unattested *tonitus, from tonō (“I thunder”) +‎ -tus, with insertion of -r- after fulgetrum (“lightning”).[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈto.ni.trus/, [ˈt̪ɔnɪt̪rʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈto.ni.trus/, [ˈt̪ɔːnit̪rus]

Noun[edit]

tonitrus m (genitive tonitrūs); fourth declension

  1. thunder

Declension[edit]

Fourth-declension noun.
