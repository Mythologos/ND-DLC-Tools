Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • appropinquō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.proˈpin.kʷoː/, [äpːrɔˈpɪŋkʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.proˈpin.kwo/, [äd̪proˈpiŋkwo]

Verb[edit]

adpropinquō (present infinitive adpropinquāre, perfect active adpropinquāvī, supine adpropinquātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of appropinquō
