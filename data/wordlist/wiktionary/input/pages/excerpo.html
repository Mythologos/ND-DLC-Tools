Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- +‎ carpere.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eksˈker.poː/, [ɛks̠ˈkɛrpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eksˈt͡ʃer.po/, [eksˈt͡ʃɛrpo]

Verb[edit]

excerpō (present infinitive excerpere, perfect active excerpsī, supine excerptum); third conjugation

  1. to choose, select (pick out)
