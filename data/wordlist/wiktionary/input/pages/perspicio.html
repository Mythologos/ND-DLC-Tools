Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- (“through”) +‎ speciō (“observe, watch, look at”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈspi.ki.oː/, [pɛrˈs̠pɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈspi.t͡ʃi.o/, [perˈspiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

perspiciō (present infinitive perspicere, perfect active perspexī, supine perspectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. to see through something
  2. to perceive or discern clearly
