Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assevērō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ad.seˈu̯eː.roː/, [äs̠ːeˈu̯eːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ad.seˈve.ro/, [äd̪seˈvɛːro]

Verb[edit]

adsevērō (present infinitive adsevērāre, perfect active adsevērāvī, supine adsevērātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assevērō
