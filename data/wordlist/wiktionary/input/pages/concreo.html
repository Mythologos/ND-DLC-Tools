Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ creō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.kre.oː/, [ˈkɔŋkreoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.kre.o/, [ˈkɔŋkreo]

Verb[edit]

concreō (present infinitive concreāre, perfect active concreāvī, supine concreātum); first conjugation

  1. to create together with another
