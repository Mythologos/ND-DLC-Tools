Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sub- +‎ dēligō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈdeː.li.ɡoː/, [s̠ʊbˈd̪eːlʲɪɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈde.li.ɡo/, [subˈd̪ɛːliɡo]

Verb[edit]

subdēligō (present infinitive subdēligere, perfect active subdēlēgī, supine subdēlēctum); third conjugation

  1. to choose or select
