Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈkum.boː/, [äkˈkʊmboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈkum.bo/, [äd̪ˈkumbo]

Verb[edit]

adcumbō (present infinitive adcumbere, perfect active adcubuī, supine adcubitum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of accumbō
