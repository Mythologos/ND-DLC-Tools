English[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • bennah

Noun[edit]

benna (uncountable)

  1. (music, Antigua and Barbuda) A calypso-like genre of traditional music from Antigua and Barbuda.

Synonyms[edit]

  • ditti

Gaulish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Celtic *bennā, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ-. Related to Old Irish buinne and Welsh ben, men (“cart”).

Pronunciation[edit]

IPA(key): /benːaː/

Noun[edit]

bennā f

  1. carriage

Descendants[edit]

  • French: benne

Italian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin benna (“carriage”), from Gaulish benna (“carriage”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈbɛn.na/
  • Rhymes: -ɛnna
  • Hyphenation: bèn‧na

Noun[edit]

benna f (plural benne)

  1. bucket, grab

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Gaulish benna.

Noun[edit]

benna f (genitive bennae); first declension

  1. kind of carriage
