Proper noun[edit]

Velīnus m sg (genitive Velīnī); second declension

  1. The river Velino.
  2. A lake in the Sabine territories, between Reate and Interamna.
Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun, singular only.

Synonyms[edit]
  • (river): Avens, Mellinus
  • (lake): Velīnus lacus
Descendants[edit]
  • Italian: Velino

Etymology 2[edit]

Velia (“a town on the coast of Lucania, a colony of the Phocœans”) +‎ -īnus

Adjective[edit]

Velīnus (feminine Velīna, neuter Velīnum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Of or pertaining to Velia, Velian
