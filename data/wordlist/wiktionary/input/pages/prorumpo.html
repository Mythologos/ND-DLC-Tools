Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From pro- +‎ rumpo.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /proːˈrum.poː/, [proːˈrʊmpoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /proˈrum.po/, [proˈrumpo]

Verb[edit]

prōrumpō (present infinitive prōrumpere, perfect active prōrūpī, supine prōruptum); third conjugation

  1. to break or burst forth or out
