Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From sub +‎ -tus. Compare intus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.tus/, [ˈs̠ʊpt̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈsub.tus/, [ˈsubt̪us]

Adverb[edit]

subtus (not comparable)

  1. below, underneath, beneath
