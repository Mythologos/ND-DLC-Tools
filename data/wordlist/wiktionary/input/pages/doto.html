Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dōs +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdoː.toː/, [ˈd̪oːt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈdo.to/, [ˈd̪ɔːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

dōtō (present infinitive dōtāre, perfect active dōtāvī, supine dōtātum); first conjugation

  1. to endow
  2. to apportion (typically richly)
