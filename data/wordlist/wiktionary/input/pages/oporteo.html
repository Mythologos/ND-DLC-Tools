Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From oportet.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /oˈpor.te.oː/, [ɔˈpɔrt̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈpor.te.o/, [oˈpɔrt̪eo]

Verb[edit]

oporteō (present infinitive oportēre, perfect active oportuī); second conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (rare, pre-Classical) to require or order (something to be done)
