Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /saˈbur.roː/, [s̠äˈbʊrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /saˈbur.ro/, [säˈburːo]

Verb[edit]

saburrō (present infinitive saburrāre, perfect active saburrāvī, supine saburrātum); first conjugation

  1. (nautical) to ballast a vessel
  2. to cram full, stuff full
