Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /peˈra.ɡi.toː/, [pɛˈräɡɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /peˈra.d͡ʒi.to/, [peˈräːd͡ʒit̪o]

Etymology 1[edit]

From per- +‎ agitō.

Verb[edit]

peragitō (present infinitive peragitāre, perfect active peragitāvī, supine peragitātum); first conjugation

  1. (transitive) to harass with repeated attacks
