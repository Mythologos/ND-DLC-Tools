Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From vīlis +‎ -itō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯iː.li.toː/, [ˈu̯iːlʲɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvi.li.to/, [ˈviːlit̪o]

Verb[edit]

vīlitō (present infinitive vīlitāre, perfect active vīlitāvī, supine vīlitātum); first conjugation

  1. to make cheap
  2. to humble, debase, degrade
