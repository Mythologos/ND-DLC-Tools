Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /suˈbuː.roː/, [s̠ʊˈbuːroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suˈbu.ro/, [suˈbuːro]

Verb[edit]

subūrō (present infinitive subūrere, perfect active subussī, supine subustum); third conjugation

  1. to singe or scorch
