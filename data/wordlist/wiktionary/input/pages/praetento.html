Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prae- +‎ tento.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /prae̯ˈten.toː/, [präe̯ˈt̪ɛn̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /preˈten.to/, [preˈt̪ɛn̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

praetentō (present infinitive praetentāre, perfect active praetentāvī, supine praetentātum); first conjugation

  1. to test, try etc. beforehand
  2. to make a pretext of
