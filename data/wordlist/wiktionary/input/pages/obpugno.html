Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈpuɡ.noː/, [ɔpˈpʊŋnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈpuɲ.ɲo/, [obˈpuɲːo]

Verb[edit]

obpugnō (present infinitive obpugnāre, perfect active obpugnāvī, supine obpugnātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of oppugnō
