Adjective[edit]

laevus (feminine laeva, neuter laevum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. left; on the left side
    Synonyms: scaevus, sinister
    Antonym: dexter
  2. (figuratively) clumsy, awkward
  3. (figuratively) foolish
  4. unlucky
