Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ob- +‎ dormiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈdor.mi.oː/, [ɔbˈd̪ɔrmioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈdor.mi.o/, [obˈd̪ɔrmio]

Verb[edit]

obdormiō (present infinitive obdormīre, perfect active obdormīvī or obdormiī, supine obdormītum); fourth conjugation

  1. to fall asleep
    Synonyms: dormiō, obdormīscō, dormītō, obdormītō, cubō
    Antonyms: expergīscor, vigilō
