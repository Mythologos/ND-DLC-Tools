Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From dis- +‎ mētior.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /diːˈmeː.ti.or/, [d̪iːˈmeːt̪iɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /diˈmet.t͡si.or/, [d̪iˈmɛt̪ː͡s̪ior]

Verb[edit]

dīmētior (present infinitive dīmētīrī, perfect active dīmēnsus sum); fourth conjugation, deponent

  1. to measure (out or off)
