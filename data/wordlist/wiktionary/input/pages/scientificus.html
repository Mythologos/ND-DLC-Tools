Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From scientia (“knowledge”) +‎ -ficus (“making”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ski.enˈti.fi.kus/, [s̠kiɛn̪ˈt̪ɪfɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ʃi.enˈti.fi.kus/, [ʃien̪ˈt̪iːfikus]

Adjective[edit]

scientificus (feminine scientifica, neuter scientificum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Of or pertaining to science; scientific; producing knowledge.
