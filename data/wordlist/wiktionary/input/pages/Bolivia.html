English Wikipedia has an article on:
Bolivia
Wikipedia
 Bolivia, North Carolina on Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

From Spanish and New Latin Bolivia (“land of Bolivar”), from the South American revolutionary leader Simón Bolívar + -ia (“forming placenames”), from the Spanish surname Bolívar, from Basque Bolibar, a village in Álava, Basque Country, Spain, from Basque bolu (“windmill”) + ibar (“valley”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /bə.ˈlɪv.i.ə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /boʊ.ˈlɪv.i.ə/, /bə.ˈlɪv.i.ə/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪviə

Proper noun[edit]

Bolivia

  2. A country in South America. Official name: Plurinational State of Bolivia. Capitals: La Paz and Sucre.
  3. A small town, the county seat of Brunswick County, North Carolina, United States.
  4. (historical) Former name of Santa Cruz, an island in Galapagos, Ecuador.
