Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kumˈseː.pi.oː/, [kɪrkũːˈs̠eːpioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kumˈse.pi.o/, [t͡ʃirkumˈsɛːpio]

Verb[edit]

circumsēpiō (present infinitive circumsēpīre, perfect active circumsēpsī, supine circumsēptum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of circumsaepiō
