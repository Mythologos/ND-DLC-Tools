Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From per- +‎ madefacio.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /per.ma.deˈfa.ki.oː/, [pɛrmäd̪ɛˈfäkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /per.ma.deˈfa.t͡ʃi.o/, [permäd̪eˈfäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

permadefaciō (present infinitive permadefacere, perfect active permadefēcī, supine permadefactum); third conjugation iō-variant, irregular passive voice

  1. to wet through
  2. to drench thoroughly
