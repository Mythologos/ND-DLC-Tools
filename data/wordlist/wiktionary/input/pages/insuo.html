Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ suō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.su.oː/, [ˈĩːs̠uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.su.o/, [ˈinsuo]

Verb[edit]

īnsuō (present infinitive īnsuere, perfect active īnsuī, supine īnsūtum); third conjugation

  1. to sew up; to sew in or into
