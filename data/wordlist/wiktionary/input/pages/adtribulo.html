Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • attribulō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /atˈdri.bu.loː/, [äd̪ˈd̪rɪbʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /atˈdri.bu.lo/, [ät̪ˈd̪riːbulo]

Verb[edit]

adtribulō (present infinitive adtribulāre, perfect active adtribulāvī, supine adtribulātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attribulō
