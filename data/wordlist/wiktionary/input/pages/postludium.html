Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • post lūdium

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /postˈluː.di.um/, [pɔs̠(t̪)ˈɫ̪uːd̪iʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /postˈlu.di.um/, [post̪ˈluːd̪ium]

Noun[edit]

postlūdium n (genitive postlūdiī or postlūdī); second declension

  1. (New Latin, music) postlude

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).
