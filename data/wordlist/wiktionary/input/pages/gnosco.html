Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡnoːs.koː/, [ˈŋnoːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈɲos.ko/, [ˈɲɔsko]

Verb[edit]

gnōscō (present infinitive gnōscere, perfect active gnōvī, supine gnōtum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of nōscō
