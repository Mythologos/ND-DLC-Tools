Italian[edit]

Verb[edit]

distillo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of distillare

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /disˈtiːl.loː/, [d̪ɪs̠ˈt̪iːlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /disˈtil.lo/, [d̪isˈt̪ilːo]

Verb[edit]

distīllō (present infinitive distīllāre, perfect active distīllāvī, supine distīllātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of dēstillō
