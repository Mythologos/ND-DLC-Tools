Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio(file)
  • IPA(key): [fisˈtulo]
  • Rhymes: -ulo
  • Hyphenation: fis‧tu‧lo

Noun[edit]

fistulo (accusative singular fistulon, plural fistuloj, accusative plural fistulojn)

  1. (medical) fistula

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From fistula (“pipe, flute”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈfis.tu.loː/, [ˈfɪs̠t̪ʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈfis.tu.lo/, [ˈfist̪ulo]

Verb[edit]

fistulō (present infinitive fistulāre, perfect active fistulāvī, supine fistulātum); first conjugation

  1. to play the flute or shepherd's pipe
