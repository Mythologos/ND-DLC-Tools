Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /perˈsul.toː/, [pɛrˈs̠ʊɫ̪t̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /perˈsul.to/, [perˈsul̪t̪o]

Verb[edit]

persultō (present infinitive persultāre, perfect active persultāvī, supine persultātum); first conjugation

  1. to leap, skip or prance about
