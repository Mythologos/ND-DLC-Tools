Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ lacrimō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kolˈla.kri.moː/, [kɔlˈlʲäkrɪmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kolˈla.kri.mo/, [kolˈläːkrimo]

Verb[edit]

collacrimō (present infinitive collacrimāre, perfect active collacrimāvī, supine collacrimātum); first conjugation

  1. to weep with others
