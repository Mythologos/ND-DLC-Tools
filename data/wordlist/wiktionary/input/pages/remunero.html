Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From re- +‎ munero.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈmuː.ne.roː/, [rɛˈmuːnɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈmu.ne.ro/, [reˈmuːnero]

Verb[edit]

remūnerō (present infinitive remūnerāre, perfect active remūnerāvī, supine remūnerātum); first conjugation

  1. to reward, recompense, remunerate
