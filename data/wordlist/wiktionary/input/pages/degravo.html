Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ gravō (“to weigh down”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈdeː.ɡra.u̯oː/, [ˈd̪eːɡräu̯oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈde.ɡra.vo/, [ˈd̪ɛːɡrävo]

Verb[edit]

dēgravō (present infinitive dēgravāre, supine dēgravātum); first conjugation, no perfect stem

  1. (transitive) to weigh down, overpower, burden
