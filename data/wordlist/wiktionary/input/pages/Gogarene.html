English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Rhymes: -iːni

Proper noun[edit]

Gogarene

  2. The Greek and Roman name of Gugark, a historical province of the ancient kingdom of Armenia, in modern northern Armenia, northeast Turkey and southwest Georgia.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek Γωγαρηνή (Gōgarēnḗ).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡoː.ɡaˈreː.neː/, [ɡoːɡäˈreːneː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡo.ɡaˈre.ne/, [ɡoɡäˈrɛːne]

Proper noun[edit]

Gōgarēnē f sg (genitive Gōgarēnēs); first declension

  1. Gogarene (The Greek and Roman name of Gugark, a historical province of the ancient kingdom of Armenia, in modern northern Armenia, northeast Turkey and southwest Georgia)

References[edit]
