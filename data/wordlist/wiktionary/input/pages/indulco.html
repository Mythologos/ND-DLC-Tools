Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈdul.koː/, [ɪn̪ˈd̪ʊɫ̪koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈdul.ko/, [in̪ˈd̪ulko]

Verb[edit]

indulcō (present infinitive indulcāre, perfect active indulcāvī, supine indulcātum); first conjugation

  1. to sweeten
