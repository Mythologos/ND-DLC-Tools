Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • brasiō

Etymology[edit]

From or related to brasium (“malt”, noun).

Verb[edit]

braciō (present infinitive braciāre, perfect active braciāvī, supine braciātum); first conjugation

  1. (Anglo-Latin) to brew beer
