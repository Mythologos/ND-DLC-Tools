Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈtri.bu.oː/, [ät̪ˈt̪rɪbuoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈtri.bu.o/, [äd̪ˈt̪riːbuo]

Verb[edit]

adtribuō (present infinitive adtribuere, perfect active adtribuī, supine adtribūtum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attribuō
