Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- + vulgō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eːˈu̯ul.ɡoː/, [eːˈu̯ʊɫ̪ɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /eˈvul.ɡo/, [eˈvulɡo]

Verb[edit]

ēvulgō (present infinitive ēvulgāre, perfect active ēvulgāvī, supine ēvulgātum); first conjugation

  1. (rare) to publish or promulgate among the people
