Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ɡroe̯nˈlan.di.kus/, [ɡroe̯nˈɫ̪än̪d̪ɪkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ɡrenˈlan.di.kus/, [ɡrenˈlän̪d̪ikus]

Adjective[edit]

groenlandicus (feminine groenlandica, neuter groenlandicum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. Greenlandic
