Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ekˈfreː.noː/, [ɛkˈfreːnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈfre.no/, [ekˈfrɛːno]

Verb[edit]

ecfrēnō (present infinitive ecfrēnāre, perfect active ecfrēnāvī, supine ecfrēnātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of effrēnō
