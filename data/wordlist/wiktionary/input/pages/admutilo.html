Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • ammutilō

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards, at”) +‎ mutilō (“clip, crop”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈmu.ti.loː/, [äd̪ˈmʊt̪ɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈmu.ti.lo/, [äd̪ˈmuːt̪ilo]

Verb[edit]

admutilō (present infinitive admutilāre, perfect active admutilāvī, supine admutilātum); first conjugation

  1. to crop or clip close, shave; fleece
  2. (figuratively) to defraud, cheat
