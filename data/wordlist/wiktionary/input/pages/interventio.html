Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English intervention), ultimately from Latin interventiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈint(ː)erʋentio/, [ˈin̪t̪(ː)e̞rˌʋe̞n̪t̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -entio
  • Syllabification(key): in‧ter‧ven‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

interventio

  1. intervention

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From interveniō +‎ -tiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈu̯en.ti.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈu̯ɛn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈven.t͡si.o/, [in̪t̪erˈvɛnt̪͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

interventiō f (genitive interventiōnis); third declension

  1. guarantee, caution

Declension[edit]
