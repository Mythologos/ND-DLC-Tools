Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈpes.koː/, [kɔmˈpɛs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈpes.ko/, [konˈpɛsko]

Verb[edit]

conpescō (present infinitive conpescere, perfect active conpescuī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of compescō
