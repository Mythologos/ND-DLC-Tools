Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ob- +‎ ambulō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /oˈbam.bu.loː/, [ɔˈbämbʊɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /oˈbam.bu.lo/, [oˈbämbulo]

Verb[edit]

obambulō (present infinitive obambulāre, perfect active obambulāvī, supine obambulātum); first conjugation

  1. to walk up to or close to
  2. to walk about, stroll
