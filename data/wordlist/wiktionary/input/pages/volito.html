Esperanto[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /voˈlito/

Noun[edit]

volito (accusative singular voliton, plural volitoj, accusative plural volitojn)

  1. singular past nominal passive participle of voli

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Frequentative verb of volō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯o.li.toː/, [ˈu̯ɔlʲɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvo.li.to/, [ˈvɔːlit̪o]

Verb[edit]

volitō (present infinitive volitāre, perfect active volitāvī, supine volitātum); first conjugation, no passive

  1. to fly about
  2. to hasten
  3. to hover
