Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈre.li.kʷoː/, [ˈrɛlʲɪkʷoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈre.li.kwo/, [ˈrɛːlikwo]

Adjective[edit]

reliquō

  1. dative/ablative masculine/neuter singular of reliquus

Verb[edit]

reliquō (present infinitive reliquāre, perfect active reliquāvī, supine reliquātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of reliquor
