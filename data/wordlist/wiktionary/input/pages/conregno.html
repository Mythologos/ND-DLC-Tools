Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ regno.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈreːɡ.noː/, [kɔnˈreːŋnoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈreɲ.ɲo/, [konˈrɛɲːo]

Verb[edit]

conrēgnō (present infinitive conrēgnāre, perfect active conrēgnāvī, supine conrēgnātum); first conjugation

  1. to reign, rule, govern with another
