Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /saˈɡit.toː/, [s̠äˈɡɪt̪ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /saˈd͡ʒit.to/, [säˈd͡ʒit̪ːo]

Verb[edit]

sagittō (present infinitive sagittāre, perfect active sagittāvī, supine sagittātum); first conjugation

  1. to shoot arrows, shoot with arrows
