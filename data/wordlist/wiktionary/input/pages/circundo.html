Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kirˈkun.doː/, [kɪrˈkʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃirˈkun.do/, [t͡ʃirˈkun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

circundō (present infinitive circundare, perfect active circundedī, supine circundatum); first conjugation, irregular

  1. Alternative form of circumdō
