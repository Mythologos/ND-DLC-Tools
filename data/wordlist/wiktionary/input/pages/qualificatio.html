Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Late medieval; from quālificāre, quālificō (“to qualify”) + -tiō.

Noun[edit]

quālificātiō f (genitive quālificātiōnis); third declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) qualification

Declension[edit]
