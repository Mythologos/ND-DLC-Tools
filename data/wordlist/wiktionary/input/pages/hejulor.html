Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈhei̯.i̯u.lor/, [ˈhɛi̯ːʊɫ̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈe.ju.lor/, [ˈɛːjulor]

Verb[edit]

hejulor (present infinitive hejulārī, perfect active hejulātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. Alternative form of heiulor
