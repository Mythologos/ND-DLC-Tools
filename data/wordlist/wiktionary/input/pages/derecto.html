Interlingua[edit]

Noun[edit]

derecto (plural derectos)

  1. (law) right

Latin[edit]

Adverb[edit]

dērēctō (comparative derectius, superlative derectissimē)

  1. straight, in straight line
  2. directly, immediately, without intervening action
    Synonym: prōrsus

Participle[edit]

dērēctō

  1. dative/ablative masculine/neuter singular of dērēctus
