Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek βαρύτονος (barútonos).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /baˈry.to.nus/, [bäˈrʏt̪ɔnʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /baˈri.to.nus/, [bäˈriːt̪onus]

Adjective[edit]

barytonus (feminine barytona, neuter barytonum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. barytone
