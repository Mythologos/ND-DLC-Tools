Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ad- +‎ bastardus +‎ -ō

Verb[edit]

abastardō (present infinitive abastardāre, perfect active abastardāvī, supine abastardātum); first conjugation

  1. (Medieval Latin) to bastardize (claim or demonstrate illegitimacy)
