Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From prē- +‎ habeō (“have, hold”).

Verb[edit]

prēhibeō (present infinitive prēhibēre, perfect active prēhibuī, supine prēhibitum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of praebeō
