Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see French adoption), ultimately from Latin adoptiō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑdoptio/, [ˈɑ̝do̞pˌt̪io̞]
  • Rhymes: -io
  • Syllabification(key): a‧dop‧ti‧o

Noun[edit]

adoptio

  1. The adoption of a child.

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

ad- +‎ optiō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /aˈdop.ti.oː/, [äˈd̪ɔpt̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /aˈdop.t͡si.o/, [äˈd̪ɔpt̪͡s̪io]

Noun[edit]

adoptiō f (genitive adoptiōnis); third declension

  1. adoption

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
