Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /re.miˈniːs.koː/, [rɛmɪˈniːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /re.miˈnis.ko/, [remiˈnisko]

Verb[edit]

reminīscō (present infinitive reminīscere); third conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (rare) Alternative form of reminīscor

Usage notes[edit]

  • This verb is very rarely used. The passive reminīscor is usually used instead.
