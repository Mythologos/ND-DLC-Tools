Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /subˈmer.ɡoː/, [s̠ʊmˈmɛrɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /subˈmer.ɡo/, [subˈmɛrɡo]

Verb[edit]

submergō (present infinitive submergere, perfect active submersī, supine submersum); third conjugation

  1. Alternative form of summergō
