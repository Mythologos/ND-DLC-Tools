Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /obˈkae̯.koː/, [ɔpˈkäe̯koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /obˈt͡ʃe.ko/, [obˈt͡ʃɛːko]

Verb[edit]

obcaecō (present infinitive obcaecāre, perfect active obcaecāvī, supine obcaecātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of occaecō
