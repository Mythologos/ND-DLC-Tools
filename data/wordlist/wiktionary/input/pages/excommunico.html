Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ex- (“out”) +‎ commūnicō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ek.skomˈmuː.ni.koː/, [ɛks̠kɔmˈmuːnɪkoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ek.skomˈmu.ni.ko/, [ekskomˈmuːniko]

Verb[edit]

excommūnicō (present infinitive excommūnicāre); first conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (Late Latin) to excommunicate
