Etymology[edit]

From ūndēquadragintā (“thirty-nine”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /uːn.deː.kʷa.draːˈɡeː.si.mus/, [uːn̪d̪eːkʷäd̪räːˈɡeːs̠ɪmʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /un.de.kwa.draˈd͡ʒe.si.mus/, [un̪d̪ekwäd̪räˈd͡ʒɛːs̬imus]

Numeral[edit]

ūndēquadrāgēsimus (feminine ūndēquadrāgēsima, neuter ūndēquadrāgēsimum); first/second-declension numeral

  1. thirty-ninth
