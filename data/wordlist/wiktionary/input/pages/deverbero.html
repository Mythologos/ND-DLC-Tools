Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ verberō (“to beat”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈu̯er.be.roː/, [d̪eːˈu̯ɛrbɛroː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈver.be.ro/, [d̪eˈvɛrbero]

Verb[edit]

dēverberō (present infinitive dēverberāre, perfect active dēverberāvī, supine dēverberātum); first conjugation

  1. (rare) to lash, beat thoroughly
