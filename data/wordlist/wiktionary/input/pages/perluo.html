Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • pelluo

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈper.lu.oː/, [ˈpɛrɫ̪uoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈper.lu.o/, [ˈpɛrluo]

Verb[edit]

perluō (present infinitive perluere, perfect active perluī, supine perlūtum); third conjugation

  1. to wash off
  2. to bathe
