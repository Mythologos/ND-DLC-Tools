Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • adtentō
  • attemptō
  • attentō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈtemp.toː/, [ät̪ˈt̪ɛmpt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈtemp.to/, [äd̪ˈt̪ɛmpt̪o]

Verb[edit]

adtemptō (present infinitive adtemptāre, perfect active adtemptāvī, supine adtemptātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of attentō
