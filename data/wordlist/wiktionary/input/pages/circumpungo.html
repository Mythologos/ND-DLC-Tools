Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From circum- +‎ pungō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kir.kumˈpun.ɡoː/, [kɪrkʊmˈpʊŋɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /t͡ʃir.kumˈpun.ɡo/, [t͡ʃirkumˈpuŋɡo]

Verb[edit]

circumpungō (present infinitive circumpungere, perfect active circumpūnxī, supine circumpūnctum); third conjugation

  1. to prick around, to puncture around
