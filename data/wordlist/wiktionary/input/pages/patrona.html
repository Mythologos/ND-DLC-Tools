Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Feminine form of patrōnus (“patron”), from pater (“father”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /paˈtroː.na/, [päˈt̪roːnä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /paˈtro.na/, [päˈt̪rɔːnä]

Noun[edit]

patrōna f (genitive patrōnae); first declension

  1. female patron, protector, protectrix
  2. foster mother

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
