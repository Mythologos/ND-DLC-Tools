Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From in- +‎ spissō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈspis.soː/, [ĩːˈs̠pɪs̠ːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈspis.so/, [inˈspisːo]

Verb[edit]

inspissō (present infinitive inspissāre, perfect active inspissāvī, supine inspissātum); first conjugation

  1. to thicken
