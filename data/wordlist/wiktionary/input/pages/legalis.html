Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

lex +‎ -ālis.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /leːˈɡaː.lis/, [ɫ̪eːˈɡäːlʲɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /leˈɡa.lis/, [leˈɡäːlis]

Adjective[edit]

lēgālis (neuter lēgāle); third-declension two-termination adjective

  1. legal
  2. (relational) law
