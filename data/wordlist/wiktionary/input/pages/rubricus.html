Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Derived from ruber.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ruˈbriː.kus/, [rʊˈbriːkʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ruˈbri.kus/, [ruˈbriːkus]

Adjective[edit]

rubrīcus (feminine rubrīca, neuter rubrīcum); first/second-declension adjective

  1. red
    Synonyms: rubeus, rubicundus, rubidus, rubricatus
