Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

trāns- +‎ fīgō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈfiː.ɡoː/, [t̪rä̃ːfˈfiːɡoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈfi.ɡo/, [t̪ränsˈfiːɡo]

Verb[edit]

trānsfīgō (present infinitive trānsfīgere, perfect active trānsfīxī, supine trānsfīxum); third conjugation

  1. to thrust through
  2. to transfix
    Synonyms: peragō, fīgō, intrō, trāiciō, percutiō, cōnfodiō, fodiō, trānsigō
