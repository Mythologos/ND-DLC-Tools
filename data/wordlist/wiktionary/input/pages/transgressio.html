Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Internationalism (see English transgression), ultimately from Latin trānsgressus.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈtrɑnsˌɡresːio/, [ˈt̪rɑ̝ns̠ˌɡre̞s̠ːio̞]
  • Rhymes: -esːio
  • Syllabification(key): trans‧gres‧si‧o

Noun[edit]

transgressio

  1. (geology) transgression

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /transˈɡres.si.oː/, [t̪rä̃ːs̠ˈɡrɛs̠ːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /transˈɡres.si.o/, [t̪ränsˈɡrɛsːio]

Noun[edit]

trānsgressiō f (genitive trānsgressiōnis); third declension

  1. transposition
  2. transition
  3. transgression

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun.
