Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /deˈnu.do/
  • Rhymes: -udo
  • Hyphenation: de‧nù‧do

Verb[edit]

denudo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of denudare

Anagrams[edit]

  • udendo

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ nūdō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /deːˈnuː.doː/, [d̪eːˈnuːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /deˈnu.do/, [d̪eˈnuːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

dēnūdō (present infinitive dēnūdāre, perfect active dēnūdāvī, supine dēnūdātum); first conjugation

  1. to denude (lay bare)
  2. to uncover
  3. to plunder
