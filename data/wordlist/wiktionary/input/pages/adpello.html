Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈpel.loː/, [äpˈpɛlːʲoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈpel.lo/, [äd̪ˈpɛlːo]

Verb[edit]

adpellō (present infinitive adpellāre, perfect active adpellāvī, supine adpellātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of appello
