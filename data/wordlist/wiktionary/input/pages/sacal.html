Fala[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old Galician-Portuguese sacar, probably from Gothic 𐍃𐌰𐌺𐌰𐌽 (sakan, “to dispute, rebuke”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /saˈkal/

Verb[edit]

sacal

  1. to pull out, to extract
