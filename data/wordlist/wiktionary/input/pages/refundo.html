Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

re- +‎ fundō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /reˈfun.doː/, [rɛˈfʊn̪d̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈfun.do/, [reˈfun̪d̪o]

Verb[edit]

refundō (present infinitive refundere, perfect active refūdī, supine refūsum); third conjugation, limited passive

  1. (transitive) to pour back, pour out
