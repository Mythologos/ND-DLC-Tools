Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

dē- +‎ madēscō (“to become wet”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /de.maˈdeːs.koː/, [d̪ɛmäˈd̪eːs̠koː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /de.maˈdes.ko/, [d̪emäˈd̪ɛsko]

Verb[edit]

demadēscō (present infinitive demadēscere, perfect active demaduī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to become wet, be moistened
