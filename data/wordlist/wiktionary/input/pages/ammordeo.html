Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • admordeō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /amˈmor.de.oː/, [ämˈmɔrd̪eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /amˈmor.de.o/, [ämˈmɔrd̪eo]

Verb[edit]

ammordeō (present infinitive ammordēre, perfect active ammordī, supine ammorsum); second conjugation

  1. Alternative form of admordeō
