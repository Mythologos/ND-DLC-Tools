Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

con- +‎ fremō (“to murmur”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.fre.moː/, [ˈkõːfrɛmoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈkon.fre.mo/, [ˈkɔɱfremo]

Verb[edit]

cōnfremō (present infinitive cōnfremere, perfect active cōnfremuī); third conjugation, no supine stem

  1. (intransitive) to resound, murmur loudly
