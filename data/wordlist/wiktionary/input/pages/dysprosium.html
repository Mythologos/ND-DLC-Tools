English[edit]

Chemical element
Dy
Previous: terbium (Tb)
Next: holmium (Ho)

Etymology[edit]

From Ancient Greek δυσπρόσιτος (dusprósitos, “hard to get”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: dĭs-prōʹzē-əm
  • (US) IPA(key): /dɪsˈpɹoʊ.zi.əm/
  • Audio (Southern England)(file)

Noun[edit]

dysprosium (uncountable)

English Wikipedia has an article on:
dysprosium
Wikipedia
  1. A metallic chemical element (symbol Dy) with atomic number 66: a rare earth element with a metallic silver lustre.

Derived terms[edit]

  • dysprosium oxide
  • dysprosium trichloride
  • organodysprosium
  • radiodysprosium
 

Related terms[edit]
