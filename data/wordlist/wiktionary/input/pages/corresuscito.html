Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • conresuscitō

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ resuscitō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /kor.reˈsus.ki.toː/, [kɔrːɛˈs̠ʊs̠kɪt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /kor.reˈsuʃ.ʃi.to/, [korːeˈs̬uʃːit̪o]

Verb[edit]

corresuscitō (present infinitive corresuscitāre, perfect active corresuscitāvī, supine corresuscitātum); first conjugation

  1. to raise from the dead together
