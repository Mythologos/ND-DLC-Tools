Finnish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *laihna, borrowed from Proto-Germanic *laihną (compare Swedish lån, English loan).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɑi̯nɑ/, [ˈlɑ̝i̯nɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑinɑ
  • Syllabification(key): lai‧na

Noun[edit]

laina

  1. loan

Ingrian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *laihna. Cognates include Finnish laina and Estonian laen.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Ala-Laukaa) IPA(key): /ˈlɑi̯nɑ/, [ˈɫɑi̯n]
  • (Soikkola) IPA(key): /ˈlɑi̯nɑ/, [ˈɫɑi̯nɑ]
  • Rhymes: -ɑi̯n, -ɑi̯nɑ
  • Hyphenation: lai‧na
  • Homophone: laine

Noun[edit]

laina

  1. loan
