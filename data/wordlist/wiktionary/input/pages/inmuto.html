Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈmuː.toː/, [ɪmˈmuːt̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈmu.to/, [inˈmuːt̪o]

Verb[edit]

inmūtō (present infinitive inmūtāre, perfect active inmūtāvī, supine inmūtātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of immūtō
