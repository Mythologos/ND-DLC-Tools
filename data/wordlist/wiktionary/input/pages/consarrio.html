Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • cōnsāriō

Etymology[edit]

From con- +‎ sarriō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈsar.ri.oː/, [kõːˈs̠ärːioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈsar.ri.o/, [konˈsärːio]

Verb[edit]

cōnsarriō (present infinitive cōnsarrīre); fourth conjugation, no perfect or supine stem

  1. (transitive, rare) to hoe or take (thoroughly or to pieces)
