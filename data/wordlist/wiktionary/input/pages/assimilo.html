Catalan[edit]

Verb[edit]

assimilo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of assimilar

Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /asˈsi.mi.lo/
  • Rhymes: -imilo
  • Hyphenation: as‧sì‧mi‧lo

Verb[edit]

assimilo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of assimilare

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • assimulō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /asˈsi.mi.loː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɪmɪɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /asˈsi.mi.lo/, [äsˈsiːmilo]

Verb[edit]

assimilō (present infinitive assimilāre, perfect active assimilāvī, supine assimilātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assimulō
