Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈsen.ti.oː/, [äs̠ˈs̠ɛn̪t̪ioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈsen.t͡si.o/, [äd̪ˈsɛnt̪͡s̪io]

Verb[edit]

adsentiō (present infinitive adsentīre, perfect active adsēnsī, supine adsēnsum); fourth conjugation

  1. Alternative form of assentiō
