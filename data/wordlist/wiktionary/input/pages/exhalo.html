Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /eksˈhaː.loː/, [ɛks̠ˈ(ɦ)äːɫ̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ekˈsa.lo/, [eɡˈzäːlo]

Verb[edit]

exhālō (present infinitive exhālāre, perfect active exhālāvī, supine exhālātum); first conjugation

  1. to breathe out, exhale
