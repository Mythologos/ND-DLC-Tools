Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • allātrō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /adˈlaː.troː/, [älˈlʲäːt̪roː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /adˈla.tro/, [äd̪ˈläːt̪ro]

Verb[edit]

adlātrō (present infinitive adlātrāre, perfect active adlātrāvī, supine adlātrātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of allātrō
