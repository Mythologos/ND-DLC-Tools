Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /peˈrur.ɡʷe.oː/, [pɛˈrʊrɡʷeoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /peˈrur.ɡwe.o/, [peˈrurɡweo]

Verb[edit]

perurgueō (present infinitive perurguēre, perfect active perursī); second conjugation, no supine stem

  1. Alternative form of perurgeō

Conjugation[edit]

  • The perfect forms are shared with perurgeō.
