Italian[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ilˈlu.do/
  • Rhymes: -udo
  • Hyphenation: il‧lù‧do

Verb[edit]

illudo

  1. first-person singular present indicative of illudere

Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • inlūdō

Etymology[edit]

From il- +‎ lūdō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ilˈluː.doː/, [ɪlˈlʲuːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ilˈlu.do/, [ilˈluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

illūdō (present infinitive illūdere, perfect active illūsī, supine illūsum); third conjugation

  1. to mock, ridicule
  2. to abuse, assault
