Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From grātus (“pleasing, acceptable”) +‎ -ō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈɡraː.tor/, [ˈɡräːt̪ɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈɡra.tor/, [ˈɡräːt̪or]

Verb[edit]

grātor (present infinitive grātārī, perfect active grātātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. (mostly poetic) to manifest joy, wish one joy, congratulate, rejoice with, rejoice
