Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From ad- (“to, towards, at”) +‎ inveniō (“find, invent”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /a.dinˈu̯e.ni.oː/, [äd̪ɪnˈu̯ɛnioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /a.dinˈve.ni.o/, [äd̪iɱˈvɛːnio]

Verb[edit]

adinveniō (present infinitive adinvenīre, perfect active adinvēnī, supine adinventum); fourth conjugation

  1. to find out, devise, invent (in addition to)
