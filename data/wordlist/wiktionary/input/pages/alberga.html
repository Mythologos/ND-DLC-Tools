Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From earlier heribergo (attested in the Reichenau Glossary), from Frankish *harjabergu (“barracks; shelter”).

Noun[edit]

alberga f (genitive albergae); first declension

  1. (Medieval Latin) encampment, barracks

Declension[edit]

First-declension noun.
