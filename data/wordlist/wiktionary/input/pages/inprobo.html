Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈin.pro.boː/, [ˈɪmprɔboː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈin.pro.bo/, [ˈinprobo]

Verb[edit]

inprobō (present infinitive inprobāre, perfect active inprobāvī, supine inprobātum); first conjugation

  1. Alternative form of improbō
