Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Univerbation of ecce (“look!”, interjection) +‎ rē (“in fact”), ablative singular of rēs.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈek.ke.reː/, [ˈɛkːɛreː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈet.t͡ʃe.re/, [ˈɛtː͡ʃere]

Interjection[edit]

eccerē

  1. there!, would you look at that!, what do you know?, here we go (calling attention to an event as a cause of some emotion)
