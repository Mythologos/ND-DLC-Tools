Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /konˈu̯or.roː/, [kɔnˈu̯ɔrːoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /konˈvor.ro/, [koɱˈvɔrːo]

Verb[edit]

convorrō (present infinitive convorrere, perfect active convorrī, supine convorsum); third conjugation

  1. Archaic form of converrō.
