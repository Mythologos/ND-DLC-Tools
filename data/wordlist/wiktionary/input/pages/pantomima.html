Catalan[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): (Central, Balearic) [pən.tuˈmi.mə]
  • IPA(key): (Valencian) [pan.toˈmi.ma]

Noun[edit]

pantomima f (plural pantomimes)

  1. pantomime, mime (form of acting without words)

Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /pan.toˈmiː.ma/, [pän̪t̪ɔˈmiːmä]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /pan.toˈmi.ma/, [pän̪t̪oˈmiːmä]

Noun[edit]

pantomīma f (genitive pantomīmae); first declension

  1. pantomime performer (female)

Portuguese[edit]
