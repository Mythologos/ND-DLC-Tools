English[edit]

Chemical element
Be
Previous: lithium (Li)
Next: boron (B)

Etymology[edit]

beryl +‎ -ium

Pronunciation[edit]

  • enPR: bərĭl'ēəm, IPA(key): /bəˈɹɪl.i.əm/
  • Audio (US)(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧ryl‧li‧um

Noun[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
beryllium
Wikipedia

beryllium (countable and uncountable, plural berylliums)

  2. The chemical element (symbol Be) with an atomic number of 4; a soft silvery-white low density alkaline earth metal with specialist industrial applications.
  3. (countable) An atom of this element.

Synonyms[edit]

  • glucinium or glucinum (archaic)
