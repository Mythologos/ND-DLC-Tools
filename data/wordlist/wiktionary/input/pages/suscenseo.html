Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • succēnseō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /susˈken.se.oː/, [s̠ʊs̠ˈkẽːs̠eoː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /suʃˈʃen.se.o/, [suʃˈʃɛnseo]

Verb[edit]

suscēnseō (present infinitive suscēnsēre, perfect active suscēnsuī, supine suscēnsum); second conjugation
