Latin[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • foeniculum
  • faeniculum
  • fēniculum

Etymology[edit]

From fēnum +‎ -uculum.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /feːˈnu.ku.lum/, [feːˈnʊkʊɫ̪ʊ̃ˑ]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /feˈnu.ku.lum/, [feˈnuːkulum]

Noun[edit]

fēnuculum n (genitive fēnuculī); second declension (Late Latin)

  1. fennel

Declension[edit]

Second-declension noun (neuter).

Case Singular Plural
Nominative fēnuculum fēnucula
Genitive fēnuculī fēnuculōrum
Dative fēnuculō fēnuculīs
Accusative fēnuculum fēnucula
Ablative fēnuculō fēnuculīs
Vocative fēnuculum fēnucula
