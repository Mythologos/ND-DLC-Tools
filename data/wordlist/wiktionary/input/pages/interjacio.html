Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /in.terˈi̯a.ki.oː/, [ɪn̪t̪ɛrˈi̯äkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /in.terˈja.t͡ʃi.o/, [in̪t̪erˈjäːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

interjaciō (present infinitive interjacere, perfect active interjēcī, supine interjectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. alternative typography of interiaciō
