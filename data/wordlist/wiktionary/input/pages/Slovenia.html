English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Slovenia
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

Slovene (from German, from Slovene) +‎ -ia.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (UK) IPA(key): /sləʊˈviː.ni.ə/, /sləʊˈviːn.jə/
  • (US) IPA(key): /sloʊˈvi.ni.ə/, /sloʊˈvin.jə/
  • Audio (US)(file)

Proper noun[edit]

Slovenia

  2. A country on the Balkan Peninsula in Central Europe. Official name: Republic of Slovenia. Capital and largest city: Ljubljana. Part of Yugoslavia until 1991.
