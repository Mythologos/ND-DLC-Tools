Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈu̯os.ter/, [ˈu̯ɔs̠t̪ɛr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /ˈvos.ter/, [ˈvɔst̪er]

Determiner[edit]

voster (feminine vostra, neuter vostrum); first/second-declension determiner (nominative masculine singular in -er)

  1. Alternative form of vester ("your", "yours, "of you").

Declension[edit]

First/second-declension adjective (nominative masculine singular in -er).
