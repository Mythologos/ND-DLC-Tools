Latin[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /rei̯ˈi̯i.ki.oː/, [rɛi̯ˈi̯ɪkioː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /reˈji.t͡ʃi.o/, [reˈjiːt͡ʃio]

Verb[edit]

rejiciō (present infinitive rejicere, perfect active rejēcī, supine rejectum); third conjugation iō-variant

  1. Alternative form of reiciō
