Ido[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /imaɡiˈnɔr/

Verb[edit]

imaginor

  1. future infinitive of imaginar

Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From imāgō (“image; semblance”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /iˈmaː.ɡi.nor/, [ɪˈmäːɡɪnɔr]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /iˈma.d͡ʒi.nor/, [iˈmäːd͡ʒinor]

Verb[edit]

imāginor (present infinitive imāginārī, perfect active imāginātus sum); first conjugation, deponent

  1. to imagine, conceive
