Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /seːˈpen.sis/, [s̠eːˈpẽːs̠ɪs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /seˈpen.sis/, [seˈpɛnsis]

Adjective[edit]

seepēnsis (neuter seepēnse); third-declension two-termination adjective

  1. Inhabiting a seafloor seep
