Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

sē- +‎ claudō

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /seːˈkluː.doː/, [s̠eːˈkɫ̪uːd̪oː]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /seˈklu.do/, [seˈkluːd̪o]

Verb[edit]

sēclūdō (present infinitive sēclūdere, perfect active sēclūsī, supine sēclūsum); third conjugation

  1. (transitive) to cut off ([+accusative = something]) from (+ ā [+ablative = from something])
