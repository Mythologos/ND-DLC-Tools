Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Late Latin action noun of īnsiliō.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /inˈsul.tus/, [ĩːˈs̠ʊɫ̪t̪ʊs̠]
  • (modern Italianate Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /inˈsul.tus/, [inˈsul̪t̪us]

Noun[edit]

īnsultus m (genitive īnsultūs); fourth declension

  1. a scoffing, reviling, insult

Declension[edit]

Fourth-declension noun.
